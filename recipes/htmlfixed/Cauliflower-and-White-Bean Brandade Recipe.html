Cauliflower, Garlic, Mashed Potato, Olive Oil, White Bean, British

Molly O'Neill Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mashed potatoes
  • ⅔ cup chopped, cooked cauliflower
  • ½ cup mashed and sieved cooked white beans
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Place the potatoes, cauliflower, beans and garlic in a food processor and pulse until combined. With the motor running, drizzle in the oil. Transfer to a bowl and season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with Cauliflower Fritters and Stilton Rouille (see recipes).
