Raw, Lemongrass, Mint Leaves, Rice Vinegar, Thai

William Norwich About 1 1/4 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 leaves fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, trimmed, inner portion thinly sliced
  • 1 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Thai fish sauce
  • 1 ½ teaspoons soy sauce
  • ¾ teaspoon crushed red-pepper flakes

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and stir until sugar dissolves.
