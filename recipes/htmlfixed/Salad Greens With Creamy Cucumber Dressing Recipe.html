Raw, Vegetarian, Cucumber, Mixed Salad, Yogurt

Jacques Pepin Eight generous servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

The dressing:

  • ½ cup cucumber, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon dill
  • ½ teaspoon jalapeno pepper
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¾ cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

The greens:

  • 12 cups salad greens

Preparation

  1. Place the cucumber, dill, jalapeno and garlic in the bowl of a mini-chop or blender and process until creamy. Mix with the remaining dressing ingredients.
  2. Cut the salad greens into two-inch pieces and wash thoroughly in cold water. Drain and spin dry.
  3. At serving time, toss the greens with the cucumber dressing and serve immediately.
