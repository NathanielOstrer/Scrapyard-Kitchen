Marian Burros 3 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds asparagus
  • 10 roasted red peppers
  • 1 to 2 tiny hot red peppers
  • 1 medium clove garlic
  • ½ cup chopped red onion
  • 1 bunch cilantro to yield 3/4 cup chopped
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim asparagus by breaking off stem at tough part. Steam asparagus for 3 to 7 minutes, depending on stalk thickness.
  2. Rinse and julienne the red peppers. Wash, seed and mince the hot peppers. Mince the garlic, and chop onion. Wash, dry and chop cilantro. Combine all these ingredients, and then add 2 teaspoons lime juice. Stir well.
  3. When asparagus is cooked, drain and mix with red pepper salsa.
