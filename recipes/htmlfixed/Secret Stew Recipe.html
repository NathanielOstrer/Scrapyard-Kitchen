Beef, Onion, Parsley

Laura Cunningham Four to six servings Time50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds stew beef (preferably round)
  • 3 large onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ⅔ cup chopped celery (use the tender, inner stalks)
  • 1 cup loosely packed chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. In a large pressure cooker, combine all the ingredients except the parsley. Add enough water to just cover the meat, being careful that the total volume doesn't exceed more that 2/3 of the pot.
  2. Following the manufacturer's instructions for the pressure cooker, cover pan and cook for 45 minutes, or until fork-tender. Stir in the parsley and serve.
