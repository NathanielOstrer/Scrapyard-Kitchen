David Tanis About 1/2 cup Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup chopped parsley
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh marjoram, or 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, stir together all ingredients. May be refrigerated several days, but tastes best freshly made.
