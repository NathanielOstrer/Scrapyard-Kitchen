Lime, Tequila, Watermelon

Rosie Schaap

Marcus Nilsson for The New York Times. Food stylist: Karen Evans. Prop stylist: Angharad Bailey.

Ingredients

  • 3 parts watermelon puree
  • 1 part silver tequila
  • ½ part fresh lime juice
  • Seltzer
  • Sliced jalapeno
  • Coarse sea salt
  • Ice
  • Agave nectar (optional)

Preparation

  1. In a large pitcher or bowl, stir together the watermelon puree, tequila and lime juice.
  2. To sweeten, adjust with agave nectar.
  3. To serve, pour into glasses over ice, top each glass with seltzer, a pinch of sea salt and slices of jalapeno.
