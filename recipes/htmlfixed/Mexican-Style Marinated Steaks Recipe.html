Elaine Louie 4 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup dry red wine
  • ½ cup lime juice
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 4 beefsteaks, about 6 ounces each

Preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except steaks.
  2. Place steaks in a wide, shallow glass or ceramic bowl and add marinade. Turn steaks to coat all sides. Cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Wipe excess marinade from steaks before cooking. Grill to taste.
