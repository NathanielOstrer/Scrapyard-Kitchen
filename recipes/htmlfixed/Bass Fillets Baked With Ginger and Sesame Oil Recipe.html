Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Bass, Ginger Root

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 bass fillets (about 4 ounces each), all bones removed
  • 2 tablespoons thinly sliced scallions
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
  • 4 small cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 4 sprigs cilantro

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Divide the fillets between 2 large sheets of aluminum foil. Sprinkle the scallions, ginger, garlic and sesame oil evenly over the bass. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Place a cilantro sprig over each fillet and loosely wrap up the foil, making 2 packages. Bake for 7 minutes. Carefully unwrap the foil and divide the bass among 4 plates. Serve immediately.
