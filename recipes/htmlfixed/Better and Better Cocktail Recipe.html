Mezcal, Rum, Velvet Falernum Liqueur

Jan Warren

William Mebane for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¼ ounce John D. Taylor’s Velvet Falernum Liqueur
  • ½ ounce Smith and Cross Jamaican Rum
  • 1 ½ ounces Fidencio Clasico Mezcal

Preparation

  1. Add ingredients to an old-fashioned glass, add ice, stir briefly and garnish with a lemon twist.
