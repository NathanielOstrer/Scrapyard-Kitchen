Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Mint Leaves, Strawberry, Yogurt

Florence Fabricant 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pint ripe strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 3 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 8 ounces plain whole milk or fat-free yogurt
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place half the berry slices in each of four large stemmed goblets. Sprinkle each portion with a teaspoon of the sugar.
  2. Stir the yogurt to smooth it, then spoon all but one-fourth cup of it over the berries. Top with the remaining berries and a dollop of the remaining yogurt. Sprinkle with the remaining sugar and garnish each serving with a mint leaf.
