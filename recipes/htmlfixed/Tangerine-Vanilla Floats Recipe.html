Gluten Free, Nut Free, Tangerine Juice, Vanilla Ice Cream, American

Julia Moskin 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 large scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 3 cups freshly squeezed tangerine juice (from about 12 tangerines) or orange juice (see note)
  • Seltzer

Preparation

  1. Divide ice cream among 6 medium-size glasses or cups. Add 1/2 cup tangerine juice to each cup and top off with seltzer. Serve with a straw.

Tip

  • Fresh orange juice can be used instead of tangerine juice, but it should be very sweet and not too acidic. Try adding superfine sugar to taste.
