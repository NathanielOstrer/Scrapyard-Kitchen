Seltzer, Watermelon

Pete Wells Makes about 4 1/2 cups Time10 minutes, plus chill time

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups watermelon chunks
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • ⅓ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup superfine sugar
  • 2 ½ cups seltzer, chilled
  • Ice

Preparation

  1. In a blender, pulse the watermelon until smooth. Push it through a fine-mesh sieve. You should have at least 1 1/4 cups of juice.
  2. In a pitcher, combine 1 1/4 cups of the watermelon juice, the lime and lemon juices and the sugar. Whisk until the sugar has dissolved. Refrigerate until chilled. Add the chilled seltzer and stir gently. Serve over ice.
