Onion, Red Bell Pepper, Tomato, American

Jonathan Reynolds About 5 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 Spanish onions, peeled and diced
  • 2 beefsteak tomatoes, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely diced
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons chicken stock
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and cook, stirring, until tender. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer for 5 minutes.
