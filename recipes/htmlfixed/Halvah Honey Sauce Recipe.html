Halvah, Honey, Tahini, Middle Eastern

Melissa Clark About 1 cup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 1 Â½ tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • Pinch salt
  • 1 ounce halvah (2 tablespoons, firmly packed)

Preparation

  1. In a saucepan, bring cream to a simmer. Whisk in the tahini, honey and salt. Simmer the mixture, whisking, until it thickens to a custardlike consistency, about 2 minutes. Let mixture cool, then crumble in the halvah. Serve sauce with the flourless chocolate cake.
