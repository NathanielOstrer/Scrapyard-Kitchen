Butter, Fish, Sole Fillet, Tarragon

Lorna J. Sass 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoon dried tarragon or 1/4 teaspoon ground fennel seed or 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 4 tablespoons soft butter
  • 2 pounds fillet of sole
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. To make herb butter combine your choice of seasoning with soft butter and let stand for a few minutes.
  2. In one or two large skillets melt as much of the butter as you wish until it sizzles. Quickly saute the fillets for a minute or two one each side.
  3. Season with salt and pepper if desired.
