Grapefruit Bitters, Maraschino Liqueur, Rye Whiskey

Florence Fabricant 2 drinks

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces rye whiskey, preferably unaged like Dad’s Hat
  • 1 ounce maraschino liqueur
  • 1 ½ ounces lime juice
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • Dash grapefruit bitters
  • Lime wedges, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all ingredients except lime wedges in a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Strain into two old-fashioned glasses filled with ice. Garnish with lime wedges.
