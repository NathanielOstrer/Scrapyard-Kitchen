Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, Basil, Garlic, Italian

Melissa Clark About 1 cup

Ingredients

  • ½ cup pine nuts
  • ¾ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 ounces basil, stemmed (about 5 cups leaves)
  • 2 to 3 garlic cloves, coarsely chopped
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat a small skillet over medium heat and add pine nuts. Toast them, shaking pan and stirring, until golden brown all over, about 3 minutes. Pour nuts onto a plate to cool.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender and purée until smooth. Use immediately or store in refrigerator for up to a week, or freeze for up to 6 months.
