Vegetarian, Vegan, Jalapeno, Pineapple, Caribbean

Molly O'Neill Four servings

Ingredients

  • 1 medium tomato, peeled and seeded
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1 pineapple, 3 pounds, peeled, cored and cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 2 small jalapeno chili peppers, seeded, deveined and minced
  • 2 scallions, trimmed and minced
  • 1 cup minced parsley
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Squeeze all of the liquid out of the tomato and chop finely.
  2. Put the brown sugar in a glass or ceramic bowl. Add the vinegar. Stir until the sugar dissolves. Add the remaining ingredients and combine. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
