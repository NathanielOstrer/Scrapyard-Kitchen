Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Fat, Grapefruit Juice, Vermouth

Molly O'Neill Four to six servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups water
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 ½ cups fresh squeezed pink grapefruit juice, strained, chilled
  • 1 tablespoon vermouth

Preparation

  1. Put the water in a saucepan. Add the sugar. Bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring until the sugar has dissolved. Refrigerate until chilled.
  2. Combine the sugar mixture, grapefruit juice and vermouth in a bowl. Pour it into a sorbet machine and proceed as directed.
