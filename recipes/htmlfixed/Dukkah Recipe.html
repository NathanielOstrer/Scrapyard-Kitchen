Raw, Vegetarian, Nut, Spice, Middle Eastern

Melissa Fernandez Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons pistachios, toasted
  • 2 tablespoons hazelnuts, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted
  • 2 tablespoons coriander seeds, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds, toasted
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds, toasted
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt

Preparation

  1. Place ingredients in a mortar and pestle and pound to a coarse consistency, or pulse in a food processor.
