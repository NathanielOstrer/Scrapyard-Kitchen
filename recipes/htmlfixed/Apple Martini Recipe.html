Apple Liqueur, Melon Liqueur, Vodka

William L. Hamilton

Ingredients

  • 5 ounces vodka
  • 2 ounces Berentzen apple liqueur
  • 1 ounce Midori melon liqueur
  • slice of green apple, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine liquid ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake until chilled, then strain into an 8-ounce martini glass, and garnish with apple.
