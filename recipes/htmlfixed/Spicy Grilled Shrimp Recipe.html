Garlic, Lemon Juice, Shrimp

Molly O'Neill Twenty servings

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups olive oil
  • ¾ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 9 large cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 ½ teaspoons freshly ground pepper
  • ¼ cup Tabasco sauce
  • 10 pounds jumbo shrimp, in the shell
  • Kosher salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Whisk the oil, lemon juice, garlic, pepper and Tabasco sauce. Divide between 2 large bowls. Place half of the shrimp in each bowl and toss with the marinade. Refrigerate overnight. Preheat a grill or broiler. Grill or broil the shrimp until just cooked through, about 1 1/2 minutes on each side. Season with salt.
