Achiote, Corn Oil

Elaine Louie 1/2 cup

Ingredients

  • ½ cup corn oil
  • 1 heaping tablespoon achiote (annato) seeds (available in specialty food markets)

Preparation

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the oil and annato seeds over medium-low heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Cool and strain into a glass or stainless steel container. Store away from light and heat.
