Raw, Raspberry, White Liqueur, French

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About 1 cup Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 10-ounce package frozen raspberries
  • 2 tablespoons kirschwasser or other white liqueur

Preparation

  1. Use the raspberries while they are partly frozen. Put them into the container of a food processor or electric blender and blend thoroughly. Line a bowl with a fine sieve and pour in the raspberries. Press to extract as much liquid as possible. There should be about one cup. Blend with the liqueur and serve.
