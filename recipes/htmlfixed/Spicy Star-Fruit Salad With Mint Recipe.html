Hot Red Chili, Mint, Starfruit, Asian

Molly O'Neill 8 appetizer or 4 main-course servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup fresh coconut juice or water
  • 1 teaspoon rice-wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 red Thai chili, seeded and finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (see note)
  • 2 star fruits, thinly sliced
  • 1 unripe banana, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 cucumber, peeled, seeded and thinly sliced
  • ½ cup chopped mint leaves

Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the coconut juice, vinegar, sugar, chili, garlic, lime juice and fish sauce. Add the remaining ingredients and toss to coat. Serve with fragrant beef (see recipe).
