Green Bean, Red Onion, Sun Dried Tomato, Mediterranean

Marian Burros 2 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons chopped, dried tomatoes (not in oil)
  • 8 ounces green beans
  • 1 small red onion to yield 2 tablespoon minced
  • 1 medium clove garlic
  • 2 fresh sprigs marjoram to yield 2 teaspoons chopped
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon no-salt-added chicken stock
  • Few shakes of salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Soak the tomatoes in hot water.
  2. Wash, trim and steam the green beans for 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Chop the onion; mince the garlic, and wash, dry and chop the marjoram.
  4. When green beans are cooked, drain and cut in quarters.
  5. Drain the tomatoes, and mix with the green beans and the remaining ingredients. Season with salt and pepper.
