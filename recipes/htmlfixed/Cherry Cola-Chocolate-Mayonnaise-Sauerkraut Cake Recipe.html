Cherry Cola, Chocolate, Mayonnaise

Rozanne Gold 12 to 16 servings

Ingredients

  • Butter or oil for greasing pan
  • 2 cups unsifted flour plus enough to coat greased pan
  • ⅔ cup unsweetened Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 ⅔ cups sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup cherry cola
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, washed and drained well

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Grease and flour 10-inch tube pan.
  3. In medium bowl, sift flour, cocoa, baking soda and baking powder.
  4. In large bowl with mixer on high speed, beat eggs, sugar and vanilla for 3 minutes. Reduce speed to low, and beat in mayonnaise.
  5. Add flour and cola alternately. Mix in drained sauerkraut.
  6. Pour into pan, and bake for 50 to 55 minutes. Cool.
