Kosher, Orange Flower Water, Sugar, Middle Eastern

Florence Fabricant Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons orange flower water

Preparation

  1. Combine water, sugar and lemon juice in saucepan. Bring to boil, then simmer until reduced to little more than 1 cup.
  2. Add orange flower water and cook 2 minutes longer. Set aside to cool.
