Maple Syrup, Walnut

Florence Fabricant 6 servings Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup dry breadcrumbs
  • ½ cup finely chopped walnuts
  • ⅓ cup dark corn syrup
  • ⅔ cup pure maple syrup
  • Baked 9-inch pastry shell
  • Whipped cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Melt the butter in the skillet and stir in the breadcrumbs. Cook a few minutes, then mix in the walnuts. Mix with the corn syrup and maple syrup and spread in the pastry shell.
  3. Bake 30 minutes. Serve with whipped cream.
