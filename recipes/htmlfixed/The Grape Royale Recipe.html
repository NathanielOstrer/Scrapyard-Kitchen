Champagne, Grape Juice, Grappa

William L. Hamilton

David Corio for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¼ ounce dark purple grape juice
  • ¼ ounce grappa (Café Gray uses Nonino Merlot grappa)
  • 4 ½ ounces Vouvray Foreau, sparkling white wine or Champagne
  • Small bunch Champagne grapes
  • 1 tablespoon superfine sugar

Preparation

  1. Stir first two ingredients in a Champagne flute and top off with chilled sparkling wine. Garnish with grapes dusted in sugar, hung on the side of the glass.
