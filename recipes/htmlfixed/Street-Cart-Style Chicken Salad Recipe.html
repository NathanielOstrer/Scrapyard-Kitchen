Ingredients

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • lemon juice
  • chopped garlic
  • fresh oregano
  • coriander
  • turmeric
  • olive oil
  • mayonnaise
  • Greek yogurt
  • lemon juice
  • chopped fresh parsley.

Preparation

  1. Combine boneless chicken thighs with lemon juice, chopped garlic, fresh oregano, coriander, turmeric and olive oil; marinate in the fridge for up to 4 hours.
  2. Grill the chicken (or cook it in a skillet), then chop it into chunks.
  3. Toss with a dressing made with equal parts mayonnaise and Greek yogurt, plus some lemon juice and chopped fresh parsley.
