Almond, Dried Fruit, Orange Juice

Mark Bittman Time12 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 pound dried fruit stone fruit (apricots, plums or cherries, for example)
  • ½ pound blanched almonds
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 2 cups fresh orange juice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon rosewater

optional

  • 1 tablespoon anise liquor or 2 or 3 star anise (optional)

Preparation

  1. Mix fruit, almonds, pine nuts, orange juice, water, cinnamon, rosewater and anise liquor or 2 or 3 star anise, if using.
  2. Cover and stir every few hours for 12 to 24 hours. Serve when fruit is tender.
