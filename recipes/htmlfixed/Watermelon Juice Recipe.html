Lime, Seedless Watermelon

Susan Spungen Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 8 cups seedless watermelon chunks, chilled or frozen
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Preparation

  1. Place watermelon chunks in a blender and process until smooth. Strain, if desired. (You may also use a vegetable juicer.)
  2. Pour into glasses and garnish each with a lime wedge.
