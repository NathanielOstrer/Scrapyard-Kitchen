Cognac, Rye Whiskey, Vermouth, French

Jim Meehan 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce rye whiskey
  • 1 ounce cognac
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • ¼ ounce Bénédictine
  • Dash of Angostura bitters
  • Dash of Peychaud’s bitters
