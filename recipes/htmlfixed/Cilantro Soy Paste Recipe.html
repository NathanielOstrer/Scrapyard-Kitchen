Cilantro, Jalapeno, Soy Sauce, Asian

Molly O'Neill One-half cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
  • 2 jalapenos, stemmed and seeded
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender. Process until smooth, stopping as needed to scrape sides of the blender.
