Vegetarian, Olive Oil, Onion, Potato, Greek

Marian Burros 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-size potatoes
  • 4 medium-size onions
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons olive oil

Preparation

  1. Scrub potatoes and parboil them for about 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Cut potatoes and onions into quarters.
  3. In large skillet, preferably nonstick, heat oil and saute potatoes and onions until onions are brown and crunchy and potatoes are soft.
