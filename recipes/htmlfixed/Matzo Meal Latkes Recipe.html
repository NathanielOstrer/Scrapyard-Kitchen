Egg, Matzo Meal, Jewish

Nick Fox 2 to 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, at room temperature, separated
  • ½ cup whole wheat matzo meal (or 1 1/2 whole wheat matzos pulverized in a food processor
  • 1 tablespoon toasted wheat germ (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon cinnamon
  • Neutral oil for frying

Preparation

  1. Beat egg whites until stiff.
  2. Mix matzo meal with 1/2 cup cold water. Let it soak for 2 to 3 minutes, then mix in wheat germ, salt, sugar and cinnamon. Mix the egg yolks in well.
  3. Put a 1/4 inch of oil in a pan over medium heat. Fold the egg whites into the batter. When the oil is hot, but not smoky, add the batter, a round tablespoon at a time. When well browned, flip and brown the other side, about 2 minutes per side. Drain quickly on paper towels and serve hot, with sugar, honey or plain.
