Gluten Free, Berry, Cream, Nectarine

Karen Baar 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups ripe nectarines, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 1 pint raspberries
  • 1 pint blackberries
  • 2 tablespoons good-quality balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • Pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 ½ cups whipping cream
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lavender flowers (optional)
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a large nonreactive bowl, mix the fruit, vinegar, sugar and pinches of salt and pepper. Let sit uncovered in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine cream, sugar, lavender, orange zest and another pinch of salt, and whip until soft peaks form.
  3. Put fruit into serving bowls, and spoon a bit of whipped cream on top. Garnish with mint.
