Beef, Scotch, Steak

Glenn Collins 4 servings

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup Scotch
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 New York strip sirloin steaks, approximately 12 ounces each

Preparation

  1. In a container large enough to hold the steaks, combine all ingredients for marinade. Place steaks in marinade and leave in refrigerator, covered, overnight.
  2. Remove steaks from marinade. Broil to desired degree of doneness.
