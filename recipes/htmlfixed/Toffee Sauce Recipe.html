Heavy Cream, Sugar

Julia Moskin 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 6 ounces (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream

Preparation

  1. Place 1/4 cup sugar in a heavy saucepan, and cook over medium heat, stirring with a wooden spoon, until sugar turns light brown. Add remaining sugar 1/4 cup at a time, allowing each batch to brown before adding next. When all sugar is in pan, keep cooking until it turns dark brown. Add butter, and stir until melted. Stir in cream, and heat through. Pour into a jug, and serve.
