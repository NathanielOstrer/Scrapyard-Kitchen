Espresso, Flour, Semisweet Chocolate Chip

Barbara Whitaker 12 to 15 large cookies

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons instant espresso powder, slightly crushed
  • 8 ounces semisweet chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Cream butter with sugars until fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla.
  3. Combine dry ingredients, and beat into butter mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Drop by large spoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes; 10 to 12 for crispier cookie.
