Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Butter, Corn On The Cob, Cumin

Marian Burros 8 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 to 16 ears of corn
  • ½ to ¾ cup butter
  • 2 to 4 teaspoons ground cumin

Preparation

  1. Shuck corn. Bring enough water to boil in large covered pot to cover corn. Add salt, if desired. When water boils, add corn and cook, covered, about 5 minutes. Drain and serve with cumin butter.
  2. To make cumin butter, melt butter and stir in cumin; let stand about 5 minutes. To serve, roll corn in cumin butter, or use pastry brush to paint corn with butter.
