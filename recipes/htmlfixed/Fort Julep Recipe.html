Ingredients

  • Small bunch of mint
  • 2 ounces Lillet Rosé
  • ½ ounce green Chartreuse

Preparation

  1. Rub 2 or 3 mint leaves inside a julep cup (you can also use an old-fashioned glass).
  2. Add Lillet Rosé and Chartreuse, fill halfway with crushed ice and swizzle or stir. Add more crushed ice until mounded over the glass.
  3. Garnish with a small bouquet of fresh mint sprigs, spanked against the back of your hand just before being inserted in the cup.
