Rosie Schaap

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Susan Ottaviano. Prop stylist: Maeve Sheridan.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rhum agricole blanc (like Neisson)
  • 1 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • ½ ounce simple syrup
  • Lemon verbena for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all ingredients except lemon verbena with ice, and strain into a cocktail glass.
  2. Garnish with verbena.
