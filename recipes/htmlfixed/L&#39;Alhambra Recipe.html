Orange Juice, Sherry, Sparkling Wine

Peter Meehan 6 to 8 servings

Gordon M. Grant for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 750-milliliter bottle fino sherry, chilled
  • 2 cups freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 5 tablespoons orzata (almond syrup)
  • 1 tablespoon orange flower water
  • 1 750-milliliter bottle Spanish sparkling wine
  • 6 orange wedges, for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a pitcher, combine sherry, orange juice, orzata and orange flower water. Add ice. Stir vigorously for a minute or so. Strain into 6 or 8 large wineglasses.
  2. Top each with 3 to 4 ounces sparkling wine, garnish with orange wedge and serve.
