Vegetarian, Mint, Sugar, Tea, Moroccan

Amanda Hesser 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons loose Ceylon tea
  • 2 large bunches mint
  • ¾ cup sugar

Preparation

  1. In a large pan or teapot, combine 10 cups water, the tea, mint and sugar. Bring to a boil, then remove from the heat and let steep for 5 minutes. Strain into juice glasses or teacups.
