Raw, Basil, Strawberry, Tequila

William L. Hamilton 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Souza Hornitos tequila
  • 2 ounces sliced fresh strawberries
  • 6 torn fresh basil leaves
  • 1 ounce fresh lime
  • splash simple syrup
  • sprig of basil, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine first five ingredients in a shaker filled with ice. Shake.
  2. Pour into a 10-ounce old-fashioned glass. Garnish with the basil sprig. Do not salt the rim.
