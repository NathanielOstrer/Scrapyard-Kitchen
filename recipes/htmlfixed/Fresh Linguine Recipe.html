Linguine, Italian

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Preparation

  1. Bring 6 quarts of water to boil in covered pot.
  2. Add linguine and cook about 1 minute after water returns to boil. Drain and serve, using vegetable mixture from fish as sauce for pasta

Tip

  • If fresh pasta is not available any thin dried pasta, such as vermicelli, spaghettini or angel hair, may be substituted, but allow longer for it to cook.
