Horseradish, Mayonnaise, White Vinegar, Southern

Steven Raichlen 1 3/4 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 10 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons prepared white horseradish (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl, and whisk until smooth. Serve with barbecued chicken, or as a dip for grilled vegetables.
