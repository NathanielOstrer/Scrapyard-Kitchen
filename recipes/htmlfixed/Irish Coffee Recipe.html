Coffee, Heavy Cream, Irish Whiskey, Irish

Patrick Farrell 1 drink Time10 minutes

Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ½ cup heavy cream, cold
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Vanilla extract, if desired
  • 1 ½ ounces Irish whiskey
  • 6 ounces hot, fresh coffee.

Preparation

  1. Whip the heavy cream. Add 1/2 teaspoon sugar and a dash of vanilla, if desired, just before the cream starts to form soft peaks. Whip until firm.
  2. Pour the remaining 1/2 teaspoon sugar into a tall ceramic cup.
  3. Add the whiskey, then pour in the coffee to about an inch below the brim.
  4. Apply a generous dollop of whipped cream to the top.
