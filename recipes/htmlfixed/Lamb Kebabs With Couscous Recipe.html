Couscous, Lamb, Whole Milk Yogurt, Mediterranean

Amanda Hesser 4 servings

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup plus 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons Aleppo pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 clove garlic, smashed and chopped
  • Juice and grated zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 ½ pounds lamb shoulder, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Coarse salt
  • 1 ½ cups couscous
  • ¼ cup whole milk yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped mint

Preparation

  1. Whisk together 1/4 cup oil, pepper, cumin, cinnamon, garlic, lemon juice and zest. Add lamb and mix to coat. Cover with plastic wrap and marinate in refrigerator, at least 2 hours.
  2. Heat broiler and line broiler pan with aluminum foil. Bring 2 cups of water with 1/2 teaspoon salt to a boil. Pour couscous into a heatproof bowl, and stir in 1 tablespoon olive oil. Add half the boiling water, cover for 5 minutes, then stir and add the remaining water. Cover.
  3. While couscous steams, thread lamb onto skewers. Scrape marinade into small saucepan. Season lamb with salt. Broil, 4 minutes a side for medium rare. Remove lamb and keep warm. Add drippings to marinade. Bring to boil for a minute, remove from heat; whisk in yogurt.
  4. Fluff couscous with a fork and place on a platter. Place skewers on top and sprinkle with mint. Have each guest spoon sauce onto lamb.
