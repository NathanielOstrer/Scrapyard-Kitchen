Chinese Dried Mushroom, Fish, Ginger, Chinese

Marian Burros 3 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large dried Oriental mushroom
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 3 green onions
  • 1 pound fresh fish fillets
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons dry sherry
  • ¼ to ½ teaspoon sesame oil

Preparation

  1. Soak mushroom in hot water for 15 minutes; drain and slice thin.
  2. Mince ginger; slice green onions thin.
  3. Wash and dry fish; place in single layer in shallow baking dish; sprinkle with soy, sherry, ginger, green onions and mushroom.
  4. Place dish over simmering water; cover and steam 8 to 10 minutes or until fish tests done.
  5. Drizzle with sesame oil and serve.
