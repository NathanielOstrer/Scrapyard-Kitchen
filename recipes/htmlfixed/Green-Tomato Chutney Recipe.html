Vegetarian, Apple Cider Vinegar, Green Tomato, Raisin, Indian

Melissa Fernandez Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound green tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ medium white onion, chopped
  • ½ cup golden raisins
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon freshly grated ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon ground allspice
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup apple cider vinegar

Preparation

  1. Simmer all ingredients in a small covered saucepan for 30 minutes.
