Bell Pepper, Egg, Tomato

Moira Hodgson 4 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 red peppers
  • 1 green pepper
  • 3 ripe tomatoes
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped basil

Preparation

  1. In a heavy skillet soften the onions in the oil. Meanwhile, char the peppers and tomatoes over a gas flame and peel off their skins. Chop and add to the onions with salt and pepper. Stir, cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile beat the eggs seasoned with salt and pepper. Add to the vegetables and scramble until just set.
