Aperol, Nocino, Scotch, Vermouth

Robert Simonson 1 drink

Maddie McGarvey for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ ounces blended Scotch
  • ½ ounce Punt e Mes vermouth
  • ½ ounce Aperol
  • ⅓ ounce nocino, preferably Watershed
  • ¼ ounce single-malt Scotch, preferably a peaty one from Islay
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass three-quarters filled with ice. Stir until chilled, about 30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
