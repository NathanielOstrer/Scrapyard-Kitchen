Green Tomato, Vodka

William L. Hamilton 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Ketel One vodka
  • 1 ounce liquid from pickled green tomatoes
  • Thin wedge of a pickled green tomato for garnishing the rim

Preparation

  1. In a shaker filled with ice, combine vodka and pickling liquid. Shake and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with tomato wedge.
