Aged Goat Cheese, Baguette, Celery Heart, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Garlic, Sichuan Peppercorn, French

Molly O'Neill Four servings TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 thin slices baguette
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and smashed
  • 2 celery hearts, very thinly sliced
  • 6 ounces aged goat cheese, cut across into very thin slices
  • 2 teaspoons Sichuan peppercorns, cracked

Preparation

  1. Heat a grill or broiler. Brush the bread on both sides with the olive oil. Grill or broil until browned and crisp, about 2 1/2 minutes per side. Rub the garlic clove over each bread slice. Top with slices of celery and goat cheese. Sprinkle with the cracked peppercorns and serve.
