Florence Fabricant 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 small ripe Haas avocado
  • 4 5-inch-square slices multigrain sandwich bread
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ pound thinly sliced smoked turkey breast
  • ½ cup tangy corn relish (recipe follows)
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped watercress leaves

Preparation

  1. Halve and pit avocado; scoop flesh into a small bowl. Mash with a fork.
  2. Spread one side of each bread slice with mashed avocado. Season to taste with salt. Ruffle sliced turkey over two bread slices. Divide corn relish over turkey. Top with watercress; cover with remaining bread, avocado side down. Cut sandwiches in half diagonally, and serve at once.
