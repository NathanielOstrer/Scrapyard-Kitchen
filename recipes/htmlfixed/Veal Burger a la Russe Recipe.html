Ground Veal

The New York Times 1 serving Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ pound lean ground veal
  • 4 tablespoons fine fresh bread crumbs
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon corn, peanut or vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Preparation

  1. Put the veal in a mixing bowl and add half the bread crumbs, salt and pepper to taste, all the nutmeg and cream. Beat with wooden spoon to blend well.
  2. Gather the mixture into a ball and dredge on all sides in the remaining bread crumbs. Flatten the mixture into a round patty about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Heat oil in a small, heavy skillet and add the patty. Cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes. Turn the patty and continue cooking about 7 minutes.
  4. Remove the patty to a warm plate. Add the butter to the skillet and cook until foamy. When butter starts to turn light brown pour it over the patty.
