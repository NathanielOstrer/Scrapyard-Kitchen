Moira Hodgson 1 cup

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups peeled and diced fresh ginger
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Preparation

  1. In a small saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to simmer over high heat and cook until tender, about 30 minutes.
  2. Transfer to a food processor and puree until smooth. Strain the contents and reserve in the refrigerator for up to one month.
