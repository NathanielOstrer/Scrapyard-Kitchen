Nut Free, Cinnamon, Milk, Sugar, Sushi Rice, Greek

Amanda Hesser 4 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup short-grain rice (sushi or Arborio)
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 two-inch pieces of lemon peel
  • Ground cinnamon, for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. In a large saucepan (4 to 6 quarts), combine the rice and 4 cups water. Bring to a boil, and then lower heat and simmer 10 minutes.
  2. Add milk, sugar and lemon peel. Bring back to a boil, stirring frequently. Lower heat, continuing to stir, so milk simmers vigorously without bubbling over. Simmer until the liquid is reduced and thickened to a creamy consistency that will coat the back of a spoon, 10 to 15 minutes. Stir frequently to keep rice from sticking to bottom of pan.
  3. Discard lemon peel. Spoon equal amounts of rice into four serving bowls, and let sit for a minute. Sprinkle with cinnamon, and serve.
