Raw, Cassis, Champagne, French

Amanda Hesser Aperitifs for 6

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Cassis
  • 1 bottle good Champagne
  • 6 twists of lemon

Preparation

  1. Fill the bottom of 6 Champagne glasses with 1/2 tablespoon Cassis. Pop open a bottle of Champagne and divide among the glasses. The color of the drink should be a sunset pink. Garnish with lemon twists.
