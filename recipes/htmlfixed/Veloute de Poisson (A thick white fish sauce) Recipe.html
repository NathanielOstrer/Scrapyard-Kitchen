Vegetarian, Nut Free, Butter, Fish Broth, Flour, French

Craig Claiborne about 1 3/4 cups TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 6 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups fish broth
  • Salt to taste if desired

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan and add the flour, stirring with a wire whisk.
  2. When blended and smooth, add the broth, stirring rapidly with the whisk. Add salt. Cook, stirring often from the bottom, about 10 minutes.
