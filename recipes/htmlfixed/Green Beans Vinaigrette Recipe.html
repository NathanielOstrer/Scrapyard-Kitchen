Low Calorie, Green Bean

Marian Burros 2 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ pound green beans
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt (optional)

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim beans. Arrange in steamer over a little water, and steam until the beans are tender but still crisp, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Whisk oil and vinegar together in a serving bowl. When beans are cooked, drain thoroughly, and stir well with dressing to coat. Season with pepper and salt.
