Maltum, Rum

Pete Wells 1 cocktail

Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces amber rum
  • 2 ounces malta (carbonated malt beverage)
  • ¾ ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • 1 egg yolk
  • Ground cinnamon for garnish

Preparation

  1. Add ice to a glass cocktail shaker. Pour in all ingredients. Shake vigorously for 20 seconds to emulsify egg yolk. Strain into a Collins glass filled with ice. Garnish with ground cinnamon.
