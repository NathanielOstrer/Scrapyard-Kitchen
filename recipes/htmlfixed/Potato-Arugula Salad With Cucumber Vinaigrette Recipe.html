Arugula, Cucumber Juice, Red Potato

Molly O'Neill 4 servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 medium red potatoes (1 1/2 pounds), cut into 1-inch dice
  • 1 cup arugula leaves, coarsely chopped
  • ½ cup cucumber vinaigrette with basil and garlic (see recipe)
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Place the potatoes in a saucepan, cover with water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes. Drain and let cool. Place in a large bowl and toss well with the arugula. Mix in the vinaigrette and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve cold or at room temperature.
