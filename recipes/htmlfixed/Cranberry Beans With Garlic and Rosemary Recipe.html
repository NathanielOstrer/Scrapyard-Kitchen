Raw, Vegetarian, Vegan, Cranberry Bean, Garlic, Rosemary

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked cranberry beans
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 small cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh rosemary
  • ¾ teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Place the cranberry beans in a bowl and toss in the remaining ingredients. Serve as a salad or as a condiment for grilled meats or toss with cooked pasta.
