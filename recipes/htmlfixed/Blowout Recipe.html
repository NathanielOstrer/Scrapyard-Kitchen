Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • 1 stick butter
  • Â½ cup heavy cream
  • parsley or chives for garnish

Preparation

  1. Boil starchy or all-purpose potatoes until soft; drain.
  2. Wipe the pot and return to very low heat.
  3. Mash with 1 stick butter; then stir in 1/2 cup heavy cream.
  4. Garnish: Parsley or chives.
