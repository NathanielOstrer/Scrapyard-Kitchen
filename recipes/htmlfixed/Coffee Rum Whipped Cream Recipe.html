Heavy Cream, Instant Espresso, Rum

Molly O'Neill Two cups

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons instant espresso powder
  • 1 tablespoon rum
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Preparation

  1. Place the espresso powder in a small bowl. Add the rum and stir until completely dissolved. Set aside. Whip the cream with an electric mixer until it begins to thicken. Gradually add the sugar. Add the espresso mixture and whip just until very soft peaks forms. Use immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 hours, stirring gently before serving.
