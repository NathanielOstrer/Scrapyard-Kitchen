Carrot, Parsnip, Potato

Pierre Franey

Ingredients

  • 6 carrots, about 1 pound, well trimmed and scraped
  • 4 parsnips, about 1 pound, well trimmed and scraped
  • 6 medium-sized potatoes, about 2 pounds, peeled
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Cut each carrot into rounds about 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Cut the parsnips crosswise into 1-inch lengths.
  3. Cut the potatoes into rounds about 1 inch thick.
  4. Put the vegetables in a saucepan and add water to cover and salt. Bring to a boil. Simmer for about 15 minutes or until tender. Drain.
  5. Put the vegetables through a food mill or ricer into a saucepan. Reheat while adding the butter, salt, pepper, parsley and nutmeg. Blend well. Serve piping hot.
