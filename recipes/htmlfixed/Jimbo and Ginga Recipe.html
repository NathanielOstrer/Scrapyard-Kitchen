Bourbon, Ginger Ale

Robert Simonson 1 drink

Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 8-ounce can of ginger ale
  • ¼ ounce fresh lemon juice
  • Angostura bitters
  • 1 50-milliliter bottle of Jim Beam bourbon
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Open the can of ginger ale and use a citrus reamer to widen the hole. Pour out 1 ounce of the soda, then pour in the fresh lemon juice and a dash of Angostura bitters.
  2. Place a straw in the can, then quickly invert the mini-bottle of bourbon into the hole in the can. Garnish top of can with the lemon wedge.
