Cornichon, Macaroni, Tuna

Amanda Hesser Serves 6 Time5 minutes

Dwight Eschliman for The New York Times. Food Stylist: Ann Belden. Prop Stylist: Hiroshi Yoshida.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • ½ pound uncooked elbow macaroni
  • ½ cup 1/4 -inch diced celery
  • 2 ounces sharp Cheddar, cut into 1/4 -inch cubes
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced scallions
  • ¼ cup sliced cornichon rounds
  • 2 tablespoons mild Tennessee chow chow (optional)
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 6-ounce cans chunk light tuna in water, drained
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni and cook. Drain and cool completely.
  2. In a large bowl, fold together the macaroni with the remaining ingredients. Season.
