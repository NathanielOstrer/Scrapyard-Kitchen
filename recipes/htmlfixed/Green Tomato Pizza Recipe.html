Vegetarian, Green Tomato, Parmesan, Pizza Dough, Italian

Mark Bittman 1 large, 2 medium, or more smaller pizzas Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large or 4 small green tomatoes
  • Coarse salt
  • Pizza dough (see recipe)
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • Â½ cup coarsely chopped or torn basil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Thinly slice tomatoes, salt lightly and let sit at least 20 minutes, then drain off any accumulated liquid.
  3. Top pizza rounds with tomato slices and Parmesan. Bake about 10 minutes, or until nearly done. Sprinkle with basil and cook until done.
