Brandy, Creme De Menthe

William L. Hamilton 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces brandy or vodka
  • 1 ounce white creme de menthe
  • Andes chocolate mint candy (optional, most bars don't do this)

Preparation

  1. Combine brandy or vodka with creme de menthe in a chilled glass, and stir. Garnish with mint candy.
