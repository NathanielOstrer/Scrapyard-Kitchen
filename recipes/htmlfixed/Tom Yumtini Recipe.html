Raw, Lemongrass, Lime Leaves, Vodka, Asian

William L. Hamilton

Phil Mansfield for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 750-milliter bottle vodka
  • 2 stalks lemon grass
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • 1 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1 3-inch piece fresh lemon grass

Preparation

  1. Infuse the vodka with the lemon grass and kaffir lime overnight.
  2. Shake 3 ounces of vodka with lemon juice, syrup and ice. Strain into a glass and garnish with lemon grass.
