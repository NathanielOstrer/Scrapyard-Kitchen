Vegetarian, Vegan, Orange Juice, Rosemary, White Wine

Molly O'Neill About 3 1/2 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orange juice
  • ¼ teaspoon grated orange zest
  • 5 branches fresh rosemary
  • 1 small jalapeno pepper, cut across into thin slices
  • ½ cup white wine
  • 2 cups water

Preparation

  1. Place the orange juice, orange zest, rosemary, jalapeno pepper, the white wine and the water into the desired steaming equipment. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 minute. Begin steaming. Add boiling water if too much of the liquid evaporates before cooking is complete.
