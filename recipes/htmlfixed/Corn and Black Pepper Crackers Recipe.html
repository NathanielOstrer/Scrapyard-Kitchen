Vegetarian, Black Pepper, Cornmeal

Melissa Clark 36 crackers Time35 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • Butter for parchment, plus 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • ½ cup medium-grind cornmeal
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¾ teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 large egg

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Line 3 baking sheets with parchment paper; butter parchment paper, or use nonstick liners.
  2. Sift cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, pepper and salt into a large bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat milk with egg. Add to dry ingredients all at once and mix together just until batter is free of lumps. Stir in melted butter.
  4. Drop batter by the tablespoonful onto prepared baking sheets. Bake until edges are a dark golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
