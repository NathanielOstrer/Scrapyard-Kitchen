Gluten Free, Greek Yogurt, Tomato

Amanda Hesser Makes 1 quart TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 21 ounces Greek yogurt
  • 8 ounces tomato water (above)
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons light corn syrup

Preparation

  1. In a small pan, dissolve sugar in 2 tablespoons water and bring to a boil. Let cool. Whisk together all the ingredients, then pour into an ice-cream maker and follow manufacturer's instructions. Serve as a garnish with gazpacho or tomato terrine.
