Cider Vinegar, Corn Syrup, Tomato Paste, American

Florence Fabricant 1 3/4 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 6-ounce can tomato paste
  • ⅓ cup white corn syrup
  • ⅓ cup cider vinegar
  • 4 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon Cayenne pepper (or to taste)
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine tomato paste, corn syrup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce.
  2. Add Cayenne pepper to taste and additional salt if desired. Use sauce uncooked or simmer briefly, about 10 minutes, to mellow flavors before using.
