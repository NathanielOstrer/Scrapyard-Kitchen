Ingredients

  • ½ cup finely chopped white or Spanish onions
  • 2 cups unpeeled, unseeded, cored tomato cut into 1/4-inch cubes
  • 1 ½ teaspoons or more finely chopped fresh, seeded jalapeno peppers
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • ¼ cup finely chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl and blend well. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
