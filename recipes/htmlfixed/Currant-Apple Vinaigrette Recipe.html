Ingredients

  • ½ pint fresh red currants
  • 1 large green apple, peeled
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste
  • Few drops porcini oil

Preparation

  1. Press currants through a fine sieve. Juice apple with a fresh-juice extractor. (If you do not have a juice extractor, you may substitute 1/4 cup fresh apple cider and a drop of lemon juice.)
  2. Combine currants with apple juice and slowly whisk in olive oil. Season with salt and white pepper, and add just a few drops of porcini oil. Serve over tuna or pork chops.
