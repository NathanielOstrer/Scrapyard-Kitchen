Vegetarian, Vegan, Potato

Molly O'Neill Six servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 baking potatoes, peeled
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil, plus additional for greasing baking sheet
  • 1 tablespoon hot chili oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Use a vegetable peeler to cut the potatoes into long, thin strips. Press between paper towels to dry. Place in large bowl and toss with the olive and chili oils.
  2. Oil 2 baking sheets well. Arrange as many potato strips as possible on the pans, keeping them in a single layer. Rotate the trays halfway through cooking if your oven heats unevenly. Bake until browned and crisp, about 10 minutes. Repeat until all of the strips are baked. Sprinkle with salt just before serving.
