Onion, Orange Juice, Red Cabbage

Pierre Franey 4 servings TimeAbout 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-size red cabbage, about 2 pounds
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 whole cloves
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice

Preparation

  1. Quarter and core cabbage and shred quarters fine. There should be about 10 cups.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in large saucepan and add onion. Cook, stirring, until wilted. Add cabbage, cloves, salt and pepper and cook, stirring, 5 minutes. Add orange juice and cover. Cook, stirring occasionally, about 45 minutes. Stir in remaining butter and serve.
