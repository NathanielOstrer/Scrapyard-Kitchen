Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Aperol
  • ½ ounce mezcal (something not too smoky, like a Fidencio Clásico)
  • 3 ounces prosecco
  • 1 ½ ounces soda water
  • Lemon for garnish.

Preparation

  1. Fill a large wineglass about three-quarters full with ice.
  2. Add ingredients, and garnish with a lemon twist.
