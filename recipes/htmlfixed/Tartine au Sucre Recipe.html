Heavy Cream, Maple Sugar, Maple White Bread, French

Florence Fabricant 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 slices maple white bread, each about 1 1/2 inches thick (recipe follows)
  • 1 cup maple sugar (see note)
  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • ½ cup sour cream

Preparation

  1. Place a slice of bread on each of four plates and sprinkle heavily with maple sugar.
  2. Whip the cream until it begins to hold its shape but can still be poured. Fold in the sour cream. Top each serving with the cream.

Tip

  • Maple sugar is sold in fancy food shops. Alternatively, you can grate molded maple sugar candies to make maple sugar.
