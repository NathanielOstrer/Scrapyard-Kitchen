Vegetarian, Brussels Sprout, Butter

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ pounds brussels sprouts, about 5 cups
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 whole cloves

Preparation

  1. Cut off the bottom of each sprout. Cut a cross in the bottom of each sprout.
  2. Put the sprouts in a saucepan and add cold water to cover and salt. Bring to the boil and let cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Drain thoroughly in a sieve.
  3. Heat the butter in a skillet and add the onion. Cook, stirring, until wilted. Add the sprouts and the cloves. Cook, stirring gently and shaking the skillet, about 1 minute.
