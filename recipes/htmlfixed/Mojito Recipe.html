Raw, Lime Juice, Mint Leaves, Rum, Cuban

William L. Hamilton 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce mint leaves, torn in half
  • 2 ounces fresh lime juice
  • 1 ½ ounces white rum
  • 1 teaspoon extra-fine granulated sugar
  • Crushed ice
  • 4 ounces club soda

Preparation

  1. Muddle (crush with a pestle) the mint with the lime juice in the bottom of a tall cocktail glass.
  2. Add the rum, sugar, crushed ice and soda. Cover and shake, and uncover, serving with a lime wedge.
