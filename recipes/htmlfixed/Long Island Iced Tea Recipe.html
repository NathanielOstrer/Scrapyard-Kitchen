Raw, Gin, Tequila, Vodka

William L. Hamilton 1 drink

Ingredients

  • ½ ounce vodka
  • ½ ounce gin
  • ½ ounce white rum
  • ½ ounce tequila
  • ½ ounce Cointreau
  • ¼ ounce dark beer like Bass
  • Sour mix
  • Splash of fresh lemon juice
  • Splash of Coke

Preparation

  1. In a shaker, mix all ingredients except Coke and sour mix; shake, and pour into a 12-ounce glass with ice. Top up glass to 1/2 inch from top with sour mix. Add Coke until drink is the color of weak iced tea. Garnish with a lemon wedge.
