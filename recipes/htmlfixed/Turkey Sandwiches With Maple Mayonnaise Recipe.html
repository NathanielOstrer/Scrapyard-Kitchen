Apple, Turkey

Melissa Clark 4 sandwiches Time15 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 ½ teaspoons maple syrup
  • ½ pound turkey or smoked turkey, sliced
  • 1 apple, thinly sliced

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise and maple syrup; spread on 4 slices of bread, going all the way to the edges. Top with the turkey and apple slices and then with the remaining bread.
