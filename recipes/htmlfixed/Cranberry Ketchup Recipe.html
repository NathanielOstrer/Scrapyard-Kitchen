Vegetarian, Vegan, Cranberry

Barbara Kafka 8 cups Time42 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds fresh cranberries (12 cups)
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • ¾ cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 pound light brown sugar
  • 1 ½ ounces fresh ginger, peeled and grated (about 2 tablespoons)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground allspice
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cloves

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a 5-quart casserole with a tight-fitting lid. Cook, covered, at 100 percent power in a high-power oven for 15 minutes.
  2. Uncover and stir well. Cook, uncovered, for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove from oven. Pass mixture through a food mill fitted with a fine disk.
  4. Pour into sterilized jars. Keep refrigerated.
