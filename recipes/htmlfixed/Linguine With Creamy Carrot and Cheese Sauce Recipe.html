Vegetarian, Carrot, Pasta

Marian Burros 2 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces peeled baby carrots or sliced ready-cut carrots
  • 2 ounces Parmigiano Reggiano (4 tablespoons)
  • ½ cup reduced-fat ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup nonfat plain yogurt
  • 8 ounces fresh spinach linguine
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Bring water to boil in covered pot for pasta.
  2. Slice baby carrots in food processor. Bring to boil in water to cover, and cook about 5 minutes, until crisp but tender.
  3. Meanwhile, wipe out food processor and grate Parmigiano; set aside.
  4. Blend ricotta and yogurt in food processor until mixture is smooth; set aside.
  5. Cook pasta according to directions.
  6. When carrots are cooked, drain and puree in food processor; mix with ricotta-yogurt mixture, Parmigiano and nutmeg; season with pepper and salt.
  7. Drain pasta and top with carrot sauce.
