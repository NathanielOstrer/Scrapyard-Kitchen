Gin, Lemon, Simple Syrup

Mark Bittman

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • ¼ cup gin
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon simple syrup
  • Club soda
  • Lemon wedge

Preparation

  1. Combine lots of ice, gin, lemon juice and simple syrup in a mixing glass and shake. Strain into a glass of ice and top with club soda. Garnish: Lemon wedge.
