Fruit Liqueur, Strawberry, Sugar

Bryan Miller Enough for 8 souffles Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ pint fresh strawberries, cleaned and sliced thinly
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons kirsch or other fruit liqueur

Preparation

  1. Place strawberries and sugar in saucepan over medium heat. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes. Add liqueur and keep warm until serving.
