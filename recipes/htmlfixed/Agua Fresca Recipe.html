Cantaloupe, Fruit, Lime Juice

Mark Bittman 4 to 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe cantaloupe or honeydew, seeded and diced; or 1 small watermelon, seeded and diced; or 1 pineapple, diced, or other fruit, similarly prepared
  • ¼ cup sugar, more or less, to taste, optional
  • Chopped mint, optional
  • Juice of 1 lime

Preparation

  1. Purée fruit in a blender, adding just enough water (or water and ice) to blend. Strain purée through a fine sieve if it is fibrous or seedy.
  2. Put purée in a pitcher, adding more water (or water and ice) to make a total of 8 cups. Stir in sugar and mint, if using, and lime juice to taste. Serve.
