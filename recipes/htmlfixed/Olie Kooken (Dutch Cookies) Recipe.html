Vegetarian, Egg, Flour, Raisin, German

Florence Fabricant About 40 small doughnut-like cookies Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup raisins
  • Scant 1/2 cup lukewarm milk

Preparation

  1. Beat the eggs and 3/4 cup of the sugar together. Whisk the flour, baking powder together and add this mixture to the egg mixture alternately with the milk. Fold in the raisins.
  2. Heat cooking oil to a depth of 2 inches in a heavy saucepan or deep fat fryer. When the oil reaches 350 degrees, drop tablespoons of the batter into the fat and fry until golden.
  3. Drain on absorbent paper and dust with the remaining sugar before serving.
