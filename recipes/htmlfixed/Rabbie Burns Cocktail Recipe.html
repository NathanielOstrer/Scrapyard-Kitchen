Rosie Schaap

Ingredients

  • 1 one-inch strip of orange peel
  • 1 ½ ounces Dewar’s White Label
  • ½ ounce Carpano Antica sweet vermouth
  • 3 dashes Bénédictine

Preparation

  1. Rub the rim of a cocktail glass with the orange peel.
  2. Shake the other ingredients in a glass with ice.
  3. Strain into the cocktail glass and garnish with the reserved peel.
