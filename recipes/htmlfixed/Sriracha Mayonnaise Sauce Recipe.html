Ingredients

  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • ½ cup sriracha hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor. Mix until thick and creamy, 2 to 3 minutes. Transfer to a covered container and refrigerate. Serve as a dipping sauce for onion rings or fries.
