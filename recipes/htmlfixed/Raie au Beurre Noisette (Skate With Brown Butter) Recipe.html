Butter, Caper, Skate, French

The New York Times 4 servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 sprigs parsley
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 2 pounds skate, cut into 4 portions
  • 3 ounces butter
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Put about 1 1/2 inches water in a large heavy pan. Simmer the onion, parsley, lemon and peppercorns for about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the pieces of skate and simmer gently for 15 minutes, covered. Remove, drain, place on a serving plate and keep warm.
  3. In a small pan heat the butter until it turns golden brown. Do not burn it. Pour the butter over the fish. Add the vinegar and capers to the saucepan, bring to boil and pour over fish.
  4. Serve immediately with boiled potatoes.
