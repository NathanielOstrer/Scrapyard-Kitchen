Bitters, Gin, Simple Syrup, Soda

William L. Hamilton 1 serving Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of gin (preferably Bombay)
  • 1 ounce of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of simple syrup
  • 4 dashes of orange bitters
  • White of one egg
  • 1 ounce of soda water
  • Orange slice for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place all the ingredients except the soda water and orange slice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake until the egg white froths. Pour into a collins glass filled with ice. Top with soda water and garnish with a slice of orange.
