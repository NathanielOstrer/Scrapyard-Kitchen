Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, Raisin, Spinach, Asian

Marian Burros 2 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces fresh, loose spinach or 10 ounces packaged, fresh spinach
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • 2 teaspoons fresh or frozen ginger
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 heaping tablespoons raisins

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim tough stems from loose spinach or wash packaged spinach.
  2. Mince garlic, grate ginger and add both to hot oil in a pot. Add spinach and raisins; cover, and reduce heat, cooking about 2 minutes, until spinach is tender.
