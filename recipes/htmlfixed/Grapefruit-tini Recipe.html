Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 ounces freshly squeezed grapefruit juice
  • 2 ounces high-quality vodka
  • 1 ½ ounces prosecco

Preparation

  1. Make simple syrup: In a small saucepan, mix the sugar with 1 cup water. Simmer over low heat until sugar dissolves. Set aside to cool, about 5 minutes.
  2. In a large shaker, combine the grapefruit juice, vodka and 1 tablespoon simple syrup. (Remaining syrup can be refrigerated for up to 1 week.) Add ice cubes and shake well. Strain into martini glass, and float (do not mix) prosecco in glass.
