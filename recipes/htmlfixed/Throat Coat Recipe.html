Honey, Vodka

Jonathan Miles 1 serving

Piotr Redlinski for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 ½ ounces Nemiroff honey pepper vodka
  • 1 ½ ounces B&B; liqueur

Preparation

  1. Add the honey to a snifter, rolling it around so that it coats the bottom of the glass. Add the vodka, then the B&B;, and serve.
