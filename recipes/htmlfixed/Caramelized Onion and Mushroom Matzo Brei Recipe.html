Egg, Matzo, Mushroom, Onion, Jewish

Melissa Clark 2 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ⅓ cup diced onions
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 matzos (about 2 ounces), broken into pieces
  • 5 large eggs, lightly beaten

Preparation

  1. In a skillet over low heat, melt 2 tablespoons butter. Add onions and cook, stirring, until caramelized, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Add mushrooms and raise heat to medium-high. Continue to cook, stirring, until mushrooms are soft, about 5 minutes. Season with plenty of salt and pepper.
  3. Add remaining tablespoon butter to pan and let it melt. Add matzo and cook, tossing to coat matzo in butter, for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour eggs into pan and season them generously with salt and pepper. Cook, scrambling mixture, until eggs are set, about 2 to 3 more minutes. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
