Bread Crumb, Brown Sugar, Dijon Mustard

Julia Moskin Enough for one 8- to 12-pound ham Time15 minutes

Karsten Moran for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup packed dark brown sugar
  • ¼ cup Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups coarse fresh bread crumbs made from rustic white bread

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, stir sugar and mustard into a smooth paste and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add bread crumbs and toast them, stirring frequently, until crisp and golden, 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Brush sugar-mustard paste over ham, then use hands to pack on bread crumbs.
