Cream Cheese, Strawberry, Sugar

William Grimes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 18-ounce package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup sugar, plus 3 tablespoons
  • Grated zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup chopped pecans, lightly toasted
  • 1 cup sweetened flaked coconut
  • 1 cup diced fresh strawberries, plus whole ones for garnish
  • 1 cup diced fresh pineapple or canned pineapple chunks, well drained and diced (do not use crushed pineapple)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 9-inch prepared graham-cracker crust

Preparation

  1. Beat the cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar, zest and lemon juice in a large bowl with the mixer at high speed until smooth. Fold in the pecans, coconut, strawberries and pineapple.
  2. Beat the heavy cream, 3 tablespoons sugar and vanilla in a chilled bowl until stiff; fold half the mixture into the cream-cheese mixture. Pour into crust; spread rest of whipped cream on top. Garnish with whole berries. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight.
