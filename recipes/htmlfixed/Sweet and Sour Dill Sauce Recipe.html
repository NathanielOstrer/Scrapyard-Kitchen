Vegetarian, Vegan, Dill, Scandinavian

Craig Claiborne About 3/4 cup Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coarsely chopped, loosely packed dill, stems included
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • ½ cup sugar

Preparation

  1. Put the dill in a small saucepan and add the vinegar and sugar. Bring to a boil and let simmer about 30 minutes.
  2. Strain the sauce.
