Nut Free, Brown Sugar, Rhubarb

Melissa Clark 2 cups, to serve 6 to 8 Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rhubarb, trimmed and sliced 1/2-inch thick (about 4 cups)
  • ½ cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Pinch kosher salt

Preparation

  1. In a medium saucepan over low heat, combine ingredients. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until rhubarb breaks down, 15 to 20 minutes.

Tip

  • The sauce is good over yogurt, ice cream, pancakes or sliced strawberries.
