Cachaca, Green Chartreuse, Lime Juice, Brazilian

Robert Simonson 1 drink

Peter DaSilva for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ½ fresh jalapeno, cut into thin wheels
  • 1 cup green Chartreuse
  • 1 ½ ounces cachaça, preferably Leblon
  • ½ ounce Benedictine
  • ½ ounce agave nectar
  • 1 ounce fresh lime juice
  • Sprig of fresh thyme

Preparation

  1. Place jalapeno in a small saucepan. Add Chartreuse and bring to a boil. Immediately remove from heat, remove jalapeno and allow infusion to cool.
  2. Pour 1/2 ounce of infusion (there will be extra) and remaining liquids into a mixing glass. Shake with ice and strain into a coupe glass. Garnish with thyme.
