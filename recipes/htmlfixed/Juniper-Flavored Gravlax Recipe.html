Dill, Fish, Gin, Juniper Berry, Kosher Salt, Salmon, Sugar, Scandinavian

Molly O'Neill 12 to 16 first courses

Ingredients

  • ½ cup kosher salt
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cracked white peppercorns
  • 2 teaspoons juniper berries, crushed
  • 2 pounds center-cut salmon fillet, skin on
  • 3 bunches fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons gin

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, combine the salt, sugar, peppercorns and juniper. Using your hands, rub 1 cup of the mixture all over the salmon. Transfer to a glass baking dish and cover with the remaining mixture. Scatter dill over the salmon and set aside at room temperature for 2 1/2 hours. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 3 to 5 days.
  2. When ready to serve, remove plastic and discard dill. Transfer the salmon to a cutting board and drizzle with the gin. Slice thinly on the diagonal. Serve with bread and garnishes of choice.
