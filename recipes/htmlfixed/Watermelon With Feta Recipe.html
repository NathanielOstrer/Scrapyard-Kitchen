Low Calorie, Feta, Mint Leaves, Watermelon, American

Florence Fabricant 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced, seeded, chilled watermelon
  • ½ pound feta cheese, diced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped mint leaves
  • Coarsely ground fresh black pepper

Preparation

  1. In a bowl gently fold the watermelon and feta cheese together.
  2. Fold in the mint and pepper, transfer to a shallow dish and serve.
