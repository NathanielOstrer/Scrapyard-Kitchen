Pecan

Marian Burros 2 cups

Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • ⅛ to ¼ teaspoon cayenne
  • 4 teaspoons neutral oil
  • 2 cups pecans

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Mix together salt, sugar, cumin and cayenne. Stir in oil to make a smooth mixture.
  2. Toss pecans with seasonings to coat. Arrange on cookie sheet in single layer. Toast 8 to 10 minutes, until they take on a little color. Cool, and serve. (They will keep in an airtight container in a cool, dry place for a week.)
