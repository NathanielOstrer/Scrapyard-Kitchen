Low Carb, Dry White Wine, Squid, Tomato

Moira Hodgson 4 to 6 servings Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (green part removed)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup canned Italian plum tomatoes chopped, with their juice
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 2 pounds squid, cleaned and sliced (see above)
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Gently saute the onion and the garlic in the olive oil. Add the tomatoes and white wine and simmer for five minutes.
  2. Add the squid, thyme, salt and pepper. Cover and simmer gently for 30 to 40 minutes or until the squid is tender. Correct seasoning and serve.
