Cilantro, Garlic, Green Chili, Asian

Molly O'Neill About 1/2cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 small green bird chilies (see note)
  • 3 to 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro leaves
  • ¼ cup lemon juice

Preparation

  1. In a blender, combine all the ingredients and process until smooth. Serve as a dipping sauce with your favorite sate.
