Dairy Free, Carrot, Garlic, Olive Oil, Reduced Sodium Soy Sauce, Scallion, Snow Pea

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ pound carrots, peeled and trimmed
  • ½ pound snow peas, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped scallions
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Slice the carrots into julienne strips about 1 1/2 inches long.
  2. Place the carrots in a saucepan, cover with water and bring to a boil. Simmer 4 minutes. Add the snow peas, bring to a boil, stir and cook for 2 minutes. Drain.
  3. Heat the oil in a skillet and add the carrots, snow peas, garlic, soy sauce, scallions and pepper. Stir and cook 1 minute. Serve.
