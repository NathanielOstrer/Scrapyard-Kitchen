Vegetarian, Leek, Oil

Emily Weinstein Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • White and pale green parts of 1/2 pound of leeks (2 medium), cut crosswise into 2-inch pieces
  • Vegetable oil for deep-frying

Preparation

  1. Cut leeks lengthwise into thin strips. Wash leeks and drain. Dry well between layers of paper towels.
  2. In a saucepan at least 3 ½ inches deep, heat 1 inch of oil to 375 degrees. Working in very small batches fry leeks (oil will bubble up quite high) until golden, about 10-15 seconds, transferring with a slotted spoon to paper towels to dry. Season leeks with salt.
  3. Leeks may be fried a day ahead and kept in an airtight container at room temperature.
  4. Serve on top of soup.
