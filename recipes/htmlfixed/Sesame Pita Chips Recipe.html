Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Nut Free, Egg White, Pita, Sesame Seed

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil spray
  • 2 whole-wheat pita pockets
  • 2 egg whites
  • ¼ teaspoon dark sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Spray a baking sheet with vegetable oil. Set aside. Separate the pita pockets into halves. Cut each half into 8 sections. Set aside.
  2. Combine egg whites and sesame oil in a glass or ceramic bowl. Brush the egg-white mixture on both sides of the pita. Sprinkle both sides with sesame seeds. Place on the baking sheet and bake until crisp, about 10 minutes. Store in a cookie tin.
