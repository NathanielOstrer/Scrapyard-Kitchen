Brown Sugar, Salt, Star Anise

Sam Sifton About 1 gallon Time30 minutes

Tom Schierlitz for The New York Times. Food stylist: Brian Preston-Campbell. Prop stylist: Bettina Budewig.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kosher salt
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 pods star anise
  • 4 bay leaves

Preparation

  1. In a large pot set over high heat, combine 1 gallon of water and the rest of the ingredients and stir to combine. Heat until the sugar and salt are dissolved, then remove from stove.
  2. Let cool completely and refrigerate until needed.
