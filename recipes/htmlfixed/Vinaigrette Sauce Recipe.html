Olive Oil, Salt, White Wine Vinegar

Craig Claiborne 10 tablespoons Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon white-wine vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ cup olive oil

Preparation

  1. Put the vinegar in a small mixing bowl and add the salt and pepper.
  2. Start beating with a wire whisk, while adding the oil. (This sauce will keep for weeks if properly refrigerated.)
