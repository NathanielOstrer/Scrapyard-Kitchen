Baking Potato, Double Smoked Bacon, Dry White Wine, Fish, Lemon Juice, Shad

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time9 hours 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 2-to-3-pound shad, scaled and cleaned, head and tail intact
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1 pound double-smoked bacon, sliced
  • ¼ cup white wine
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3 baking potatoes, cut across into 1/2-inch slices

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees. Season the shad inside and out with the pepper. Wrap the fish in bacon until completely covered. Place the shad in a baking pan and add the wine, lemon juice and potatoes. Cover with foil. Bake for 8 to 10 hours, basting every 2 hours. Serve with the potatoes and pan juices.
