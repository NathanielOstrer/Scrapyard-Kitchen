Cauliflower

Marian Burros 3 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds whole cauliflower or 1 1/2 pounds ready-cut cauliflower florets (7 to 8 cups)
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons anchovy paste
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Preparation

  1. Trim whole cauliflower, steam over hot water for 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. In a serving bowl, mix vinegar, anchovy paste, lime juice and oil.
  3. When cauliflower is cooked, drain and stir into dressing, mixing well to coat.
