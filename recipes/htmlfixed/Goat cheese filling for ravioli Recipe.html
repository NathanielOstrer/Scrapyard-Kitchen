Vegetarian, Goat Cheese, Ricotta, Italian

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey 6 - 8 appetizer Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups firmly packed chopped goat cheese
  • ¾ cup ricotta cheese
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh chives
  • 1 egg yolk
  • Tomato sauce (see recipe)

Preparation

  1. Put the goat cheese, ricotta, salt, pepper, cream, chives and egg yolk in a mixing bowl. Blend well.
  2. When ready to use the filling, scoop out about one and one-half teaspoons of the mixture, and roll into a ball. There should be about 32 balls in all. Fill the rolled-out pasta dough. When the ravioli are cooked, serve with tomato sauce. Serve four or more portions as an appetizer. Serve eight to 10 portions as a main course.
