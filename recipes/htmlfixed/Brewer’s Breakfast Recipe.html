Sake, Whiskey

Robert Simonson One drink Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces sake, like Masumi Arabashiri
  • 1 ounce unaged whiskey
  • ¼ ounce Galliano, or Galliano L’Autentico if available
  • Honey Nut Cheerios, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Stir in shaker with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with Honey Nut Cheerios on a cocktail pick.
