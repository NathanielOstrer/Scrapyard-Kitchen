Lime Juice, Tabasco Sauce, Whiskey

William L. Hamilton 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of Wild Turkey rye
  • 1 teaspoon of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • ¾ teaspoon of superfine sugar
  • 2 dashes of Tabasco sauce

Preparation

  1. Mix all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake well. Serve straight up in a shot glass.
