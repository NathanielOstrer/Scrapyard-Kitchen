Turkey

Jonathan Reynolds 12 servings TimeAbout 4 hours 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 12-pound turkey (see note)
  • ½ pound butter
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 cup water

Preparation

  1. Boil all the ingredients except the turkey. Brush the mixture on the turkey before, halfway through and at the end of smoking.
  2. Water-smoke for 4 to 5 hours. When the meat thermometer reads 160 degrees, take the turkey out. It can rest for as much as an hour before serving.
