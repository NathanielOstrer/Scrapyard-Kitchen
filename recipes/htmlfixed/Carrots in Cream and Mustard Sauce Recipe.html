Vegetarian, Carrot, Dijon Mustard, Heavy Cream

Pierre Franey 4 servings TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound baby carrots, about 36
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped onion
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. Using a swivel-blade paring knife, peel carrots and trim off the ends. Put carrots in a saucepan and add water to cover. Bring to boil and cook about 7 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat butter in a saucepan and add onion. Cook, stirring, until wilted. Add carrots and stir. Add cream, salt, pepper, mustard and parsley. Cook, stirring, about 1 minute.
