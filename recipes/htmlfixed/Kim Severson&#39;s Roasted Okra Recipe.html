Vegetarian, Basil, Mint, Okra, Vidalia Onion, Southern

Kim Severson 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound okra pods, washed, capped and cut in half lengthwise
  • 1 Vidalia (or other sweet) onion, small, sliced into crescents
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Chopped fresh basil and mint

Preparation

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees. Combine okra and onion in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper, drizzle with olive oil and toss well to coat. Roast until vegetables are just tender, 10 to 12 minutes. Toss with herbs and serve.
