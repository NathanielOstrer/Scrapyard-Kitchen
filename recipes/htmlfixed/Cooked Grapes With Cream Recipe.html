Vegetarian, Red Grape, Sour Cream

Jacques Pepin 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds seedless Red Flame grapes, stems removed (approximately 4 cups)
  • 1 tablespoon red-wine vinegar
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon rind
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon shredded mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon Cognac or other brandy (optional)
  • ½ cup sour cream

Preparation

  1. Place grapes, vinegar and water in a stainless-steel saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook gently for about 5 minutes. The grapes will lose their red color and begin to crack open.
  2. Meanwhile, in a bowl combine the lemon rind, lemon juice, honey and mint leaves. When grapes are cooked, add them to lemon-honey mixture and stir well. When cool, add the Cognac. Serve in wine glasses, garnished with a spoonful of sour cream.
