Gluten Free, Ginger, Potato, Salted Cod

Molly O'Neill Six servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds salt cod, soaked overnight, boiled until tender, then drained and cooled
  • 1 ½ pounds peeled potatoes, boiled and drained
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh ginger
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons heavy cream
  • ¼ pound butter, for pan frying

Preparation

  1. Using a fork, flake the fish into very fine pieces. Using a ricer or electric hand mixer, mash the potatoes with the fresh ginger and pepper to taste.
  2. Place the fish and the mashed potatoes in a large bowl. Add the egg yolks and heavy cream and combine well.
  3. Form the mixture into 12 patties and fry in butter over medium heat until golden on each side, about 4 minutes per side.
