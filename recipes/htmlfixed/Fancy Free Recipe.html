Maraschino Liqueur, Rye Whiskey

Jim Meehan 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rye whiskey
  • ½ ounce maraschino liqueur
  • Dash of Angostura bitters
  • Dash of orange bitters
  • Orange twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Stir all liquid ingredients with ice. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. Garnish with an orange twist.
