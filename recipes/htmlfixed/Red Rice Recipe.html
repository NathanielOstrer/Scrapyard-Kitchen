Gluten Free, Rice, Southern

Marian Burros 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • ¼ cup tomato paste
  • ½ cup finely chopped smoked beef sausage
  • 4 cups water

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in pot; add onion and saute a few minutes until onion is soft.
  2. Add rice and cook, stirring, a minute or two with onion, tomato paste and sausage.
  3. Stir in water; partly cover and bring to boil. When water boils, stir once; cover tightly and reduce heat to simmer. Cook until water has evaporated, about 17 minutes.
