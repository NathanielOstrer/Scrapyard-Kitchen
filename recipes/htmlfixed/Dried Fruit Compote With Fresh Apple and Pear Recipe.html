Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Kosher

Martha Rose Shulman Serves 6 to 8

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups water
  • 3 tablespoons mild honey, such as clover (more to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 strips orange zest
  • 1 strip lemon zest
  • 1 firm but ripe pear, peeled, cored and diced (optional)
  • 1 apple, preferably a slightly tart variety like Pink Lady, peeled, cored and diced (optional)
  • Whipped cream or plain yogurt for serving, if desired

Preparation

  1. Cut large pieces of dried fruit into smaller pieces.
  2. Combine all of the ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 5 minutes. Turn off heat and allow fruit to steep for another 30 minutes or longer. Remove cinnamon stick and orange and lemon zest. Serve topped with whipped cream or yogurt if desired, or stir into your morning yogurt.
