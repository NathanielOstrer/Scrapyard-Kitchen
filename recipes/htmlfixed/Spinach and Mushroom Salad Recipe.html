Vegetarian, Portobello, Spinach

Marian Burros 2 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces portobello mushrooms
  • ½ of 10-ounce package of fresh spinach
  • 1 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

Preparation

  1. Turn on a toaster oven to broil, or use a regular oven.
  2. Wash and slice mushrooms, and broil until they soften, about 7 minutes.
  3. Wash spinach, remove tough stems, break into small pieces and dry.
  4. In a serving bowl, mix the soy sauce and balsamic vinegar, and then thoroughly stir the spinach and the mushrooms into the mixture. Serve.
