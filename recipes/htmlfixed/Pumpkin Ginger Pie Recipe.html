Vegetarian, Nut Free, Candied Ginger, Pie Shell, Pumpkin

Moira Hodgson 6 to 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked, strained pumpkin
  • 1 ¼ cups lightly packed light brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped crystalized ginger, plus extra for garnish
  • Grated zest of 1 lemon, orange or tangerine
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 tablespoons brandy
  • 1 tablespoon bourbon
  • 1 partly baked 9-inch pie shell

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Combine all the ingredients for the filling and beat well. Pour into the partly baked pie shell.
  3. Bake for about 50 minutes or until the center of the pie is set. Garnish with a little crystalized ginger.

Tip

  • This is good with lightly sweetened sour cream or yogurt served on the side.
