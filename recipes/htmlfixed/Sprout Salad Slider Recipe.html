Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Kosher

Tara Parker-Pope 4 to 6 sandwiches Time5 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 3 cups leftover brussels sprouts, quartered
  • 1 green apple, small dice
  • 2 scallions, sliced up to the pale green portion
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons cherry peppers, diced (to taste, depending on spice tolerance)
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup walnuts, lightly toasted
  • Last night’s unbaked rolls, halved, buttered and heated on a griddle

For garnish

  • Roma tomato, sliced
  • Crisp iceberg lettuce

Preparation

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Adjust seasoning to taste. Serve on roll with tomato slices and lettuce.
