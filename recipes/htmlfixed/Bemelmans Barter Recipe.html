Cognac, Curacao

Warren St. John 1 cocktail Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces V.S. Cognac, like Courvoisier V.S. or Hennessy V.S
  • 5 ounces orange Curaçao
  • 1 ounce pineapple juice
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • Freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 twist orange peel

Preparation

  1. In a mixing glass, combine the Cognac, Curaçao, pineapple juice and Angostura bitters. Add ice and mix well.
  2. Strain drink into a chilled cocktail glass. Lightly dust with nutmeg. Holding the orange twist with tongs, touch a flame to it so the oil in the skin briefly lights. Add to glass and serve.
