Raw, Chili Pepper, Cumin, Olive Oil, Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, Moroccan

Pierre Franey About 1/3 cup Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 hot green or red peppers
  • 3 large cloves garlic, peeled
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cumin
  • Tabasco or Louisiana hot sauce to taste, optional

Preparation

  1. Cut off stems of peppers. Cut away and discard veins and rest of stems. Chop peppers. There should be 1/2 cup.
  2. Add peppers, garlic, oil and cumin to container of an electric blender. Blend at high speed to fine liquid. Add Tabasco or Louisiana hot sauce to taste.
