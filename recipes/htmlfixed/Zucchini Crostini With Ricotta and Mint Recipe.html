Vegetarian, Mint, Ricotta, Zucchini

Mark Bittman Time10 minutes

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound zucchini
  • 1 cup ricotta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Toasted crostini, bruschetta or any crusty bread

Preparation

  1. Shred 1 pound zucchini, and toss it with 1 cup ricotta, 2 tablespoons olive oil, the juice of a lemon, salt and pepper.
  2. Serve on toasted crostini, bruschetta or any crusty bread.
  3. Use mint for garnish.
