Bitters, Irish Mist, Irish Whiskey, Lemon

William Grimes 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ ounces Irish whisky
  • 1 ¼ ounces Irish Mist
  • Dash lemon juice
  • Dash bitters
  • 1 piece lemon rind

Preparation

  1. Mix whisky, Irish mist, lemon juice and bitters, and serve on the rocks or straight up. Garnish with lemon rind.
