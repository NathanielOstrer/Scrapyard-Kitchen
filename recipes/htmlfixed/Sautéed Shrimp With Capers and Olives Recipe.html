Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 3 sliced garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • ½ cup chopped pitted black olives
  • 1 ½ pounds peeled shrimp and 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes

Garnish

  • Basil

Preparation

  1. Put 1/4 cup olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat.
  2. Add 3 sliced garlic cloves, 2 tablespoons capers and 1/2 cup chopped pitted black olives, and cook until the garlic is golden.
  3. Stir in 1 1/2 pounds peeled shrimp and 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes.
  4. Cook, turning the shrimp once or twice, until they are pink, 5 to 10 minutes.
