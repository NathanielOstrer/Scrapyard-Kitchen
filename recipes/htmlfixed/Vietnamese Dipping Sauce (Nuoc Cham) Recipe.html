Carrot, Chili Pepper, Lime, Nuoc Mam, Sugar, Vietnamese

Nina Simonds 1 cup (for 20 rolls)

Ingredients

  • 1 small fresh red chili pepper, minced (ends and seeds removed), or 1 teaspoon crushed dried red pepper flakes
  • Juice of 3 limes or 2 lemons
  • ¼ cup nuoc mam (Vietnamese fish sauce)
  • 4 tablespoons sugar, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons grated carrots

Preparation

  1. Soak red peppers or chili flakes in the juice for 2 minutes.
  2. Add the fish sauce, sugar and garlic, and stir to dissolve the sugar.
  3. Pour into a serving bowl, and add the shredded carrots.
