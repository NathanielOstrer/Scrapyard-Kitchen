Raw, Black Pepper, Kosher Salt, Mustard, Southern

Rena Coyle One-and-a-half to two cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoon dry mustard
  • 1 ½ tablespoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix thoroughly. Place the spices in a jar and cover until ready to use.
