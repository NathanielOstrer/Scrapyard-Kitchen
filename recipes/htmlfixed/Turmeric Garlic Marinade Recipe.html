Steak, Turmeric

Marian Burros 1/2 cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 6 4-ounce pieces of steak
  • ½ cup water

Preparation

  1. Stir garlic and turmeric into 1/2 cup water. Brush over steaks, and marinate overnight in refrigerator.
