Black Olives, Flour, Garlic, Mediterranean

Molly O'Neill 8 servings Time1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 4 heads garlic, roasted, squeezed from their husks and mashed until smooth
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1 teaspoon red-pepper flakes
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¾ cup oil-cured olives, pitted and coarsely chopped

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the mashed garlic, melted butter, orange zest and pepper flakes. Beat in the egg and milk. Beat in the flour mixture in 3 batches, fully incorporating each addition. Fold in the olives.
  3. Pour into a greased 8 1/2-by-4 1/2-by-2 3/4-inch loaf pan. Bake for 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Remove from the pan and cool on a wire rack.
