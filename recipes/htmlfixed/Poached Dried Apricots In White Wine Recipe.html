Gluten Free, Dried Apricot, Dry White Wine, Mascarpone

Moira Hodgson 4 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ pound dried apricots
  • 2 ½ cups dry white wine
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cardamom seeds
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese at room temperature

Preparation

  1. Simmer apricots in the wine and sugar with cardamom seeds until soft (about 20 minutes).
  2. Pour apricots into a serving dish and let them sit at room temperature until ready to be eaten. Serve the apricots with mascarpone cheese and, if you like, sugar cookies.
