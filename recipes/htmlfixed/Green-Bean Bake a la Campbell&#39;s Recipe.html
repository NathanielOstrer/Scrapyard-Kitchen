Canned French Fried Onion, Cream Of Mushroom Soup, Frozen Green Bean, Milk, American

Molly O'Neill 6 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 can (10 ounces) Campbell's condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 9-ounce packages frozen french-cut green beans, defrosted, cooked and drained
  • 1 can (2 8/10 ounces) french-fried onions

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. In a casserole, mix the soup, milk, soy sauce and pepper until smooth. Stir in the beans and half the onions. Bake uncovered for 25 minutes. Stir. Top with the remaining onions. Bake 5 to 10 minutes more, until the onions are golden brown.
