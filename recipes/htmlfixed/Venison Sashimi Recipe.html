Soy Sauce, Venison, Wasabi

Elaine Louie 4 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon wasabi (Japanese horseradish available in specialty stores and greengrocers)
  • 1 pound filet of venison
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Mix the water and wasabi to form a paste.
  2. Slice venison into paper-thin pieces.
  3. Arrange venison around the mound of wasabi and put soy sauce in a small bowl.
