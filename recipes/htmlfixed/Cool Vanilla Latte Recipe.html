Raw, Bacardi Vanala, Espresso, Milk

William L. Hamilton 1 serving Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces cool espresso
  • 2 ounces milk foam
  • 1 ½ ounces Bacardi Vaníla
  • ½ ounce white crème de cacao
  • 1 ounce skim milk
  • Dash nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Pour espresso into chilled martini glass. Top with foam. Combine liquor, crème de cacao and skim milk in cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake well. Strain ingredients into glass, pouring at the edge so as not to disturb foam. Dust with nutmeg.
