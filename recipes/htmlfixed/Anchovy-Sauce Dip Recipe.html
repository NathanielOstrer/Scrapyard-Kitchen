Anchovy Fillet, Lemongrass, Pineapple, Rice Wine Vinegar, Thai Chili, Asian

Molly O'Neill 1 cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons minced anchovies
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 teaspoons rice-wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons crushed pineapple
  • 1 4-inch piece lemongrass, finely chopped
  • 1 red Thai chili pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Pinch black pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients and stir until sugar is dissolved. Serve as a dipping sauce for meats and salads.
