Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Peach, Prosecco, Italian

Nigella Lawson 4 to 6 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 ripe white peaches
  • 1 bottle of prosecco, chilled

Preparation

  1. Cut peaches into quarters; discard pits. Hold each quarter over mouth of blender, peel off skin, and drop fruit into blender. Blend until very smooth. Measure out 1 cup of puree and chill.
  2. Pour prosecco into a large pitcher. Add chilled peach puree, and stir gently. Serve immediately, before mixture separates.
