Caviar, Noodle, Soba Noodle, Asian

Molly O'Neill Six servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

The vinaigrette:

  • ½ cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • ¼ cup rice-wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped shallot
  • 4 ounces osetra caviar
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste

The noodles:

  • 6 ounces high quality dry soba noodles, cooked and chilled
  • ¼ cup fine julienne carrot
  • ¼ cup fine julienne daikon radish
  • 4 scallions, finely chopped

Preparation

  1. Whisk together the vinaigrette ingredients, reserving 1 teaspoon caviar. Combine the noodles, carrot, radish and scallions. Toss with the vinaigrette. Divide among 6 plates. Garnish with the remaining caviar.
