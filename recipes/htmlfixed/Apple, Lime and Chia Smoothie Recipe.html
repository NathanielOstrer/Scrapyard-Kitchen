Vegetarian, Healthy, Gluten Free, Kosher, High Fiber

Martha Rose Shulman Serves 1 Time2 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 organic Granny Smith or Pippin apple, cored and cut in chunks
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • 1 tablespoon honey or agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 cup plain low-fat yogurt
  • 1 or 2 ice cubes

Preparation

  1. Place all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Tip

  • Advance preparation: This is best when freshly made.
