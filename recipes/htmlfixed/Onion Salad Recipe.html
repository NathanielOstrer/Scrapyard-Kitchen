Vegetarian, Vegan, Lemon Juice, Orange, White Onion, Mexican

Molly O'Neill Six servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 white onions, peeled and sliced thin
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt or sea salt
  • 2 teaspoons grated orange rind
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 6 tablespoons orange juice

Preparation

  1. Place the sliced onions in a ceramic bowl, sprinkle with the salt, toss and allow to sit for 5 minutes. Add the orange rind and the lemon and orange juices. Allow to sit for 10 more minutes and serve.
