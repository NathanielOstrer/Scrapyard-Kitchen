Carrot, Celery, Yellow Pepper

Marian Burros 1 1/2 cups (3 servings)

Ingredients

  • 1 large red or yellow pepper
  • 5 celery ribs
  • 3 shallots, peeled and minced
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled
  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Hot pepper flakes, optional

Preparation

  1. Finely chop the pepper, celery, shallots, carrots and parsley, and combine. Place them in a small saucepan with the oil and salt. Cook over a medium flame for 30 minutes. Add the vinegar and sugar, and cook for 5 more minutes.
