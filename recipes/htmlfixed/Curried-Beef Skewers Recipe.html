Gluten Free, Beef, Curry Powder

Molly O'Neill Ten servings

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup curry powder
  • ½ cup peanut oil
  • 2 pounds beef filet, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Kosher salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Place the curry powder in a small heavy skillet over very low heat. Stir constantly until toasted and fragrant, being careful not to burn it, about 10 minutes. Place in a large bowl and let cool. Stir in the oil. Add the beef and toss to coat well. Refrigerate overnight.
  2. Bring the beef to room temperature. Preheat a grill or broiler. Thread the beef onto 6-inch skewers, using 3 cubes for each. Grill or broil until medium rare, about 1 minute per side. Sprinkle with salt, place on a platter and serve.
