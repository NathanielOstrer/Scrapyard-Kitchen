Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 pound snow peas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons chopped scallions
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Put the water in a saucepan and add salt. Bring to a boil and add the snow peas. Blanch them for 1 minute and drain.
  2. Heat the oil in a nonstick skillet. Add the sesame seeds and brown them lightly. Add the snow peas, scallions, salt and pepper. Saute for 1 minute and serve.
