Goose Fat, Matzo, Parsley, Jewish

David Sax 20 matzo balls Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 5 whole matzos
  • 150 milliliters liquid goose fat
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon dry ginger (or ground fresh)
  • ½ cup fresh chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Crumble matzos, mix in other ingredients slowly, rolling dough into squash-ballsize balls. Place in salted, boiling water with salted goose meat and vegetables.
