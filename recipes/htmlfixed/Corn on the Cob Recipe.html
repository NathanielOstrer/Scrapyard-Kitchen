Vegetarian, Vegan, Corn, Corn On The Cob

Marian Burros 3 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 to 6 ears of corn
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons ground cumin

Preparation

  1. Husk the corn, and steam over hot water for about 2 minutes.
  2. As soon as corn cobs are removed from the water, sprinkle with the cumin and serve.
