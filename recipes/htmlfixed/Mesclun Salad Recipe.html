Vegetarian, Vegan, Mesclun, Salad

Marian Burros 2 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces mesclun or other assorted greens
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Preparation

  1. Wash and dry mesclun.
  2. Whisk the lemon juice, vinegar and oil and mix with greens.
