Almond Milk, Oil

Anita M. Samuels About 3 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk or unflavored soy milk (original flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt or salt substitute
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cups oil (corn, canola or safflower)

Preparation

  1. Put the almond milk or soy milk in a food processor, and add the onion powder, sea salt, honey and lemon juice.
  2. Process with the S-blade or whisk attachment for 30 seconds as you slowly add the oil. The mixture will begin to thicken.
  3. Longer processing will result in thicker mayonnaise.
