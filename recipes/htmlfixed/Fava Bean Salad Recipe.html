Vegetarian, Almond, Fava Bean, Garlic

Molly O'Neill Four to five servings

Ingredients

  • 6 pounds fava beans (or lima beans), shelled, blanched 2 minutes, run under cold water and popped out of the tough outer skins (3 cups)
  • 4 ounces Parmesan cheese, broken into bite-size chunks
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh mint
  • 3 medium cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup almonds, lightly toasted
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Toss all ingredients and marinate overnight at room temperature or for 2 days in the refrigerator
