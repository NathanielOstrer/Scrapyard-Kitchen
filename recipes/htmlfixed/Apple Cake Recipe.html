Ingredients

  • 1 pound apples
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 5 tablespoons Calvados
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 package yeast
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

Preparation

  1. Peel apples and dice.
  2. In a pan, saute apples in butter until golden. Add Calvados and flambe.
  3. In a bowl, rapidly whisk egg yolks, sugar, oil and cream. When blended well, about five minutes, add yeast and flour slowly and continue to whisk until they are well mixed.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until they peak.
  5. Add apples and egg whites to the first bowl. Fold gently.
  6. Preheat oven to 390 degrees. Butter a cake mold and pour in batter. Bake for one hour.
