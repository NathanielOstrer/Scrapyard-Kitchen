Mayonnaise, Watercress, White Bread, British

Craig Claiborne 8 sandwiches Time5 minutes

Hiroko Masuike for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 8 thin slices white bread
  • ½ cup mayonnaise, approximately
  • 1 bunch watercress
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Spread mayonnaise on the top of each slice of bread.
  2. Cut off and discard the tough stems from the watercress. Coarsely chop the remaining watercress leaves and stems.
  3. Top 4 of the mayonnaise-smeared slices of bread with the chopped watercress. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and cover with remaining slices.
  4. Cut away the crusts of each sandwich. Cut each sandwich diagonally in half to make triangles.
