Cucumber, Veev Acai

Jonathan Miles 1 serving

Ethan Pines for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces VeeV Açaí
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • ¾ ounce simple syrup
  • 5 mint leaves
  • 5 cucumber slices, thin
  • Sugar, for rimming the glass
  • A twist or cucumber slice, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Rim a cocktail glass with lime, then dip rim in sugar. Muddle cucumber and mint together, and then add liquid ingredients. Shake and strain into glass, filled with ice. Garnish and serve.
