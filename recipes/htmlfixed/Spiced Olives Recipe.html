Vegetarian, Vegan, Black Olives, Mediterranean

Molly O'Neill Four cups

Ingredients

  • 4 cups high-quality black olives
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and smashed
  • 2 teaspoons crushed red pepper
  • 2 teaspoons dried rosemary

Preparation

  1. Place the olives in a bowl and toss with the olive oil. Add the garlic, red pepper and rosemary and mix well. Let the olives marinate overnight at room temperature.
