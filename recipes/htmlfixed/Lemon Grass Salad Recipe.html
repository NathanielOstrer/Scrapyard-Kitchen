Raw, Ginger, Lemongrass, Shallot, Thai

Marian Burros 5 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons kaffir lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar
  • ½ cup very thinly sliced shallots
  • ½ cup very thinly sliced ginger
  • ½ cup very thinly sliced lemon grass
  • ½ cup green onions
  • 1 tablespoon or more nam pla (fish sauce)
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves, very thinly shredded

Preparation

  1. Mix tamarind paste with 1/2 cup hot water. Set aside 2 tablespoons; reserve remainder for another recipe.
  2. Combine tamarind water in a bowl with all remaining ingredients. Taste, and add more fish sauce if you prefer a saltier flavor.
  3. Serve over grilled fish, shrimp, octopus, beef, pork or chicken. Or mix with cooked flaked fish or pork.
