Flour, Lard, British

Craig Claiborne 2 pounds Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • ⅔ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • ½ pound lard
  • Salt to taste, if desired

Preparation

  1. Put the flour in a mixing bowl.
  2. Combine the water, milk, lard and salt in a saucepan and bring to the boil. Let simmer until the lard melts.
  3. Add the liquid to the flour, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon to blend thoroughly. This dough should be shaped or rolled while it is still hot or quite warm.
