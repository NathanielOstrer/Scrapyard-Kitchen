Ingredients

  • 2 cups Swedish brown beans or red kidney beans (canned red kidney beans can be used to avoid preliminary cooking)
  • 5 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ cup golden syrup or to taste (dark Karo syrup can be substituted)
  • ¼ cup vinegar or to taste

Preparation

  1. Rinse dry beans, if used, and add water and salt. Simmer until tender, 1 to 2 hours. Add more water if beans tend to boil dry.
  2. When cooked, add syrup and vinegar, adjust seasonings and serve hot. Dish can be prepared day before.
