All Purpose Flour, Cranberry

Patricia Brooks Time1 hour 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cranberries
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 2 cups flour
  • A pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. Mix together, then pour into a greased tin melon mold only two-thirds full. Steam for 1 1/2 hours.
  2. When cooked, serve with a sauce or glaze of your choice.
