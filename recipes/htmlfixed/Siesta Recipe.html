Campari, Citrus, Tequila

Jim Meehan 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces tequila
  • ¾ ounce Campari
  • ½ ounce lime juice
  • ½ ounce grapefruit juice
  • Grapefruit twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all liquid ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a grapefruit twist.
