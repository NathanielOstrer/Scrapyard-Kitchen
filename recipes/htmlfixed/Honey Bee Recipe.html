Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces white rum
  • 1 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • ½ ounce honey
  • Lemon twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a shaker with ice and add rum, lemon juice and honey. Shake and strain into chilled coupe. Garnish with lemon twist.
