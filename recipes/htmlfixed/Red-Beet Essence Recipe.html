Red Beet, Red Wine Vinegar

Florence Fabricant

Ingredients

  • 1 pound red beets, peeled and juiced, or 1 cup juice from health-food store
  • ½ teaspoon red-wine vinegar
  • A few drops lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Place beet juice in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Slowly reduce to no more than 1/4 cup; it should be thickened to a glaze. Add vinegar and lemon juice, and set aside.
