Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Ground Beef, Tomato, Mexican, Caribbean

Daisann Mclane Four to six servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 ½ pounds ground round beef
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 3 to 4 whole allspice berries (available in most health-food stores)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro leaves
  • 1 cup peeled and chopped tomatoes
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 2 teaspoons drained capers
  • ¼ cup olives stuffed with pimentos
  • White rice

Preparation

  1. In a skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat and add the onions and garlic. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the onions are translucent. Add the meat, separating it into small pieces as you stir.
  2. Add the bay leaf, salt, pepper, oregano, allspice and cumin and cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Lower the heat to medium, then add the cilantro, tomatoes, raisins, capers and olives, and mix. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring from time to time, adding a small amount of water if the mixture begins to dry. Serve with white rice
