Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Shiitake, Japanese

Craig Claiborne 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 large fresh shiitake mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons mirin (see note)
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped scallions
  • ¼ teaspoon Japanese powdered chili mixture (hichimi togarashi) or more to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat charcoal or gas-fired grill.
  2. Wipe mushrooms with damp cloth and place them, stem side down, on hot grill and cook 2 or 3 minutes. Turn and cook 2 or 3 minutes more.
  3. Meanwhile, blend soy sauce, lemon juice, mirin, chopped scallions and chili mixture. Serve mushrooms immediately with sauce on side for dipping.

Tip

  • Mirin is a sweet sake-type wine used in Japanese cooking.
