Flour, Yeast, Italian

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey Two 12-inch pizza crusts TimeAbout 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ envelopes dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups lukewarm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups flour
  • Pinch of salt, if desired

Preparation

  1. Put the yeast, water and oil into the container of a food processor.
  2. Add the flour and salt and process until the mixture becomes a soft but firm and kneadable mixture.
  3. If a food processor is not used, soak the yeast in the water and oil. Put the flour and salt into a mixing bowl. Add the yeast liquid and blend by hand.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured board and knead briefly. Shape into a ball. Place the ball in a mixing bowl and cover. Let stand until double in bulk, about 45 minutes to an hour. It is now ready to be used for pizzas, calzoni and soon.
