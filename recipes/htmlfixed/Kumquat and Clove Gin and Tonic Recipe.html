Gin, Kumquat, Tonic Water

Jeff Gordinier 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 3 kumquats, cut in half
  • 3 to 5 cloves
  • 3 ounces gin, preferably Dorothy Parker American Gin
  • 1 10-ounce bottle Schweppes tonic, cold

Preparation

  1. In an 18-ounce glass, place both halves of 1 kumquat. Muddle lightly. Fill glass with ice, and place remaining kumquats and cloves on top. Add the gin, and tonic water to taste.
