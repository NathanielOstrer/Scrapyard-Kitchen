Blueberry, Butter, Vanilla Ice Cream

Linda Wells Six servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons sweet, unsalted butter
  • 1 quart blueberries
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • Vanilla ice cream

Preparation

  1. In a large skillet, over low heat, melt the butter. Add the berries, sprinkle with the sugar and cook until they give off their liquid and collapse, about eight minutes. Remove from the heat.
  2. Serve hot over the vanilla ice cream.
