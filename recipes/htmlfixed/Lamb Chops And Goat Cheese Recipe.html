Goat Cheese, Lamb Chop

Marian Burros 3 servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 to 5 double-rib lamb chops
  • 1 ½ to 1 ¾ teaspoons fresh thyme or 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 3 to 5 ounces sharp goat cheese

Preparation

  1. Preheat broiler of toaster-oven or oven broiler.
  2. Sprinkle pepper over chops and broil on one side for about 7 or 8 minutes on each side.
  3. Remove fresh thyme leaves from stems and mix with cheese. .
  4. Turn chops; top with cheese and thyme and continue broiling until chops have reached desired degree of doneness.
