Cointreau, Genever Gin, Lillet Blanc

Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Photograph by Grant Cornett. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • ¾ oz. Boomsma Oude Fine Old Genever
  • ¾ oz. Cointreau
  • ¾ oz. Lillet Blanc
  • ½ oz. fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ oz. fresh lime juice
  • Scant teaspoon of Grande Liqueur de Sapins
  • Orange twist

Preparation

  1. Chill a coupe glass, and evenly coat inside with liqueur de sapin. Shake remaining ingredients with ice. Strain into coupe. Express the orange twist over the cocktail and drop it in.
