Barbara Kafka about 2 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 medium papayas, seeded, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 cup simple syrup (see note)
  • Juice of 3 limes

Preparation

  1. Puree papaya until smooth. There should be about 1 1/2 cups puree. Add the simple syrup and lime juice.
  2. Freeze in an ice cream machine according to the manufacturer's directions.

Tip

  • Make simple syrup by simmering together 1/2 cup water and 1/2 cup sugar for 5 minutes. Allow to cool for 2 hours in the refrigerator
