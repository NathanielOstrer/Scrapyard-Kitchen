Egg

David Latt 4 servings Time35 minutes

Alan Zale for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-cooked eggs, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon Italian parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon capers, dried, finely chopped
  • 1 large shallot, peeled, finely chopped
  • 1 slice of bacon, crisp, finely chopped
  • 1 ½ tablespoons mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Put the eggs into a gently boiling pot of water and cook for 30 minutes. Cool with water, peel, and chop. (A food processor can be used although I prefer the therapy of hand chopping.)
  2. Toss the eggs in a bowl with the parsley, capers, shallots, and bacon. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then add the mayo and mix well. Serve with fresh bread, crackers, on hearts of romaine, or in a salad.
