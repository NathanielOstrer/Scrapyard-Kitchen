Honey, Peach, American

Dena Kleiman 1 1/2 to 2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh ripe peaches, peeled, pitted and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons honey

Preparation

  1. Puree the peaches and honey in a food processor for three minutes until smooth. The peach honey will keep in the refrigerator for about a week.
