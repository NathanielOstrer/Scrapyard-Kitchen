Ingredients

  • 1 to 5 fresh Serrano chilies, minced, depending on desired piquancy
  • 1 to 10 fresh jalapenos, minced, depending on desired piquancy
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 medium vine-ripened red tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 bunch cilantro, minced
  • ½ cup minced carrot
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
