Rum, Watermelon Punch

Pete Wells Makes 1 cocktail

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces watermelon punch (see recipe)
  • 2 ounces white rum
  • Ice

Preparation

  1. Pour the watermelon punch and rum into a tall highball glass filled with ice. Stir to combine.
