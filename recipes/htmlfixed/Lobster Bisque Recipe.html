Cream, Lobster

Robert Farrar Capon 4 modest servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons lobster butter, from recipe above
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups lobster stock, from recipe above
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste, or more, as desired
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and white pepper as desired

Preparation

  1. Melt the lobster butter over low heat, mix in the flour and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes.
  2. Add the lobster stock and, whisking constantly, bring to a boil.
  3. Add tomato paste, whisk to dissolve it completely and add the cream. (If you add more tomato paste for color, be judicious: stop before you have made the mixture taste like tomato soup.)
  4. Season to taste with salt and white pepper. Serve hot.
