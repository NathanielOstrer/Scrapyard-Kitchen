Bay Leaves, Garlic, Onion

Molly O'Neill 1 1/2 cups Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh bay leaves
  • 1 medium Bermuda onion, coarsely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled and coarsely chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, coarsely chopped
  • ½ teaspoon allspice
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon powdered ginger
  • 2 teaspoons cracked black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Place half of each ingredient in a blender and process to a fine paste. Scrape into a small bowl and repeat with the remaining ingredients. Combine the mixtures. This can be refrigerated for up to 2 weeks and can be used to marinate oily fish, chicken, turkey or veal.
