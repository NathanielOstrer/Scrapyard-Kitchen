Vegetarian, Vegan, Cabbage

Mark Bittman 4 small servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Canola oil as needed
  • Soy sauce as needed
  • 3 or 4 green cabbage leaves, torn into 1-inch pieces.

Preparation

  1. Put a large dry skillet over high heat. Put the canola oil and soy sauce in separate spray bottles. Spray the cabbage with just enough canola oil and soy sauce to coat each piece lightly. When the pan is very hot, add the cabbage and cook for 20 seconds per side; remove.

To Serve:

  1. Serve the vegetables with the cabbage and a bit of each of the sauces, along with a small squeeze of fresh lemon if you like. You will have lots of leftover Worcestershire sauce: enjoy it on any grilled vegetable, broiled or roasted tofu or -- putting veganism aside -- roasted meat or poultry.
