Amaretto, Cinnamon, Rum

William L. Hamilton

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon sugar
  • 1 ounce Gosling's Black Seal Rum
  • 1 ounce Amaretto di Saronno
  • 2 tablespoons fresh sour mix (combine 1 tablespoon each of sugar, water, lemon and lime juices)

Preparation

  1. Rim martini glass with sugar mix.
  2. Shake rum, amaretto and sour mix with ice. Strain into glass and serve.
