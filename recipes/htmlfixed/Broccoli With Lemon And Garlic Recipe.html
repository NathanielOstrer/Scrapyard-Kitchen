Vegetarian, Vegan, Broccoli, Lemon

Marian Burros 3 servings TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces whole broccoli or 12 ounces ready-cut broccoli florets (4 to 5 cups)
  • 1 large clove garlic
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Trim tough stems from whole broccoli and cut florets into bite-size pieces. Steam broccoli, about 7 minutes.
  2. Mince garlic and saute in hot oil in nonstick skillet.
  3. When broccoli is cooked, add it and lemon juice to garlic in pan and stir well. Cook briefly to mix well.
