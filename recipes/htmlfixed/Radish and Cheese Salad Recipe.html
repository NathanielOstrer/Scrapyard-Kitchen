Onion, Radish, Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Marian Burros 3 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces radishes
  • 1 large or 2 small green onions
  • ¼ cup coarsely grated, well-packed sharp cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon white or red wine vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Trim radishes and slice.
  2. Slice green onion thin.
  3. Grate cheese.
  4. Beat oil with vinegar and mustard and mix with radishes and onions. Season.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top.
