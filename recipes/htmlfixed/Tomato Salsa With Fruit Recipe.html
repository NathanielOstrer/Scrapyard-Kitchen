Vegetarian, Cilantro, Mango, Tomato, Mexican

Mark Bittman About 2 cups Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 ½ cups tomatoes, in 1/4-inch dice
  • 1 habanero chili, or to taste, seeded, stemmed and minced
  • ⅓ cup chopped cilantro (stems are O.K.)
  • ¾ cup chopped ripe mango, peach, nectarine or pear, in 1/4-inch dice
  • Salt as needed

Preparation

  1. Soak onion in the lime juice for 20 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients.
  2. Combine the tomato, chili, cilantro and fruit. When the onion is ready, stir it in, along with some salt. Add more lime juice if necessary, and serve with duck, chicken, grilled halibut or other fish.
