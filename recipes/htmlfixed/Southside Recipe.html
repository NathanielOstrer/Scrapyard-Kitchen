Gin, Lemon, Mint Leaves

William L. Hamilton 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of gin (or vodka or white or dark rum)
  • juice of half a lemon
  • sugar to taste
  • leaves of fresh mint

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients in a shaker with ice, and shake. Strain into a double Old-Fashioned glass filled with ice, and garnish with a sprig of mint.
