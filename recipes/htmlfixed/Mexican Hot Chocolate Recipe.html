Molly O'Neill Three to 4 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • 1 3 3/10-ounce tablet Mexican chocolate, roughly chopped (see note)

Preparation

  1. In a medium saucepan over medium-low heat, simmer the milk and chocolate for a few minutes, stirring constantly to dissolve the chocolate.
  2. Either pour into a pitcher and beat well with a Mexican molinillo (chocolate beater), a whisk or an electric mixer, or pour into a blender, cover loosely and blend until thoroughly mixed. Either way, you should wind up with a nicely frothy drink. Serve immediately.

Tip

  • Mexican chocolate is available at Spanish markets and by mail order from Old Southwest Trading Company, P.O. Box 7545, Albuquerque, N.M.87194; (505) 836-0168.
