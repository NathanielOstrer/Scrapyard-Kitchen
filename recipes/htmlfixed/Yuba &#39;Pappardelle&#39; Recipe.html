Milk, Olive Oil, Yuba

Daniel Patterson Serves 4 as an appetizer Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • 3 sheets yuba, cut into strips about 1 1/4 inches wide
  • â…“ cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons fruity olive oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Bring a large saucepan of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the yuba and cook for 1 minute. Turn off the heat and allow to rest for 2 minutes.
  2. Drain the yuba and return to the pot. Add the milk and olive oil and season to taste with salt and pepper. Divide among four plates and serve.
