Honey, Persimmon

Molly O'Neill About 2 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 persimmons, preferably Fuyu, peeled and diced
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon seeded, finely diced jalapeño pepper
  • 1 tablespoon peeled, minced ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon almond extract
  • ¼ cup sliced almonds

Preparation

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the persimmons, honey, lemon juice, jalapeño pepper and ginger and bring to a boil over medium heat. Cook, stirring, for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in the almond extract and almonds. Serve with duck dumplings.
