Pluot

David Latt 4 servings Time30 to 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 pluots or plums, ripe, washed, quartered, the pit removed
  • Raw sugar

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Place the pluot quarters on a Silpat sheet, parchment paper, or aluminum foil on a baking sheet. Sprinkle raw sugar on top. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes, until the pluots soften.
  2. Serve with ice cream, yogurt or another fruit like mango or cantaloupe.
