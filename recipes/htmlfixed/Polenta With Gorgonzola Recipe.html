Gorgonzola, Polenta

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups no-salt-added chicken or beef stock
  • ½ cup nonfat buttermilk
  • ½ cup quick-cooking polenta
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons Gorgonzola or other blue cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Bring stock and buttermilk to boil in covered pot.
  2. Slowly stir in the polenta and nutmeg, and continue cooking over medium heat, stirring often, until polenta has thickened.
  3. Stir in the Gorgonzola thoroughly, and season with salt and pepper.
