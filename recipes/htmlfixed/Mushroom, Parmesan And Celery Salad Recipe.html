Raw, Celery, Mushroom, Parmesan

Moira Hodgson 6 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mushrooms, washed and thinly sliced
  • 2 ounces Parmesan cheese, in small pieces
  • ¾ cup sliced celery
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine the mushrooms, Parmesan and celery in a serving bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the lemon juice, oil and salt and pepper to taste. Pour over the salad and toss it to mix. Serve at once.
