Lime Juice, Seedless Watermelon, Sugar

Liz Smith 2 to 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 5 cups watermelon, seeds removed
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons superfine sugar or to taste
  • 2 to 4 tablespoons fresh lime or lemon juice (to taste)
  • Ice cubes

Preparation

  1. Purée the watermelon, 3 tablespoons sugar and 2 tablespoons lime or lemon juice in a blender. Taste and adjust the sugar and citrus juice if desired. Pour through a strainer into a large pitcher and drain without pressing the solids. Skim off any foam and serve over ice in tall glasses.
