Vegetarian, Vegan, Escarole, Ginger, Scallion

Moira Hodgson 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 head escarole
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3 scallions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, chopped
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash the escarole and dry the leaves.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet and gently saute the garlic, scallions and ginger without browning for one to two minutes.
  3. Add the escarole and saute until the leaves are wilted. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and serve hot or at room temperature.
