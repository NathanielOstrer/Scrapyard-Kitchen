Vegetarian, Cucumber, Potato

Marian Burros 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds boiling potatoes
  • 6 tablespoons coarsely chopped cucumber
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Scrub potatoes; do not peel.
  2. Cook in water to cover until tender; drain and peel.
  3. Cut potatoes in large chunks; add cucumber, scallions, yogurt, milk, salt and pepper and serve.
