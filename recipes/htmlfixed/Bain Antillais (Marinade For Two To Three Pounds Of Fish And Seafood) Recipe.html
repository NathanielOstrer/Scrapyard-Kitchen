Lemon Juice, White Wine, Caribbean

Barbara Kafka Time1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ fresh hot pepper, deveined, seeded and minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
