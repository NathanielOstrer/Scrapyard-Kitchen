Dry White Wine, Herb, Onion, French

Craig Claiborne 1 1/4 cups Time35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups dry white wine
  • ¾ cup water
  • ¼ lemon
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • 1 small onion, peeled and stuck with 1 clove
  • 1 clove
  • 10 peppercorns, crushed
  • 1 cup chopped leeks
  • ½ cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme or 1/4 teaspoon dried
  • 1 whole shallot, peeled

Preparation

  1. Combine wine and water in saucepan.
  2. Trim away and discard all skin and outer pulp of lemon. Add peeled lemon to saucepan. Add remaining ingredients and cook about 30 minutes or until liquid is reduced to about 1 1/4 cups. Strain, discarding solids.
