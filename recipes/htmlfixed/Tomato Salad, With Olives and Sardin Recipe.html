Olive Oil, Sardine, Tomato

Moira Hodgson 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 large ripe tomatoes
  • 2 cans sardines
  • 1 red onion
  • 12 black oil-cured olives
  • About 4 tablespoons basil leaves, left whole
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar (balsamic if possible)

Preparation

  1. Slice the tomatoes and arrange them on a serving plate. Arrange the sardines over the top in a fan shape.
  2. Thinly slice the onion and arrange over the top. Add the olives and the basil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Dribble on the oil and vinegar.
