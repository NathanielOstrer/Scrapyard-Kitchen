Beet, Coffee, Cream, Potato

Mark Bittman Time35 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound beets
  • ¾ pound potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup chopped ham
  • ⅓ cup coffee
  • 2 tablespoons cream

Preparation

  1. Peel and grate beets and potatoes.
  2. Put butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. When it’s hot, add onion and ham, and cook until the onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Add the beets and potatoes and cook until tender, 15 minutes; remove from skillet.
  3. Deglaze skillet with coffee and cream. Spoon over the vegetables. Garnish with scallions.
