Mark Bittman Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • Mint leaves
  • 1 ½ tablespoons simple syrup
  • ¼ cup gin
  • Lemon juice
  • 1 glass of ice
  • Club soda
  • Garnish: more mint

Preparation

  1. Muddle mint leaves and simple syrup in a mixing glass; add the gin and lemon juice; shake. Strain into a glass of ice and finish with club soda. Garnish: more mint.
