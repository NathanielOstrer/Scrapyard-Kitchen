Ingredients

  • 6 large beets
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Trim, scrub and peel the beets, toss with olive oil, salt and fresh ground pepper. Wrap each in aluminum foil.
  3. Roast for 45 to 90 minutes, depending on the size of the beets, until tender.
