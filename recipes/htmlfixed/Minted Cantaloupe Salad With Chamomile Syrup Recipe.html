Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, Cantaloupe, Chamomile Syrup

Melissa Clark 6 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed cantaloupe (about 1 small cantaloupe)
  • 3 tablespoons chopped mint (about 1 large sprig)
  • 3 tablespoons chamomile syrup, or to taste (see recipe)

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, toss cantaloupe with mint until well combined. Add syrup, taste and add more if desired.
