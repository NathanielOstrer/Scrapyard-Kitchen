Celery, Cooked Chicken, Grape, Lemon Juice, Lettuce, Mandarin Orange, Mayonnaise, Poultry, Slivered Almond

Marian Burros 6 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 cups cooked, diced chicken
  • 2 tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 3 tablespoons minced onion
  • ⅓ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup toasted slivered almonds
  • 1 11-ounce can mandarin orange slices, drained, plus additional slices for garnish
  • 1 cup seedless grapes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste Lettuce

Preparation

  1. Combine chicken, lemon juice, celery, onion and mayonnaise; chill.
  2. Add almonds, orange sections and grapes; season with salt and pepper. Toss until well mixed.
  3. Serve on lettuce leaves with additional orange sections, if desired.
