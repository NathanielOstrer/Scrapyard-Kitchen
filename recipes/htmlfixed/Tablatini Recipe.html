Pineapple Juice, Vodka

William L. Hamilton 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 8 stalks fresh lemon grass, 6 coarsely chopped and 2 halved crosswise
  • 4 small fresh pineapple wedges
  • 8 ounces of Absolut Citron vodka
  • Juice of 1 lime

Preparation

  1. In medium saucepan, simmer pineapple juice with chopped lemon grass for 15 minutes; let cool. Strain into a jar, and chill.
  2. Spear the pineapple wedges with the halved lemon grass stalks. To a pitcher with ice, add pineapple juice, vodka and lime juice. Mix, and strain into 4 chilled martini glasses. Add pineapple-wedge garnish.
