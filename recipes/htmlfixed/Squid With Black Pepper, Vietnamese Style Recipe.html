Squid, Vietnamese

Mark Bittman 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons neutral oil, like corn or grapeseed
  • 1 ½ pounds squid, cleaned, cut into rings if large
  • 1 or 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (nam pla or nuoc mam), more to taste
  • Salt if necessary
  • Chopped fresh coriander leaves for garnish, optional
  • Cooked white rice for serving

Preparation

  1. Put oil in a large skillet or wok and turn heat to high. A minute later add squid and cook, stirring frequently, just until it becomes opaque, about 30 seconds.
  2. Taste; the moment the rawness is gone, add garlic and stir; add at least a teaspoon of black pepper. Stir in fish sauce, cook another 10 seconds, and taste; add more sauce and salt, if necessary. Garnish and serve.
