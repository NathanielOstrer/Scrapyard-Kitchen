Pimm's No. 1, Red Zinfandel

Jonathan Miles 1 drink

Hiroko Masuike/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces red zinfandel
  • ¾ ounce Pimm’s
  • Ginger beer, to top
  • Cucumber slice
  • halved strawberry, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Add wine and Pimm’s to a highball glass filled with ice, then top with ginger beer. Garnish with cucumber and strawberry.
