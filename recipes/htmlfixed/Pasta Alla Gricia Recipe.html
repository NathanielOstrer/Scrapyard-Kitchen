Mark Bittman TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • coarse-chopped slab bacon
  • olive oil
  • linguine
  • grated pecorino
  • black pepper

Preparation

  1. Cook coarse-chopped slab bacon in olive oil until done; toss with cooked linguine. Toss with bacon, fat, lots of grated pecorino and lots of black pepper; stir.
