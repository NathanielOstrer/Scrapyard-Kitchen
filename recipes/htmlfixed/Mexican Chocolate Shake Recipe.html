Almond Milk, Semisweet Chocolate, Tofu

Mark Bittman 6 regular servings, 4 supersize Time30 minutes' freezing

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1 pound silken “lite” tofu
  • 12 ounces good semisweet chocolate, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon, optional
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder, or more to taste, optional
  • Chocolate shavings

Preparation

  1. Put all the ingredients except for shavings in a blender and purée until smooth. Chill for at least 30 minutes in the freezer. Garnish with chocolate shavings before serving.
