Chicken, Mayonnaise, Potato

Florence Fabricant 4 to 6 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced chicken, turkey or duck
  • 2 cups diced cooked new potatoes, unpeeled
  • ½ cup chopped scallions
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon Djion mustard
  • ⅔ cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 4 slices crisp bacon, crumbled

Preparation

  1. Combine poultry, potatoes, scallions and celery in a bowl. Mix vinegar, oil, mustard and mayonnaise together until smooth and fold into the poultry mixture.
  2. Season with salt, pepper and dill. Sprinkle with parsley and bacon and serve.
