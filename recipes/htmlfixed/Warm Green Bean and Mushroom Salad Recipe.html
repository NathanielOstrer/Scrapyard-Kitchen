Green Bean, Mushroom

Marian Burros 2 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces green beans
  • 8 ounces mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash, trim and steam green beans over hot water, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Wash, trim and slice mushrooms.
  3. Whisk oil, stock and vinegar in serving bowl; add mushrooms.
  4. When green beans are cooked, drain stir into bowl and season with salt and pepper.
