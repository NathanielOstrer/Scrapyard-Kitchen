Chicken Breast, Kaiser Roll, Spice, Caribbean

Jonathan Reynolds 10 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon allspice
  • 1 teaspoon thyme (dried)
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 3 pounds chicken breast, boned and skinned
  • 10 kaiser rolls or 20 slices of bread

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients (except bread!) and mix with lime juice to form a paste. Rub paste all over chicken.
  3. Place breasts in an oiled pan large enough to hold them, and roast for 17 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven, let cool and place breasts on rolls or bread, adding relish, mayo, salsa, a variety of lettuces or anything else you like.
