Chicken Broth, Mushroom, Rice

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup finely chopped onion
  • ¼ pound fresh mushrooms, cut into small pieces, about 1 1/2 cups
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1 ⅓ cups fresh or canned chicken broth

Preparation

  1. Heat half of the butter in a saucepan and add the onion. Cook, stirring occasionally, until wilted. Add the mushrooms and cook, stirring, about three minutes. Add salt and pepper. Add the rice and stir.
  2. Add the broth and bring to a boil. Cover closely and let simmer for 17 minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and fluff the rice and mushrooms. Stir in the remaining one tablespoon butter.
