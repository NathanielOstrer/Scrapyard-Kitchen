Cheddar, Flour

Craig Claiborne about 40 biscuits Time35 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound freshly grated Cheddar cheese
  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¾ cup bread crumbs made from whole wheat bread

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Put cheese into the container of a food processor.
  3. Add the butter and flour and cayenne and blend thoroughly.
  4. Scrape the mixture onto a lightly floured board. Shape the dough into approximately 40 marble- shaped rounds. Roll each in the bread crumbs.
  5. Place the rounds one inch apart on a baking sheet.
  6. Place in the oven and bake 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool. The cooked biscuits may be frozen if desired.
