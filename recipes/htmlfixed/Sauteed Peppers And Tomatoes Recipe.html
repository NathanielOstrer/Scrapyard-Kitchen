Marialisa Calta Four servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • About 3 tablespoons olive oil, or enough to coat the peppers
  • 4 large green peppers, seeded and cut lengthwise into 1-inch slices
  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, chopped coarsely, or 1 16-ounce can of whole tomatoes with juice reserved
  • 2 leaves fresh basil, chopped, or 1/2 teaspoon dried
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a large saute pan over medium-high heat. Add the peppers and then shake the pan to coat them with oil.
  2. Add the tomatoes and, if using canned tomatoes, about 1/2 cup of the juice. Add the basil, salt and pepper to taste and cook, stirring from time to time, for 20 to 30 minutes.
