Mark Bittman Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons of triple sec
  • 1 tablespoon cranberry juice
  • ¼ cup vodka
  • Soda
  • Garnish: orange twist

Preparation

  1. Add lime juice, triple sec and cranberry juice to the vodka and soda. Shake and strain into a chilled glass. Garnish: orange twist.
