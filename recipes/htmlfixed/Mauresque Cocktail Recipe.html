Johnny Miller for The New York Times; Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces pastis
  • 1 ounce orgeat syrup
  • Chilled or sparkling water, as you prefer.

Preparation

  1. Chill a highball or Collins glass well. Add ice, give it a good stir and dump out the ice.
  2. Add pastis and orgeat syrup.
  3. Fill to top with club soda or water.
