Egg, Ground Beef, Ground Turkey, American

Jonathan Reynolds 6 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups Tomato Relish (see recipe)
  • Leaves from half a bunch Italian parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Combine all the ingredients, mix well and divide among 6 greased mini loaf pans. (Each should hold about 1 1/2 cups.) Or form by hand into small loaves and bake on a sheet pan. Bake 25 to 30 minutes, or until a thermometer inserted into the center reaches 165 degrees. Remove from the pans and serve.
