Vegetarian, Low Calorie, Low Fat, Low Cholesterol, Nut Free, Apple, Yogurt

Marian Burros 15 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon mace or nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon salt (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon grated orange rind
  • 1 ½ cups nonfat plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch dissolved in 1/4 cup nonfat milk
  • ¼ cup nonfat milk
  • 1 large apple, peeled and coarsely chopped
  • 2 to 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Oil to grease the pan

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Sift flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, mace and salt into a large bowl. Add the remaining ingredients and blend by hand until well mixed.
  3. Spoon into a greased 9-by-13-inch baking pan and bake 35 to 40 minutes, until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. Serve warm or cool.
