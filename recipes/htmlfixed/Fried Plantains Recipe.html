Plantain, Cuban

Moira Hodgson 12 - 14 servings TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe plantains
  • Approximately 6 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Coarse salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Peel the plantains and cut into 1/4-inch slices.
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Fry the plantains in several batches until golden brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt. Keep the fried plantains in a warm place until ready to serve.
