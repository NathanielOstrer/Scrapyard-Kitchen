Mint, Seedless Watermelon, Sugar

Florence Fabricant 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups cubed, seeded watermelon
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Mint sprigs for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place watermelon, lime juice and sugar in a blender, and process at high speed until smooth.
  2. Fill a 12-ounce glass with ice cubes, pour watermelon mixture over the ice and garnish with mint.
