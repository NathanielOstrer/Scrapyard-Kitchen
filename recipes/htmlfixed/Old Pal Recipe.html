Bourbon, Rye Whiskey

Robert Simonson 1 cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces bourbon or rye whiskey
  • ¾ ounce dry vermouth
  • ¾ ounce Campari
  • Orange peel, sliced.
