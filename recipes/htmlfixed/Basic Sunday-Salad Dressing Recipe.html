Lemon Juice, Olive Oil

Sam Sifton 4 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red-wine vinegar
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine the lemon juice, vinegar, shallot and mustard in a small bowl. Slowly whisk in the olive oil until the dressing emulsifies. Add salt and pepper to taste. Whisk again before dressing salad.
