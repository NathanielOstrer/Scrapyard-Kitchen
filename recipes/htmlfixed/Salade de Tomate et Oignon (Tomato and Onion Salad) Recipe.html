Raw, Garlic, Onion, Tomato, French

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 5 red ripe tomatoes, about 1 1/4 pounds
  • 2 cups coarsely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon finely minced garlic
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried hot red pepper flakes

Preparation

  1. Core the tomatoes but do not peel them. Cut the tomatoes into half-inch-thick slices. Stack the slices and cut them into 1/2-inchthick strips. Cut the strips into 1/2-inch-thick cubes.
  2. Put the tomatoes in a salad or mixing bowl and add the remaining ingredients. Blend thoroughly. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
