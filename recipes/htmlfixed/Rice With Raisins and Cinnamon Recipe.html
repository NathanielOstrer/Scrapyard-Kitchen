Vegetarian, Cinnamon, Raisin, Rice

Pierre Franey 4 servings TimeAbout 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • ¼ cup white or black raisins
  • 1 cup converted rice
  • ¼ cup chopped blanched almonds
  • 1 ½ cups fresh or canned chicken broth
  • 1 stick cinnamon, 2 inches long
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Melt butter in a small saucepan.
  2. Add onions and garlic. Cook, stirring, until wilted. Do not brown. Add raisins, rice and almonds and stir. 2.Add broth, cinnamon, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, stirring, and cover. Simmer 17 minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
  3. Remove cinnamon stick and serve.
