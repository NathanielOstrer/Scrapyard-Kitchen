Cabbage, Mayonnaise, Pickled Onion, Southern

Glenn Collins 4 servings

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard, Dijon-style
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup sweet pickled Vidalia onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cups shredded cabbage

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, sugar, salt and pickled onions.
  2. Add shredded cabbage and mix thoroughly. Refrigerate several hours before serving.
