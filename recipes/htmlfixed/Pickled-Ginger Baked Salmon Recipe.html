Ginger, Salmon Fillet, Sesame Oil

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 8-ounce fillets salmon
  • 2 teaspoons dark sesame oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 24 slices pickled ginger (available in specialty stores)
  • 4 slices smoked salmon

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Lay the fillets in a small roasting pan. Drizzle with sesame oil and season with salt and pepper. Place the pickled ginger in an even layer over the fillets. Top each with a slice of smoked salmon. Bake for 12 minutes, or until the fish is translucent pink in the center.
