Vegetarian, Vegan, Asparagus, Balsamic Vinegar, Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Marian Burros 3 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 24 stalks asparagus
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons balsamic vinegar

Preparation

  1. Wash asparagus and break off stems at the point where they yield easily. Steam 5 to 7 minutes, depending on thickness of stems.
  2. Stir oil and vinegar in serving dish. Drain asparagus and stir well into dressing.
