Fish Sauce, Lemon Juice, Soy Sauce, French

Pierre Franey about 1/3 cup

Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • ¾ teaspoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon nuoc mam (Vietnamese fish sauce)
  • ½ teaspoon soy sauce
  • Freshly ground white pepper to taste
  • ⅓ cup virgin olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon whole coriander seeds

Preparation

  1. Combine the garlic, lemon juice, nuoc mam, soy sauce and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the olive oil slowly, whisking briskly to emulsify the dressing.
  3. Add the coriander seeds, and mix well.
