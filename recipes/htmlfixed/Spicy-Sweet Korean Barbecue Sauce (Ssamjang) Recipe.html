Garlic, Korean Red Chili Paste, Soybean Paste, Korean

Julia Moskin 1/3 cup Time5 minutes

Morgan Ione Yeager for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup fermented soybean paste (doenjang)
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons Korean red chile paste (gochujang)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 scallion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sugar or honey
  • 2 teaspoons roasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, toasted

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, combine all ingredients, adjusting the amount of chile paste to taste. Use immediately or cover and refrigerate for up to 1 week.
