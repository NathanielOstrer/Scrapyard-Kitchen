Vegetarian, Vegan, Peach

Mark Bittman About 1 cup (enough for 6 to 8 cups of greens) Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup peach purée
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup balsamic or sherry vinegar
  • 10 leaves fresh basil, washed

Preparation

  1. Combine all of the ingredients in a blender, and blend until they are smooth. Use to dress a green salad within 1 hour.
