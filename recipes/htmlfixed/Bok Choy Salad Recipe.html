Dairy Free

Mark Bittman Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • chopped slab bacon
  • Saute chopped bok choy
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Cook chopped slab bacon on medium-high until done; remove with slotted spoon. Saute chopped bok choy. Combine cooled bok choy and bacon with sesame oil and soy sauce; toss.
