Vegetarian, Mayonnaise, Parsley, Sour Cream

Linda Wells About two cups of dip

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped parsley
  • 3 tablespoons chopped chives
  • 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, combine all of the ingredients, mixing them well. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Serve with crudites, including cherry tomatoes; yellow-pear tomatoes; red, yellow and green peppers; seeded cucumbers; scallions; baby carrots; French radishes; blanched broccoli, and cauliflower.
