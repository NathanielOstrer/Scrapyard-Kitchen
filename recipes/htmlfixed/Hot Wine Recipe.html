Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Beaujolai, Lemon, Sugar

Jacques Pepin 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 cups (about 2 bottles) robust, fruity, acidic wine (Beaujolais, cabernet sauvignon, merlot or zinfandel)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 large lemon, cut into 6 wedges

Preparation

  1. Place the wine in a saucepan with the sugar and heat the mixture slowly until just below a boil. Meanwhile, squeeze a wedge of lemon into each of six mugs and drop the wedges into the mugs.
  2. Divide the wine among the mugs and serve immediately.
