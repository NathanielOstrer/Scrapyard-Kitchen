Egg Yolk, Herb, Oil

Pierre Franey About 1 1/2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste
  • 1 cup vegetable or olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon minced scallions
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh chives
  • 2 tablespoons minced parsley
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon or any fresh herb of your choice
  • ½ teaspoon chopped garlic

Preparation

  1. Combine the egg yolk, mustard, vinegar, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the oil in a thin stream while beating vigorously with a wire whisk. When the mixture is thick and smooth, stir in the scallions, chives, parsley, basil, tarragon and garlic and blend well.
