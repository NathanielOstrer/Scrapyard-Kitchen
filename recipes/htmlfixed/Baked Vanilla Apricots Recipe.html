Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Apricot, Vanilla Bean, White Wine

Moira Hodgson 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds apricots
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • ¾ cup sugar

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Put apricots in shallow baking dish. Add remaining ingredients and bake about 30 minutes, turning apricots occasionally. Cool them and serve at room temperature.
