Vegetarian, Fresh Herb, Lime Juice, Shallot, Mexican

Mark Bittman About 1 cup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tightly packed chopped herbs like parsley, cilantro or mint leaves, alone or in any combination
  • ½ cup peeled and chopped shallots
  • 1 small jalapeno or other hot chili (or more to taste), seeded, stemmed and minced
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, combine all ingredients and let sit 5 minutes. If mixture seems thick, thin with a little water, neutral oil (like canola or corn oil) or additional lime juice. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve with grilled meat, chicken or fish.
