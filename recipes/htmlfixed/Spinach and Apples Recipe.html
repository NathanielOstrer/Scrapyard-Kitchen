Vegetarian, Vegan, Apple, Pear, Spinach

Mark Bittman Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 unripe pears
  • 2 crisp apples
  • 4 cups fresh spinach
  • ¼ cup raisins

Preparation

  1. Thinly slice 2 unripe pears and 2 crisp apples; put on a bed of 4 cups fresh spinach. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup raisins and drizzle with dressing. Dressing variation: Add 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary and 1 tablespoon honey.
