Butter, Flour, French

Marian Burros 4 pastry rounds

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons water
  • Melted unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. Sift flour and salt into a mixing bowl.
  2. Cut in butter until it is the size of large peas.
  3. Add water tablespoon by tablespoon; knead lightly to form a dough and chill before using.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  5. To make pastry lids for pies, divide dough into 4 portions and roll each portion into a round 1/8 inch thick. Crumple 16 pieces of foil into large marble-size pieces and spread melted butter over them to prevent pastry from sticking to foil. Place each pastry round on top of 4 pieces of crumpled foil and bake until golden brown, 15 minutes. It may be necessary to turn over the pastries to brown on both sides.
