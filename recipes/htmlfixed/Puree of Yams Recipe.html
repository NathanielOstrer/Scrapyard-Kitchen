Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Heavy Cream, Milk, Yam

Pierre Franey About 3 1/2 cups Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 yams or sweet potatoes, about 2 pounds
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup milk

Preparation

  1. Place yams in a saucepan and add cold water to cover. Bring to the boil and cook 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. When yams are cool enough to handle peel them. Cut yams into 1-inch cubes and put them through food mill or potato ricer.
  3. Put potatoes into a skillet and add butter, cream and milk. Heat thoroughly, stirring.
