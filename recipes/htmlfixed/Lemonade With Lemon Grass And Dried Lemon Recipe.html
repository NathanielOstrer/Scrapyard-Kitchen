Vegetarian, Vegan, Lemon Juice, Lemongrass

Melissa Clark 8 to 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 stalk lemon grass, chopped
  • 1 dried lemon (see note)
  • 3 cups fresh lemon juice (from about 24 lemons)

Preparation

  1. Combine sugar, lemon grass and dried lemon in a saucepan. Add 2 cups water, and bring mixture to a boil. Simmer 5 to 7 minutes or until dried lemon is tender. Use a spoon to squash lemon against side of pan.
  2. Turn off heat, and let mixture rest for 10 minutes, then strain it into a pitcher. Add lemon juice and 6 cups cold water. Chill until thoroughly cold, at least 2 hours. Serve over ice.
