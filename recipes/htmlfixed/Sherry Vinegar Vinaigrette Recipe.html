Raw, Corn Oil, Sherry Vinegar, New England

Craig Claiborne About 1 cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Dijon-style mustard
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped shallots
  • ½ teaspoon finely minced garlic
  • ¾ cup peanut, corn or vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup vinegar, preferably sherry-wine vinegar
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Put the mustard, shallots and garlic in a mixing bowl and gradually add half the oil, beating vigorously with a wire whisk.
  2. Alternately beat in the remaining oil and the vinegar. Add salt and pepper. When finished the dressing should be thoroughly homogenized.
