Vegetarian, Low Calorie, Cucumber, Cumin, Mint Leaves, Pine Nut, Yogurt

Suzanne Hamlin 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 medium to large cucumber, peeled, seeded and chopped
  • ½ cup spearmint leaves, washed and dried
  • 2 cups low-fat yogurt
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons lightly toasted pine nuts or roughly chopped walnuts or salted green pistachios (optional, for garnish)

Preparation

  1. In a food processor, combine cucumber chunks, spearmint leaves and 1 cup yogurt. Pulse until pureed. Add the rest of the yogurt, lime juice, cumin and salt, and blend.
  2. Transfer to a bowl, cover and refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.
  3. To serve, stir throughly, taste for seasoning and ladle into small bowls or cups. Sprinkle each with 1 teaspoon nuts, if desired.
