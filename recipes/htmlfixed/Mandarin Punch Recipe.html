Candied Ginger, Citrus, Spice

Steven Stern About 10 drinks

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 dozen whole cloves
  • 2 large cinnamon sticks
  • 2 cups orange juice
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon candied ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, slivered
  • ½ teaspoon spearmint or peppermint extract
  • Club soda

Preparation

  1. Combine sugar, 1/2 cup water and spices in a small pan and heat gently, stirring, until melted to a thick, clear syrup. Take off heat, add citrus juices, stir again and let cool, at least 1/2 hour. Strain into a bowl, add candied ginger, mint and extract, stirring to mix.
  2. Fill a wine goblet or tall glass with crushed ice and ladle in 1/4 to 1/2 cup of the mixture. Stir, then top with club soda.
