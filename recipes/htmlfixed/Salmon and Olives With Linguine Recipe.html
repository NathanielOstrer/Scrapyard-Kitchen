Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Nut Free, Linguine, Olives, Salmon Fillet, Italian

Marian Burros 2 to 3 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon fillets, skinned
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons chopped Greek olives, or other olives packed in brine
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • ½ pound linguine
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Broil fillets 5 to 7 minutes, depending on thickness.
  2. In hot oil, saute onion and garlic until onion is soft. Add olives and oregano and stir; cook 2 minutes over low heat.
  3. Meanwhile, cook linguine in 4 quarts boiling water until it is al dente. Drain.
  4. Cut salmon into small pieces and add to onion. Reheat and serve mixed with pasta.
  5. Sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper.
