Potato, French

Pierre Franey 8 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds russet potatoes
  • 1 cup coarsely sliced onions
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 6 tablespoons butter

Preparation

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into 2-inch cubes.
  2. Place the potatoes and onions in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Add salt. Bring to a boil and simmer 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Do not overcook them.
  3. Meanwhile, heat the milk until hot.
  4. Drain the potatoes and onions and put them through a food mill or potato ricer. Return them to the saucepan. Using a wooden spatula, add the butter and blend well. Mix in milk. Keep warm until ready to serve.
