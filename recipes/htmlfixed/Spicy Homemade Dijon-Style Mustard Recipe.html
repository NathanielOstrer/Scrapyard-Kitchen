Vegetarian, Vegan, Mustard Powder, Shallot, White Wine

Molly O'Neill About 3/4 cup Time1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups fresh white grape juice
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 ½ tablespoons white-wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 large shallot, very finely chopped
  • ⅔ cup mustard powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Honey to taste

Preparation

  1. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of the grape juice, the wine, vinegar, salt, pepper and shallot. Place over medium heat and cook until reduced by half. In another saucepan, simmer the remaining 1/2cup grape juice over medium-low heat for 5 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Place the mustard powder in the container of a blender. With the motor running, and using a fine-mesh sieve, strain the wine-and-juice reduction through the lid opening of the blender. Add the oil and blend for a few more seconds.
  3. Scrape the mustard into a bowl and let stand 1 hour. Correct the texture and seasoning by adding 1 teaspoon at a time of the reserved grape juice and a little honey. Keeps refrigerated for 1 month.
