Vegetarian, Apple, Cranberry

Nancy Arum 12 to 15 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh cranberries
  • 3 medium apples, unpeeled
  • ½ cup walnuts, chopped
  • ½ cup celery, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons crystallized ginger, chopped (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon rind
  • 3 tablespoons Cherry Heering
  • ½ cup sugar

Preparation

  1. Coarsely chop cranberries and apples in food processor and transfer to a bowl.
  2. Combine remaining ingredients with the fruit and refrigerate, covered, overnight or two days before serving.
