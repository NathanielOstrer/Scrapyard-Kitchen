Creme Fraiche, Horseradish, Oyster

Florence Fabricant 6 servings as a first course, 12 or more with drinks Time20 minutes

Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons crème fraîche
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • 1 teaspoon ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons minced chives
  • Ground white pepper
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 3 dozen oysters on the half-shell

Preparation

  1. Heat broiler. Place crème fraîche in a medium-size bowl and mix in horseradish, ketchup and chives. Season with pepper. Beat egg whites until they hold peaks. Fold into crème fraîche mixture.
  2. Arrange oysters on a baking pan lined with crumpled aluminum foil to hold them steady, or place them in madeleine pans. Spoon about a tablespoon of the crème fraîche mixture on each. Broil about 3 inches from the source of heat, a minute or so, until lightly dappled with brown on top. Arrange on a platter or on individual plates and serve.
