Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix, Egg, American

Marian Burros About 2 dozen Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 7 cups chocolate chip cookie mix (see recipe)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten

Preparation

  1. Stir the mix to distribute chips and nuts evenly. Combine with vanilla and eggs; this creates a heavy batter.
  2. Drop batter by the heaping tablespoon on greased baking sheets.
  3. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
  4. Cool slightly on baking sheets.

Tip

  • For flatter cookies, pat batter down with back of spoon.
