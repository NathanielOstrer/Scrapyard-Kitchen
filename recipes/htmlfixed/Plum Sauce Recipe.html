Plum, Chinese

Moira Hodgson 2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 pound plums
  • ½ cup port wine
  • 2 tablespoons minced lemon peel
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoons sugar (or more to taste)

Preparation

  1. Pit the plums and put them in a saucepan. Add the port, lemon peel, lemon juice and sugar. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Taste for sweetness, adding more sugar if necessary. You may put the sauce through a strainer or serve it as a compote. Cool and serve at room temperature.
