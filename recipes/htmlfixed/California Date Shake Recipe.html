Date, Vanilla Ice Cream

Kim Severson 1 shake (1 1/3 cups) Time3 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 4 pitted Medjool dates (about 3 ounces), coarsely chopped
  • ¼ cup very cold milk
  • 1 ¼ cups vanilla ice cream

Preparation

  1. In a blender, blend dates and milk until smooth and very frothy. Add ice cream and pulse a few times, until just blended.
