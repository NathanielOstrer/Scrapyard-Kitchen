Vegetarian, Vegan, Berry, Cherry, Watermelon

Karen Baar 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds watermelon
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup lime juice
  • 1 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 1 cup cherries
  • 1 cup strawberries

Preparation

  1. Remove rind and seeds from watermelon. Cut flesh into chunks, and puree in blender. Strain into bowl (there should be about 4 cups).
  2. Mix puree with sugar and lime juice. Chill 2 hours.
  3. To serve, cut blackberries and raspberries in half. Pit cherries, and cut in half. Slice strawberries.
  4. Divide fruits into six chilled soup bowls. Pour watermelon mixture over them, and serve immediately.
