Fig, Honey, Mascarpone

Moira Hodgson 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh figs
  • 1 cup mascarpone
  • 2 tablespoons thin honey

Preparation

  1. Wipe the figs with a clean kitchen towel and cut off the stems. Slice the figs in half and arrange them in a circle on a platter, overlapping them slightly. In the center, pile up the mascarpone.
  2. Pour the honey over the figs in a very thin stream (if necessary, heat the honey to thin it out). Serve immediately with thin sugar cookies.
