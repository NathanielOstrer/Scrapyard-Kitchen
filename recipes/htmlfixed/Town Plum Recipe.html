Grape Vodka, Plum Nectar, Red Grape

William L. Hamilton 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Cîroc grape vodka
  • ¾ ounce plum nectar
  • ¼ fresh squeezed lime
  • ¼ fresh squeezed lemon
  • A few purple grapes

Preparation

  1. Shake strained liquid ingredients lightly with ice. Pour into a deep tumbler. Garnish with grapes sliced in half. Stir.
