Cream Cheese, Lobster

Bob Morris About 2 quarts Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ pound cooked lobster meat
  • 2 ½ pounds cream cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream

Preparation

  1. In a food processor, finely chop the lobster meat. Place cream cheese in the bowl of a mixer and blend on medium speed until smooth. Add the sour cream and blend to combine. Add lobster and continue blending until smooth. Serve with raw vegetables, thin toasts or chips.
