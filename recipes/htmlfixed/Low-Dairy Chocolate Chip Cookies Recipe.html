Chocolate

Kate Betts 30 2-inch cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons flaxseed oil
  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup Sucanat or white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups spelt flour, sifted
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a blender, whip flaxseed oil and 1/2 cup water together at high speed for 2 minutes.
  3. Add canola oil, vanilla and maple syrup.
  4. Add remaining ingredients, saving chocolate chips for last. If batter appears too wet, add a little extra flour.
  5. Drop teaspoons of batter onto greased cookie sheet. Bake 8 or 9 minutes.
