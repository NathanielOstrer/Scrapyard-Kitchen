Ingredients

  • 1 small garlic clove, halved, green shoot removed
  • ½ cup cooked white beans, drained and rinsed if using canned beans
  • ½ cup whole milk or 2 percent Greek yogurt or regular yogurt
  • 1 ice cube, if using Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Process garlic in a food processor fitted with a steel blade until minced garlic is adhering to sides. Stop processor and scrape down. Add beans, yogurt, ice cube, tarragon, parsley and chives and process until smooth. With machine running, add lemon juice, salt, and olive oil and process until mixture is smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  2. Scrape into a bowl. Serve as a dip or use with crisp salads (it’s a bit too thick for delicate lettuces like spring mixes).
