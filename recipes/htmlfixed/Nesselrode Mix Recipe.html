Candied Fruit, Chestnut, Cognac, Rum

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About two cups Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups mixed, diced candied fruits
  • 12 marrons (chestnuts) in syrup, chopped, about 1/2 cup
  • ¼ cup dark rum or Cognac
