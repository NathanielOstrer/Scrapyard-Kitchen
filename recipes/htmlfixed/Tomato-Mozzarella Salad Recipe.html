Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Basil, Mozzarella, Tomato, Italian

Florence Fabricant 6 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ripe plum tomatoes
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese
  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup finely minced fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon drained capers
  • Generous pinch hot red pepper flakes
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Dice the tomatoes into one-inch pieces. Place in a bowl.
  2. Dice the mozzarella into one-half-inch pieces. Add to the tomatoes along with the rest of the ingredients and gently fold everything together.
  3. Allow salad to marinate an hour at room temperature, then serve.
