Corn Oil, Hominy, Salt

Molly O'Neill Serves 6 as a snack Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pint corn oil
  • 1 pound dry hominy, washed and thoroughly dried
  • Salt
  • Lime juice (optional)

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet to 375 degrees. Fry the hominy in 1/2-cup batches until golden, about 1 to 2 minutes per batch. Remove from the skillet and drain on paper towels. Add salt to taste and lime juice if desired. Allow to cool.
