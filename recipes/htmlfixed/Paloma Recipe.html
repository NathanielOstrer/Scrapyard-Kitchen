Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • Salt, for rim (optional)
  • 2 ½ ounces tequila
  • Squeeze of lime juice
  • Grapefruit soda (like Squirt or Jarritos), to top
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice (dip the rim in salt first, if you like). Add tequila and lime juice, then top with grapefruit soda. Stir ingredients together gently. Garnish with a wedge of lime.
