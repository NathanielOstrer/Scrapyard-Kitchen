Ingredients

For the dressing

  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • ½ teaspoon lightly toasted cumin seeds, ground
  • ½ teaspoon honey
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons walnut oil or grapeseed oil, or 1 tablespoon each

For the Salad

  • 2 cups diced or shredded turkey
  • 1 tart apple, such as a Granny Smiith, diced
  • 1 ½ cups diced celery
  • ¼ cup raisins
  • ⅓ cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 cups chopped radicchio or endive

Preparation

  1. In a bowl or measuring cup, whisk together mayonnaise, yogurt, curry powder, cumin, honey, lemon juice, salt and oil.
  2. In a large salad bowl combine turkey, apples, celery, raisins, walnuts and radicchio or endive. Add dressing, toss together, and serve.

Tip

  • Advance preparation: This keeps well for a couple of days in the refrigerator but the lettuce will not retain its crisp texture.
