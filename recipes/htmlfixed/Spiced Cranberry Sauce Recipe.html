Muriel Jacobs Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cranberries
  • ⅓ teaspoon each of cinnamon, allspice and nutmeg (or) 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Wash and clean the cranberries, place them in a pan with vinegar and cook over low heat until the berries pop and soften. Put through a sieve and return to the pan.
  2. Add spices, salt and sugar. Continue to cook over low heat until the spices are blended. Pour into hot sterilized jars and seal.
