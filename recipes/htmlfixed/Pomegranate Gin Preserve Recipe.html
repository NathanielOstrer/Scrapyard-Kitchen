Gin, Pomegranate Seed

Melissa Clark About a pint

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pomegranate seeds (from about 3 pomegranates)
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 3-inch strip orange zest
  • About 2 cups gin

Preparation

  1. In a large jar, stir or shake together pomegranate seeds, sugar and orange zest. Pour in gin; cover seeds by about an inch. Cover jar and let sit in a dark, cool place (but not the refrigerator) for at least 3 weeks.
