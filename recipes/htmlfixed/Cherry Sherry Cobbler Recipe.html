Cherry, Sherry, Sugar

Melissa Clark 1 serving

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 cup superfine sugar
  • 1 lemon wedge (1/6 of a medium sized lemon)
  • 4 pitted cherries (or raspberries or blueberries)
  • 3 ounces dry amontillado sherry
  • Orange slice, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Make simple syrup by combining the superfine sugar with 1 cup water in a jar; shake until sugar is dissolved. (This will keep in the refrigerator for weeks.)
  2. In a highball glass, muddle lemon and 3 cherries with 3/4 of an ounce of simple syrup. Add sherry, stir to combine and fill glass with crushed ice. Garnish with another cherry and an orange slice. Serve with a straw.
