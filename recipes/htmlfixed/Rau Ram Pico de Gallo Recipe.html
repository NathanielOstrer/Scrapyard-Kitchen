Jalapeno, Rau Ram, Southwestern

Sam Sifton Serves 6-8 Time15 minutes

Peden & Munk for The New York Times. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Lauren Smith Ford.

Ingredients

  • 2 small jalapeño peppers, stemmed, seeded and finely minced
  • 1⁄2 medium-size onion, peeled and diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons roughly chopped cilantro leaves
  • 2 tablespoons roughly chopped rau ram (Vietnamese coriander)
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons lime juice

Preparation

  1. In a medium-size bowl, combine the peppers, onion, cherry tomatoes, cilantro and rau ram.
  2. Add the fish sauce and lime juice a tablespoon at a time, tasting as you go.
