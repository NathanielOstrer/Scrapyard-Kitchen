Cod, Fish, Portobello, Shiitake, White Mushroom

Molly O'Neill Four main courses Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 6- to 8-ounce fillets of cod
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup mushroom broth (reserved from ragout recipe above)
  • 2 cups mushroom ragout

Preparation

  1. Warm the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the cod, sprinkle with salt and pepper and cook for 3 minutes. Add the broth. Spread the mushroom ragout evenly over the fillets. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until the fish is completely cooked, 8 to 10 minutes.
