Dairy Free

Mark Bittman Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • smoked chopped slab bacon
  • sauerkraut
  • bay leaves
  • thyme
  • dry white wine
  • greens or sausages

Preparation

  1. Cook smoked chopped slab bacon until fat begins to render. Add sauerkraut, thyme, bay leaves and a splash of dry white wine. Cook, stirring occasionally, for up to an hour. Discard bay leaves, and serve with greens or sausages. (And beer.)
