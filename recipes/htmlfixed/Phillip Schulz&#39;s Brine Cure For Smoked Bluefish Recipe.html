Ingredients

  • 1 quart cool water
  • ½ cup kosher salt
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup golden rum
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 10 cloves
  • 10 black peppercorns
  • 10 allspice berries
  • 1 bay leaf

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a ceramic, glass or stainless-steel bowl. Mr. Schulz marinates large bluefish fillets, more than 1 pound each, 2 days in this brine. Smaller fillets can be marinated 1 day or overnight.
