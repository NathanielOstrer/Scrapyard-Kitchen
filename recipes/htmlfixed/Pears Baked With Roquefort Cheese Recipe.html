Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Bartlett Pear, Roquefort Cheese, Walnut

Florence Fabricant 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup ground walnuts
  • 4 tablespoons Roquefort cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 4 ripe but firm Bartlett pears
  • 1 cup dry white wine

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine the walnuts, cheese and lemon juice in a bowl, mashing the ingredients together to make a paste.
  3. Peel, halve and core the pears. Stuff the cavity of each pear half with a spoonful of the Roquefort mixture, mounding it up slightly. Place the stuffed pears in a shallow baking dish and pour the wine around them.
  4. Bake about 30 minutes, until the pears are tender. Allow to cool about 20 minutes before serving.
