Aperol, Orange, Prosecco

Toby Cecchini Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces prosecco
  • Sparkling water
  • 1 ½ to 2 ounces Aperol
  • Orange
  • Lemon
  • 1 green olive

Preparation

  1. In a rocks glass over three or four ice cubes, pour the prosecco, a dash of sparkling water and the Aperol, being sure to follow that order lest the Aperol sink to the bottom without mixing. Garnish with a slice of orange, a twist of lemon (or both) and an olive.
