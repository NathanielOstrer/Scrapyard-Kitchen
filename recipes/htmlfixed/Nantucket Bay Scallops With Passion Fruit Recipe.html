Raw, Bay Scallop, Jalapeno, Passion Fruit Puree, New England

Trip Gabriel 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen Nantucket bay scallops
  • ½ cup passion fruit purée
  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 red jalapeño peppers julienned
  • 12 cilantro leaves, chopped
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients except scallops in glass bowl.
  2. Spoon over freshly shucked scallops on the half shell.
  3. Serve on crushed ice on half shell.
