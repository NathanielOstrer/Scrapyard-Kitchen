Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Nut Free, Orzo, Tomato, Italian

Bryan Miller And Pierre Franey Four servings

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups fresh orzo
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons finely chopped onions
  • ¾ pound ripe fresh tomatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon hot red-pepper flakes

Preparation

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan. Add salt. Cook the orzo according to package directions. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a saucepan. Cook the onions until they are wilted. Add the tomatoes, sprinkle with salt and pepper and the pepper flakes. Stir gently and bring the pan juices to a boil.
  3. Add the orzo and stir gently to blend.
