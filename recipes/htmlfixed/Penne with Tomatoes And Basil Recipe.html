Vegetarian, Basil, Penne, Tomato, Italian

Moira Hodgson 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound penne
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • A large bunch of basil leaves, torn into small pieces
  • Course salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. Cook the penne in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and place in a large bowl with the olive oil. Cover.
  2. Add the tomatoes, garlic and basil, season and mix well. Serve immediately, passing the cheese separately.
