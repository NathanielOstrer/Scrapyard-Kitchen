Barbara Kafka 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 7 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 2-inch piece vanilla bean, split lengthwise
  • 12 ripe figs, preferably black

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, stir together orange juice, lemon juice, sugar, pepper and vanilla bean.
  2. Place an inverted 3-cup souffle dish in the center of a 10-inch pie plate to create a ring mold. Pour the liquid mixture into the dish.
  3. Arrange the figs in a circle, leaning them against the souffle dish with the stems facing up. Invert a second 10-inch pie plate over the first to form a cover.
  4. Cook at 100 percent power in a high-power oven for 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and uncover. Let figs cool before serving warm, or let cool completely. Serve with vanilla ice cream or heavy cream.
