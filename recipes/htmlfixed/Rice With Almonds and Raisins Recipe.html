Almond, Raisin, Rice

Pierre Franey 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼ cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 cup converted rice
  • 2 tablespoons white or black raisins
  • ⅓ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • ⅛ teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 ½ cups fresh or canned chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander

Preparation

  1. Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan. Add the onion, stir and cook until wilted. Add the rice, raisins, almonds, cinnamon, salt and pepper. Stir and blend well.
  2. Add the broth, bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 17 minutes or until tender and all the liquid is absorbed.
  3. Stir in the butter and coriander and serve.
