Oil, Tamari, Wine Vinegar

Marian Burros 6 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup safflower oil
  • 3 tablespoons herb-flavored wine vinegar
  • 2 to 3 teaspoons tamari

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients and whisk to blend or shake well. Serve over assorted wild and cultivated greens
