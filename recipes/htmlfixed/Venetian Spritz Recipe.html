Aperol, Green Olives, Prosecco

Elisabetta Povoledo 1 drink

Dave Yoder for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces prosecco
  • 1 ½ ounces Aperol or other bitter aperitif
  • 1 ½ ounces soda water
  • Green olive, for garnish
