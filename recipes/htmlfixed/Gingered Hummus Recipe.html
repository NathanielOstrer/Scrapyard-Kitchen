Vegetarian, Vegan, Chickpea, Ginger

Florence Fabricant 1 1/2 cups TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons finely minced fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 can chick peas, well drained
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and cayenne pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine ginger, garlic and lemon juice in blender or food processer and process until well mixed.
  2. Add half chick peas, process until pureed, then add remaining chick peas and process until mixture is smooth.
  3. With machine running slowly, drizzle in olive oil.
  4. Season to taste with salt and cayenne pepper. Serve with bread, crackers or toasted triangles of pita bread.
