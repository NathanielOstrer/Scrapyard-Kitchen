Baguette, Egg

Julia Moskin 12 toasts Time5 minutes

Evan Sung for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 12 thin slices baguette, brushed with olive oil and toasted
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, preferably still warm, peeled and quartered
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • 3 slices cooked bacon, broken into bite-size pieces
  • 1 teaspoon finely snipped chives

Preparation

  1. Spread a little mayonnaise in center of each toast. Place an egg quarter on top, cut side up. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Dab a little mayonnaise on center of each egg yolk and rest a piece of bacon on top. Sprinkle with chives and serve.
