Healthy, Lime, Peanut, Sugar

Patricia Leigh Brown 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 limes
  • ½ cup roasted unsalted peanuts
  • ½ cup superfine sugar

Preparation

  1. Wash and quarter 3 limes, and put them in a blender with the peanuts, sugar and 2 cups cold water. Blend at high speed for about 45 seconds, until all ingredients are finely crushed.
  2. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh strainer into a glass jar or pitcher, pressing down on the solids to obtain as much liquid as possible.
