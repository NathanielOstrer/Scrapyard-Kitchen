Vegetarian, Vegan, Mushroom, Soy Sauce, Spice, White Onion

Molly O'Neill One quart

Ingredients

  • Stems from 6 pounds of mushrooms
  • 1 small white onion
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 4 black peppercorns
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf

Preparation

  1. Place all the ingredients in a soup pot. Cover with half a gallon of cold water, place over low heat and simmer for 2 hours, until the liquid has reduced by half. Strain, cool and refrigerate.
