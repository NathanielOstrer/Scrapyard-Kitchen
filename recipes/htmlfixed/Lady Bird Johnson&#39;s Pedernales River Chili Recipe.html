Gluten Free, Ground Beef, Tomato

Jennifer Steinhauer 12 servings Time1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons chile powder, or to taste
  • 1 ½ cups canned tomatoes, cut up
  • 2 to 6 dashes hot sauce, or to taste
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Cook the meat, onion and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat, stirring, until lightly browned. Stir in the oregano, cumin, chile powder, tomatoes, hot sauce, salt and 2 cups hot water. Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer, covered, for about 1 hour, skimming off the fat as it cooks.
