Brandy, Dark Rum, Port

Rosie Schaap Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 parts ruby port
  • 2 parts brandy
  • 1 part dark rum
  • 1 part spiced clementine syrup
  • 1 part cranberry juice
  • One handful clementine peels (with a paring knife, remove as much pith as possible).

Preparation

  1. To a large punch bowl, add all the ingredients, plus reserved clementine peels. Stir gently, then add a large block of ice.
  2. For each serving, add as much seltzer as you like (it’s strong) and a little grated nutmeg.
