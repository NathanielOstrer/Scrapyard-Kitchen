Campari, Orange

Eric Asimov One drink

Hiroko Masuike/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 wedge roasted orange
  • 1 ounce sweet red vermouth
  • 1 ¼ ounces Campari
  • 1 ¼ ounces Lini Lambrusco Bianco, or other sparkling white wine
  • Orange peel for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place the orange in a mixing glass and add vermouth. Muddle the two so that the charred bits are released into the vermouth.
  2. Add Campari and ice, and stir.
  3. Strain into a chilled rocks glass that is filled with ice and top with sparkling wine. Do not stir as this will dissipate the bubbles. Garnish with orange peel.
