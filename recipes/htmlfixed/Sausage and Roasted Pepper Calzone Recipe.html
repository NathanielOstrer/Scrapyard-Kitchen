Pie Crust, Red Pepper, Sweet Italian Sausage, Italian

Marian Burros 4 to 6 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 15-ounce package refrigerated pie crusts
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ½ cup sliced green onions
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon basil leaves
  • ¼ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅓ cup ricotta cheese
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes, 2 cups
  • 7 ounce jar roasted red peppers, drained, chopped
  • ¼ cup pine nuts

Preparation

  1. Allow both crust pouches to stand at room temperature 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in large skillet brown sausage with onions and garlic; drain. Stir in basil, Italian seasoning and pepper. In small bowl, beat eggs with fork. Reserve 1 1/2 tablespoons; set aside. Add ricotta cheese to remaining eggs; mix well. Stir into sausage mixture. Add mozzarella cheese, red peppers and 3 tablespoons of the pine nuts; mix well.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  4. Remove crusts from pouches; unfold. Press out fold lines. If crusts crack, wet fingers and push edges together. Place one prepared crust on end of large ungreased cookie sheet with about 1/3 of crust hanging over edge. Spoon half of sausage mixture over half of crust on cookie sheet, leaving about 1-inch border and mounding filling towards center. Fold pie crust half over filling; press edges with fork to seal. Cut four 1-inch slits in top of crust. Repeat with second crust and remaining filling at opposite end of cookie sheet. Brush with reserved 1 1/2 tablespoons egg. Sprinkle with remaining pine nuts.
  5. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until crust is golden brown. To serve, cut each calzone in half or into 3 wedges.
