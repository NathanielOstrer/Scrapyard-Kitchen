Rosie Schaap 1 drink Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 ounces light rum
  • Cola (preferably cane cola, such as Mexican Coca-Cola or Boylan), to fill
  • Squeeze of lime, plus a lime wedge for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice and add rum, cola to fill and squeeze of lime. Garnish with a wedge of lime.
