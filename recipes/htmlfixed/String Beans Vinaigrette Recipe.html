Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Green Bean, Parmesan, Tomato

Marialisa Calta Four servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound green beans, washed, ends snipped off and sliced on the diagonal
  • ½ medium onion, chopped fine
  • 1 small clove garlic, chopped fine
  • ⅓ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons tarragon vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced in wedges
  • ⅓ cup black olive

Preparation

  1. Fill a large saucepan with water and bring to a boil. Add salt to taste and the beans. Cook until tender but still firm, about 4 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Place beans in a bowl with the remaining ingredients except the tomatoes and olives. Mix well and chill. Serve with the tomatoes and olives.
