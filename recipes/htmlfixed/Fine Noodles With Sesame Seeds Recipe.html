Vegetarian, Noodle, Olive Oil, Sesame Seed

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ pound fine noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Boil the noodles in a large quantity of water for 2 or 3 minutes or according to package instructions. Do not overcook. Drain.
  2. Heat the oil in a nonstick skillet. Add the sesame seeds and saute them until lightly browned. Add the noodles, cumin, parsley, salt and pepper, tossing them well. Serve immediately.
