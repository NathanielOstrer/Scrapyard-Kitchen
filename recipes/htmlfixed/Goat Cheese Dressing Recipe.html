Fresh Herb, Goat Cheese, Low Fat Milk

Marian Burros 4 servings, enough for a total of 6 ounces greens Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons fresh goat cheese
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons skim milk
  • 3 or 4 sprigs thyme, marjoram or oregano to yield 1 1/2 tablespoons of chopped leaves

Preparation

  1. In small bowl, mash cheese with milk until smooth. Stir herbs into cheese mixture. Toss with greens.
