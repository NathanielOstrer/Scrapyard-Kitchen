Grape Brandy, Passion Fruit Puree

William L. Hamilton

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces pisco
  • 1 ounce passion fruit purée
  • 1 ounce orange juice
  • Orange twist

Preparation

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add first three ingredients, shake, and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with the twist.
