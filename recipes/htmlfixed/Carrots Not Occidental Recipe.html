Vegetarian, Butter, Carrot

Robert Farrar Capon 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound carrots, peeled and cut crosswise
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil (or more, as desired)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh coriander
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes, plus 1 teaspoon sugar (or 1 teaspoon hot pepper jelly)
  • Salt as desired

Preparation

  1. Cook carrots in boiling water until just tender. Drain and reserve.
  2. Melt butter in skillet and cook ginger over low heat 1 minute. Add carrots and heat through, tossing to coat with ginger-butter mixture. Add remaining ingredients and toss to coat.
