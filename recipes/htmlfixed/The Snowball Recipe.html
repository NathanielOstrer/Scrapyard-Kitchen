7 Up, Advocaat

Rosie Schaap

Sarah Anne Ward for The New York Times. Prop stylist: Maeve Sheridan.

Ingredients

  • 1 part advocaat
  • 3 to 4 parts U.K.-style lemonade
  • 7-Up or other lemon-lime soda

Preparation

  1. Fill a Collins glass with ice.
  2. Add advocaat, then top up with the lemonade or lemon-lime soda.
  3. Stir gently.
  4. Squeeze in a little more lime juice, if you like.
