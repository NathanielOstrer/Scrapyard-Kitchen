Mandarin Vodka, Mint, Pomegranate Juice

William L. Hamilton

Alan Decker for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Absolut Mandrin vodka
  • 1 ounce pomegranate juice
  • Sprig of mint

Preparation

  1. Shake vodka and pomegranate juice with ice. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with mint.
