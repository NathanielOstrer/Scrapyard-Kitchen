Vegetarian, Pizza Dough, Red Onion, Tomato, Italian

Mark Bittman 1 large, 2 medium, or more smaller pizzas Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 ripe tomatoes
  • Coarse salt
  • Pizza dough (see recipe)
  • 1 medium red onion or 4 shallots, peeled and chopped
  • 20 black olives like calamata, pitted and chopped
  • Olive oil as needed

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Core tomatoes, then cut in half horizontally. Gently squeeze out liquid and shake out most seeds, then slice tomatoes as thin as possible. Salt lightly and let sit at least 10 minutes, then drain off any excess liquid.
  3. Top pizza rounds on baking sheet with tomatoes, onions, olives and a little olive oil. Bake for about 15 minutes, or until nicely browned.
