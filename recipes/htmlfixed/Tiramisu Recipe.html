Espresso, Ladyfinger, Mascarpone, Italian

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey Eight or more servings

Ingredients

  • 5 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups mascarpone cheese, available in specialty food shops
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 6 to 9 double ladyfingers or Champagne biscuits
  • ¾ cup freshly made espresso coffee
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder, approximately

Preparation

  1. Beat the yolks with the sugar until the mixture is thickened and light lemon colored. Add the mascarpone and beat until thoroughly blended.
  2. Whip the cream and fold the cheese mixture into it.
  3. Dip the ladyfingers in the espresso and arrange them on a serving dish measuring about 9 1/2 by 13 1/2 by 2 inches. Pour in the cheese and cream mixture and smooth over the top.
  4. Put the cocoa in a small sieve and dust the top of the dessert evenly. Refrigerate.
