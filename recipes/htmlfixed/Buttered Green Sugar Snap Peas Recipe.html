Ingredients

  • 1 pound sugar snap peas
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon shredded fresh mint

Preparation

  1. Pluck off and discard the string from each pea pod.
  2. Bring salted water to boil; there should be enough to cover peas when added. Add peas. When water returns to a boil, cook about 3 minutes. Do not overcook. Drain.
  3. Return peas to saucepan. Add pepper, salt, butter and mint. Stir to blend until the pieces are well coated and hot. Serve immediately.
