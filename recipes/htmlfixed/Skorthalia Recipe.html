Garlic, Ground Almond, Mashed Potato, Greek

Moira Hodgson

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons mashed potato
  • 1 tablespoon ground almonds
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Place all the ingredients in a food processor. Blend until smooth and chill in a small bowl.
