Vegetarian, Nut Free, Buttermilk, Flour, Pumpkin Puree

Florence Fabricant 4 to 6 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup whole-wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 extra-large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup cooked pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Warm maple syrup

Preparation

  1. Mix the flours, baking powder, salt, sugar and cinnamon together in a bowl. Beat the egg, melted butter and pumpkin together and add them along with the buttermilk to make a batter that is a little thicker than the buttermilk. Add more buttermilk if necessary.
  2. Heat a large skillet or griddle and brush with butter or cooking oil. When the surface is very hot, fry the pancakes, a few at a time, turning them as they brown on one side to brown on the other.
  3. Serve with warm maple syrup.
