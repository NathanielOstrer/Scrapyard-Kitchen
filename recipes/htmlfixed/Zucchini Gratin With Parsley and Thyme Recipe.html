Zucchini

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 medium zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon white wine
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chopped Italian parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Sprinkle the zucchini rounds with 2 teaspoons of the salt, place in a sieve and let drain for 1 hour. Pat dry. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Arrange the zucchini in a 10-inch quiche dish in concentric circles, overlapping the pieces slightly.
  2. Whisk together the wine, oil, parsley, thyme, remaining salt and pepper to taste and spoon over the zucchini. Bake until zucchini is tender but not too soft, about 25 minutes.
