Pomegranate

Eugenia Bone 2 cups Time10 minutes

Karsten Moran for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pomegranate juice
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients in a small pot. Cook over medium heat for a few minutes, stirring to dissolve sugar and salt. Set aside to cool.
  2. For the best result, pour into a bottle or jar that has been sterilized (boil it and its cap or lid for 10 minutes). Syrup will hold in the refrigerator for about a month.
