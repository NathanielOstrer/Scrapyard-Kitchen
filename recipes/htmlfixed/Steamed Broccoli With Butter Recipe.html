Vegetarian, High Fiber, Broccoli, Butter

Robert Farrar Capon 12 servings Time50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 bunches broccoli
  • ¼ pound butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Trim bottom inch off broccoli stalks and discard. Cut each stalk crosswise into 3 pieces.
  2. Cut topmost piece of each stalk into small flowerets. Cut remaining pieces into fine julienne. Put all into cold water for at least 1/2 hour to refresh them.
  3. Bring suitably large steamer of water to boil, add all broccoli and steam, covered, for 5 minutes, tossing from top to bottom a few times.
  4. When just barely tender, remove to collander, spray liberally with cold water and drain.
  5. When ready to serve, melt butter in skillet and toss broccoli with salt and pepper to taste, until heated through.
