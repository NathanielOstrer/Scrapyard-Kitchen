Dill, Red Potato

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 small red potatoes, washed thoroughly, about 1 pound
  • 4 sprigs dill
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes in a pot, cover with water; add dill and salt. Bring to a boil, and simmer for 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  2. Drain potatoes, and discard dill sprigs. Place potatoes in a saucepan; add butter, chopped dill, salt, pepper and cumin. Stir gently to coat potatoes. Serve immediately.
