Low Fat, Parsley, Rice Vinegar

Mark Bittman About 1 1/2 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 cups loosely packed parsley, coarsely chopped
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • ½ cup rice vinegar or white wine vinegar

Preparation

  1. Combine parsley, garlic and salt to taste in a small bowl and stir in vinegar. Add water a tablespoon at a time and taste; acidity should be tolerable and mixture quite thin.
  2. Let the sauce rest for 10 to 30 minutes before serving. Stir again right before serving. Use the same day: this sauce does not keep well, even when refrigerated.
