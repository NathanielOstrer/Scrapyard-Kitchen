Vegetarian, Potato

Molly O'Neill 4 cups Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-size potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes in a saucepan. Cover with water. Simmer over medium heat until tender. Drain.
  2. Pass potatoes through a ricer. Warm the milk and stir it and cream, butter, salt and pepper into the potatoes.
