Gluten Free, Nut Free, Balsamic Vinegar, Citric Acid, Heavy Cream, Italian

Melissa Clark 10 servings Time15 minutes plus chilling time

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 4 cups heavy cream
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon citric acid (sold in health food stores).

Preparation

  1. Stir together all the ingredients in a heavy-bottomed pot and bring to a simmer over medium heat (it will immediately thicken and then thin out).
  2. Divide mixture among 10 four-ounce ramekins. Cool, then cover tightly with plastic wrap and chill for at least 1 hour or until ready to serve, up to 3 days.
