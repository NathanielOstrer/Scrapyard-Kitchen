Champagne, Cynar, Simple Syrup, Vodka

Jonathan Miles 1 serving

Katie Orlinsky for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Zubrowka Bison Grass vodka
  • ½ ounce Cynar
  • ½ ounce simple syrup
  • One sprig fresh mint
  • Champagne, for topping

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients except Champagne in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake vigorously, then strain into a chilled cocktail glass and top with Champagne.
