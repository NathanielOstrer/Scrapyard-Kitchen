Dry Vermouth, Gin

William L. Hamilton Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce (3 tablespoons) gin
  • 1 ounce (3 tablespoons) dry vermouth
  • 2 dashes Pernod
  • 2 dashes maraschino liqueur
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Preparation

  1. Stir ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with maraschino cherry.
