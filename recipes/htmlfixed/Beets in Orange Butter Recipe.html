Vegetarian, Beet, Butter, Orange Juice

Moira Hodgson 4 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 small cooked beets
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup fresh orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange peel
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh tarragon or parsley leaves, chopped

Preparation

  1. While the beets are cooking, prepare the butter. Melt the butter, add the orange juice, peel, salt and pepper and tarragon or parsley. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Slice the cooked beets or chop them in the food processor. Pour on the butter sauce and serve.
