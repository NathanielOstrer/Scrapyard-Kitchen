Cranberry Juice, Grapefruit Juice, Vodka

Rosie Schaap Time5 minutes

William Brinson for The New York Times. Food stylist: Ed Gabriels. Prop stylist: Susan Brinson.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 2 ounces grapefruit juice
  • 2 ounces cranberry juice
  • Salt-and-chili-powder mix for rim, optional
  • Tabasco sauce, optional
  • Lime wedge

Preparation

  1. Add vodka and juices to an ice-filled highball glass (with or without salt-chili rim).
  2. Add hot sauce to taste.
  3. Garnish with lime wedge.
