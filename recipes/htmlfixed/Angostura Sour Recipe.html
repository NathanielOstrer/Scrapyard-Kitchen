Angostura Bitters, Egg White, Lime Juice

Steven Stern 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 1 egg white
  • ¾ ounce fresh lime juice
  • 1 ½ ounces Angostura bitters
  • 1 ounce simple syrup

Preparation

  1. Pour the egg white and lime juice into a cocktail shaker and shake for 30 seconds. Add the bitters and syrup, fill with ice and shake hard for another 30 seconds. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Tip

  • To make simple syrup, combine 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water in a small saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until sugar dissolves. Let cool, then transfer to a very clean jar or bottle. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.
