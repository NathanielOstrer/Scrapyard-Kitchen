Raw, Vegetarian, Blue Cheese, Mesclun, Yogurt

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce mache or 1 1/2 ounces mesclun
  • 1 tablespoon blue cheese
  • 2 tablespoons nonfat yogurt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash the mache or mesclun, and dry.
  2. Meanwhile, mash the blue cheese with the yogurt to make a smooth dressing. Mix greens with dressing, and season with pepper.
