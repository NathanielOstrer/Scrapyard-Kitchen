Banana Chip, Pineapple

Anita M. Samuels

Ingredients

  • 2 cups unsweetened banana chips
  • 1 cup dried pineapple
  • 1 cup chopped pitted dates

Preparation

  1. In a food processor, process banana chips until they are of a flour-like consistency.
  2. Add the dried pineapple and chopped dates. Process all of the ingredients until they begin to combine and ball up.
  3. Spread mixture into a 9-inch pie pan. Press dough out until it is evenly distributed in the pan. Edges may be fluted if desired.
