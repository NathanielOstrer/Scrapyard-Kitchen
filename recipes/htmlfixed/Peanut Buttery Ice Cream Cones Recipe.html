Ice Cream, Peanut Butter

Rena Coyle 12 cones Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ⅓ cup egg whites (3 to 4 large eggs)
  • ¼ cup flour
  • ¼ cup milk

Preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter and sugar. Using an electric mixer, blend this mixture together. Add the egg whites and blend. Add the flour and blend. Add the milk and blend smooth.
  2. Heat a pizelle iron. Place about one tablespoon of the mixture onto the iron. Press close and cook until golden brown, about 35 seconds. Remove one at a time and, using a metal cone as a guide, roll into a cone shape.
