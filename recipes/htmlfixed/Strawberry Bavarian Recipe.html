Raw, Cream, Strawberry, Unflavored Gelatin, German

Marian Burros Time30 minutes

Paul J. Richards/Agence France-Presse — Getty Images

Ingredients

  • 1 quart strawberries, hulled
  • 2 envelopes unflavored gelatin
  • ¼ cup cold water
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cherry-flavored liqueur
  • 2 cups heavy cream, whipped
  • Whole strawberries for garnish

Preparation

  1. Puree strawberries in blender.
  2. In saucepan soften gelatin in cold water. Add to puree with sugar.
  3. Heat and stir until gelatin is dissolved. Cool and add cherry liqueur.
  4. Chill, stirring occasionally, until mixture begins to thicken.
  5. Fold in whipped cream and spoon into eight-cup mold or serving bowl until mixture is set.
  6. To serve, unmold and top with whole berries.
