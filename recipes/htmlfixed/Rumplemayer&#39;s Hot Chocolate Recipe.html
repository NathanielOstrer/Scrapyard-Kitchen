Milk, Semisweet Chocolate

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 7 ounces semisweet chocolate,chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Ground cinnamon, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine the milk, sugar and chocolate in a large saucepan. Heat, stirring frequently, until the chocolate is melted and the mixture comes to a simmer.
  2. Meanwhile, whip the heavy cream to soft peaks. Ladle the hot chocolate into 4 mugs, top with whipped cream and sprinkle lightly with sifted cinnamon. Serve immediately.
