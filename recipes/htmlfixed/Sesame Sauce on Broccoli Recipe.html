Broccoli, Dry Sherry, Sesame Seed, Asian

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound whole broccoli or 8 ounces florettes (3 1/2 to 4 cups)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • Fresh or frozen ginger to yield 2 teaspoons coarsely grated
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon hot pepper flakes
  • 1 or 2 green onions to yield 2 tablespoons chopped

Preparation

  1. Remove tough stems from whole broccoli, and cut florettes into bite-size pieces. Steam 5 to 7 minutes, depending on size of the pieces.
  2. Meanwhile, toast the sesame seeds in a toaster oven (or a conventional oven at 400 degrees).
  3. Grate the ginger.
  4. Heat sesame oil in a nonstick pan. Add ginger; cook 30 seconds over low heat. Add sherry, soy and pepper flakes, and heat through. Remove from heat. When broccoli is cooked, drain, and stir into sesame-sherry mixture. Coat well.
  5. Meanwhile, wash and chop green onion, and sprinkle over broccoli with toasted sesame seed.
