Peach, White Port

Robert Willey 1 drink Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 3 ounces white port
  • ½ cup fresh peach slices, plus 1 additional peach slice for garnish

Preparation

  1. Mix honey well with a teaspoon of hot water and cool. Put the honey syrup, port and peach slices in a cocktail shaker, add 1 1/2 cups of ice and shake vigorously. Pour everything into a highball glass. Garnish with the remaining peach slice.
