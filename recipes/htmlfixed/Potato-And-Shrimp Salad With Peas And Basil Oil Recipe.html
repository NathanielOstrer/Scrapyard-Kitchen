Green Pea, Red Potato, Shrimp

Molly O'Neill Four servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 small red potatoes, steamed until tender and quartered
  • 1 pound cooked and peeled shrimp
  • 1 cup shelled fresh green peas, cooked until tender
  • 1 jalapeno, stemmed, seeded and minced
  • 4 teaspoons basil oil (recipe above)
  • 1 teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine potatoes, shrimp, peas and jalapeno in a bowl. Toss in the basil oil, salt and pepper. Taste and adjust seasoning if desired. Can be served cold or at room temperature.
