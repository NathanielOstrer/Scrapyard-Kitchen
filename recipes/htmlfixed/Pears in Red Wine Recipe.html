Pear, Red Wine

Moira Hodgson 12 servings Time5 hours 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 pears Approximately
  • ½ bottle dry red wine
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • Dash cinnamon
  • ½ pint heavy cream, lightly whipped

Preparation

  1. Peel and core the pears but leave stems intact.
  2. Put pears in a heavy casserole large enough to hold them comfortably, stems up. Pour in the wine and enough water to cover the pears. Add the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Simmer, uncovered, for about 5 hours, or until the liquid has reduced to a glaze. If the syrup is too liquid but the pears are cooked, remove them to a serving dish. Boil the liquid down until it is thick enough to coat the spoon. Pour over the pears and allow them to cool at room temperature.
  4. Serve the cream separately.
