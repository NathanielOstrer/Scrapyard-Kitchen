Peppercorn, Red Wine Vinegar, Shallot, French

Moira Hodgson Four servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 shallots
  • 16 peppercorns
  • 8 tablespoons red wine vinegar

Preparation

  1. Thinly slice shallots. Crush the peppercorns by wrapping in a towel and hitting them with a hammer.
  2. Combine the shallots, peppercorns and vinegar in a small bowl. Mix thoroughly.
