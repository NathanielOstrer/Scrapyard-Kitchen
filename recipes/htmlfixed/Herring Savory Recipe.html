Bread Crumb, Salt Herring, Scandinavian

Robert Farrar Capon

Ingredients

  • 2 salt herrings
  • ⅓ cup dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • ⅓ cup cream
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives

Preparation

  1. Soak whole salt herrings for 12 hours in several changes of cold water. Remove from water and bone and skin them, producing 4 fillets.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Coat fillets thoroughly with bread crumbs. Butter small au gratin dish with 1 tablespoon butter, lay on fillets and dot with remaining butter.
  4. Pour cream over and bake 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped chives before serving.
