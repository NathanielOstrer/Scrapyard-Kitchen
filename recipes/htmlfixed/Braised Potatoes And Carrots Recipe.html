Carrot, Chicken Broth, Potato

Barbara Kafka Four servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 12 ounces carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • ½ cup chicken broth, canned or homemade

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a half-quart souffle dish. Cover tightly with microwave plastic wrap and cook at 100 percent power in a 650- to 700-watt microwave oven for 10 minutes. Prick the plastic to release steam.
  2. Remove from the oven and uncover. Add to the roast chicken.
