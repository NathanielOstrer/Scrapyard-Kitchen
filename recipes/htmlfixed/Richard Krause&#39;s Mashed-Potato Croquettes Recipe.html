Mashed Potato

Marian Burros 12 croquettes Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup leftover mashed potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon potato flour or cornstarch
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 scallion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tablespoon yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups peanut oil for frying
  • 4 tablespoons creme fraiche
  • 2 tablespoons finely julienned prosciutto

Preparation

  1. Mix everything together but creme fraiche, peanut oil and prosciutto. Form mixture into 12 equal-size flat ovals.
  2. Deep-fry the ovals in oil heated to 375 degrees until brown on both sides. Do not crowd. Drain on paper towels.
  3. Top each with 1 teaspoon creme fraiche and a pinch of julienned prosciutto. Arrange on serving platter.
