Gin, American

Robert Simonson 1 drink

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ ounces Old Tom gin, preferably Hayman’s
  • ½ ounce fresh lime juice
  • ½ teaspoon sugar

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients in a cocktail shaker three-quarters filled with ice. Shake vigorously for about 30 seconds. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
