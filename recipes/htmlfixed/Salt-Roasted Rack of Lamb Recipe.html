Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Kosher Salt, Lamb

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups kosher salt
  • 1 rack of lamb
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Spread the salt over the bottom of a medium-size roasting pan. Rub some of the salt over both sides of the rack of lamb. Season both sides with pepper.
  2. Roast until the lamb is cooked medium-rare, about 35 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before carving.
