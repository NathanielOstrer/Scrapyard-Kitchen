Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces white rum
  • ½ ounce fresh lime juice
  • ½ teaspoon superfine sugar

Preparation

  1. Fill a shaker with ice and add all ingredients. Shake well and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
