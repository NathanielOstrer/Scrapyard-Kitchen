Vegetarian, Nut Free, Buttermilk, Molasses, Raisin

Molly O'Neill 3 loaves (about 9 servings)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raisins
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 1 ½ cups whole-wheat flour
  • 1 cup white flour
  • 2 ½ cups bran flakes
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup dark corn syrup
  • ½ cup molasses
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon butter for greasing the tin cans

Preparation

  1. Soak raisins in rum 2 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine flours, bran and baking soda. In a separate bowl, combine egg, sugar, corn syrup, molasses, buttermilk and raisins (with liquor). Mix well. Stir in the flour mixture.
  3. Pour the mixture into three clean, well-greased tin cans (2 1/2-cup capacity each), filling each about two-thirds full. Cover each tightly with foil. Place in a baking dish and add water to reach halfway up the side of the cans. Cover baking dish with foil, place in oven and bake 3 hours. Cool 15 minutes before removing from cans.
