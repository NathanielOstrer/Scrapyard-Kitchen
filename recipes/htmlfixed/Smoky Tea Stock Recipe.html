Vegan, Ginger, Tea

Mark Bittman About 6 cups of stock Time15 minutes

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Maeve Sheridan.

Ingredients

  • Sliced ginger
  • ¼ cup Lapsang souchong tea leaves (green tea is also good) or 4 tea bags
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper

Preparation

  1. Drop some sliced ginger into 6 cups water, bring to a boil, then turn off the heat.
  2. Let rest for a few minutes, then stir in 1/4 cup Lapsang souchong tea leaves (green tea is also good). If you can’t find loose tea leaves, use 4 tea bags.
  3. Steep for 5 or 10 minutes, then strain. Season with soy sauce if you like, and add some black pepper.
