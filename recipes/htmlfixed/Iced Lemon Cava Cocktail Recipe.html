Lemon, Sparkling Wine

Matt Lee And Ted Lee Serves 4 TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup plus 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 ½ cups cava sparkling wine
  • 2 cups crushed ice
  • Mint leaves, chopped, for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a container fitted with a tight lid, combine the sugar and 1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon water. Cover and shake for 3 minutes. Let rest, then shake again for 30 seconds. Transfer to a small pitcher. Stir in the lemon juice, then the cava.
  2. Fill 4 tall, narrow half-cup tumblers with crushed ice. Pour in enough of the cava mixture to just cover the ice. Garnish with fresh mint.
