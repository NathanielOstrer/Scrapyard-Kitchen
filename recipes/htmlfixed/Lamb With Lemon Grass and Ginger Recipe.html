Lamb Shoulder

Mark Bittman

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Susan Ottoviano.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup minced lemon grass
  • 2 tablespoons minced ginger
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • black pepper
  • 2 pounds of lamb shoulder

Preparation

  1. Combine lemon grass, ginger, soy sauce, orange juice, minced garlic, and pepper. Rub half the mixture over the lamb, and marinate overnight.
  2. Heat a grill or broiler with the rack 4 to 6 inches from the flame. Wipe off the marinade; grill or broil, turning once, until medium, 4 or 5 minutes per side.
  3. Serve with the remaining sauce.
