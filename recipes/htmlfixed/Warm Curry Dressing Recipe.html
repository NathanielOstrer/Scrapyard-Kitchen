Dairy Free, Curry Powder, Vinegar

Pierre Franey About 1 cup

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons Dijon-style
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons Balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup chopped scallions
  • ⅓ cup olive or vegetable oil
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup coarsely chopped fresh basil or chervil

Preparation

  1. Place the mustard, curry powder, vinegar and scallions in a saucepan. Blend well over low heat with a wire whisk. Add the oil, blending well. Remove from the heat and add salt, pepper and basil. Keep warm.
