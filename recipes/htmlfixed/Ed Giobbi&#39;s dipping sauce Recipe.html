Basil, Oil, Pine Nut, Barbecue

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About four cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup safflower oil
  • ¼ cup coarsely chopped garlic
  • ½ cup pine nuts
  • 12 cups loosely packed fresh basil
  • 3 cups loosely packed mint leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste, if desired

Preparation

  1. Combine three-quarter cup of the olive oil, the safflower oil, garlic and pine nuts in the container of a food processor or electric blender. Blend well.
  2. Add the basil, mint, pepper and salt. Blend.
  3. Pour the mixture into a one-quart jar and pour the remaining oil on top. This keeps well in the refrigerator.
