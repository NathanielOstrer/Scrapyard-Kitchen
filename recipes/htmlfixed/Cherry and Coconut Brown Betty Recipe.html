Cherry, Coconut, Southern

Kim Severson 6 to 8 servings Time1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Butter, for greasing baking dish
  • 4 cups halved and pitted cherries (from about 1 1/2 pounds whole cherries)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup freshly grated coconut (see note)
  • 2 cups fresh bread crumbs (preferably from brioche, challah or country bread)
  • Chilled heavy cream, for serving

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Butter an 8-inch-square baking dish. Spread 1 cup cherries in bottom of dish. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 tablespoons coconut and 1/2 cup bread crumbs. Repeat three more times with cherries and dry ingredients.
  2. Cover baking dish with foil. Bake until cherry juice bubbles and candies a bit on the edges, about 1 hour. Remove foil and place dish under a broiler to toast the top layer, 1 to 3 minutes.
  3. Serve warm; pass a pitcher of chilled cream at the table for drizzling on top.

Tip

  • Frozen coconut may be used. If it is sweetened, reduce the amount of sugar to 1/4 cup.
