Vegetarian, Celery Root

Moira Hodgson Four servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 knobs celery root, peeled and cut into one-inch cubes
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup heavy cream, approximately
  • Dash freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. Simmer the celery root in water to cover until soft (about 10 to 15 minutes).
  2. Puree in a food mill and return to saucepan. Add salt, pepper, cream and nutmeg. The puree should have a consistency similar to mashed potatoes.
  3. Before serving, dot with butter.
