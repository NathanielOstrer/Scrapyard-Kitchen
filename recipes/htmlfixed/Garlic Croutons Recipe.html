Bread, Garlic, Italian

Pierre Franey About 1 cup TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 very thin slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled but left whole

Preparation

  1. Trim crusts from bread. Cut bread into 1/2-inch cubes or smaller. There should be about 1 cup.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet; when hot, add bread and garlic cloves. Cook, shaking skillet and stirring, until bread cubes are golden brown. Drain in a sieve. Discard garlic.
