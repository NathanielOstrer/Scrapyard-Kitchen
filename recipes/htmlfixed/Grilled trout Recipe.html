Fennel, Sage, Trout

Bryan Miller Eight servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 trout (see note)
  • 1 bunch fresh sage, with stems
  • 1 bunch fresh fennel, with stems
  • ¼ cup olive oil, or 1/4 pound butter, melted

Preparation

  1. Tie the herbs to the body of the trout and secure with string. Brush with oil or butter.
  2. Grill for five to seven minutes on each side. Be careful when turning the fish as it breaks easily. Fish is done when firm and white.

Tip

  • To serve 15 people, you will need 15 trout, and seasonings should be adjusted accordingly.
