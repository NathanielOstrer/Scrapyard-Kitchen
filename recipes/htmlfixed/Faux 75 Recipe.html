Superfine Sugar

Melissa Clark 4 cocktails Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup superfine sugar
  • 4 ounces fresh lemon juice
  • 4 bottles cold bitter lemon soda, such as Fever-Tree
  • 4 lemon twists, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake sugar, lemon juice and 2 tablespoons hot tap water in a cocktail shaker until sugar dissolves. Strain into Champagne flutes. Top with the bitter lemon. Garnish with twists.
