Vegetarian, Mushroom, Orange Juice, Spinach

Marian Burros 2 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces fresh spinach, washed, drained, stems removed
  • 4 medium mushrooms
  • 4 water chestnuts, diced
  • 1 orange, peeled and diced
  • 1 tablespoon salad oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons orange juice
  • ¾ teaspooon soy sauce Scant
  • ⅛ teaspoon dry mustard

Preparation

  1. Tear spinach coarsely and place in large bowl.
  2. Wash mushrooms and trim stem ends. Slice mushrooms. Add them with the water chestnuts and the orange to the spinach.
  3. Whisk remaining ingredients together and toss with salad just before serving.
