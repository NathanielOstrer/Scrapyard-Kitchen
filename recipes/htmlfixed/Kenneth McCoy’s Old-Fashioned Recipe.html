Bitters, Orange, Whiskey

Rosie Schaap

Ralph Smith for The New York Times; Food stylist: Michelle Gatton. Prop stylist: Randi Brookman Harris. Glass from Baccarat.

Ingredients

  • Dash of Regans’ orange bitters
  • Dash of Angostura bitters
  • Dash of simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of brandied cherry juice
  • 2 ounces of your favorite whiskey
  • Orange peel

Preparation

  1. Stir ingredients with ice in a mixing glass. Strain into an old-fashioned glass over a large chunk of ice. Garnish with a thick twist of orange peel.
