Fiddlehead Fern, Lemon

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon oil
  • 3 cups trimmed and cleaned fiddleheads
  • ¾ teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine the water and the lemon oil in a pot with a steamer insert. Bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer. Place the fiddleheads in the steamer basket, cover and steam until tender, about 6 minutes. Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper. Divide among 4 plates and serve immediately.
