Grapefruit, Pomelo, Sherry, Sotol Blanco, Southwestern

Rosie Schaap

Ingredients

Oleo-saccharum from:

  • Peel of 1 whole pomelo
  • Peel of half a grapefruit
  • 2 oz. palm sugar
  • 2 oz grated panela (also called jaggery)

For the punch:

  • 8 oz. sotol blanco
  • 8 oz. Palo Cortado sherry
  • 4 oz. reposado tequila
  • 4 oz. Mandarine Napoleon liqueur
  • 12 oz. sparkling white wine, chilled
  • The chilled juice of 3 pomelos and 1 lemon
  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 1 cup ice
  • Whole nutmeg

Preparation

  1. In a punch bowl, muddle the peels and sugars together to draw the oils from the peels, and let sit for one hour. Then add sotol, sherry, tequila, liqueur, white wine, juice of pomelos, juice of lemon, water and ice.
  2. Before serving, remove peels (if you wish) and grate a little nutmeg over the punch.
