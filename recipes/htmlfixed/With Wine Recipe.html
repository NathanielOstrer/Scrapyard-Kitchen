Mark Bittman Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • ¼ pound diced pancetta
  • olive oil
  • chopped garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup stock
  • pancetta for garnish
  • parsley for garnish

Preparation

  1. Sauté 1/4 pound diced pancetta in a little olive oil until crisp; remove with a slotted spoon.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon chopped garlic and the onion; cook for a moment, then add potatoes.
  3. Use 1 cup dry white wine and keep 1 cup stock for liquid and cook until done.
  4. Garnish: Pancetta and parsley.
