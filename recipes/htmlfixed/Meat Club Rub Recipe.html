Chipotle, Coffee, Rib Eye Steak

Amanda Hesser Serves 4 Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon ground coffee
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground chipotle
  • 4 rib-eye steaks

Preparation

  1. Mix together the coffee, coriander, salt, pepper and chipotle. Rub this mixture on all sides of the steaks about 30 minutes before you want to broil or grill.
