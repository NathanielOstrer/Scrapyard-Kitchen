Vegetarian, Potato, Vegetable

Jacques Pepin Six to eight servings TimeAbout 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, peeled
  • 1 rib celery, cleaned
  • 4 scallions, cleaned
  • 1 tablespoon corn oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 carrots, peeled
  • 1 zucchini, trimmed and rinsed
  • 1 white turnip, peeled
  • 1 piece white cabbage (about 4 ounces)
  • 2 or 3 potatoes, peeled
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 7 cups water
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • About 1/2 cup fresh parsley or basil leaves, or a mixture of both
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Fit a food processor with the 1 millimeter slicing blade and process the onion, celery and scallions.
  2. Heat the oil and butter in a large stockpot and add the sliced vegetables. Saute about 3 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, slice the carrots, zucchini, turnip, cabbage and potatoes in the food processor. Add these vegetables to the stockpot along with the salt and the water, and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce the heat and boil gently for 20 minutes.
  4. Mince the garlic and herbs together in a food processor fitted with a steel chopping blade. Add the herb mixture to the stockpot, mix well, season with pepper and serve.
