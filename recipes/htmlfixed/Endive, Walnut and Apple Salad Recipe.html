Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Cholesterol, Apple, Endive, Walnut

Florence Fabricant 8 servings

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 6 heads endive, separated into leaves, rinsed and dried, cores removed
  • 1 small bunch watercress, rinsed, dried and chopped coarse
  • 1 small red onion, sliced very thin
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, cored and sliced vertically very thin
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl beat the olive oil and vinegar together.
  2. In a large bowl combine the remaining ingredients. Beat the dressing ingredients again, pour over the salad ingredients in the bowl, toss well and serve.
