Fig, Lemon, Sugar

Julia Reed 2 pints Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups figs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ lemon, thinly sliced and seeded

Preparation

  1. Trim stem ends and rinse figs. Put into a heavy-bottomed saucepan with sugar, lemon slices and 1 cup water. Simmer over low heat, stirring occasionally, until a thick syrup forms.
  2. Transfer hot figs to 2 sterilized 1-pint Mason-type jars, top with sterilized lids and seal in a hot-water bath according to the jar manufacturer's directions. Cool and store in a dark place.
