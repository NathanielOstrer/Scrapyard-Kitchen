Ginger

Mark Bittman 1 1/4 cups Time10 minutes

Suzy Allman for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 2-inch piece of ginger, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sherry vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • Salt
  • freshly ground pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender, using plenty of black pepper. Add 1 tablespoon warm water and blend until dressing is emulsified. Best if left to rest for a day before using, to enhance flavors.
