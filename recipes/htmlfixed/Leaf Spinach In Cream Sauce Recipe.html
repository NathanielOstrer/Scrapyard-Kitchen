Vegetarian, Heavy Cream, Spinach

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ pounds fresh spinach
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ⅓ cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped shallots

Preparation

  1. Pick over the spinach and discard any tough stems and blemished leaves. Rinse the spinach well and pat or spin dry.
  2. Heat the butter in a deep heavy skillet or casserole and add the spinach. Turn it over until it wilts. Add the cream, salt, pepper and nutmeg and toss. Cook about 1 minute and add the shallots. Cook about 30 seconds and serve.
