Anchovy, Egg, Ham

Amanda Hesser Serves 4 TimeAbout 15 minutes

Dwight Eschliman for The New York Times. Stylist: Kevin Crafts.

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 eggs, cracked into a bowl
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced anchovy
  • 3 tablespoons finely minced Virginia ham, or other smoked ham

Preparation

  1. Rub the inside of a large skillet with the garlic clove. Place over medium-high heat and add the butter. When it's nice and foamy, pour in the eggs. Sprinkle the anchovy and ham over the eggs, then begin scrambling them, stopping when they're done to your liking.
