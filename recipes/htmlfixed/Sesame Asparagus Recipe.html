Low Calorie, Low Fat, Asparagus, Sesame Oil, Sesame Seed, Soy Sauce

Marian Burros 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds asparagus, peeled if desired
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Asian sesame oil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 ½ teaspoons reduced-sodium soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Steam asparagus until tender, about 7 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. To serve, heat oil until it is warm, and add sesame seeds and asparagus, stirring to coat, and warm asparagus slightly. Stir in soy sauce, and serve.
