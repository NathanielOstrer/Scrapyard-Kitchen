Peach Liqueur, Pineapple, Rum

Pete Wells 1 drink

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 10 leaves fresh mint
  • ½ ring of peeled pineapple cut 1/2-inch thick, core removed
  • 2 tablespoons (1 ounce) lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon (1/2 ounce) peach liqueur, preferably Leopold Brothers or Mathilde
  • 1 tablespoon honey syrup (see note)
  • 2 tablespoons (1 ounce) clear rum, more if necessary
  • Pineapple juice, if necessary

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender with about 1 1/2 cups ice. Purée until smooth. If too thick, add a little pineapple juice or extra rum, to taste. It should look like a slushy mint pesto. Pour into a chilled cocktail glass or goblet, and serve with a straw snipped to rise 2 to 3 inches above rim of glass.

Tip

  • To make honey syrup, combine equal measures of honey and water, and stir well.
