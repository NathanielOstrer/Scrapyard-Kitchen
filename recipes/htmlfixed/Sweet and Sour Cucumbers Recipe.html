Low Calorie, Low Fat, Cider Vinegar, Kirby Cucumber, Sugar

Marian Burros 2 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
  • 6 ounces Kirby cucumbers

Preparation

  1. Mix vinegar and sugar in a saucepan; heat just to melt sugar.
  2. Wash, trim and slice cucumbers 1/8 inch thick, and stir into vinegar mixture.
