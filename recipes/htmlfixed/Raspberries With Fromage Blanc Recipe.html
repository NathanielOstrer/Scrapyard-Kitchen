Raw, Vegetarian, Fromage Blanc, Raspberry, Sugar, French

Florence Fabricant 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pint fresh raspberries
  • ⅓ cup sugar, or to taste
  • 1 ⅓ cups fromage blanc or soft, mild goat cheese

Preparation

  1. Force half the raspberries through a sieve and sweeten with the sugar, using more or less depending on the sweetness of the berries. Gently fold in the whole raspberries.
  2. Put a scoop or neat mound of the cheese on each of four plates and spoon the raspberry mixture around it.
