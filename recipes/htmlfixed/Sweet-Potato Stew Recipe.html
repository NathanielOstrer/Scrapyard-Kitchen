Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Kosher, Coconut Milk, Curry, Sweet Potato

Mark Bittman Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes
  • Apples
  • Onions
  • Coconut milk
  • Chilis (dried or fresh)
  • Ginger
  • Vegetable stock or water
  • Peanuts
  • Cilantro

Preparation

  1. Saute 1 pound cubed sweet potatoes, a peeled and cubed apple, chopped onion, minced chili (dried or fresh) and minced ginger, along with a sprinkle of curry powder in a large saucepan until the onion is soft, 5 or 6 minutes.
  2. Add coconut milk thinned with vegetable stock or water to about halfway up the sides of the mixture; cook until soft, then mash about half of the mixture.
  3. Garnish with chopped peanuts and cilantro and serve.
