Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Calorie, Low Fat, Low Sugar, Grape, Lemon Juice, Raisin, Sugar

Jacques Pepin 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 3 cups white seedless grapes
  • 1 cup dark raisins

Preparation

  1. Mix the lemon juice and sugar in a bowl large enough to hold the fruit.
  2. Wash the grapes and separate them from their stems. Add the grapes and the raisins to the mixture in the bowl, and mix well. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for up to 4 hours before serving in wine glasses.
