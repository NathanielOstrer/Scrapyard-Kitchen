Flour, Maple Sugar, Walnut

Molly O'Neill 2 cups, enough for topping 2 pies Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • ¼ cup dark brown sugar
  • ¼ cup maple sugar
  • ¼ cup chopped walnuts

Preparation

  1. Cut all the ingredients together with a fork or pastry cutter until evenly crumbly, and spread over the top of a filled pie.
