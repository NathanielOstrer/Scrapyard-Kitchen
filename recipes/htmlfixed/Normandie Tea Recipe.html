Assam Tea, Brown Sugar, Calvado, Cinnamon Stick, French

William Grimes 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Calvados
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 4 ounces strong hot assam tea
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Preparation

  1. In a pan, warm the Calvados.
  2. In a tall pedestal mug, mix the Calvados with the sugar. Ignite with a match and let burn for 10 seconds. Add tea and garnish with cinnamon stick.
