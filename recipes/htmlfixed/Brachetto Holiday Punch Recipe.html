Aperol, Cognac

Jonathan Miles 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Cognac
  • 1 ounce Aperol
  • ½ ounce fresh lemon juice
  • ½ ounce fresh orange juice
  • 3 to 4 ounces Brachetto d'Acqui wine
  • Orange twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine the first four ingredients with ice in a cocktail shaker; shake and strain into a champagne flute or martini glass, and fill the rest of the glass with the Brachetto d'Acqui. Garnish with the orange twist.
