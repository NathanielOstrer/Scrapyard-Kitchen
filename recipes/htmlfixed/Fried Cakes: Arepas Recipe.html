Preparation

  1. Substitute butter for oil.
  2. Use 1 cup cornmeal; skip the flour and baking powder.
  3. Beat the egg with 1 cup milk and add 1 cup grated Cheddar; use just 1/2 cup corn kernels. (Batter will be thinner.)
  4. Cook like pancakes, until golden and fluffy.
  5. Garnish: Cheddar and avocado.
