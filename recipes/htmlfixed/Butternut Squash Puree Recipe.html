Gluten Free, Butternut Squash

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash, about 2 pounds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground allspice
  • Pinch cinnamon
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Pare the squash, remove the seeds and cut into 1 1/2-inch cubes. Put in a saucepan, cover with water and add salt. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until tender.
  2. Drain the squash and put it through a food mill or processor. Return the puree to the saucepan and add butter, honey, nutmeg, allspice, cinnamon, salt and pepper. Blend well until smooth. Serve hot.
