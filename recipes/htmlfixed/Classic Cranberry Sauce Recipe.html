Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, Cranberry, American

Julia Moskin 2 cups Time15 minutes, plus chilling

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole cranberries
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • ¼ cup orange juice or Grand Marnier (optional)
  • ⅔ cup coarsely chopped toasted pecans (optional)

Preparation

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine cranberries, 2 cups water, the sugar and salt. Let the sauce simmer until it thickens; the water will evaporate and the pectin in the cranberries will be activated as it cooks. Let it simmer for at least 10 minutes, and up to 25 minutes. Remove from heat.
  2. Stir in juice or liqueur, if using, then cover sauce well and chill. Sauce can be made up to this point 1 week in advance and kept refrigerated. A few hours before serving, stir in the pecans, if using.
