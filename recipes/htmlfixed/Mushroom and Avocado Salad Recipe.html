Vegetarian, Vegan, Avocado, Mushroom

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped shallots
  • ¼ pound fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 2 firm, medium-size ripe avocados
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine mustard, vinegar, oil, shallots and salt and pepper, and blend thoroughly with a wire whisk in a salad bowl. Add the mushrooms and blend.
  2. Peel the avocados, and cut them in halves. Remove and discard the pits. Cut avocados into 1-inch cubes.
  3. Add the avocados and parsley to the salad bowl. Toss gently and blend.
