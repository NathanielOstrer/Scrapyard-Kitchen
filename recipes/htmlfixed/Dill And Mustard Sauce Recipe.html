Raw, Dill, Mustard, Vinegar

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About one and one-quarter cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Gulden's spicy brown mustard
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard, preferably Dijon
  • 3 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ cup corn, peanut or vegetable oil
  • ½ cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon Cognac

Preparation

  1. Put the two mustards, vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  2. Start beating with a wire whisk while adding the oil in a thin stream. When all the oil is added, stir in the dill and Cognac.
