Noodle, Pecorino, Zucchini

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 small zucchini, about 1/3 pound
  • ½ pound fine noodles
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon chopped garlic
  • ¼ cup grated pecorino or Parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. Trim the zucchini but do not peel them. Cut them into 1/4-inch cubes. Put the noodles in a saucepan with salted water to cover and cook to the desired degree of doneness.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet and add the zucchini, salt and pepper. Cook over medium-high heat for about 2 minutes. Add the garlic and cook 30 seconds; do not brown. Set aside.
  3. When the noodles are ready, drain quickly but thoroughly. Spoon the zucchini and cheese over them and toss quickly. Serve immediately.
