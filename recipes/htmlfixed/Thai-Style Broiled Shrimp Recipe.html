Fish Sauce, Lime Juice, Shrimp, Thai

Mark Bittman TimeAbout 10 minutes

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Susan Ottoviano.

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • minced Thai chiles
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • black pepper
  • 1 Â½ pounds peeled shrimp
  • Cilantro and mint

Preparation

  1. Turn on the broiler, and put the rack close to heat. Mash 1 garlic clove and a few minced Thai chiles with 1 teaspoon salt until it forms a paste. Add to it 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 tablespoon fish sauce, 2 tablespoons lime juice and a little black pepper. Rub paste all over1 1/2 pounds peeled shrimp. Broil, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Garnish: Cilantro and mint.
