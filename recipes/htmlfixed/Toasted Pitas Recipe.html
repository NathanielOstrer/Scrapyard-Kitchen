Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 to 4 small whole-wheat pitas
  • Butter (optional)

Preparation

  1. Cut pitas into 2 rounds. Butter lightly, if desired.
  2. Toast for 5 to 7 minutes until pitas are crisp.
