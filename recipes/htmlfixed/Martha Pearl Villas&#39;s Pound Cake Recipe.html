Nut Free, Butter, Egg, Flour

Julia Reed 12 servings Time1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (4 sticks) butter
  • 1 pound (2 cups) sugar
  • 9 large eggs
  • 1 pound (4 cups) all-purpose flour
  • Dash salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a large bowl, cream the butter with an electric mixer, then gradually add the sugar, continuing to beat until well creamed and smooth. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually add the flour and salt, beating constantly. Add the vanilla and lemon juice and continue beating until well blended.
  2. Grease and flour a 10-inch tube or bundt pan. Pour in the batter and ''spank'' the bottom of the pan to distribute the batter evenly. Bake until a straw inserted into the cake comes out clean, about 1 hour 15 minutes, taking care not to overcook. Turn cake out onto a rack and let cool.
