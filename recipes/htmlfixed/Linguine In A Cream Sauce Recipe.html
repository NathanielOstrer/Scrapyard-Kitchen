Linguine

Pierre Franey 4 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 cups water
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • ½ pound fresh or dried linguine
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 tablespoons cream sauce prepared in the accompanying hali but fillets with leeks

Preparation

  1. The cooking time will vary depending on whether you use fresh or dried linguine. Bring the water to a boil and add salt. Add the linguine and cook until tender. Fresh linguine should require about 1 to 1 1/2 minutes. Dried linguine will require about 7 to 9 minutes. Drain.
  2. Add the butter and cream sauce to the linguine and toss.
