Heavy Cream, Horseradish, French

Pierre Franey About 2/3 cup Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • ½ cup fresh or canned chicken broth (see recipe)
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup horseradish, preferably freshly grated (see note)
  • ¼ teaspoon Tabasco sauce

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan and add the flour, stirring with a wire whisk. When blended and smooth add the broth, stirring rapidly with the whisk.
  2. When thickened and smooth add the cream and bring to a boil, stirring. Add salt and pepper. Stir in the horseradish and Tabasco sauce.

Tip

  • If bottled horseradish is used, squeeze it in cheesecloth to extract most of the liquid.
