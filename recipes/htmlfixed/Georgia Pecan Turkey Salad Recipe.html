Pecan, Turkey

Florence Fabricant 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups very finely minced cooked turkey
  • ½ cup chopped pecans
  • ¼ cup sweet pickle relish
  • ½ teaspoon celery salt
  • ⅔ cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Mix turkey, pecans, relish and celery salt together. Fold in the mayonnaise. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and serve.
