Vegetarian, Apple, Apple Cider, Rosemary

Alice Hart 10 servings TimeAbout 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 6 large cooking apples like Cortland or Rome, peeled, cored and roughly chopped
  • 3 tart eating apples like Granny Smith or Macoun, peeled, cored and roughly chopped
  • ⅔ cup apple cider
  • 1 ½ tablespoons finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar, more if needed

Preparation

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees. In a 9-by-13-inch baking dish, combine the apples, cider and rosemary. Roast uncovered until the apples are soft, 30 to 40 minutes, stirring once or twice. Remove from the oven.
  2. Add the sugar and mash the mixture roughly with a fork. Add more sugar if desired. Cool.
