Raw, Basil, Gorgonzola, Tomato

Robert Farrar Capon 4 to 6 servings as an appetizer or side dish Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes, sliced 1/2 inch thick
  • Extra-virgin olive oil to taste (about 1/3 cup)
  • Chopped fresh basil leaves to taste (about 1/4 cup)
  • Black pepper to taste
  • ½ pound imported Gorgonzola cheese
  • 2 tablespoons toasted pine nuts (pignoli)

Preparation

  1. Arrange the tomato slices on 4 to 6 plates, drizzle some olive oil over them and sprinkle them with basil and black pepper.
  2. Crumble the Gorgonzola and distribute it over the tops of all the slices. Do the same with the pine nuts. Sprinkle on the remaining olive oil and serve.
