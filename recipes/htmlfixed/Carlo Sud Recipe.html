Benedictine, Rum

Pete Wells 1 drink

Ethan Kaplan for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ½ ounce (1 tablespoon) Benedictine
  • 2 ounces (1/4 cup) amber rum
  • 1 or 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 strip lemon peel, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients except lemon peel in a shaker half-filled with ice and stir until well chilled. Strain into a cocktail glass. Run lemon twist around rim and drop into drink.
