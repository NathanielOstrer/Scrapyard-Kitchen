Raw, Low Carb, Blue Cheese, Mixed Green, Red Wine Vinegar

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon finely minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon-style mustard
  • ½ cup crumbled blue cheese like Roquefort, Saga or Danish blue
  • 6 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 10 cups loosely packed bite-size pieces watercress, red-leaf lettuce and Belgian endive

Preparation

  1. Combine the vinegar, garlic, mustard and cheese in a mixing bowl. Blend well. Add the oil, salt and pepper in a small mixing bowl and blend well with a wire whisk.
  2. Place the salad greens in a bowl. Pour the dressing over the greens and toss well.
