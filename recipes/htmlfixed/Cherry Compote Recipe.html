Cherry, Honey, Vodka

Pierre Franey 8 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup vodka
  • ½ cup honey
  • 1 orange cut into 10 slices
  • 2 sticks cinnamon (each about 2 inches long)
  • 2 pounds pitted cherries

Preparation

  1. Combine vodka, honey, orange and cinnamon in saucepan over medium-high heat, and bring to boil. Add cherries, cover, reduce to simmer and cook for 2 minutes. Transfer mixture to storage container, and allow to cool. Serve over poundcake.
