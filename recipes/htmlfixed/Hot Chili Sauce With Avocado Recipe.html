Vegetarian, Vegan, Avocado, Jalapeno, Mexican

Craig Claiborne About one and one-quarter cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup finely chopped fresh or bottled jalapeno pepper
  • ¼ cup seeded, finely diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup finely diced red onions
  • ¼ cup avocado, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Preparation

  1. Combine all of the ingredients in a mixing bowl and serve at room temperature.
