Maple Syrup, Sirloin, Soy Sauce, Chinese

Marian Burros 2 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ teaspoons canola oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 ½ tablespoons red-wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic in oil
  • 12 ounces boneless sirloin steak, 3/4 inch thick

Preparation

  1. Combine oil, soy sauce, maple syrup, vinegar, ginger and garlic.
  2. Trim the external fat from the beef and cut into slices 3/4 inch thick. Marinate beef in mixture until it is time to cook it.
  3. Remove steaks from marinade and reserve the marinade. Prepare stove-top grill or preheat broiler.
  4. Grill or broil beef about 5 minutes, turning once or twice and brushing with marinade each time.
