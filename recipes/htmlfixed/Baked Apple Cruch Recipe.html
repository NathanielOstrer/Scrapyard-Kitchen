Vegetarian, Nut Free, Tart Apple

Molly O'Neill 6 servings

Ingredients

For the filling:

  • 2 pounds tart apples, peeled, cored and sliced 1/4-inch thick
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves

For the crunch:

  • ½ cup flour
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Confectioners' sugar for garnish

Preparation

  1. To make the filling, combine the apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves in a large bowl. Stir to coat the apples. Spoon the mixture into an 8- to 9-inch pie plate and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  3. To make the crunch, combine flour, sugar, butter and vanilla in a medium-size bowl. Work mixture between fingers and drop crumbs on top of apple filling.
  4. Bake for one hour, or until topping is a light gold. Allow to cool slightly, sprinkle with confectioners' sugar and serve.
