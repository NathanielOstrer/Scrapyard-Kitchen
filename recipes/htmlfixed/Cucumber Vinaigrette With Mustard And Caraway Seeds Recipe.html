Raw, Caraway Seed, Cucumber Juice, Dijon Mustard

Molly O'Neill 1 1/3 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cucumber juice (see recipe)
  • 3 tablespoons plain lowfat yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds, crushed
  • 4 teaspoons finely chopped scallions
  • ½ teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Whisk the cucumber juice, yogurt and mustard together in a medium bowl. Whisk in the caraway seeds, scallions, salt and pepper. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
