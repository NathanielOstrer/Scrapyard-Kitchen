Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar

Preparation

  1. Wash cucumbers and pat dry. Pound them lightly all over with a kitchen mallet.
  2. Trim off the ends. Peel and cut cucumbers lengthwise into quarters.
  3. Cut or scrape away seeds.
  4. Cut each cucumber strip into 1 1/2-inch lengths. Trim corners of each piece to make them neater. Put pieces in a bowl and add remaining ingredients. Chill and serve.
