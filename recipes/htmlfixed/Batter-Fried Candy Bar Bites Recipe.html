Mini Candy Bar

Melissa Clark 4 to 6 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen mini candy bars
  • 1 ¼ cup flour, more for dredging
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup seltzer
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Freeze the candy bars for 30 minutes.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder and salt in a large bowl. Whisk in seltzer. Heat 4 inches of oil to 375 degrees in a deep fryer or saucepan.
  3. Dredge candy bars in flour, then coat evenly with batter. Working in batches, fry until golden and puffed all over, turning if needed, about 2 to 3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.

Tip

  • Variations: Use for fish fillets, peeled and sliced sweet potatoes or asparagus.
