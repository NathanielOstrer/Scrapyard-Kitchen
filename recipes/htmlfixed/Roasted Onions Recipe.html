Vegetarian, Butter, Vidalia Onion

Jonathan Reynolds 6 servings Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • 6 large Vidalia or Spanish onions
  • ¾ cup melted butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 teaspoons thyme

Preparation

  1. Peel and core onions.
  2. Baste the outside with butter. Fill cores with seasonings and butter.
  3. Brush tops with melted butter. Wrap one-third the way up in foil to hold them together, and smoke for 45 minutes to an hour.
