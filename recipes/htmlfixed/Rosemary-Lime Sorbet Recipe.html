Low Fat, Nut Free, Half, Lime Juice, Rosemary

Amanda Hesser about 1 quart

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 3 cups half-and-half
  • 2 tablespoons chopped rosemary
  • ¼ cup light corn syrup
  • Juice of 3 limes

Preparation

  1. In a large saucepan, combine sugar, half-and-half, rosemary and corn syrup. Whisk lightly and bring to a boil, making sure mixture does not boil over, then strain into a bowl. Let cool, then chill in refrigerator. Remove from refrigerator and slowly whisk in lime juice. If mixture shows signs of curdling, whisk harder or transfer to a blender.
  2. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and follow manufacturer's instructions.
