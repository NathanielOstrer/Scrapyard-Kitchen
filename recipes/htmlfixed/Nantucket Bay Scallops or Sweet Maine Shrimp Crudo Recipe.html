Bay Scallop, Lemon Juice, Shrimp, New England

Jonathan Reynolds 4 servings Time10 minutes, plus chill time

Ingredients

  • ½ pound Nantucket bay scallops or sweet Maine shrimp
  • Juice of 1/2 lime (for scallops) or lemon (for shrimp)
  • Pinch coarse sea salt
  • 2 turns fresh black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons extra-virgin cold-pressed olive oil
  • 4 leaves fresh chervil

Preparation

  1. Mix all the ingredients except the chervil in a bowl, then divide into four small bowls.
  2. Top with the chervil, chill, then serve.
