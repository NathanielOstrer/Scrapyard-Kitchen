Jalapeno, Smoked Turkey

John T. Edge 6 to 8 appetizer servings Time5 minutes

Allison V. Smith for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 pound smoked turkey
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • Salt, optional
  • Pepper, optional
  • 1 jalapeño, halved lengthwise, seeded and thinly sliced
  • Corn chips, like Fritos, or sturdy potato chips, for serving

Preparation

  1. Finely grind the turkey in a food processor. Transfer to a serving bowl, stir in mayonnaise and season to taste with salt and pepper, if using. Top with jalapeño slices and serve as a dip with chips.
