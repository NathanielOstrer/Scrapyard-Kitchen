Gin, Seltzer

Ingredients

  • ½ tablespoon superfine sugar
  • ½ ounce lemon juice
  • 2 ounces gin, preferably Plymouth
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 ounces seltzer

Preparation

  1. Dump two or three handfuls of cracked or shaved ice into a cocktail shaker and add everything but the seltzer. Shake energetically for a minute or more, then strain into a 6- to 8-ounce highball glass. Add seltzer and stir gently.
