Shrimp, Caribbean

Nancy Harmon Jenkins 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 pounds medium-size shrimp, cooked, peeled and deveined
  • 1 avocado, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes

Preparation

  1. Combine the milk, sugar, mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and pepper in a bowl. Set aside 6 shrimp to garnish the avocado mousse. Gently fold the remaining shrimp and avocado cubes into the mayonnaise mixture. Chill until ready to serve.
