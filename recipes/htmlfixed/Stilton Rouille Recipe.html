Egg Yolk, Olive Oil, Stilton, British

Molly O'Neill Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces (1/3 cup) crumbled Stilton cheese
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Place the Stilton and yolks in a food processor and pulse until combined. With the motor running, slowly add the oil and season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with Cauliflower Fritters and Cauliflower-and-White-Bean Brandade (see recipes).
