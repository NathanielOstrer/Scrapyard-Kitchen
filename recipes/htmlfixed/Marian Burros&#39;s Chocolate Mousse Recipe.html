Egg Yolk, Heavy Cream, Sabra Liqueur, Semisweet Chocolate, Superfine Sugar, French

Marian Burros 16 or more servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 egg yolks
  • 3 ounces superfine sugar
  • 3 or 4 ounces Sabra liqueur
  • 1 pound semisweet chocolate
  • 1 quart heavy cream

Preparation

  1. Beat the yolks and sugar together until light. Mix in liqueur.
  2. Meanwhile melt chocolate over hot water and whip the cream. Mix a little of cream into yolk mixture.
  3. Then mix in the chocolate until it is smooth. Fold in whipped cream and spoon into individual serving dishes. Chill.
