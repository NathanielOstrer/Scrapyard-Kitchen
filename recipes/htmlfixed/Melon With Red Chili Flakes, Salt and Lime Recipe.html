Vegetarian, Melon, Red Pepper Flake

Jeff Schwarz And Greg Kessler 4 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Greg Kessler

Ingredients

  • 1 muskmelon (honeydew or cantaloupe), rind and seeds removed
  • 8 six-inch skewers
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 jalapeño, finely diced (seeds removed)
  • ½ lime

Preparation

  1. Cut the melon into 1-inch cubes.Skewer the melon pieces (about four pieces for each skewer), and place them on a serving plate.
  2. Place the salt and red chili flakes in a coffee or spice grinder and pulse until finely ground. Sprinkle about a teaspoon of the salt/chili mixture over the melons, followed by the diced jalapeño.
  3. Squeeze the lime over the melons, and serve.
