Garlic, Parsley, Tomato

Molly O'Neill Two cups Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 15 plum tomatoes, cored and halved lengthwise
  • 1 ½ teaspoons olive oil
  • ⅛ teaspoon orange oil
  • 5 cloves roasted garlic, peeled
  • ½ teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • Pinch of cayenne
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 4 teaspoons chopped Italian parsley

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Lightly oil a baking sheet and place the tomatoes on it cut side down. Roast until soft and lightly browned, about 20 minutes.
  2. Place the tomatoes in a food processor with the olive oil, orange oil, garlic, lemon juice, cayenne, salt and pepper. Place in a bowl and stir in the parsley. Serve with cut vegetables, bread or crackers.
