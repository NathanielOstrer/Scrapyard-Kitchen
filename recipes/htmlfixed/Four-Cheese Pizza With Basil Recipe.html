Vegetarian, Fontina, Mozzarella, Pizza Dough, Italian

Mark Bittman 1 large, 2 medium, or more smaller pizzas Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (see recipe)
  • ½ cup shredded or cubed mozzarella
  • ½ cup shredded or cubed fontina or taleggio
  • ½ cup freshly grated pecorino Romano
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped or torn basil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Top dough with all cheeses. Bake about 10 minutes, or until nearly done. Sprinkle with basil and finish baking.
