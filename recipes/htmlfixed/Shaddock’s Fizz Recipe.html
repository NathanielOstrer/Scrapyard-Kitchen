Aperol, Champagne, Grapefruit Juice, Italian

Jennifer Steinhauer 1 drink.

Lauren DeCicca for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Aperol
  • ¾ ounce grapefruit juice
  • ½ ounce St.-Germain liqueur
  • 2 ounces Champagne, or to taste
  • Grapefruit twist

Preparation

  1. Pour Aperol, grapefruit juice and St.-Germain into a cocktail shaker. Shake well.
  2. Fill a highball glass with ice, and pour in contents of shaker. Top with Champagne and add grapefruit twist.
