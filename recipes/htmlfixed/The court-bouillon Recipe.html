Low Fat, Carrot, Celery, Leek, French

Craig Claiborne About 16 cups Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 carrots, about 1/3 pound
  • 1 leek, about 1/4 pound
  • 3 ribs celery, about 1/4 pound, trimmed
  • 1 small onion, about 1/4 pound, peeled and chopped
  • 12 tail ends of parsley
  • Â½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 10 anise seeds
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • 1 sliver lemon peel
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • 12 peppercorns, lightly crushed
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 12 cups water

Preparation

  1. Peel carrots and cut into chunks. Put in saucepan.
  2. Trim off ends of leek and cut it into 1-inch lengths. Cut celery into 1-inch lengths. Add leeks and celery and onion to pot.
  3. Tie parsley tails, thyme and bay leaf into a bundle and add this. Add anise seeds, garlic, lemon peel, salt, peppercorns, wine and water. Bring to boil. Let simmer 20 minutes.
