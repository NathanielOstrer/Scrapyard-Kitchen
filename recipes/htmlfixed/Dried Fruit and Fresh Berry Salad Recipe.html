Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Berry, Grape, Peach

Molly O'Neill Six cups TimeAbout 3 hours

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • 8 cups seedless grapes (about 3 pounds), stemmed and halved
  • 10 firm, ripe peaches, pitted and cut into 1-inch-thick slices
  • Fresh fruit like strawberries, blueberries and huckleberries

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 275 degrees. Oil 4 baking sheets well and divide the grapes between 2 sheets and the peaches between 2 sheets. Bake until the fruit has reduced by about two-thirds (it should still be somewhat soft and juicy), about 2 to 3 hours; the grapes may be done sooner than the peaches.
  2. Let cool and combine the grapes and peaches. Toss with fresh fruit like strawberries, blueberries and huckleberries before serving.
