Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Banana, Heavy Cream, Yogurt

Christopher Idone About one quart Time20 minutes, plus freeze time

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, mash the bananas until smooth or puree them in a blender or food processor.
  2. Fold in the lemon juice, sugar and yogurt.
  3. Cover tightly and refrigerate.
  4. In a chilled bowl, beat the cream until it falls in soft peaks.
  5. Churn the banana mixture in the ice-cream maker for about three minutes.
  6. Add the whipped cream and freeze according to the manufacturer's directions, then transfer to air-tight containers for storage
