Bourbon, Creme De Banane, Southern Comfort, White Creme De Cacao

William Grimes 1 serving

Ingredients

  • ½ ounce bourbon
  • ½ ounce Southern Comfort
  • ¼ ounce white creme de cacao
  • ¼ ounce creme de banane

Preparation

  1. Mix bourbon, Southern Comfort, creme de cacao and creme de banane. Serve on the rocks or straight up.
