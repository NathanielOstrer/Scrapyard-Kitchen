Vegetarian, Apple, Nut

Molly O'Neill Ten servings Time1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon grated orange zest
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup pecans
  • ½ cup walnuts
  • ½ cup dried sour cherries, plumped in hot water and drained
  • 10 Granny Smith apples, cored

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place butter, zests, ginger, salt, honey, sugar and lemon juice in a food processor and process until smooth. Add nuts and cherries and process until mixture forms into a paste. Fill the centers of the apples with 2 to 3 tablespoons of the nut mixture.
  2. Place the apples in a large, shallow baking dish. Bake for 35 minutes. Cover the apples with aluminum foil and bake until the apples are soft when pierced with a knife, about 20 minutes longer. Serve with vanilla ice cream, if desired.
