Cucumber, Raisin, Yogurt, Middle Eastern

Joan Nathan 4 1/2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 large seedless cucumber, peeled and diced
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 3 cups yogurt
  • ½ cup sour cream (optional)
  • ¼ cup chopped scallions
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 3 tablespoons chopped walnuts
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. In a large serving bowl, combine all ingredients. Mix thoroughly, and season to taste with salt and pepper.
  2. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
