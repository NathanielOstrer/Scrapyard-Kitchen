Apple Cider, Cranberry Juice, Ginger Ale, Southern

Eric V. Copage 16 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups boiling water
  • 4 bags cinnamon tea
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • 1 teaspoon allspice
  • 4 cups cranberry juice
  • 4 cups apple cider
  • 4 cups ginger ale
  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • ½ cup brown sugar, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Steep the tea bags in the boiling water for 5 minutes. Discard the bags and add the spices. Add the cranberry juice, cider, ginger ale, lemon juice and brown sugar.
