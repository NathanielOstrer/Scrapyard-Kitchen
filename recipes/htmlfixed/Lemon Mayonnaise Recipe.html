Vegetarian, Egg, Lemon Juice, Mustard

Amanda Hesser 1 cup

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 1 cup olive oil

Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg yolk, 2 teaspoons lemon juice, mustard and a little salt. Whisk in the olive oil, a few drops at a time at first, then pour it in a steady thin stream, while continuing to whisk. Stir in the rest of the lemon juice, and adjust the salt to taste.
