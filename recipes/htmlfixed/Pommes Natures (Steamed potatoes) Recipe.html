Potato

Pierre Franey 6 servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 to 12 small, new red-skinned potatoes, about 1 to 1 1/4 pounds
  • Salt to taste, if desired

Preparation

  1. Peel the potatoes and put them in a saucepan with water to cover. Add salt to taste and bring to a boil.
  2. Let simmer 20 minutes or until tender. Drain and serve.
