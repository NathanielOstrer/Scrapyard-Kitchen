Vegetarian, Potato, Tomato, Whole Grain Mustard

Marian Burros 2 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 9 ounces tiny new potatoes, scrubbed but not peeled
  • 6 ounces ripe tomatoes, yellow or red, any size or shape
  • 1 tablespoon corn, safflower or canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons grainy mustard
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar

Preparation

  1. Cook potatoes in water to cover in covered pot until they are tender, 10 to 20 minutes depending on size. Drain and cool to room temperature.
  2. Wash and dry tomatoes; cut into bite-size chunks.
  3. In serving bowl, whisk oil and vinegar and beat in mustard and brown sugar.
  4. Combine potatoes and tomatoes and stir gently into dressing.
