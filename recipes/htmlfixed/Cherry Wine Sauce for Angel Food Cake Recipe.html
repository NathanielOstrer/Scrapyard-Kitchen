Angel Food Cake, Cherry, Wine

Marian Burros 4 to 6 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup dried tart cherries
  • ¾ cup dessert wine, like late harvest riesling
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 ½ cups plain nonfat yogurt
  • 1 small angel food cake

Preparation

  1. Combine the cherries, wine and sugar, and cook over medium heat just until cherries are plump and soft. Puree with 3/4 cup of the yogurt until smooth. Refrigerate until serving time.
  2. Return to room temperature, and serve angel cake slices topped with the cherry sauce and remaining yogurt.
