Vegetarian, Nut Free, Beef Suet, Bread Crumb, Oyster

Marian Burros 4 servings Time35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups minced raw oysters
  • ¾ cup chopped beef suet
  • 2 cups fresh white bread crumbs
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon allspice
  • ⅛ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients but butter and oil and mix well. Using sausage stuffer, stuff into casing, making each sausage about 5 inches long. Twist and tie.
  2. Prick with fork and poach in salted water 10 to 15 minutes, until firm. To serve, brown quickly on both sides in butter and oil.
