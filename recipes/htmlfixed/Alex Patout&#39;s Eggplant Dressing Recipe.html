Butter, Crab Meat, Eggplant, Green Pepper, Shrimp, Spice, Cajun

Marian Burros 6 to 8 servings Time1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound raw shrimp
  • ¾ pound butter
  • 3 medium-large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 green peppers, finely chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 4 large eggplants
  • ¾ teaspoon cayenne
  • ¾ teaspoon white pepper
  • ¾ teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 dashes hot pepper sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ pound crab meat
  • ¼ cup each chopped green onion and parsley
  • 1 cup oil, approximately

Preparation

  1. Peel shrimp; set aside. Cover shells with 2 cups water; boil to reduce to 1 cup. Strain; set stock aside.
  2. Heat butter in large skillet; add onion, peppers and celery. Cook slowly, 15 minutes, until mixture is very soft.
  3. Peel and cut two eggplants into 3/4-inch cubes; place in saucepan with water to cover half way up eggplant. Cook until eggplant is tender, about 7 minutes; drain to remove as much water as possible; puree.
  4. Add eggplant, stock and seasonings to sauteed vegetables; cook over medium-low heat about 20 minutes.
  5. Add shrimp; cook 7 to 10 minutes over moderately high heat until shrimp are pink. Add crab meat; heat through.
  6. Stir in green onion and parsley.
  7. Peel and slice remaining eggplant into 12 1/2-inch-thick slices. Brown in hot oil. Repeat until all slices are done. Drain.
  8. Serve dressing over slices of fried eggplant.
