Grapefruit, Orange, Sugar, French

Marian Burros

Ingredients

  • 1 lime
  • 1 orange
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 small grapefruit
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 pint water
  • Zest from 5 limes

Preparation

  1. Cut lime, orange, lemon and grapefruit into thin slices. Then cut each slice of the lime, orange and lemon into 4 quarters. Cut the grapefruit slices into eighths.
  2. Make a syrup by boiling sugar, water and lime zest for 15 minutes. Strain. Cool, then steep citrus fruits overnight in the syrup.

Tip

  • This mixture will keep several weeks in the refrigerator.
