Gluten Free, Nut Free, Egg Yolk, Heavy Cream, Milk, White Peppercorn, French

Bryan Miller 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups heavy cream
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • ¼ cup plus one tablespoon sugar
  • 20 crushed white peppercorns
  • 6 egg yolks

Preparation

  1. In a heavy saucepan over medium heat, combine cream, milk, sugar and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  2. Beat egg yolks in a mixing bowl until pale -- 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Pour about 1/2 cup of the hot mixture into the egg yolks while whisking. Pour yolk mixture back into hot cream and stir over medium heat until it thickens slightly, about 3 minutes. Do not boil.
  4. Remove pot from heat; chill over ice, stirring if lumps appear.
  5. Strain mixture, and transfer to an ice cream machine. Follow manufacturer's instructions
