Gin, Lime Juice, Orange Curacao

Jim Meehan 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces gin
  • ¾ ounce orange Curaçao
  • ¾ ounce lime juice
  • Dash of Angostura bitters
  • Dash of orange bitters
  • Lime wheel, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all liquid ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lime wheel
