Broth, Carrot, Celery, Garlic, Onion

David Tanis 6 cups Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, peeled and halved
  • 3 or 4 scallions
  • 3 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 small celery stalk
  • 1 small carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 thyme branch

Preparation

  1. Put all ingredients in a large saucepan. Cover with 7 cups water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer gently for 30 minutes. Strain.
