Coconut Milk, Lime Juice, Sugar

Pete Wells 1 drink

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • 1 to 1 ½ tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar

Preparation

  1. In a blender, purée all ingredients with 3 ice cubes. Pour into any type of glassware and serve with a straw cut to a comfortable length.
