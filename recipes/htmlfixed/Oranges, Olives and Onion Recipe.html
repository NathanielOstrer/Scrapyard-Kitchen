Marian Burros 2 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon white wine vinegar
  • 2 large navel oranges
  • 4 medium or large black Italian, French or Greek olives
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion

Preparation

  1. In a serving bowl mix oil and vinegar.
  2. Peel oranges with knife to remove pith along with skin; slice. Add to bowl.
  3. Pit olives. Cut up and add to bowl.
  4. Stir onion into bowl, mixing all the ingredients well.
