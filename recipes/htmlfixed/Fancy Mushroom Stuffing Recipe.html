Preparation

  1. Using a sharp knife, mince the chanterelles and the shiitakes (a food processor will mince the mushrooms too fine). In step 1 of the recipe for basic bread stuffing, add the minced mushrooms to the skillet with the minced onion. Cook over low heat for 15 minutes, rather than 5 minutes. Add all remaining ingredients except bread cubes and broth and stir well. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl, add the bread cubes and broth and toss to combine well. If baking in a casserole, it will not be necessary to add more chicken broth.
