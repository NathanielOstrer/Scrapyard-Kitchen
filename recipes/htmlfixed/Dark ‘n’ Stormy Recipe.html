Ginger Beer, Rum

Jonathan Miles 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Gosling’s Black Seal rum
  • About 4 ounces Gosling’s Stormy Ginger Beer (or other ginger beer)
  • Lime slice, for garnish (optional)

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice and add the rum. Top with the ginger beer and garnish with the lime slice.
