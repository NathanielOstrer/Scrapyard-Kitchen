Oyster, Wild Rice

Marian Burros 4 servings, about 1 quart Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pint shucked oysters
  • 2 cups cooked wild rice (about 1/2 cup uncooked)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 strips cooked, crumbled nitrite-free bacon
  • ¼ teaspoon sage
  • ¼ teaspoon thyme
  • 1 tablespoon minced parsley
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Simmer the oysters in their own liquor for three minutes.
  2. Combine all of the ingredients but the oysters and mix well.
  3. Carefully stir in the oysters and their liquid.
  4. Grease a 1 1/2 quart casserole and fill with dressing. Cover and bake at 350 degrees about 15 or 20 minutes, until dressing is thoroughly heated.

Tip

  • To make a little wild rice go a long way, combine it with other rice in a main dish.
