Cointreau, Gin

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces gin
  • 1 ounce Cointreau
  • ¾ ounce lemon juice
  • ½ ounce Campari
  • Lemon wheel, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all liquid ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon wheel.
