Chicken Breast, Italian

Craig Claiborne 2 cooked chicken breasts Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 whole, skinless, boneless chicken breasts, about TK pounds
  • Water to cover
  • 1 small rib of celery, chopped
  • 1 small onion, quartered
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 small sprig thyme or 1/2 teaspoon dried
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • 12 peppercorns

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan. Partly cover and bring to the boil. Let simmer 15 minutes. Let stand until ready to use.
