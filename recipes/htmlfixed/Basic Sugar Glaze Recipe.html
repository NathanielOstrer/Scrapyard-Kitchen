Raw, Confectioners' Sugar

Dena Kleiman

Ingredients

  • ½ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 teaspoons water, approximately (see note)

Preparation

  1. Place the sugar in a bowl and whisk in the water until the glaze is smooth. Add water, drop by drop, to achieve desired consistency.
  2. Drizzle freely with a spoon over cookies. May also be applied with a feather or a pastry brush.

Tip

  • For a different taste, substitute fresh lemon juice, orange liqueur or Cognac for the water.
