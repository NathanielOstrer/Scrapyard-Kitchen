Sweet Corn

Denise Landis 8 servings Time1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 7.5-ounce package John Cope's Toasted Dried Sweet Corn
  • 5 cups milk
  • 3 ½ tablespoons melted butter, plus additional for the baking dish
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 4 eggs, beaten until well blended

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Using a blender or food processor, chop the dried corn until finely ground.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground corn, milk, butter, salt, sugar and eggs. Mix well.
  3. Butter a shallow 2-quart baking dish. Pour in the corn mixture. Bake until set and golden brown on top, about 1 hour. Serve hot.
