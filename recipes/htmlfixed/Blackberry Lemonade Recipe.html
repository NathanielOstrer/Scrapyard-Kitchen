Vegetarian, Vegan, Blackberry, Lemon Juice

Elaine Louie 1 serving

Ingredients

  • ½ cup blackberries
  • ⅓ cup lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup ice water

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender, and puree 1 minute. Add sugar to taste. Pour into ice-filled glass.
