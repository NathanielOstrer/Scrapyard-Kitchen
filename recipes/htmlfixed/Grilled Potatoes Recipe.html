Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Fat, New Potato

Marian Burros 2 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound new potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

Preparation

  1. Scrub potatoes; do not peel. If potatoes are large, slice 1/8-inch thick. If potatoes are very small, cut in quarters.
  2. Mix oil and cumin, and stir potatoes into mixture, coating well.
  3. Prepare stove-top grill. Arrange potatoes on grill; cook over medium heat, turning when potatoes brown on one side, and cook through.
