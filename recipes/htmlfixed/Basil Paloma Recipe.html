Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 basil leaves
  • 1 grapefruit segment
  • 2 ounces silver tequila
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • ½ ounce simple syrup
  • 3 ounces grapefruit juice
  • Soda water
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Put 2 basil leaves and 1 grapefruit segment in a highball glass and muddle gently. Add 2 ounces silver tequila, juice of 1/2 lime, and 1/2 ounce simple syrup. Stir gently. Add ice, 3 ounces grapefruit juice and soda to top. Stir gently again. Garnish with a lime wedge.
