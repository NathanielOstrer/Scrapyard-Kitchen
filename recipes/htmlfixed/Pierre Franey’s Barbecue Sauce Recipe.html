Honey, Ketchup, Lemon Juice, Southern

Pierre Franey About 1 cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 3 thin, seeded lemon slices
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan. Blend well, bring to a boil and remove from heat.
