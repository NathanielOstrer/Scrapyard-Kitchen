Moria Hodgson 4 to 6 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound string beans
  • 1 tablespoon wasabi paste (Japanese green horseradish)
  • ½ cup tamari soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Trim the string beans.
  2. Steam for 7 minutes.
  3. Cool for 10 minutes in the freezer.
  4. Moisten wasabi with a few drops of water to form a pale green ball, then mix with tamari. Put in a small bowl to serve as a dip for beans.
