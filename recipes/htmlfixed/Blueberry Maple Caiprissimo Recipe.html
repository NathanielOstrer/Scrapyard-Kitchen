Blueberry, Cognac, Maple Syrup

Pete Wells Two drinks Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ ounces maple syrup
  • 2 rosemary sprigs, cut into short pieces
  • 3 ounces Cognac
  • 3 ounces blueberries
  • 2 ounces lemon juice

Preparation

  1. In a microwave-safe glass or mug, stir together the syrup and rosemary. Microwave on high for about 30 seconds. When cool, discard rosemary.
  2. Add rosemary-flavored syrup to remaining ingredients in a blender. Add 1 1/2 cups ice. Purée until smooth, adding more ice if needed to achieve a consistency that is no longer liquid, but still pours freely.
