Jamie Boudreau 6 6-ounce drinks.

Sarah Anne Ward for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Paola Andrea.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces cognac
  • 3 ounces Ardbeg whisky
  • 3 ounces Tempus Fugit crème de cacao
  • 6 ounces water
  • 7 ounces milk
  • 1 ounce cream
  • 3 ounces egg white
  • ½ ounce Angostura bitters.

Preparation

  1. Add all ingredients to a soda siphon, and charge with one N2O cartridge. Serve in a fun glass, with a side of Fernet Branca ‘‘Oreo’’ cookies.
