Honey, Low Fat Yogurt, Ricotta

Nancy Harmon Jenkins 1 1/4 cups, enough for 8 servings Time5 minutes, plus refrigeration

Ingredients

  • 1 cup low-fat ricotta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons low-fat yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon

Preparation

  1. In the bowl of a food processor, combine all the ingredients and mix until they are smooth. Put the cream in the refrigerator to chill slightly before serving.
  2. Serve over the oven-poached pears or any other warm fruit.
