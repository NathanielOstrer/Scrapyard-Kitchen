Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • Scant teaspoon simple syrup
  • 1 ½ ounces gin
  • ½ ounce fresh lemon juice
  • Chilled ginger beer, to top
  • Lemon twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice. Add simple syrup, gin and lemon juice, then top with ginger beer. Stir gently. Garnish with a lemon twist.
