Vegetarian, Vegan, Campari, Cranberry, Rose Water

Christine Muhlke Serves 6 Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • Salt
  • 3 ½ cups cranberries
  • 2 strips orange rind
  • Pinch of allspice
  • 3 tablespoons Campari
  • 2 teaspoons rosewater

Preparation

  1. In a medium saucepan, stir together the sugar, 1 cup of water, a pinch of salt and 1 cranberry and bring to a boil. Add the remaining berries and orange rind and simmer, covered, over low heat for 5 minutes. Uncover, raise the heat to medium and simmer for 5 minutes more. Stir in the allspice, Campari and rosewater. Remove from heat and cool at room temperature. The sauce will thicken as it cools.
