Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Asparagus, Coriander, Mushroom

Bryan Miller And Pierre Franey Four to six servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed of woody sections and scraped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ pound sliced mushrooms (about 2 cups)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped shallots
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves

Preparation

  1. Cut the asparagus spears on the diagonal into one-inch pieces.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a nonstick frying pan. Add the mushrooms and saute over over high heat, tossing, until the mushrooms are lightly browned. Add the asparagus pieces. Cook, stirring and tossing, for about a minute. Add the shallots, salt and pepper. Sprinkle with the coriander. Cook for 30 seconds and serve.
