Tequila

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces reposado tequila, preferably Milagro
  • ½ ounce dry amontillado sherry, preferably Los Arcos from Lustau
  • ½ ounce Drambuie liqueur
  • 2 dashes Regan’s bitters, or other orange bitters
  • Twist of orange peel, for garnish
  • Small sprig of fresh thyme, for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine the tequila, sherry, Drambuie and bitters.
  2. Fill shaker with ice, shake well and strain mixture into a chilled cocktail glass. Float the orange peel on top like a boat (adding ice to glass may help), and place the thyme in the center.
