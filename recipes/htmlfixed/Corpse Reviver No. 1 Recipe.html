Brandy, Calvado

Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. brandy
  • ¾ oz. Calvados
  • ¾ oz. sweet Italian vermouth

Preparation

  1. Chill a coupe glass. Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass. Strain into coupe.
