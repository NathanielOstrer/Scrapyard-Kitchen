Vegetarian, Vegan, Carrot, Lemon, Soy Sauce

Marian Burros 2 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces small peeled carrots
  • 1 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon sugar

Preparation

  1. Wash and halve carrots, and steam for 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. In serving bowl, mix soy sauce, lemon juice and sugar. When carrots are cooked, drain, and stir into soy mixture.
