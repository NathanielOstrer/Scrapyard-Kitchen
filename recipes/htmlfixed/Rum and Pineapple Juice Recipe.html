Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 ounces dark rum
  • 4 ounces chilled pineapple juice
  • Splash of soda water
  • Nutmeg
  • Pineapple chunk, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice and add rum, pineapple juice and soda. Grate a little nutmeg on top and garnish with a chunk of pineapple.
