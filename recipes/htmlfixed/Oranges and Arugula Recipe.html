Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Calorie, Arugula, Orange

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces arugula
  • 2 eating oranges
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Trim tough stems from arugula. Wash, dry and slice arugula into thin strips.
  2. Peel oranges over the bowl in which the salad will be served, cutting each section in half and allowing juices to fall into bowl with the halved sections.
  3. In the bowl, stir in the mustard and soy sauce.
  4. Stir in the arugula.
