Gluten Free, Nut Free, Butter, Chive, Turkey Breast

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 turkey breast steaks, about 1 1/2 pounds
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh sage or 2 teaspoons dried
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 tablespoons finely chopped chives

Preparation

  1. Preheat a charcoal grill until quite hot. The coals should be placed quite close to the grill because the meat will only be seared.
  2. Place each turkey steak between sheets of clear plastic wrap. Pound lightly and evenly with a flat mallet or meat pounder to make slices about 1/4-inch thick.
  3. Season the turkey with salt and pepper. Brush both sides with olive oil and sprinkle both sides with sage.
  4. Heat the butter until melted. Add the lemon juice, cumin, Worcestershire, salt, pepper and chives. Blend well. Keep warm.
  5. Place the steaks on the grill and cook on both sides for about 2 minutes to a side or until done. Do not overcook. Transfer the steaks to a serving dish. Pour the chive butter over them and serve.
