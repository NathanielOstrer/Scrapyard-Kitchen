Arugula, Corn On The Cob, Pizza Dough, Italian

Mark Bittman 1 large, 2 medium, or more smaller pizzas Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (see recipe)
  • 6 cups loosely packed arugula, measured after washing, drying and shredding
  • Kernels from 4 ears of corn
  • ½ cup minced bacon

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Top prepared pizza rounds with arugula, corn and bacon. Bake about 15 minutes, or until nicely browned.
