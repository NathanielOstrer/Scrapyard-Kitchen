High Fiber

Mark Bittman TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • coarse-chopped slab bacon
  • olive oil
  • chopped onion
  • carrot
  • celery
  • lentils
  • stock
  • Parsley

Preparation

  1. Cook coarse-chopped slab bacon in olive oil until done; add chopped onion, carrot and celery, and cook for a few minutes. Add lentils and any stock to cover; bring to a boil, then reduce heat and slowly simmer until lentils are tender. Garnish: Parsley.
