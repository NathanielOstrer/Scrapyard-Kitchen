Dairy Free, Mussel, Pasta, Italian

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Mussels marinara (see recipe)
  • 1 pound penne
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the penne and cook until al dente, about 10 minutes. Drain and toss with the mussels marinara. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Divide among 4 plates and serve immediately.
