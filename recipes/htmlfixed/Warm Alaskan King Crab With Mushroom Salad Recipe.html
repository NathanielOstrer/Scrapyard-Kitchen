Gluten Free, Crab, Cremini, Pearl Tapioca

Melissa Clark 4 servings Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup large pearl tapioca
  • Salt
  • 4 bunches shiso leaves, available at Japanese markets
  • 1 bunch chives
  • ⅓ cup peanut oil
  • 3 tablespoons rice-wine vinegar
  • 8 large cremini mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 8 ounces cooked Alaskan king crab meat, cleaned

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the tapioca, lower the heat, and simmer until tapioca is tender, about 1 hour. After 45 minutes, add a large pinch of salt. Drain tapioca, and rinse well under cold water.
  2. Place 3 bunches of the shiso leaves, chives, peanut oil and rice-wine vinegar in a blender, and blend until smooth. Season with salt to taste. In a medium bowl, combine half the shiso-chive puree with the tapioca, and toss well. Reserve remaining puree for garnish.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mushrooms, olive oil and lemon juice, and toss well.
  4. Melt butter in a small saucepan, and add the crab. Saute until crab is heated through, about 1 minute.
  5. Chop remaining shiso, and stir into tapioca mixture. To serve, divide tapioca among 4 plates, and top with mushrooms and then with warm crab. Serve immediately.
