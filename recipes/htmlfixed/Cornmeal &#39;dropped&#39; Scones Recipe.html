Vegetarian, Nut Free, All Purpose Flour, Buttermilk, Cornmeal, Southern

Sarah Belk About 18 to 24 1 1/2-inch scones Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup cornmeal, preferably stone-ground
  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • Oil or drippings for the pan

Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, combine dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, beat egg and buttermilk. Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients and blend until just combined. Do not overmix. May be prepared up to 1 hour in advance to this point.
  2. Grease a skillet or griddle as you would for pancakes. Heat over medium-high heat. Drop in batter by scant tablespoons and cook about 1 minute each side, or until puffy and golden brown. Drain briefly on paper towels and serve hot.
