Vegetarian, Grapefruit, Olive Oil

Melissa Clark 4 servings Time5 minutes

Elizabeth D. Herman for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 3 Ruby Red grapefruit
  • Good quality extra-virgin olive oil
  • Flaky sea salt

Preparation

  1. Peel fruit, pulling and scraping off as much of the white pith as possible. Slice in rounds 1/2-inch thick and lay them out on a platter.
  2. Drizzle grapefruit lightly with oil and sprinkle with sea salt. Serve.
