Raw, Lemon Juice, Parsley, Sour Cream, French

Marian Burros Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces (1 pint) sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • White pepper to taste
  • 1 ½ tablespoons finely minced shallots
  • 2 tablespoons minced parsley

Preparation

  1. Mix the sour cream with the lemon juice, salt, plenty of pepper, shallots and parsley.
