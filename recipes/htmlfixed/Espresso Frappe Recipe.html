Espresso, Sweetened Condensed Milk

Molly O'Neill 1 large serving or 2 medium ones

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chilled espresso
  • 12 ounces ice cubes, about 12
  • 2 tablespoons fat-free sweetened condensed skim milk

Preparation

  1. Place the espresso, ice cubes and condensed milk in a blender, and process until smooth and frothy.
  2. Pour into a tall glass or into two 8-ounce glasses, and serve immediately.
