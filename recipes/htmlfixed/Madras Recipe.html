Cranberry Juice, Orange Juice, Vodka

Rosie Schaap

William Brinson for The New York Times. Food stylist: Ed Gabriels. Prop stylist: Susan Brinson.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 2 ounces cranberry juice
  • 2 ounces orange juice
  • Splash of triple sec
  • Splash of club soda
  • Orange peel

Preparation

  1. Add vodka, juices and triple sec to an ice-filled highball glass.
  2. Stir gently and add a splash of club soda.
  3. Garnish with a twist of orange peel.
