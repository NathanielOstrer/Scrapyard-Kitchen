Butter, Chicken Broth, Curry Powder, Flour

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About five tablespoons Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon flour
  • ¼ cup unsalted chicken broth

Preparation

  1. Heat the butter in a small saucepan and stir in the curry powder and flour, blending well.
  2. Add the broth, stirring constantly with a wire whisk. When mixture is thickened and smooth, remove it from the heat. Let cool.
