Dairy Free, Nut Free, Barbecue Sauce, Ground Beef

Ann Barry 4 servings Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup Show-Me Bar-B-Q Sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef

Preparation

  1. Mix 2/3 cup of the barbecue sauce with remaining ingredients.
  2. Form a loaf, and put into a pan.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
  4. Pour another 2/3 cup of the sauce over the loaf, and bake 15 minutes more.

Tip

  • 1/2 cup each chopped onion and green pepper may be added if desired.
