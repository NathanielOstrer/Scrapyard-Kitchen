Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Cilantro, Green Chili, Tomato, Mexican

Marian Burros 2 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, mashed
  • 6 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 6 mild green chilies, chopped
  • 1 medium white onion, chopped
  • Salt to taste (optional)
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 4 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons canola oil
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients and serve. Without the tomatoes the salsa can be refrigerated for several hours. Add the tomatoes just before serving.
