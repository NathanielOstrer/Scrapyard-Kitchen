Avocado, Tomato, Mexican

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey 2 1/2 cups TimeAbout 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cubed fresh red ripe tomatoes
  • 6 tablespoons chopped onions, preferably red
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh coriander
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped seeded jalapeno peppers
  • 1 ½ cups cubed avocado
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl and blend well.
