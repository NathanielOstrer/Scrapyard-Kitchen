Vegetarian, Butternut Squash, Candied Ginger, Lemon, Asian

Kay Rentschler 1/2 cup Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup minced butternut squash
  • 4 teaspoons minced candied ginger
  • 1 tablespoon minced lemon peel (cut from thin lemon slices)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients in small bowl, and stir well. Macerate 30 minutes. Turn into small saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring frequently, until tender and relatively dry, 10 to 15 minutes. Remove from heat and cool. Use as garnish for pies.
