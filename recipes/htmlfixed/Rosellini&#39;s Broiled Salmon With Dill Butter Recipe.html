Butter, Salmon Fillet

Marian Burros 6 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 six-ounce salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • ¼ cup dry white wine
  • 2 teaspoons chopped shallots
  • 2 tablespoons chopped dill
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • ½ pound cold butter, cut in small pieces

Preparation

  1. Brush salmon with oil and broil until it flakes, 4 to 5 minutes. Remove to heat-proof serving plate and keep warm.
  2. Meanwhile, heat wine and shallots to boiling in saute pan. Add dill and lemon juice and reduce to three tablespoons. Let cool.
  3. Place pot over low heat and gradually add butter, piece by piece, whisking constantly until mixture thickens. Pour over salmon fillets and serve.
