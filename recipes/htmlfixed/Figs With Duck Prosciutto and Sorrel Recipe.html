Duck, Fig, Sorrel

Molly O'Neill 12 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 5 ounces duck prosciutto, cut thinly into 4-inch-long slices (see note)
  • 12 sorrel leaves, stacked, sliced in half lengthwise
  • 12 ripe figs, halved lengthwise

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey and mustard.
  2. On a work surface, lay out the prosciutto slices and top each with a sorrel strip. Using a pastry brush, coat each lightly with some of the honey mustard. Place a fig, rounded side down, in the middle of each strip and wrap the prosciutto and sorrel around it. Push a toothpick from the base of each parcel through the top, pinning together the overlapping prosciutto and sorrel. Transfer to a serving platter and serve.
