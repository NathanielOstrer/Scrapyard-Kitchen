Heavy Cream, Rhubarb, Sugar

Christine Muhlke Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 1 pound trimmed rhubarb, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • Half a vanilla bean
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. In a bowl, toss the rhubarb with 1/2 cup of sugar. Spread evenly on a nonstick baking sheet. Roast about 20 minutes. Transfer to a container, including any juices from the sheet. Let cool, and then cover and refrigerate.
  2. To assemble, drain rhubarb in a strainer over a bowl, reserving juices; set aside. Split vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape the pulp into the remaining sugar. Mix well, and then add to cream. Beat until stiff. In a glass bowl or individual tall glasses, layer rhubarb and cream, starting with rhubarb and ending with cream. Drizzle rhubarb juice over the top. Serve immediately or chill for up to 4 hours.
