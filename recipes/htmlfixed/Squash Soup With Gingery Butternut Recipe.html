Vegetarian, Vegan, Butternut Squash, Ginger

Florence Fabricant 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3 cups coarsely diced butternut squash (about 1 large squash)
  • 4 cups water
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh cilantro

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a heavy saucepan, add the ginger, garlic and cumin and saute, stirring, until the garlic turns golden.
  2. Add the squash and water, bring to a simmer and cook until the squash is soft, about 20 minutes. Puree the mixture in a food processor, then pass through a sieve and return to the saucepan.
  3. Reheat, adding lemon and salt and pepper to taste. Thin with a little additional water if desired. Fold in the cilanto and serve.
