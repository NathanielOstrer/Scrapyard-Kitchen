Pierre Franey 1/3 cup Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 jalapeno peppers
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon ground coriander
  • ¼ cup olive oil

Preparation

  1. Cut off the stems of the jalapeno peppers. Cut off and discard the veins and seeds. Chop the peppers coarsely.
  2. Put the peppers and all of the remaining ingredients in a food processor or blender. Blend to a fine liquid texture.

Tip

  • When refrigerated, the sauce can be kept for future use.
