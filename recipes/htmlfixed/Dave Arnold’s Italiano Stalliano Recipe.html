Dave Arnold 2 5-ounce drinks

Sarah Anne Ward for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Paola Andrea.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 2 ounces 84 6/10-proof Galliano
  • 2 ounces water (use the freshest water possible; avoid water that has a bad taste or aroma)
  • 3 ounces orange juice (fresh-squeezed and strained of pulp)
  • 1 ounce lemon juice (fresh-squeezed and strained of pulp)
  • 1 pinch salt

Preparation

  1. Make sure your freezer is set to its coldest setting. Combine all ingredients in a Ziploc bag, as airtight as possible, and place in freezer.
  2. Freezing will take several hours. (It is best to make the mix in the morning and serve it in the evening. The quality of the lemon juice will degrade gradually if it is stored longer.) The mix will never get completely solid because of the alcohol and sugar. When ready to serve, blend for a few seconds until it becomes homogeneous. Serve in coupe glasses.
