Mark Bittman TimeAbout 30 minutes

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Susan Ottoviano.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds shrimp
  • olive oil
  • lemon juice
  • ¼ cup breadcrumbs

Garnish

  • Parsley and lemon

Preparation

  1. Heat the oven to 500.
  2. Put 1 1/2 pounds shrimp in the bottom of a roasting pan, and toss with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Scatter 1/4 cup breadcrumbs on top, and drizzle with more oil.
  4. Roast, turning the shrimp once, until they’re pink all over, 10 to 15 minutes.
