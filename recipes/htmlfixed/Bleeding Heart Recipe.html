Blood Orange Juice, Rum

William L. Hamilton Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce white rum
  • 1 ounce dark (or gold) rum
  • ½ ounce Cointreau or triple sec
  • Juice of 1 lime wedge
  • 4 ounces blood orange juice
  • Wedges of orange, lime and tangerine, as a garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all liquid ingredients together with ice, and strain into an 8-ounce highball glass filled with crushed ice.
  2. For garnish, thread two skinned tangerine or orange wedges on each of two wooden skewers. Run skewers, crossing, through a lime wedge.
