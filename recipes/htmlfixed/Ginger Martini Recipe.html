Cointreau, Ginger, Vodka

Abdul Tabini

William Mebane for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 pieces fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 ounces Ketel One Citroen vodka
  • ½ ounce lemon juice
  • ½ ounce simple syrup
  • ¾ ounce Cointreau
  • 1 dash orange juice

Preparation

  1. Muddle ginger in a mixing glass. Add ice and the remaining ingredients. Shake, and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
