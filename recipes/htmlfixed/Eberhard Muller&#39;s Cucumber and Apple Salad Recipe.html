Raw, Vegetarian, Vegan, Apple, Carrot, Cucumber

Mark Bittman 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 English cucumber, washed
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and julienned or very thinly sliced
  • 1 green apple, washed and julienned or very thinly sliced
  • 1 large or 2 small cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 or 2 jalapenos or other hot peppers, minced, or 1/2 tablespoon hot pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro, minced
  • ½ cup rice vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Cut the cucumber in half. Use a spoon to scoop out the seeds; discard them. Julienne or thinly slice the cucumber, then combine with the carrots, apple, garlic, peppers and cilantro.
  2. Toss with the vinegar, then add salt and pepper.
