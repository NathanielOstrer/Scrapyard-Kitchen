Carrot, Sweet Potato

Mark Bittman Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Carrots
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Radishes or turnips
  • Garlic
  • Limes
  • Chili powder
  • Scallions
  • Fish sauce

Preparation

  1. Cook a thinly sliced medium onion in olive oil over medium-high heat until tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, peel and grate a few carrots, sweet potatoes and radishes or turnips; add them, along with some minced garlic, to the pan and continue to cook, stirring occasionally, for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add the juice of half a lime (this will loosen the mixture a bit), a sprinkle of chili powder, a handful of chopped scallions and fish sauce to taste
  4. Garnish with lime wedges and serve.
