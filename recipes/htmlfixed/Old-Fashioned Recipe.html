Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoon Demerara sugar
  • 1 orange slice
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 2 ounces bourbon or rye
  • Dash of club soda (optional)
  • 1 Luxardo Marasca cherry or other high-quality macerated cocktail cherry

Preparation

  1. Place sugar, orange slice and bitters in an old-fashioned glass. Gently muddle until sugar has absorbed some liquid and resembles a fine paste. Add the bourbon or rye, along with a large ice cube or two, and stir well, about 20 times. Top with just slightest dash of club soda, if desired, and garnish with cherry.
