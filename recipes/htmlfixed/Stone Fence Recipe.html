William Brinson for The New York Times; Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Deborah Williams.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot Old Overholt (or whatever rye you like)
  • Ice
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 8 to 10 ounces cider
  • Apple slice for garnish

Preparation

  1. Pour rye into a pint glass, then add ice and bitters, top up with cider and garnish with apple.
