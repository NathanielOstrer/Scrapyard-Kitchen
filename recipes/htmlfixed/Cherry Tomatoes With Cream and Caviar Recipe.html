Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Caviar, Cherry Tomato, Sour Cream

Florence Fabricant 36 hors d'oeuvres, 8 to 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 36 firm but ripe cherry tomatoes about an inch in diameter
  • ½ cup sour cream or creme fraiche
  • ½ ounce sturgeon, salmon or whitefish caviar (see note)

Preparation

  1. Remove any stems from the cherry tomatoes. Turn them stem-side down on a cutting board or other work surface. Carefully slice off the top third of each tomato, reserving these pieces for another use.
  2. Stir the sour cream or creme fraiche to make it smooth. Using a small spoon, dab a scant teaspoon of the cream carefully and neatly on the top of each tomato.
  3. Using the tip of a knife, place a tiny dab of the caviar on the cream. Arrange the tomatoes on a platter and serve, or refrigerate for up to one-half hour before serving.

Tip

  • Tiny sprigs of herb, like dill, can be used instead of the caviar.
