Ingredients

  • green beans
  • olive oil
  • lemon juice
  • garlic.

Preparation

  1. Blanch green beans in boiling water, then plunge them into an ice bath to stop the cooking.
  2. Toss with a vinaigrette made with olive oil, lemon juice and a tiny bit of minced garlic. (To turn this into a main dish, sear some tuna until crusty on the outside, but still raw in the middle. Cut into thick slices or chunks, and toss with the green beans and dressing.)
