Dark Rum, Unsweetened Pineapple Juice

Elaine Louie 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 curl of lemon rind
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 16 ice cubes
  • 2 ounces dark rum
  • 2 ounces unsweetened pineapple juice
  • 1 ounce port, preferably vintage

Preparation

  1. Rub lemon rind around rim of a chilled cocktail glass, and roll rim of glass in the sugar.
  2. Put the ice in a cocktail shaker, add the rum and pineapple juice and shake. Strain into the sugar-rimmed glass. Drizzle the port on top so it swirls on top of the foamy head.
