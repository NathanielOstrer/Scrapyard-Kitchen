Raspberry, Strawberry, Sugar, French

Ken Hom 1 1/2 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh raspberries or strawberries
  • ¼ cup sugar

Preparation

  1. Puree the berries in a blender. Strain into a small bowl through a fine mesh strainer. Mix in the sugar. Refrigerate until ready to use.

Tip

  • This recipe can be easily doubled or tripled.
