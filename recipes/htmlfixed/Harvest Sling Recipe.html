Apple Brandy, Sweet Vermouth

Jim Meehan 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 1½ ounces apple brandy
  • ½ ounce sweet vermouth
  • ½ ounce Bénédictine
  • ½ ounce Cherry Heering
  • ½ ounce lemon juice
  • Splash of ginger ale
  • orange wheel, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all liquid ingredients, except for the ginger ale, with ice. Strain into a Collins glass filled with ice. Top with a splash of ginger ale and garnish with half an orange wheel.
