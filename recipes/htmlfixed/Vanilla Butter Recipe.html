Butter, Powdered Sugar, Vanilla, American

Marian Burros

Ingredients

  • 12 tablespoons sweet butter
  • ¾ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Make the vanilla butter while the bread is rising. Cream the butter and sugar. Slowly beat in the vanilla.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a serving bowl, cover it and chill. Remove it from the refrigerator at least 30 minutes before serving. The butter should be soft and spreadable.
