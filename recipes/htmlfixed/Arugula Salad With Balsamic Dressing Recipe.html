Arugula, Egg, Red Onion, Italian

Pierre Franey 4 to 6 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Dijon-style mustard
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon finely minced garlic
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cold water
  • ⅔ cup olive oil
  • ½ pound arugula leaves
  • ¾ cup coarsely chopped red onion
  • 2 hard-cooked eggs
  • Garlic croutons (see recipe) coarsely chopped

Preparation

  1. Put mustard in a small mixing bowl and add vinegar. Beat to a thin paste. Add garlic, salt, pepper and water. Blend well.
  2. Beat the mixture vigorously while adding the oil.
  3. Rinse arugula thoroughly and pat leaves dry. There should be about 8 cups. Put in a mixing bowl.
  4. Add onion and eggs and pour the dressing over all. Toss to blend well and add croutons
