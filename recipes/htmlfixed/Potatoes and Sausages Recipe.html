Portuguese Chourico, Potato, Portuguese

Isabel Costa 20 servings Time1 hour 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 15 medium-size potatoes (5 to 5 1/2 pounds), peeled and left whole
  • 1 8-inch chouriço (Portuguese sausage), thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ pound country bacon cut in 1 1/2-inch dice
  • 1 teaspoon red hot peppers or hot pepper sauce
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 to 1 ¼ cups red wine, approximately

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Arrange potatoes and sausage in 9-by-14-inch casserole. Sprinkle with garlic, salt, bacon, hot peppers and paprika. Pour 1 cup wine over all.
  3. Cover with foil and cook in oven about an hour, checking occasionally. After an hour, add more wine and cook, uncovered, for 20 minutes, until potatoes are browned.
