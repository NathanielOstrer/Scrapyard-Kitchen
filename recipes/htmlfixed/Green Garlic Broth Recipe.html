Chicken Broth, Green Garlic, Leek

David Tanis 4 to 6 servings Time20 minutes

Evan Sung for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 7 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • ½ pound green garlic shoots or ramps, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups diced leeks, white and tender green part
  • 4 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 6 slices toasted baguette, optional
  • 2 tablespoons slivered chives

Preparation

  1. In a large saucepan, bring chicken broth to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Add green garlic shoots and leeks. Simmer 5 minutes. Check seasoning and adjust. (Soup may be made in advance up to this point, then reheated.) Just before serving, stir in scallions.
  3. Ladle into soup bowls, add baguette slice if using, and sprinkle generously with chives.
