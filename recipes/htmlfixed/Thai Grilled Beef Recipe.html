Beef, Thai

William Norwich 8 salad portions

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef eye of round
  • ½ cup Thai Beef Marinade (see recipe)

Preparation

  1. Place the meat in a bowl and coat with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Grill or broil, browning all sides, until rare to medium rare, about 25 minutes.
