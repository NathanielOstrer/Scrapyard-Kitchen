Coconut Water, Gin

Philip Greene

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces London dry gin
  • 4 ounces coconut water (not milk)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 to 4 dashes Angostura bitters

Preparation

  1. Shake all ingredients well with ice, then transfer contents of shaker into a Collins glass, adding more ice as needed. Garnish with a lime wedge or peel.
