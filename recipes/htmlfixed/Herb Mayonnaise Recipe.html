Herb, Olive Oil

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About one and one-quarter cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons imported mustard
  • 4 teaspoons red-wine vinegar
  • ½ cup safflower oil
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh herbs, such as basil, parsley and dill
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Put the egg yolk, mustard and vinegar in a mixing bowl. Start beating with a wire whisk while gradually adding the safflower and olive oils. Beat until thickened and smooth.
  2. Beat in the herbs, salt and pepper.
