Mezcal, Tequila

Florence Fabricant 1 cocktail

Ingredients

  • ¼ ounce mezcal, preferably Del Maguey Tobala, or more to taste
  • 1 ounce or less silver tequila, depending on amount of mezcal
  • ¼ ounce triple sec or Cointreau
  • ¼ ounce agave nectar
  • ½ ounce fresh lime juice
  • Dash Fernet Branca
  • 1 egg white
  • Lime wedge

Preparation

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine all ingredients and shake vigorously without ice. Add ice, shake again, and taste for balance.
  2. Strain into a rocks glass over ice and garnish with lime wedge.
