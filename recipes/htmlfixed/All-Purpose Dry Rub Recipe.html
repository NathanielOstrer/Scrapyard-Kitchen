Cayenne Pepper, Chili Powder, Coriander, Cumin, Paprika

Sam Sifton 2 3/4 cups Time5 minutes

Melina Hammer for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ½ cup paprika, or 1/3 cup smoked paprika
  • ¼ cup kosher salt
  • ¼ cup freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup chile powder
  • 3 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well with a fork to break up the sugar and combine the spices. Mixture will keep in an airtight container, out of the light, for a few months.
