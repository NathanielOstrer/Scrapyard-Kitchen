Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Chervil, Peppercorn, German, Austrian

Marian Burros Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon green peppercorns
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chervil, chopped
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Press the peppercorns through a sieve and mix with the oil. Add chervil, lemon juice and season with salt.
