Butter, Milk, Potato

Bryan Miller 6 to 8 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 7 medium size Idaho potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 stick sweet butter
  • ¼ cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Boil the potatoes until they are very well cooked, nearly falling apart, about 30 minutes.
  2. Drain potatoes thoroughly. In a large bowl mash the potatoes well with a hand masher. Add butter and mash more; add milk slowly while mashing until desired consistency is reached (some potatoes absorb more liquid than others). Season to taste and serve.
