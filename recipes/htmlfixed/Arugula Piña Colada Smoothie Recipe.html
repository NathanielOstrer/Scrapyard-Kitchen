Ingredients

  • ¼ cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • ½ cup light coconut milk
  • 1 ¼ cups chopped pineapple (about 180 grams)
  • 1 cup arugula (30 grams), rinsed
  • 1 quarter-size piece of ginger, peeled
  • 2 or 3 ice cubes

Preparation

  1. Place all of the ingredients in a blender and blend for 1 full minute. Pour into a glass, garnish with an orange slice and enjoy.

Tip

  • This is best enjoyed right away.
