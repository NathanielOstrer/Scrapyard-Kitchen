Gluten Free, Black Peppercorn, Heavy Cream, Milk

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time15 minutes, plus freeze time

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 5 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons black peppercorns, freshly ground
  • 3 egg yolks

Preparation

  1. In a medium-size saucepan, combine the cream, milk, sugar and pepper. Bring to a boil. Meanwhile, whisk the yolks in a a mixing bowl until light. Whisking constantly and rapidly, gradually pour the boiling cream mixture into the yolks.
  2. Place over a bowl of ice to cool. Cover and refrigerate until cold. Freeze in an ice cream machine according to manufacturer's instructions.
