Blood Orange Puree, Cabernet Merlot

William L. Hamilton Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce blood-orange purée
  • 1 ounce Stoli Oranj vodka
  • 2 ounces cabernet-merlot
  • ½ ounce simple syrup
  • 1 edible flower

Preparation

  1. Shake liquid ingredients with ice, and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with the flower.
