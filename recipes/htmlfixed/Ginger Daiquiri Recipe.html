Raw, Cointreau, Ginger, Sugar

William L. Hamilton

Phil Mansfield for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • orange peel
  • ginger sugar (seal 6 ounces turbinado sugar with 1 ounce fresh ginger root in a jar for a week; shake the jar every day)
  • 2 ounces white Haitian rum
  • ½ ounce Cointreau
  • ½ ounce Meyer lemon juice
  • ½ ounce ginger-infused simple syrup (simple syrup macerated with peeled fresh ginger root; 4 ounces ginger will yield one pint; macerate two days)

Preparation

  1. Swipe the orange peel on the rim of a cocktail glass; press the wet rim down into a shallow dish of the ginger sugar, to ''frost'' the rim.
  2. Combine the remaining ingredients in a shaker with ice; shake and strain into the glass, to the rim. Garnish with peel.
