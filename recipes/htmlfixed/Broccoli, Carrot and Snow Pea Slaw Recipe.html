Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Broccoli, Carrot, Snow Pea

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 medium-size broccoli stems, trimmed, peeled and julienned
  • 5 medium-size carrots, trimmed, peeled and julienned
  • 2 cups snow peas, trimmed and julienned
  • 1 teaspoon cracked coriander seeds
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ⅛ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the broccoli, carrots and snow peas and blanch for 30 seconds. Drain, refresh under cold running water and drain again. Place in a large bowl. Add the remaining ingredients and toss to coat. Let stand for 1 hour. Divide among 4 plates and serve.
