Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup plus 1 tablespoon unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon superfine sugar
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 ½ tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • ⅛ teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Combine the flour, superfine sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Stir the melted butter and vanilla together in another small bowl. Gradually stir the butter mixture into the flour mixture, just until evenly moistened (you may not need all of the butter). Squeeze the mixture in your hands until thoroughly combined. Crumble the mixture in the bowl to make fine crumbs with some small lumps.
