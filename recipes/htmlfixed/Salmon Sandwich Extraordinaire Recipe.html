Bread, Salmon

Marian Burros 2 servings Time7 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 6- or 7-ounce can of wild salmon
  • 1 hard-cooked egg, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • 4 teaspoons finely chopped celery
  • 4 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 leaves lettuce
  • 4 slices good soft white bread or whole-wheat bread
  • 2 to 4 slices ripe tomato, optional

Preparation

  1. Drain salmon, and if it contains skin and bones, remove them (for a more neutral taste, run drained salmon under warm water.) Add egg, relish, celery, mayonnaise, lemon juice and salt and pepper, and mix thoroughly, mashing the salmon while mixing.
  2. Place a lettuce leaf on top of each of two slices of bread. Add tomato, if desired; top with salmon mixture.

Tip

  • Well covered, this will keep in the refrigerator for several days.
