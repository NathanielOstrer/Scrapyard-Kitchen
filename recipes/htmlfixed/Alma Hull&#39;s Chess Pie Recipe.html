Vegetarian, Egg Yolk, Milk, Sugar, Southern

Joan Cook 8 servings Time1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 stick margarine
  • 3 tablespoons cornmeal
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 ½ cups milk

Preparation

  1. Mix first seven ingredients, add egg yolks, mix well. Add milk and stir. Let stand 30 minutes. Pour into unbaked pastry shell and bake at 350 degrees about 40 minutes or until firm.
