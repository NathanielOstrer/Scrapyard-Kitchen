Chicken Broth, Chili Sauce, Onion, Barbecue

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey TK Time35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups finely chopped onions
  • ½ cup butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tablespoons chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon horseradish
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Tabasco sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon ground allspice

Preparation

  1. In a heavy saucepan, saute the onions in the butter until golden (not brown). Add the garlic and cook a few minutes. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer, uncovered, 25 to 30 minutes.
