Brandy, Concord Grape

Melissa Clark 1 quart Time5 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 quart (about 1 1/4 pounds) Concord grapes, washed, stems removed and deseeded (optional)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 to 3 cups brandy

Preparation

  1. Place grapes inside a large jar and, using a wooden spoon, break the skins. Stir in the sugar.
  2. Pour brandy over the grapes to cover by an inch. Cover and store in a dark, cool place (but not the refrigerator) for at least one month.
