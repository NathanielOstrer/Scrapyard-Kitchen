Ginger Beer, Lime, Rum

William L. Hamilton

Michael C. York for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Gosling's Black Seal rum
  • 5 ounces AJ Stephans ginger beer
  • Lime wedge

Preparation

  1. Fill a Collins glass with ice. Pour rum over ice, then ginger beer. Garnish with lime.
