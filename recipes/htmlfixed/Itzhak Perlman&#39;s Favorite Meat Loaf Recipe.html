Bread Crumb, Onion, Veal

John Rockwell 10 to 12 servings Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 small onions or 2 large ones
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 ½ pounds ground veal
  • Ketchup to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Garlic salt to taste
  • A cup or two of boiling water
  • 8 ounces, approximately, of flavored bread crumbs

Preparation

  1. Chop the onions and saute them until translucent, adding garlic clove, minced or put through garlic press, toward the end.
  2. Put the meat in a big bowl. Add the onions, garlic, ketchup, pepper and garlic salt. Knead by hand. Add the water and the bread crumbs and mix well by hand.
  3. Shape into 2 loaves in baking tins. Cook 30 to 40 minutes in a preheated 350-degree oven. (''Stick a knife in,'' Mr. Perlman says. ''If it comes out cold, it's not ready; if it's warm, it is.'')
