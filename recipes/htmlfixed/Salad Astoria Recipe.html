Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Calamata Olives, Lettuce, Onion

William Grimes Two servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 head soft-leaf lettuce (like bibb or Boston)
  • Minced Bermuda onion to taste
  • 1 dozen pitted calamata olives, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon red-wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
