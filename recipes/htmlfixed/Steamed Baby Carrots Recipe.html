Kosher, Carrot, Mint, Sugar

Colette Rossant Six to eight servings Time15

Ingredients

  • 2 bags of baby carrots, about 12 ounces each, scraped and washed
  • 3 tablespoons margarine
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint

Preparation

  1. Steam the carrots for five minutes or until barely tender. Melt the margarine in a saucepan. Add the carrots, sprinkle with sugar, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Cook over high heat for five minutes, stirring all the time.
  2. Remove to a serving bowl.
  3. Sprinkle the mint over the carrots and serve.
