Espresso, Simple Syrup

Oliver Strand 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 1 shot fruity espresso
  • 4 teaspoons simple syrup

Preparation

  1. Make 1 shot fruity espresso and pour immediately into a cocktail shaker with 5 to 6 ice cubes. Add 4 teaspoons simple syrup and shake vigorously for 15 seconds. Strain into a glass. Serve immediately.
