Goat Cheese, Lettuce, Radicchio

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 3 cups lettuce, washed and dried
  • 2 cups radicchio, washed and dried
  • ¼ pound goat cheese, crumbled

Preparation

  1. To make the dressing, place mustard in bowl and add vinegar and garlic. Start beating with wire whisk and add oil slowly, then salt and pepper to taste. Blend well.
  2. Add the lettuce, radicchio and cheese, toss well and serve.
