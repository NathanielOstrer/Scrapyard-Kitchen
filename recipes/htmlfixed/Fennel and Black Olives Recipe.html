Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Calorie, Black Olives, Fennel

Marian Burros 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces fennel (1 1/2 cups)
  • 6 large Italian, French or Greek black olives
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Preparation

  1. Wash fennel bulb and trim off leaves and stems. Slice off bottom of bulb; then, thinly slice the remainder of it and separate into rings. Arrange on two salad plates.
  2. Pit olives, and sprinkle over fennel. Dribble olive oil on top.
