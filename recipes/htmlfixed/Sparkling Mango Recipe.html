Mango Liqueur, Vodka

William L. Hamilton 1 serving Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ ounces of 80 proof vodka
  • ¾ ounces of Hiram Walker Fruja Mango
  • ½ ounces of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Chilled Champagne or sparkling water
  • Thin slice of fresh mango

Preparation

  1. Pour all liquid ingredients except the Champagne into a mixing glass. Add ice. Shake well for several seconds. Strain into a chilled 5-ounce martini glass, and top to the brim with Champagne. Garnish with a mango slice.
