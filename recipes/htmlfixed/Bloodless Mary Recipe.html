Celery, Horseradish, Vodka

Matt Lee And Ted Lee 1 cocktail

Ingredients

  • Celery salt
  • 2 ounces horseradish-infused vodka, well chilled
  • 1 celery stalk

Preparation

  1. Dip rim of a martini glass in water, then in celery salt to coat. Add chilled vodka. Garnish with celery cut into very fine julienne on a mandoline or with a vegetable peeler. Serve.
