Vegetarian, Vegan, Eggplant, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Mediterranean

Moira Hodgson 4 servings Time30 minutes, plus 1 hour's sit

Ingredients

  • 8 baby eggplants
  • Coarse salt
  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons fresh basil leaves, chopped

Preparation

  1. Slice off the green tops and cut the eggplants in half. Score in a criss-cross pattern using a knife. Salt and leave the eggplants for one hour. Pat dry.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  3. Sprinkle the eggplant with garlic and breadcrumbs. Place the eggplants in an oiled baking tin. Trickle on the olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake the eggplants for 20 to 30 minutes or until they are soft and golden. Sprinkle with basil and serve at room temperature.
