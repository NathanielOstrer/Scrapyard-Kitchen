Gluten Free, Cucumber, Yogurt, Middle Eastern

Marian Burros Eight servings

Ingredients

  • 4 cups plain yogurt
  • 2 cups peeled, seeded, grated cucumber
  • ¾ cup seedless raisins
  • 2 small cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and white pepper to taste
  • 1 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • ½ tablespoon minced fresh dill

Preparation

  1. Beat the yogurt in an electric mixer or with a rotary beater.
  2. Combine the yogurt, cucumber, raisins and garlic with one cup cold water.
  3. Season with salt and pepper and refrigerate for at least one hour.
  4. When ready to serve, add the walnuts and dill to the soup.
