Butternut Squash

Molly O'Neill Ten to 12 servings TimeAbout 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-size butternut squash, peeled, halved, fibers scooped out, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 3 teaspoons canola oil
  • 1 cup carrots, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 1 cup leeks, cleaned and cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 1 cup zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 1 cup Golden Delicious apples, peeled, cored and cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • ½ cup minced shallots
  • 1 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons salt, plus more to taste
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Place the squash in a roasting pan and toss with 2 teaspoons of the oil. Roast until just tender, tossing from time to time, about 25 minutes.
  2. Heat the remaining teaspoon of oil in a large, nonstick skillet. Add the roasted squash and the carrots and cook for 3 minutes. Add the leeks and cook for 2 minutes. Add the zucchini, apples and shallots and cook for 3 minutes longer. Stir in the vegetable or chicken broth, salt and pepper and simmer until the vegetables are tender but not too soft, about 15 minutes. Serve warm.
