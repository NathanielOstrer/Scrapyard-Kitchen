Absinthe, Apricot Liqueur, Cocchi Apertivo Americano, Plymouth Gin

The New York Times 1 drink Time2 minutes

Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¾ ounce apricot liqueur
  • 1 ounce Cocchi Apertivo Americano
  • 1 ½ ounce Plymouth gin
  • absinthe, to rinse the interior of the glass

Preparation

  1. First swirl some absinthe in a glass, then pour it out. Next, combine three-fourths of an ounce of apricot liqueur, an ounce of Cocchi Aperitivo Americano and one and a half ounces of Plymouth gin. Stir with ice and strain into the glass. Spritz with lemon peel oil (by squeezing a zest of lemon over the drink) and garnish with a twist of lemon peel.
