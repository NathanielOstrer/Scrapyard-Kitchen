Milk, Pea, Shrimp

Robert Farrar Capon 4 servings TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh shrimp
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • ½ teaspoon dry mustard, or as desired
  • 1 ¼ cups milk or half-and-half
  • ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, or as desired
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup frozen peas

Preparation

  1. Peel and devein shrimp. Mix shrimp with lemon juice.
  2. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat and saute shrimp until they turn opaque (about 2 minutes). Remove and reserve.
  3. Mix flour with dry mustard and add to skillet, stirring to form a smooth roux. Add milk and bring to the boil, whisking constantly to produce a smooth sauce. Season to taste with Worcestershire, salt and pepper. Add frozen peas, return to the boil and cook 1 minute, or until peas are done to taste.
  4. Return shrimp to skillet to heat through and finish cooking (about 1 minute). Pour mixture into serving dish. Serve over rice.
