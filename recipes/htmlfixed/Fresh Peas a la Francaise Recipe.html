Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Lettuce, Onion, Pea, French

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shelled peas (If fresh peas are not available, frozen peas may be used.)
  • 4 small white onions, peeled and sliced
  • 8 Boston lettuce leaves, shredded
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon flour or cornstarch

Preparation

  1. Combine peas, onions, lettuce, salt, pepper, sugar and 1 tablespoon of the butter in a bowl. Mix well and add the water.
  2. Place in a saucepan. Cover and bring to a boil. Simmer until tender.
  3. Meanwhile, cream together the remaining 1 tablespoon butter and the flour. Add to the saucepan, stirring with a wooden spatula. Blend well. When liquid has thickened and returned to a boil, remove from heat and serve immediately.
