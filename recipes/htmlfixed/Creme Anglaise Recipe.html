Gluten Free, Egg Yolk, Heavy Cream, Milk, French

Marian Burros Time10 minutes, plus chill time

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces milk
  • 3 ounces heavy cream
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • Seeds from one vanilla bean

Preparation

  1. In a nonaluminum mixing bowl incorporate egg yolks, sugar and vanilla seeds.
  2. Bring milk and cream just to a boil in heavy-bottom sauce pot. Remove one-quarter of mixture and mix well with egg mixture. Return to pot, beating slowly and cook over low heat for two minutes.
  3. Cool immediately in ice bath and chill.
