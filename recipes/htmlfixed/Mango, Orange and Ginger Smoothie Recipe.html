Ingredients

  • 1 cup orange juice
  • ½ large mango
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons ginger juice (see note below)
  • 4 ice cubes
  • Optional: 1/2 ripe banana (about 2 ounces peeled banana)

Preparation

  1. Combine the orange juice, mango, ginger juice and ice cubes in a blender. Blend until frothy and smooth. If you want a thicker drink, add the banana. Serve right away for the best flavor.

Tips

  • To make the ginger juice, grate about 2 teaspoons ginger. Place on a piece of cheesecloth. Gather together the edges of the cheesecloth, hold over a bowl and twist to squeeze out the juice.
  • Advance preparation:This is best served right away.
