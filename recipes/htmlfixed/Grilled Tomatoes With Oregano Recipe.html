Low Calorie, Garlic, Herb, Tomato

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe red tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic, each cut into 4 slivers
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano or 1 tablespoon dried
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat broiler to high
  2. Core tomatoes and slice them in half
  3. Place tomatoes in a baking dish large enough to hold tomato halves cut-side up. Insert 2 slivers of garlic in each. Sprinkle each half with chopped oregano, olive oil, salt and pepper
  4. Place tomatoes under broiler for about 5 minutes
