Moya McAllister for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Clear Creek Kirschwasser
  • 1 ounce Gran Duque d'Alba Brandy de Jerez
  • ¾ ounce grapefruit juice
  • ½ ounce Lustau Pedro Ximenez sherry
  • ½ ounce Cherry Heering
  • 3 brandied cherries, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all the ingredients except the cherries with ice and strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with the cherries on a pick.
