Chardonnay, Lemon, Spice

Molly O'Neill About three and one-half cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups chardonnay
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • ½ teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced

Preparation

  1. Combine the wine, water, bay leaf, rosemary, thyme, peppercorns and lemon in the bottom of the desired steaming equipment. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 minute. Begin steaming. Add boiling water if too much of the liquid evaporates before cooking is complete.
