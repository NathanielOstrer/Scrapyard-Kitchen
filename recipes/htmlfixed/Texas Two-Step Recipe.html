Rum, Tequila

William L. Hamilton

Phil Mansfield for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of tequila
  • 2 ounces of rum
  • ¼ ounce blue Curaçao
  • ¼ ounce Cointreau
  • Splash of fresh lime juice
  • Dash of sour mix
  • Orange twist

Preparation

  1. Place all liquid ingredients and sour mix in a mixing glass with ice, shake and strain into chilled martini glass. Garnish with twist.
