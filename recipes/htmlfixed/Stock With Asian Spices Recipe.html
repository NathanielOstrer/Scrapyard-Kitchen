Black Bean, Chicken Broth, Star Anise, Asian

Mark Bittman about 4 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (about 4 cups) chicken broth
  • ¼ cup fermented black beans, rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 2 star anise

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan, and bring just about to a boil. Turn the heat a little lower, and simmer, uncovered, for about 15 minutes. Strain.

Tip

  • Use for hot and sour soup or as a broth for wontons or for Asian soup-and-noodle dishes.
