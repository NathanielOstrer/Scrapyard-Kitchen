Butter, Flour, Sugar, American

Molly O'Neill About 10 dozen tiny cookies Time1 hour 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cold water

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter until smooth. Beat in the sugar a little at a time. Beat in the salt. Add the flour in three batches, alternating with additions of 1/4 cup of water, stirring well after each addition. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Remove the chilled dough from the refrigerator and use your hands to roll it into very small balls about 1/2 to 3/4 of an inch in diameter. Transfer the cookies to ungreased cookie sheets.
  3. Bake until the bottoms of the cookies just turn golden, about 15 to 20 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.
