Ingredients

  • 1 pound Velveeta cheese
  • 3⁄4 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons sambal oelek

Preparation

  1. Cut cheese into chunks, and place in a microwave-safe bowl. Add cream and sambal oelek.
  2. Microwave on high setting for 3 minutes, then stir, and microwave again for a few minutes until the cheese is molten. Stir again to combine.
  3. Add a splash of water to thin mixture if necessary, then microwave for 20 seconds more. Serve immediately, with tortilla chips.
