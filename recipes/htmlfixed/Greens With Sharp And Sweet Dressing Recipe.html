Raw, Balsamic Vinegar, Mixed Green, Soy Sauce

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces mixed greens or mesclun or arugula
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • Pinch sugar

Preparation

  1. Wash and dry the greens.
  2. In a serving bowl whisk the oil, vinegar, soy sauce and sugar; mix in the greens.
