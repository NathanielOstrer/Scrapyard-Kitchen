Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chopped tomatoes
  • 1 peeled and diced cucumber
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 slices day-old bread
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic in a food processor or blender; process until chunky-smooth

Preparation

  1. Combine 2 pounds chopped tomatoes with 1 peeled and diced cucumber, 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 slices day-old bread and 1 tablespoon minced garlic in a food processor or blender; process until chunky-smooth.
  2. Garnish: Olive oil
