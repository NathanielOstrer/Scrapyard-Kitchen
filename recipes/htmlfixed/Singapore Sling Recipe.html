Cherry Brandy, Club Soda, Gin, Lime Juice, Sour Mix

William L. Hamilton

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces of gin
  • 1 ½ ounces of cherry brandy
  • Sour mix
  • Soda
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Maraschino cherry and orange slice

Preparation

  1. Fill a pint glass with ice. Add liquors. Fill nearly to brim with sour mix.
  2. Add two splashes of soda plus a dash of lime juice. Pour in a shaker, shake to chill. Return to glass and garnish.
