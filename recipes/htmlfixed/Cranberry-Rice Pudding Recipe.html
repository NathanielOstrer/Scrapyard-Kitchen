Dried Cranberry, Long Grain Rice

Florence Fabricant 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 6 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange peel
  • 1 cup dried cranberries
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Preparation

  1. Combine the rice, sugar and milk in a heavy saucepan. Bring to a simmer and stir in the orange peel and cranberries.
  2. Cook, covered, stirring from time to time, until the rice is very tender, about 45 minutes.
  3. Transfer to a serving bowl or six individual dessert dishes or stemmed goblets. Refrigerate.
  4. Top each serving with whipped cream if desired.
