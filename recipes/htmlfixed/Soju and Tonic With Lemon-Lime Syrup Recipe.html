Lemon, Lime, Soju, Korean

Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Grant Cornett for The New York Times. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • ½ ounce lemon-lime syrup
  • 2 ounces soju
  • Tonic water (I like Q Tonic for this, or Schweppes)
  • Lemon peel

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice. Add syrup, then soju, then tonic to fill. Give the drink a gentle stir. Garnish with lemon peel.
