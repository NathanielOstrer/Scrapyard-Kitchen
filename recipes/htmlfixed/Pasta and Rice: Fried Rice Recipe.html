Dairy Free, Gluten Free

Mark Bittman Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • coarse-chopped slab bacon
  • soy sauce
  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • neutral oil
  • cooked rice
  • chopped scallions
  • minced ginger
  • garlic

Preparation

  1. Cook coarse-chopped slab bacon in neutral oil until done; add cooked rice, chopped scallions, minced ginger and garlic; cook until rice begins to brown. Crack in 1 or 2 eggs, and continue to stir; finish with soy sauce.
