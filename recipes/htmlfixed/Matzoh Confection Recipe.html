Kosher, Cinnamon, Matzo, Sugar, Middle Eastern

Marian Burros 48 pieces TimeAbout 20 minutes

Ingredients

Sugar syrup:

  • 1 ¼ cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Few drops lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Matzohs:

  • 8 sheets plain matzoh
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • Sugar and cinnamon mixture, optional

Preparation

Sugar syrup:

  1. Simmer the water, sugar and lemon for 10 minutes; add cinnamon and cool.

Matzohs:

  1. Break each matzoh into 6 equal pieces. If matzoh crumbles, put a damp cloth on it to soften.
  2. Heat oil in pan.
  3. Beat eggs thoroughly; dip matzoh into eggs and fry in hot oil until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Dip hot matzohs in cold syrup and sprinkle, if desired, with a mixture of sugar and cinnamon.
