Gluten Free, Mortadella, Parmigiano Reggiano, Ricotta, Italian

Frank Bruni 8 to 10 servings Time5 minutes

Photo Illustration by Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces mortadella, cubed
  • 4 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 ounces Parmigiano-Reggigano
  • Â¼ cup heavy cream
  • Pinch of freshly ground nutmeg
  • Sea salt
  • black pepper

Preparation

  1. Pulse the mortadella in a food processor until finely ground. Add ricotta, Parmigiano and cream. Blend until smooth. Add nutmeg, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with thin slices of toasted ciabatta.
