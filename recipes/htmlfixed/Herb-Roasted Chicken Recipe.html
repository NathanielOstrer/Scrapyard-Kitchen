Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Kosher, Halal, Chicken, Fresh Herb

Mark Bittman TimeAbout 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Parsley
  • Dill
  • Mint
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Olive oil (or butter)

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 425. Chop 1/2 cup parsley, dill, mint or a combination (a little minced garlic wouldn't hurt).
  2. Put 4 tablespoons olive oil or butter in a roasting pan and put it in the oven for a couple of minutes until oil is hot or butter melts.
  3. Roll the chicken in the herb mixture, then add it to the pan; return the pan to the oven.
  4. Roast, turning once or twice, for 10 to 15 minutes, or until just cooked through.
  5. Garnish: More herbs.
