John Willoughby 4 to 6 side-dish servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • 5 large carrots, peeled and coarsely grated (3 1/2 to 4 cups)
  • ½ cup golden raisins
  • 1 tablespoon rose water
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped parsley
  • Salt
  • black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Toast the coriander and cumin in a dry pan over medium-high heat, shaking often to avoid burning, until just fragrant and slightly darker, about 3 minutes.
  2. Combine spices with remaining ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Refrigerate, covered, for one hour or up to 24 hours. Stir before serving.
