Vegetarian, Vegan, Red Bell Pepper

Enid Nemy 4 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet red bell peppers
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place peppers under high broiler, 3 inches from heat, turning until they blister and lightly blacken. Or hold over gas flame with long-handled fork, until blistered and lightly blackened.
  2. Place peppers in a plastic bag for 5 minutes; remove, peel and seed.
  3. Puree in food processor until smooth. Add salt and pepper
