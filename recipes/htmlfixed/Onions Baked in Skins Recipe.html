Moira Hodgson 4 servings TimeAbout 1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 medium onions
  • Coarse salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Make a tinfoil basket for each onion so that it will stand up straight. Bake the onions for one to two hours, until they are tender when pierced by a fork.
  3. Open the onions with a fork and eat with butter, salt and pepper.
