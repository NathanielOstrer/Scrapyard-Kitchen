Butter, Lemon Juice

Pierre Franey about 6 tablespoons Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter cut into small cubes
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. Combine the ingredients in a small mixing bow
  2. Let stand until room temperature is reached.
  3. Blend the ingredients well, using the fingers. Set aside.
