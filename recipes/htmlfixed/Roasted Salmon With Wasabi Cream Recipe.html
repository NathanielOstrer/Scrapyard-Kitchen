Salmon, Sour Cream, Wasabi, Japanese

Amanda Hesser Serves 4 Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 Â¾ pounds wild king salmon fillet
  • Sea salt
  • 6 teaspoons wasabi powder
  • 1 cup sour cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Sprinkle a roasting pan (large enough to fit the salmon) with olive oil. Lay the salmon in the pan skin-side down. Rub a thin slick of oil over the salmon and season with salt. Roast until just cooked through, 20 to 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, dissolve wasabi powder in 8 teaspoons water. Whisk into sour cream and season to taste with salt. Add more wasabi, if desired. Serve with the salmon.
