Vegetarian, Vegan, Cumin, Lemon Juice, Romaine, Middle Eastern

Molly O'Neill 8 - 10 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • A few grinds fresh black pepper
  • ⅔ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large heads romaine lettuce, rinsed, dried and torn into bite size pieces

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, cumin, salt and pepper. Very slowly whisk in the oil. Place the lettuce in a large salad bowl and toss with the dressing.
