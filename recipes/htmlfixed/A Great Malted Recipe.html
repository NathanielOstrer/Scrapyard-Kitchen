Raw, Flavored Syrup, Malted Milk Powder, Vanilla Ice Cream

Jonathan Reynolds 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 scoops (about 8 ounces) vanilla ice cream, slightly softened (aficionados claim the ice cream should be vanilla regardless of the flavor of the syrup)
  • ½ cup 1 percent, 2 percent or whole milk
  • ¼ cup chocolate, strawberry or any flavored syrup
  • 2 tablespoons malted-milk powder, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch nutmeg or whipped cream, optional

Preparation

  1. In a blender, pulse all the ingredients on low speed just until smooth, about 20 seconds. (Don't overblend -- the shake will become too soupy.) Pour the shake into a soda glass and top with nutmeg or whipped cream, if desired.

Tip

  • For a vanilla malted, increase the milk to 3/4 cup and omit the syrup.
