Raw, Buttermilk, Dijon Mustard, Mixed Green

Marian Burros 3 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup low-fat buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ tablespoons grated cucumber
  • ⅓ of bunch of scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 ½ teaspoons chopped parsley
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 ounces mixed greens such as Boston and red lettuce, mache or arugula

Preparation

  1. Mix or shake ingredients well to blend. Serve over greens.
