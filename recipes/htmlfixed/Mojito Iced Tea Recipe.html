Harold Mcgee About 2 quarts

Richard Perry/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 10 sprigs mint, rinsed, patted dry and lightly crushed, plus more for garnish
  • 2 limes
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons loose-leaf green oolong tea, preferably scented with osmanthus
  • 3 to 4 sprigs fresh lemon balm, optional
  • 1 peach, sliced, with pit optional; use with unscented tea
  • Sugar to taste

Preparation

  1. Place 6 cups water, mint sprigs, slices of 1 lime, tea, lemon balm and peach with pit, if using, in a large bowl or pitcher. Let rest for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight. Strain into a clean pitcher and sweeten with sugar to taste. Stir in ice and serve garnished with mint sprigs and lime slices.
