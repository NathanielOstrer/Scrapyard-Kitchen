Mark Bittman Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • ¼ cup tequila
  • 1 ½ tablespoon triple Sec
  • 1 ½ tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons crème de cassis
  • Ginger beer
  • Garnish: lemon wedge

Preparation

  1. Combine lots of ice, the tequila, triple sec and lime juice in a mixing glass and shake. Add 1 1/2 tablespoons crème de cassis. Shake and strain into a glass with ice and top with ginger beer. Garnish: lemon wedge.
