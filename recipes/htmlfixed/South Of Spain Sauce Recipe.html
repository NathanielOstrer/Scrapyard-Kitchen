Pepper, Tomato, Vidalia Onion

Barbara Kafka 2 cups Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 medium Italian frying peppers or 1 medium green bell pepper, seeded, deribbed and finely diced
  • ½ medium Vidalia or other sweet onion, finely diced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, cored and finely diced
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Pour the mixture on top of the roe that has been poached in water.
