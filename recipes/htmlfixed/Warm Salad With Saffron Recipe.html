Vegetarian, Butter, Corn, Parmesan, American

Mark Bittman Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Corn kernels
  • Chopped onion
  • Pinch of saffron

Garnish

  • Parmesan

Preparation

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet on medium-high heat.
  2. Add corn kernels along with chopped onion and a pinch of saffron.
  3. Saut√©, stirring occasionally, until each kernel is deeply browned on at least one surface.
  4. Turn off heat.
  5. Garnish: Grated Parmesan.
