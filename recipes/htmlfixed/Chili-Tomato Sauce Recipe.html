Vegetarian, Vegan, Chili Powder, Spice, Tomato Puree, White Vinegar

Marian Burros Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 1-pound can tomato puree (2 cups)
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons mild to hot chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon coriander
  • Few drops hot pepper sauce
