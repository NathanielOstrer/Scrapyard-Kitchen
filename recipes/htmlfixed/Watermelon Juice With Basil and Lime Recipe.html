Basil, Juice, Lime, Watermelon

Jeff Schwarz And Greg Kessler 2 servings

Greg Kessler

Ingredients

  • 1 small, cold watermelon, rind and seeds removed
  • 4 to 6 basil leaves
  • Juice of Â½ lime

Preparation

  1. Cut the watermelon into quarters, and then roughly chop each quarter into 2-inch pieces.
  2. Fill a blender with watermelon chunks until it is halfway full.
  3. Add the basil and lime juice to the blender.
  4. Cover and pulse on low until the ingredients are mostly incorporated. Once incorporated, blend on high for 10 to 15 seconds.
  5. Serve immediately.
