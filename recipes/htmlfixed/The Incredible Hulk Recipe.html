Cognac, Maraschino Cherry

William L. Hamilton 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce of Hennessy
  • 1 ounce of Hpnotiq
  • 3 maraschino cherries with stems

Preparation

  1. Fill glass with ice. Pour the first layer, the Hennessy.
  2. Pour the second layer, the Hpnotiq. Garnish with three cherries and a sip stick.
