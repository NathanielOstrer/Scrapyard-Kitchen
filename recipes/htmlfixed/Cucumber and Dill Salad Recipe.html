Raw, Cucumber, Sugar, White Vinegar, French

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 to 3 cucumbers, depending on size
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • ¼ cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • Freshly ground pepper, preferably white pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Peel the cucumbers and split them in half. Scoop out the seeds with a melon ball cutter. If using seedless cucumbers, it is not necessary to cut in half or to remove the seeds.
  2. Slice the cucumbers crosswise. There should be about 4 cups. Put the slices in a mixing bowl. Add the remaining ingredients and toss to blend. Add more seasonings such as vinegar, sugar and so on to taste, if desired. Serve at room temperature.
