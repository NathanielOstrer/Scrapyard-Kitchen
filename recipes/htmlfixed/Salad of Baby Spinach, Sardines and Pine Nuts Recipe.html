Gluten Free, Baby Spinach, Sardine, Italian

Florence Fabricant 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons olive oil, plus oil for baking sheet
  • ½ cup pine nuts
  • 8 whole fresh sardines, gutted but not boned
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Sea salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 6 cups baby spinach, rinsed and dried

Preparation

  1. Heat broiler. Brush a baking sheet with a little of the oil. Put 2 tablespoons oil in a small sauté pan, add pine nuts. Cook on low, stirring frequently, until lightly browned. Set aside.
  2. Rinse and dry sardines, place on the baking sheet and dribble with 2 tablespoons oil and half the lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper. Broil sardines until lightly browned, turning once, about 2 minutes per side. Remove from heat.
  3. Place spinach in a shallow serving bowl, add remaining 2 tablespoons oil, remaining lemon juice, the pine nuts and salt and pepper to taste. Toss. Arrange sardines on top and serve.
