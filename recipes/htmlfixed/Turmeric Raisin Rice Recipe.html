Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Raisin, Rice, Turmeric

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1 cup converted rice
  • ⅓ cup raisins
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme or 1/2 teaspoon dried
  • 4 drop Tabasco sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • Salt and ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Melt 1 tablespoon of the butter in a saucepan and add the onion and garlic. Cook, stirring, until wilted. Add the rice, raisins, turmeric, thyme, Tabasco, bay leaf, water, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, stirring. Cover the pot tightly and simmer for 17 minutes.
  2. Discard the thyme sprigs and bay leaf. Distribute the remaining butter through the rice. Keep the rice covered in a warm place until it is served.
