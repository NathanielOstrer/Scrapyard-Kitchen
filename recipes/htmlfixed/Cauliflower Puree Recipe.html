Vegetarian, Cauliflower

Marian Burros 10 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 medium heads cauliflower
  • 12 tablespoons unsalted butter, or 1 1/2 sticks
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Nutmeg to taste
  • Mild Hungarian paprika to taste

Preparation

  1. Break cauliflower into florets and steam or cook in boiling water until barely tender. Drain and run under cold water to stop cooking.
  2. Place in a food processor and, using steel blade, process together with butter to a puree.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg and paprika. Refrigerate if desired.
  4. To serve, heat thoroughly, thinning with milk if necessary.
