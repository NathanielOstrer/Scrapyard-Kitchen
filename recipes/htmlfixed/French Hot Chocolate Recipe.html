Bittersweet Chocolate, Semisweet Chocolate

Molly O'Neill Four servings

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 ounces bittersweet (not unsweetened) chocolate, chopped
  • 4 cups milk

Preparation

  1. Combine the chocolates and milk in a large saucepan and bring just to a boil, stirring occasionally. Lower the heat and cook the mixture at a slow simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat and whisk thoroughly. Strain through a fine sieve. Ladle into 4 mugs and serve immediately.
