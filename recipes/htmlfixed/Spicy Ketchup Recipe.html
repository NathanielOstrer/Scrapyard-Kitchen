Vegetarian, Vegan, Pepper, Raisin, Tomato Puree, Middle Eastern

Molly O'Neill 1 cup Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup golden raisins
  • 1 ½ cups tomato puree
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¾ teaspoon Aleppo pepper (see note) or cayenne pepper to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Place the raisins in a small cup, cover with boiling water and let soak for 20 minutes. Drain the raisins and place them in the container of a blender. Add all the remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a small saucepan and simmer until thickened, about 20 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature with ijeh.
