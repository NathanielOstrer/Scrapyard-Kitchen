Gin, Orange Juice, Tonic Water

William L. Hamilton Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Bombay gin
  • ¼ ounce orange juice
  • ½ ounce fresh lime juice
  • 3 ounces club soda
  • 3 ounces tonic
  • dash Grenadine
  • 1 fresh mint leaf for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake liquid ingredients in a shaker, and pour into a highball glass over ice.
  2. Float the mint leaf.
