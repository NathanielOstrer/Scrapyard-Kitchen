  • 3 tablespoons sugar, approximately, or more to taste

Preparation

  1. Put berries and sugar in blender or food processor, and purée. Add sugar if needed.
  2. Press coulis through a strainer to eliminate seeds. Use immediately or refrigerate for up to a day.
