Chili Powder, Cumin, Pumpkin, Southwestern

Florence Fabricant 6 servings Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons pure chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • 1 3-pound pumpkin, peeled, seeded, cored and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 tablespoon toasted pine nuts

Preparation

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan. Saute the onions until translucent, add the garlic and saute until the vegetables are golden.
  2. Stir in the chili powder, cumin and cinnamon. Cook for a couple of minutes, then add the pumpkin and the lime juice. Toss well with other ingredients in the pan, cover and cook over low heat until the pumpkin is not quite tender, about 15 minutes.
  3. Add the cream, uncover and continue cooking until the cream has thickened and the pumpkin is tender, another five to eight minutes.
  4. Season to taste with salt and pepper, fold in the coriander, transfer to a serving dish and sprinkle with pine nuts.
