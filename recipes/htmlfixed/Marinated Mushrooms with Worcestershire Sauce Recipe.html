Grapeseed Oil, Mushroom, Onion, Red Wine Vinegar, Sugar, Worcestershire Sauce

Marian Burros 16 to 20 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds small mushrooms
  • 1 cup grape seed oil
  • ½ cup red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced

Preparation

  1. Wash and stem mushrooms. Set aside stems for other use.
  2. Combine other ingredients thoroughly and pour over mushrooms. Refrigerate and marinate for two days.
