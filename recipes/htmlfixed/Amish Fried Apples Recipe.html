Nut Free, Apple, American

Marcia Adams Four to six servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 tablespoons sweet butter
  • 6 cups sliced, unpeeled Jonathan apples, no substitutes
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon grated lemon rind
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter in a large heavy skillet. Add the rest of the ingredients and cook over medium-low heat, uncovered, for 10 minutes. Turn the apples carefully with a spatula as they cook. Apples will be tender and still hold their shape.
