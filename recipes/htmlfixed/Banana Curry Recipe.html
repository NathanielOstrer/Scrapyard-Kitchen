Banana, Curry

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 small red onion to yield 1/4 cup finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 large, slightly underripe bananas
  • ¼ cup no-salt-added chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons currants

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in nonstick pan until it is medium hot. Saute onion until it begins to brown; stir in curry, and cook 30 seconds more.
  2. Slice the bananas about 1/4 inch thick.
  3. Add the bananas, stock and currants to onion. Cook a couple of minutes longer, until bananas begin to soften.
