Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Espresso, Heavy Cream

Molly O'Neill Four to six servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups espresso
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Sambuca or anisette
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

Preparation

  1. Combine the espresso, sugar and Sambuca. Refrigerate until chilled. Pour the espresso mixture into a large shallow pan and place in the freezer. Stir every 20 minutes until there is no liquid in bottom of the pan, about 1 hour. Spoon the granite into four chilled dessert glasses. Top with a dollop of whipped cream and serve.
