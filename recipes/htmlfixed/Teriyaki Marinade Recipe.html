Soy Sauce, Steak

Marian Burros 1 cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 piece ginger, minced
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 6 4-ounce pieces of steak
  • ½ cup water

Preparation

  1. Combine garlic, ginger, sugar and soy sauce with 1/2 cup water. Brush on steaks, and marinate overnight in refrigerator.
