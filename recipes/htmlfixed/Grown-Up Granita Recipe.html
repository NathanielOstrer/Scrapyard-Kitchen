Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, Dry Prosecco, Lemon Sorbet

Mark Bittman 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pint lemon sorbet
  • ¼ to ½ cup gin
  • ½ cup dry Prosecco
  • 6 to 8 fresh mint leaves for garnish

Preparation

  1. Put the sorbet and gin in a food processor or blender and blend until well combined but still slushy. Pour into champagne flutes, top with Prosecco, garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.
