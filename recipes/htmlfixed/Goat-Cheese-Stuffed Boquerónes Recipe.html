Anchovy, Goat Cheese, Shallot

Melissa Clark 12 amuse-bouches Time10 minutes

John Lei for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces fresh mild goat cheese
  • 1 tablespoon minced shallots
  • 1 teaspoon sherry vinegar
  • 12 boquerónes (white anchovies)

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, combine goat cheese, shallots and vinegar. Form into 12 balls. Wrap each with a boquerón. Serve.
