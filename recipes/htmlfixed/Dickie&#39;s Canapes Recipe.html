Vegetarian, Baguette, Cheese

William Norwich 8 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • ½ cup Swiss cheese, grated
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 12 to 16 baguette pieces, thinly sliced and lightly toasted

Preparation

  1. Mix the cheese and butter into a paste. Spread on the baguettes. Place on a pan in a 400-degree oven until sizzling and brown on top.
