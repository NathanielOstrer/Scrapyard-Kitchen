Heavy Cream, Mushroom, French

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound medium-size mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped shallots
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Rinse mushrooms if desired and pat dry.
  2. Heat butter in a heavy skillet and add shallots and mushrooms. Sprinkle with lemon juice, salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, without browning about 5 minutes.
  3. Add cream and nutmeg and cook over relatively high heat, stirring often, about 2 minutes or until sauce has thickened slightly.
