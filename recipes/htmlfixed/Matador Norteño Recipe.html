Agave Syrup, Habanero Bitters, Pineapple Juice, Sotol, Mexican

Florence Fabricant 1 drink

Karsten Moran for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • A few cilantro leaves
  • 2 ounces sotol
  • ¾ ounce pineapple juice
  • ¾ ounce lemon juice
  • ½ ounce agave syrup
  • 6 dashes habanero bitters

Preparation

  1. Muddle cilantro in a cocktail shaker. Add remaining ingredients.
  2. Add ice, shake briefly and strain into a glass. Pour back into the shaker, strain again into a stemmed cocktail glass and serve.
