Butternut Squash, Chicken Broth, Milk, Orange, Rosemary, Tomato

Molly O'Neill Six appetizers Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes, stem ends removed
  • 4 cups roasted-squash compote
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Roast tomatoes for 25 to 30 minutes, until they begin to collapse. Combine compote, broth and milk and bring to a boil in a medium saucepan. Lower the heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Pass through the fine disk of a food mill. Season with salt.
