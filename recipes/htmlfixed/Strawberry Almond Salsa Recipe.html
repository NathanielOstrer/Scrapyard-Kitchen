Amaretto, Mint Leaves, Serrano Pepper, Sliced Almond, Strawberry

Bryan Miller 6 to 8 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup toasted sliced almonds
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and rinsed
  • ½ teaspoon chopped serrano or jalapeno chilies
  • 2 teaspoons chopped mint leaves
  • ¼ cup Amaretto
  • ¼ cup sugar

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in bowl of a food processor or blender. Puree until smooth but slightly rough-textured
