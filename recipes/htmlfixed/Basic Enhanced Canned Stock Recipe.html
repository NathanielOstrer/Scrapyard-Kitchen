Carrot, Chicken Broth, Onion

Mark Bittman about 4 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (about 4 cups) chicken broth
  • 1 carrot, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1 medium onion, unpeeled, roughly chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, unpeeled
  • 3 peppercorns
  • Several sprigs fresh parsley, if you have them
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients except salt and ground pepper in a medium saucepan, and bring just about to a boil. Turn heat a little lower, and simmer, uncovered, for 10 to 20 minutes. Strain, taste and adjust seasonings.
