Butter, Carrot, Cumin

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 18 baby carrots, about 3/4 pound
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. Peel the carrots, and trim off the ends.
  2. Put the carrots in a saucepan, and add water to cover and salt. Bring to a boil, and cook until tender, about 12 minutes.
  3. Drain the carrots. Add the butter and cumin; toss to coat the carrots. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.
