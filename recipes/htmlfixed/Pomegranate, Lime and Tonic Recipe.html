Angostura Bitters, Pomegranate Juice

Melissa Clark 4 cocktails Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces pomegranate or cranberry juice
  • 4 wedges lime
  • 4 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Tonic water, for serving

Preparation

  1. Pour an ounce of pomegranate or cranberry juice in each of 4 highball glasses. Squeeze in the juice from each lime wedge, then drop wedges in glasses. Top with a dash of bitters, add ice, then fill each glass with tonic water to taste (about 6 to 8 ounces a glass). Stir before serving.
