Dried Apricot, Orange Juice, Plum, Jewish

Joan Nathan Serves 6 to 8 Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried plums
  • 8 ounces dried apricots
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cups orange juice

Preparation

  1. Place dried plums, apricots, lemon and cinnamon stick in a pot with 3 cups of orange juice or to cover fruit.
  2. Bring to boil over high heat, turn heat to low and simmer, uncovered, about 15 minutes.
