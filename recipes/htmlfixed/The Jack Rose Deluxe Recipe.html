Applejack, Grenadine, Lemon Juice

Rosie Schaap

Davide Luciano for The New York Times. Food stylist: Claudia Ficca. Prop stylist: Micah Morton.

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces applejack
  • 1 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • ½ ounce grenadine
  • Lemon twist for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine applejack, lemon juice and grenadine in a shaker filled with ice (preferably cracked ice). Shake vigorously, and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with lemon peel.
