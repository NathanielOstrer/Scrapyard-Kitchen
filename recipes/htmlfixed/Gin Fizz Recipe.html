Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar, preferably superfine
  • 2 ounces London Dry-style gin
  • Club soda, to top
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add lemon juice, sugar and gin. Shake vigorously and strain into a highball glass. Top with club soda and garnish with a wedge of lemon.
