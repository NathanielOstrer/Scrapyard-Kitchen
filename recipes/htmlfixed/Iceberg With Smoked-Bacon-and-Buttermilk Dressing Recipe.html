Raw, Low Carb, Bacon, Iceberg Lettuce, Tomato

Julia Reed 8 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 heads iceberg lettuce
  • 8 ripe Roma tomatoes, sliced thin
  • 2 cups buttermilk herb dressing (see recipe)
  • 12 slices smoked bacon, diced into 1/2-inch pieces (about 2 cups) and fried

Preparation

  1. Trim the loose outer leaves from the heads of iceberg, and cut each into 4 wedges and place in the freezer for 10 minutes before serving, along with 4 serving plates.
  2. Remove the chilled plates and arrange each with a circle of sliced tomatoes. Set a wedge in the center of each plate. Nap with 1/4 cup dressing.
  3. Sprinkle bacon over each wedge. Serve immediately.
