Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Fresh Herb

Sam Sifton Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch fresh thyme
  • ½ bunch fresh rosemary
  • 1 bunch fresh sage
  • 5 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 2 ½ cups extra-virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Put herbs, garlic and salt into a food processor. Add a splash of olive oil, and pulse to combine. Place mixture into a jar.
  2. Add the rest of the olive oil. Store in the refrigerator. Lasts about a week.
