Rice, Wild Rice

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About three and one-half to four cups TimeAbout 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wild rice
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste, if desired

Preparation

  1. Combine the wild rice, water and salt in a heavy saucepan. Bring to the boil. Cover closely and cook over gentle heat about 45 minutes.
  2. Spoon and scrape the rice into a mixing bowl and let cool.
