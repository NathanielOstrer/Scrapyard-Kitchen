Beer, Steak, Latin American

Bryan Miller 4 servings TimeAbout 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces beer
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon powdered cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ green pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 pounds lean flank steak

Preparation

  1. In a large pot, combine beer, onion, cumin, salt, cayenne pepper and green pepper. Add the water, and bring to a boil. Add the meat, lower heat, cover and braise the meat until it is very soft and tender - about 1 1/2 to 2 hours, depending on thickness.
  2. Drain the meat well, and grill it over a charcoal fire or under a broiler just to heat it and impart a crispy texture. Serve with aji pique on the side.
