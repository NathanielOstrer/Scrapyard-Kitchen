Gluten Free, Nut Free, Confectioners' Sugar, Fromage Blanc, Vanilla, French

Christine Muhlke Serves 4 Time20 minutes, plus freeze time

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • Pinch powdered vanilla or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fromage blanc

Preparation

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine 5 tablespoons water, the sugar and vanilla (if using extract, add after cooking) and cook over medium-high heat until syrupy. Let cool completely.
  2. Put the fromage blanc in a bowl and add the chilled syrup. Mix well. Churn in an ice-cream maker following the manufacturer's instructions.
