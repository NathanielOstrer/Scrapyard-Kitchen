Gluten Free, Apple, Walnut, Jewish

Horacio Silva 1 1/4 cups TimeAbout 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 apple, quartered and cored
  • ¼ cup pecans or walnuts
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • Dash of nutmeg
  • Dash of ginger
  • Honey to taste
  • Red wine

Preparation

  1. Combine the apples, nuts, spices, honey and wine in food processor until mixture resembles a mortar.
