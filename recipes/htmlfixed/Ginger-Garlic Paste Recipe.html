Garlic, Ginger, Indian

Jonathan Reynolds About 6 tablespoons Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 13 1/2-inch piece peeled ginger root, thinly sliced
  • 8 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon canola or vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients and 2 tablespoons water in a blender. Blend until smooth. Add more water if necessary. Will keep up to one week refrigerated.
