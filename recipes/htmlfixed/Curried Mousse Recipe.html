Beef Broth, Cream Cheese, Southern

Molly O'Neill 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cream cheese
  • 2 cups canned beef consomme
  • ¾ teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 3 ounces caviar

Preparation

  1. Put the cream cheese, beef broth, curry powder and garlic in a blender and puree until completely smooth. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. Using a rubber scraper, divide the mixture among 6 small ramekins or other individual cups and refrigerate them for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Remove the cups from the refrigerator 1 hour before serving. Top each serving of mousse with a spoonful of caviar and serve with crackers.
