Dairy Free, Gluten Free

Mark Bittman Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • dried beans
  • rosemary sprigs
  • slab bacon
  • water

Preparation

  1. Put dried beans, rosemary sprigs and a chunk of slab bacon in a pot with water to cover by an inch or so. Bring pot to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the beans are done. Garnish: More rosemary sprigs.
