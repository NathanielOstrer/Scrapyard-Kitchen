Johnny Miller for The New York Times; Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces grapefruit juice
  • 2 ounces arak
  • Seltzer, to taste
  • Grapefruit slice
  • Basil sprigs.

Preparation

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice, and add the grapefruit juice and arak, and top with seltzer.
  2. Garnish with a thick grapefruit twist and a few sprigs of basil.
