Bitters, Citrus, Rum

Rosie Schaap

Aya Brackett for The New York Times. Food stylist: Kayoko Akabori

Ingredients

  • 1 slice each of lime
  • 1 slice lemon
  • 1 slice orange
  • 3 dashes Hella Bitter citrus bitters
  • 2 ounce light rum
  • ½ ounce Campari
  • Enough sparkling Moscato to "float."

Preparation

  1. In a mixing glass, muddle the fruits.
  2. Fill with ice, add the other ingredients and shake.
  3. Strain into a highball over ice.
  4. Float the Moscato on top. Garnish with a lemon twist.
