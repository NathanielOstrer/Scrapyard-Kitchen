Vegetarian, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Dill, Pea, Sour Cream

Jonathan Reynolds 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh shelled peas
  • ⅔ cup sour cream
  • ¾ cup chopped fresh dill, chives or a combination of both
  • 2 tablespoons sliced mint leaves
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Boil peas in salted water until very tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain and immediately plunge peas into ice water until cool. Drain again.
  2. Mix the remaining ingredients with the peas and refrigerate for one hour before serving.
