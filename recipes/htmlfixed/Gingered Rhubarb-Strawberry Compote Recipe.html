Vegetarian, Rhubarb, Strawberry, Sugar, American

Marian Burros 4 cups Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds rhubarb
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Grated rind of one orange
  • 1 ½ pounds strawberries
  • 3 tablespoons chopped crystallized ginger

Preparation

  1. Trim rhubarb, removing any peel that comes off easily. Cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces. Combine with sugar and orange rind and bring slowly to a boil, covered, in a non-aluminum saucepan. Simmer gently for 15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile wash and stem strawberries and if they are large, halve them lengthwise. Add to rhubarb with the ginger and continue cooking for about 5 minutes, until berries are soft but hold their shape.
  3. Cool and, if there is time, chill.
