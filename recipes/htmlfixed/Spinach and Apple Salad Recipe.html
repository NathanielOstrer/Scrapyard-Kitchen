Low Calorie, Apple, Spinach

Marian Burros 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces fresh spinach
  • 1 small tart apple
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons white wine vinegar
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash spinach and remove tough stems. Break spinach into bite-size pieces.
  2. Wash apple and cut into thin strips, leaving peel on.
  3. In a serving bowl, whisk the oil, lemon juice and vinegar; add the spinach and apple, and mix thoroughly to coat well. Season with pepper.
