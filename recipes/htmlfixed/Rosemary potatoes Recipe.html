Potato, Rosemary

Bryan Miller 10 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 11 Idaho potatoes
  • ½ cup clarified sweet butter
  • ½ teaspoon crushed dried rosemary or one-quarter teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Cut 22 two-and-one-half-inch olive shapes from the potatoes. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Place potatoes and clarified butter in a pan and roast for 15 minutes. Sprinkle with the rosemary and return them to the oven for an additional five minutes, or until done.
  3. Toss lightly with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
