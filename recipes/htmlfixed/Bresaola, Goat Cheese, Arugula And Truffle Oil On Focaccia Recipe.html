Arugula, Black Truffle Oil, Bresaola, Focaccia Roll, Goat Cheese, Italian

Eric Asimov 1 sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sandwich-size fresh focaccia, split
  • 2 ounces goat cheese, like Italian Caprini
  • 4 ounces top-quality bresaola (air-cured beef)
  • 6 leaves arugula
  • 4 drops Italian black truffle oil

Preparation

  1. Spread half focaccia with goat cheese. Top with bresaola, arugula and truffle oil. Cover with the other half. Serve immediately.
