Rye Whiskey, Sweet Vermouth

Robert Simonson One drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rye whiskey
  • ½ ounce Punt e Mes or other sweet vermouth
  • ¼ to ½ ounce maraschino liqueur

Preparation

  1. Pour ingredients into a shaker with a generous handful of ice. Stir briskly until well chilled. Strain into a cocktail glass and serve.
