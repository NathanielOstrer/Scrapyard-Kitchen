Johnny Miller for The New York Times; Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • Pastis (Ricard, for example)
  • 1 pitcher very cold water

Preparation

Per serving

  1. Per serving: Pour 1 ounce of pastis and 4 to 5 ounces of water into a highball glass.
  2. Add ice to fill, and stir.
