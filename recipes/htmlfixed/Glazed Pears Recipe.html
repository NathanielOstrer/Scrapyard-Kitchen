Butter, Pear

Moira Hodgson 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds seckel or Bosc pears
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 3 tablespoons clarified butter
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Peel, core and slice the pears a quarter of an inch thick. Sprinkle with sugar and lemon juice.
  2. Melt the butter in a large, heavy frying pan. Saute the pears in a single layer a few at a time until golden. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
