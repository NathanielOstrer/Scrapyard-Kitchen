Grand Marnier, Raspberry

Moira Hodgson Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh raspberries (or two 12-ounce packages of frozen raspberries)
  • ¼ to ⅓ cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons Grand Marnier

Preparation

  1. Press the raspberries through a sieve, and mix the puree with the sugar and Grand Marnier until the sugar is dissolved. Taste for sweetness and adjust. Chill until ready to use.
