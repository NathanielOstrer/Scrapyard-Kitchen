Cream Of Coconut, Pineapple Juice, Rum

Rosie Schaap

William Brinson for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Susan Brinson.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces golden rum
  • 4 ounces pineapple juice
  • 4 ounces Coco Lopez cream of coconut
  • 3 cups ice

Preparation

  1. Blend ingredients until smooth and frosty.
  2. Pour into tall, chilled glasses and garnish with pineapple slices, maraschino cherries (and paper umbrellas, if you’ve got ’em).
