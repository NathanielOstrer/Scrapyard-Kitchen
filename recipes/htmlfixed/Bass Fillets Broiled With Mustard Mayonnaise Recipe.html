Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Bass, Dijon Mustard

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • Pinch of cayenne
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 4 bass fillets (about 4 ounces each), all bones removed
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Preparation

  1. Preheat broiler. Stir the mayonnaise and mustard together until well combined. Season with the cayenne and salt. Coat the flesh side of the fillets with the mayonnaise mixture and place on a baking sheet, skin side down. Broil until the fish is just cooked through and the top browns, about 5 minutes. Place 1 fillet on each of 4 plates and serve immediately with lemon wedges.

Tip

  • Sea bass or black bass can be substituted in this and any of the following recipes.
