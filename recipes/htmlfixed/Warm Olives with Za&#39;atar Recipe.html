Olive Oil, Olives, Za'atar, Middle Eastern

Julia Moskin 8 to 12 servings Time5 minutes

Jim Scherer for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • ¾ pound niçoise or other brine-cured black olives with pits, drained
  • 2 heaping tablespoons za'atar, preferably Jordanian (sold at Middle Eastern markets)

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in pan over low heat. Add olives and stir gently until warm. Stir in za'atar, transfer to bowl and serve with crusty bread.
