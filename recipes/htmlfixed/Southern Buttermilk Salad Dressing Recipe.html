Raw, Buttermilk, Dijon Mustard, Mayonnaise, Southern

Mark Bittman And Sam Sifton 8 servings TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 scallions, white and green parts minced
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Kosher salt and black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine everything but the buttermilk and seasoning and mix well. Whisk in the buttermilk until the dressing is smooth. Season to taste.
