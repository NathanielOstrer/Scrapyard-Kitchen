Benedictine, Rye

Jonathan Miles 1 serving

Yana Paskova for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¾ ounce Bénédictine
  • ¾ ounce yellow Chartreuse
  • ¾ ounce freshly-squeezed lemon juice
  • ¾ ounce Rittenhouse 100-proof rye whiskey

Preparation

  1. Mix the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice, then strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a lemon twist.
