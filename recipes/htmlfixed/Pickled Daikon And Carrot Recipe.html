Raw, Carrot, Daikon, Sugar, Korean

Molly O'Neill 1 1/2 cups Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 medium (8-ounce) daikon, peeled
  • ½ carrot, peeled
  • 2 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt

Preparation

  1. Using the slicing disk of a food processor or a very sharp knife, slice the daikon into paper-thin disks. Use a knife to cut the disks into long, very thin strips. Slice the carrot lengthwise into very thin slices, and cut each slice into very thin strips.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the vinegar, sugar and salt and mix until the sugar has dissolved. Add the daikon and carrot and toss. Serve at room temperature along with barbecued meat.
