Vegetarian, Vegan, Long Grain Rice, Mushroom

Marian Burros 2 servings TimeAbout 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup long-grain rice
  • ¼ pound white mushrooms, portobellos or creminis
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ cup frozen peas

Preparation

  1. Combine the rice with 1 1/2 cups water in a heavy-bottomed pot, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer, cooking a total of 17 minutes, until water is absorbed and rice is tender.
  2. Meanwhile, wash, trim and slice mushrooms. Heat oil in a nonstick pan, and saute the mushrooms until they begin to give off liquid.
  3. Two minutes before rice is cooked, stir in the mushrooms and peas and finish cooking.
