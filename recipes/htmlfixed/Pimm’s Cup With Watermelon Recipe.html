Ginger Ale, Pimm's No. 1, Watermelon, British

Robert Simonson 1 drink

Ingredients

  • Medium-size watermelon
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 ounces Pimm’s No. 1
  • 1 ounce lemon juice
  • 2 ounces ginger ale or 7Up
  • Sliced cucumber, berries of any type and mint sprig for garnish

Preparation

  1. Make an infused syrup: Purée and strain enough watermelon to equal one cup of juice. In a small pan, heat the sugar and watermelon juice and boil until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat. (Mixture keeps in the refrigerator for weeks).
  2. In a cocktail shaker with ice, add Pimm’s, lemon juice and 1 ounce syrup-fruit mixture and shake. Strain into an ice-filled Collins glass. Top with ginger ale or 7Up and garnish generously with cucumber, berries and mint.
