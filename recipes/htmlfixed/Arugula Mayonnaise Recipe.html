Arugula, Egg Yolk, Oil

Amanda Hesser about 1 1/4 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • Sea salt
  • ¾ cup vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 ½ cups tightly packed arugula

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together egg yolk, mustard, vinegar and large pinch of salt. Beginning a drop at a time, whisk in oils. As it emulsifies, add oil a little faster in a slow, steady stream. When mixture is thick, taste, and adjust seasoning. It should be highly seasoned.
  2. Transfer mixture to a blender or food processor, add arugula, and puree. (Add a tablespoon of water, if necessary, to get arugula flowing.) Taste, and adjust seasoning before serving.
