Butter, Chocolate Wafer

Marian Burros 16 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups chocolate wafer crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Preparation

  1. Crush cookies to make crumbs. Use rolling pin, blender or food processor.
  2. Mix with melted butter and pat into bottom and sides of 10-inch spring form. Bake at 325 degrees eight to 10 minutes. Cool and fill.
