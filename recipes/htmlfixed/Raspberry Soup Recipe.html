Raw, Gluten Free, Dry Red Wine, Raspberry, Sour Cream

Florence Fabricant 6 servings Time10 minutes, plus chill time

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup sour cream
  • ¾ cup dry red wine
  • 1 ½ cups cold water

Preparation

  1. Puree raspberries through a sieve or a food mill.
  2. Blend sugar and sour cream together and stir into raspberry puree. Stir in wine and water.
  3. Chill thoroughly before serving.
