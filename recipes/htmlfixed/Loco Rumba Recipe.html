Loco Mango, Rum

Michael Caruso 1 drink Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces of light rum
  • 3 ½ ounces of Loco Mango
  • Splash of grenadine
  • Lime or lemon wedges, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Pour rum over ice in a wineglass. Add Loco Mango; 3 1/2 ounces is about half the bottle. Add grenadine for color. Garnish with wedges of lime or lemon or both. Serve with mariachi music.
