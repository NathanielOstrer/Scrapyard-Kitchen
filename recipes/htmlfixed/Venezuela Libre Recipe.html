Angostura Bitters, Fernet Branca, Rum

Rosie Schaap

William Brinson for The New York Times; Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Maeve Sheridan.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Santa Teresa ‘‘1796’’ Solera Rum
  • ½ ounce Fernet Branca
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters

Preparation

  1. To a highball glass, add the rum, Fernet Branca and bitters; add ice, then fill the balance with Mexican cane cola. Garnish with lime wedge.
