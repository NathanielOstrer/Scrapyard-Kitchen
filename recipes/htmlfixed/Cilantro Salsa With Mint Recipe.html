Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Cholesterol, Basil, Cilantro, Lime

Karen Baar 2/3 cup (6 servings)

Ingredients

  • 1 large bunch cilantro, stems removed
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded
  • ½ cup basil leaves
  • ¼ cup mint leaves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ cup plus 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 or 2 limes, to taste
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Finely chop cilantro, jalapeno, basil, mint and garlic. Mix.
  2. Stir in oil, lime juice, salt and 1/4 cup water. Adjust salt and lime juice to taste. Use as a sauce for grilled fish or vegetables, or on tomatoes.
