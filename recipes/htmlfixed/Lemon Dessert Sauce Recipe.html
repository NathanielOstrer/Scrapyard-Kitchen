Vegetarian, Blueberry, Lemon, Sugar

Craig Claiborne 1 1/4 cups Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon rind
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg, optional
  • ½ cup blueberries

Preparation

  1. Combine sugar and cornstarch in small saucepan.
  2. Over low heat gradually add boiling water, stirring rapidly. Let simmer about 4 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in butter, lemon rind and lemon juice, nutmeg and blueberries. Serve hot, warm or chilled.
