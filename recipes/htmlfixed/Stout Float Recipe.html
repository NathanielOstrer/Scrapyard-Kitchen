Stout

Rosie Schaap

Grant Cornett for The New York Times. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Rebecca Bartoshesky.

Ingredients

  • ½ oz. coffee liqueur (preferably Fair Café)
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 6 oz. stout (I like Young’s Double Chocolate)
  • Unsweetened whipped cream flavored with vanilla seeds
  • Dark chocolate for grating

Preparation

  1. To a tall, soda-fountain-style glass (or an imperial pint glass), add the coffee liqueur and one scoop of the ice cream. Slowly add the stout and the second scoop of ice cream. Top with as much freshly whipped cream and grated dark chocolate as you like.
