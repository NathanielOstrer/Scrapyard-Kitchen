Vegetarian, Vegan, Baguette, Mushroom, Olive Oil, Italian

Trish Hall 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups mushrooms, any kind, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme
  • 8 slices, whole-wheat baguette, toasted

Preparation

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet on high heat, add mushrooms, and cook until tender. Add thyme.
  2. Put a few mushrooms on each piece of toast, and serve.
