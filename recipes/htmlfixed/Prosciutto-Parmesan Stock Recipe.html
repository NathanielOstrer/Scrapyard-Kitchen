Parmesan, Prosciutto, Italian

Mark Bittman About 6 cups of stock Time15 minutes

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Maeve Sheridan.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces sliced prosciutto (or some prosciutto rinds or ends)
  • 3 pieces Parmesan rind
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • A rosemary sprig

Preparation

  1. Combine some prosciutto rinds or ends (or 4 ounces sliced prosciutto), a few pieces of Parmesan rind, 2 crushed garlic cloves, a rosemary sprig and 6 cups water.
  2. Bring to a boil, simmer, then strain.
