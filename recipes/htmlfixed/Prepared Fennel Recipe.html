Chicken Stock, Fennel, German

Marian Burros 2 bulbs of fennel for use in another recipe. Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 bulbs fennel
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cloves garlic, mashed
  • 1 branch fresh thyme
  • 1 ½ to 2 cups chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Trim the fennel. Quarter the bulbs, core and cut them into quarter-inch slices.
  2. Place the fennel in a deep saute pan or pot and add the remaining ingredients, using enough stock to cover. Bring to boil; lower heat and simmer for five minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and allow to cool in the liquid. Drain and reserve the fennel.
