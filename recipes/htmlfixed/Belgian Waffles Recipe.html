Egg White, Flour, Belgian

Dorie Greenspan 4 waffles Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 9 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • ⅔ cup milk, warmed
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large egg whites
  • Powdered sugar
  • Ice cream (optional)

Preparation

  1. Heat waffle iron. Whisk butter and milk together. Sift flour into mixture, and whisk until smooth; stir in sugar, salt and vanilla.
  2. Beat egg whites until they hold firm, glossy peaks. Fold batter gently into whites. Batter will be very thin.
  3. Pour batter onto grids. Bake 2 minutes; turn, and bake until golden. Serve dusted with sugar and ice cream, if desired.
