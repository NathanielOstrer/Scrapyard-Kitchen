Salmon

Bryan Miller

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless salmon fillets
  • ½ cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 cup thinly sliced Bermuda onion
  • 2 tablespoons white-wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon hot red pepper flakes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Lettuce for garnish

Preparation

  1. Slice salmon thinly ( 1/4 inch or less) widthwise, leaving strips about 2 inches long. Place in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add lime juice, onion, vinegar, oil, coriander, ginger, hot pepper, salt and pepper. Stir gently, and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours. Taste for seasoning before serving (it may need more salt; if so, blend it in thoroughly).
  3. Line small serving plates with lettuce of your choice, and place a serving of salmon over the lettuce.
