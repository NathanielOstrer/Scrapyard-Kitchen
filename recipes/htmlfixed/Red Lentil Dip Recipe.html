Vegetarian, Vegan, Red Lentil

Molly O'Neill Four cups TimeAbout 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried red lentils
  • 6 cups water
  • 16 cloves roasted garlic, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons minced parsley

Preparation

  1. Place lentils and water in a large saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the lentils are very soft, about 45 minutes. Drain through a fine-mesh sieve until mixture is thick but not dry.
  2. Place the lentils in a food processor. Process until smooth. Add the remaining ingredients except the parsley. Process until smooth. Serve with crackers, and garnish with parsley.
