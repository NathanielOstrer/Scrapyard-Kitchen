Butter, Fig

Patricia Wells Four to six servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 16 fresh figs
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Preparation

  1. Wash figs, blanch in boiling water for 30 seconds, rinse with cold water and carefully peel. Ten minutes before serving, saute gently in butter until heated through.
  2. To serve, arrange several figs on each plate, surrounding the sauced su-premes.
