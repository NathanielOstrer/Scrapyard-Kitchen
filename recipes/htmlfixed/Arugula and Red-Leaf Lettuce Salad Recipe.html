Raw, Arugula, Parsley, Red Leaf Lettuce

Pierre Franey 4 to 6 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ pound arugula
  • ½ pound red-leaf lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon-style mustard
  • 1 tablespoon red-wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 4 tablespoons olive, corn or vegetable oil
  • 4 tablespoons finely chopped parsley or chervil

Preparation

  1. Pick over the arugula and lettuce and wash in cold water. Rinse and pat dry.
  2. In a salad bowl, put the mustard, vinegar, garlic, salt, pepper and cumin. Beat gradually with a whisk while adding the oil. Add the lettuce and arugula, and toss well to blend.
