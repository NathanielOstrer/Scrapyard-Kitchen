Basil, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Italian

Bryan Miller

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1 ¼ cups virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Bring a medium saucepan full of lightly salted water to a boil. Add basil. Blanch 20 to 30 seconds. Drain and squeeze dry.
  2. Put basil in a blender with 1/4 cup olive oil. Blend. With motor on high, gradually add remaining cup olive oil. Blend until smooth. Pour mixture into a glass jar or bottle and leave to steep for one day. Store in refrigerator.
