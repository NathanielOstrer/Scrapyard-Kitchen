Vegetarian, Bread, Cheese

Barbara Gamarekian Twelve servings Time2 hours 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, softened
  • 14 slices good-quality white bread
  • 4 ounces American cheese, sliced
  • 4 ounces Swiss cheese, sliced
  • 2 ½ cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 6 eggs
  • ½ cup grated onion
  • ¼ cup chopped chives
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon paprika

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees. Butter a four-quart baking dish.
  2. At the bottom of the baking dish, arrange a layer of one-half the bread. Layer the American and Swiss cheese slices over it. Layer the remaining bread on top.
  3. Combine the remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl and beat.
  4. Pour the mixture over the bread and cheese and let it stand for one hour at room temperature. Bake for one hour. Remove from the oven and serve immediately.
