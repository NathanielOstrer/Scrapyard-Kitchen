Gin, Ginger Juice, Lime

Eric Asimov

ABC Cocina

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces dry gin
  • 1 ½ ounces pure coconut water
  • ¾ ounce fresh lime juice
  • Dash pressed ginger juice
  • Large ice cubes
  • Fresh lime wedge for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients with ice in a cocktail shaker and stir for 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with fresh lime wedge.
