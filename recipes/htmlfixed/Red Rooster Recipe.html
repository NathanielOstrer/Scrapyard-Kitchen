Ingredients

  • 48 ounces (about 3/4 of a 64-ounce container) Ocean Spray Cran-Tangerine Juice Drink (see note)
  • 6 ounces frozen orange juice concentrate, defrosted
  • 2 cups vodka

Preparation

  1. In a 2 1/2-quart freezer-safe container, mix together all the ingredients. Cover and freeze for at least 5 hours, or up to 1 week; the mixture will become slushy but will not solidify.
  2. To serve, spoon into martini glasses.

Tip

  • To make a Del Ray East Luray Snow Day-tini, substitute white cranberry juice for the Cran-Tangerine.
