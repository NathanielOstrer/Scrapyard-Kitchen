Black Peppercorn, Cumin Seed, Turmeric, Middle Eastern

Molly O'Neill About one-third cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons black peppercorns
  • 3 tablespoons cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons tumeric
  • 1 tablespoon ground cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander

Preparation

  1. Grind all ingredients together with a mortar and pestle or spice grinder. Store in an airtight container.
