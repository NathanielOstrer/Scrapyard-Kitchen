Cinnamon, Tequila

Jonathan Miles 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces cocoa-infused tequila (see note)
  • ½ ounce bourbon
  • ½ teaspoon simple syrup
  • Dash cinnamon tincture (see note)
  • Lemon twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine liquid ingredients in a mixing glass with ice and stir. Strain into a chilled rocks glass and garnish with lemon twist.

Tip

  • To make cocoa-infused tequila, add 1/2 cup pure cocoa powder to a 1-liter bottle of blanco tequila. Allow to sit overnight, then strain through a coffee filter. For the cinnamon tincture, toast 3 sticks cinnamon (2 minutes at 300 degrees) then crumble cinnamon into 12 ounces of high-proof vodka; steep for 3 days, then strain.
