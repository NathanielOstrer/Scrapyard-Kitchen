Raw, Arugula, Balsamic Vinegar, Tomato

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Small bunch arugula, to yield 2 ounces
  • 1 large ripe tomato
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon hot-sweet mustard

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim arugula; dry.
  2. Wash, trim and cut tomato into small wedges.
  3. In serving bowl, whisk vinegar with oil and mustard; stir in arugula and tomato.
