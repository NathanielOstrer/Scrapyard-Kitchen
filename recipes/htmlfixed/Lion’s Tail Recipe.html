Allspice Dram, Bourbon

Rosie Schaap

Grant Cornett for The New York Times. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Jocelyne Beaudoin.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. bourbon
  • ¾ oz. allspice dram
  • ½ oz. fresh lime juice
  • 1 tsp. gomme syrup (or more to taste)
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
