Lime Juice, Soda, Tequila

Rosie Schaap 1 drink Time5 minutes

Grant Cornett for The New York Times. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. reposado tequila
  • ½ oz. Élixir Combier
  • ½ oz. thyme syrup
  • ¾ oz. lime juice
  • Finely crushed pink sea salt
  • White peppercorn
  • Sugar
  • Dr. Brown’s Cel-Ray Soda

Preparation

  1. Shake tequila, Élixir Combier, thyme syrup and lime juice in a shaker filled with ice. Rim a highball glass with sea salt, peppercorn and sugar. Strain into glass. Top with soda.
