High Fiber, Blueberry, Mint, Strawberry, French

Craig Claiborne Four to six servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh blueberries
  • 4 cups fresh, ripe, unblemished strawberries
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 6 tablespoons freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 3 tablespoons Cointreau
  • 50 small mint leaves

Preparation

  1. Rinse the blueberries and strawberries and drain thoroughly.
  2. Put the berries in a bowl.
  3. Blend the sugar, lemon juice, orange juice and Cointreau and stir until the sugar melts. Pour this over the berries. Sprinkle the mint leaves on top and serve.
