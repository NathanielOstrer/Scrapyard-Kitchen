Buttermilk, Walnut, Eastern European

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey Eight or more servings Time5 minutes, plus chill time

Ingredients

  • 1 quart buttermilk
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped walnuts, preferably black walnuts although English walnuts may be used
  • 2 tablespoons finely minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons finely minced dill
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red-wine vinegar
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl. Chill and serve.
