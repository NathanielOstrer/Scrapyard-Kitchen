Raw, Apple Cider, Calvado, Cream, Southern

Betty Fussell 4 servings Time3 hours' standing

Ingredients

  • 1 cup apple cider
  • ¼ cup Calvados or other brandy
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg, freshly grated
  • 2 cups milk or cream

Preparation

  1. Put all ingredients in blender and blend until frothy.
  2. Pour into parfait or Sherry glasses and let stand 2 or 3 hours at room temperature.
