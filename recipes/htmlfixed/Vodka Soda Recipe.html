Ingredients

  • Ice
  • ¼ cup vodka
  • ½ cup soda
  • Lime juice (optional)
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a glass with ice. Add vodka and top with soda; stir. If you like, add fresh lime juice. Garnish with a lime wedge.
