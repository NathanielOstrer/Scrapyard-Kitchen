Coffee Ice Cream, Sweetened Condensed Milk

Steven Stern 1 milkshake Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups coffee ice cream, made with egg yolks
  • ¼ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • ¼ cup whole milk
  • 1 ounce Kahlúa
  • Whipped cream, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Combine ice cream and liquid ingredients in a blender or a milkshake canister. Blend at medium speed until just combined. Avoid over-blending. Pour into a chilled pint glass and top with whipped cream.
