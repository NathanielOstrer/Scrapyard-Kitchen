Aquavit, Elderflower Cordial, Lemon, American

Merrill Stubbs Serves 1

Ilan Rubin for The New York Times. Food Stylist: Victoria Granof. Prop Stylist: Michele Faro.

Ingredients

  • 1 strip lemon zest
  • 1 stripe lemon zest
  • 3 ounces Aquavit New York White Cranberry (go to www.aquavitnewyork.com for purchasing information) or plain aquavit
  • 2 ounces dry vermouth
  • Â½ ounce elderflower cordial
  • Pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. Put the zest in the bottom of a large martini glass. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add the remaining ingredients. Shake and strain into the glass.
