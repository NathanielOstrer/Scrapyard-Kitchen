Soybean

Amanda Hesser Serves 4 Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shelled soybeans (sold frozen in a bag)
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • ¼ cup seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 ½ cups canola oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 24 rice crackers, fried

Preparation

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil. Add the soybeans and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Drain.
  2. In a food processor, pulse together the soybeans and vinegars. With the machine running, add the oil in a slow stream. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice crackers -- either the discs or warped sheets. Ilene Rosen, the savory chef at City Bakery, calls this her version of rice and beans.
