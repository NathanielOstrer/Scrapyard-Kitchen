Vegetarian, Potato, Russet Potato

Jacques Pepin 4 servings Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • 4 large Idaho or Russet potatoes, about 8 to 9 ounces each

Preparation

  1. Move an oven rack to the center of the oven. Heat the oven to 425 degrees. Wash potatoes thoroughly.
  2. Place the potatoes on the oven rack. Do not wrap them in aluminum foil. Cook about 50 minutes or until tender.
