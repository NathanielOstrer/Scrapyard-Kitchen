Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Fat, Firm Tofu, Potato, Vegetable

Densie Webb 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large baked potato
  • 2 teaspoons margarine
  • 10 ounces firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • Pinch of turmeric
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • ¾ cup chopped red pepper
  • 1 cup slice fresh mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons salsa

Preparation

  1. Peel potato and cut into small cubes.
  2. Melt margarine in a nonstick skillet and add tofu. Add turmeric and cook over high heat 5 minutes. Add vegetables and cook 5 minutes, or until vegetables are tender but crisp. Stir in salsa and serve.
