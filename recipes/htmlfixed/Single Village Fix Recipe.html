Mezcal

Jonathan Miles 1 serving

Peter DaSilva for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces mezcal
  • ¾ ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • ¾ ounce pineapple gum syrup (see note)

Preparation

  1. Combine the ingredients with ice in a shaker, and shake. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and serve.
