Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Red Onion, Watercress

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches watercress
  • 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup sliced red onion rings
  • ¼ cup finely chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. Cut off and discard the tough stems of the watercress. Rinse and spin dry.
  2. Place the vinegar in a salad bowl and add salt and pepper. Beat with a wire whisk while adding the oil. Add the watercress, onion rings and parsley and toss well to blend. Serve.
