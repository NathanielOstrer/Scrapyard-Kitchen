Whole Almond

Amanda Hesser 2 cups Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almonds, blanched and skinned
  • Â¼ cup peanut oil
  • Coarse sea salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Place the almonds in a bowl, pour over the peanut oil and toss to coat. Spread on a baking sheet and bake until lightly browned and sizzling, about 10 minutes. Remove from the oven, season with salt and let cool. Once cool, season again, if needed.
