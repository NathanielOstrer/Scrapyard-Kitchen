Gluten Free, Cream Cheese, Salmon

Marian Burros About 1 cup Time7 minutes

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Neufchâtel cream cheese
  • 3 tablespoons plain nonfat yogurt
  • 1 6- or 7-ounce can or pouch of salmon, drained, bones and skin removed
  • 1 teaspoon grated onion
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoon chopped pecans

Preparation

  1. In bowl, mash cream cheese with yogurt until smooth.
  2. Drain salmon and add to bowl (for a more neutral taste, run drained salmon under warm water). Add remaining ingredients, and stir well to mash salmon.
  3. Serve with crackers or on little squares of thin pumpernickel or rye bread.
