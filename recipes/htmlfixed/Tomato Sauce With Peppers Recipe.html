Pierre Franey about 2 1/4 cups Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups canned tomatoes, preferably imported
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced garlic
  • ¾ cup chopped sweet green peppers, preferably long Italian
  • ¼ teaspoon dried, crumbled oregano
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Blend tomatoes thoroughly in food processor or electric blender.
  2. Heat oil in sauce pan and add garlic and peppers. Cook, stirring occasionally, about 3 minutes. Do not brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, oregano, salt and pepper. Simmer, uncovered, about seven minutes.
