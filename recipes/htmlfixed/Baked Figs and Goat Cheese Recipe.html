Vegetarian, Fig, Goat Cheese

David Tanis 6 servings Time25 minutes

Karsten Moran for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 8-ounce goat cheese log
  • 4 fresh fig leaves, optional
  • 10 ripe figs, stems on, halved lengthwise
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme or rosemary
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Cut goat cheese into 6 thick slices. Line an 8-by 12-inch earthenware baking dish with fig leaves (if using). Arrange goat cheese in center of dish and surround with fig halves. Season lightly with salt and pepper, then sprinkle with thyme. Drizzle with olive oil.
  2. Bake uncovered for 15 minutes, until both cheese and figs are softened. Run under broiler for 1 minute to brown. Let cool slightly before serving.
