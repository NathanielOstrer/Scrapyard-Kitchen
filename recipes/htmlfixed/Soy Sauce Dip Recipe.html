Dairy Free, Lime Juice, Soy Sauce, Thai Chili, Vietnamese

Molly O'Neill 2/3 cup

Ingredients

  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 red Thai chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped roasted peanuts
  • 4 teaspoons lime juice

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients, and stir until sugar is dissolved. Serve with pancakes.
