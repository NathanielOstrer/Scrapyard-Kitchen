Chipotles In Adobo, Kewpie Mayo, Southwestern

Sam Sifton Serves 8-10 Time5 minutes

Peden & Munk for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 5 canned chipotle peppers (‘‘en adobo’’)
  • 2 cups Kewpie mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons shrimp paste

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients in a food processor and pulse to combine.
  2. Adjust flavors, adding some of the sauce from the canned chipotles for heat, more shrimp paste for funkiness. Covered, the sauce will keep in the refrigerator for at least a week.
