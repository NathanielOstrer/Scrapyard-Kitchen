Banana, Flour, Nut, American

Glenn Collins 1 loaf Time1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 very ripe bananas (medium size), mashed
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ cup chopped honey-roasted peanuts or other nuts
  • ⅓ cup semisweet chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Cream together butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs, scrape bowl well and then add bananas, flour and baking soda.
  3. Mix in nuts and chocolate chips.
  4. Pour into a large greased loaf pan, 8 1/2 by 4 1/2 inches or 9 by 5 inches.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees 1 hour.
