Allspice Liqueur, Bourbon, Satsuma

Steven Stern 3 to 4 drinks

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces bourbon
  • 4 ounces fresh satsuma or tangerine juice
  • 2 ounces maple syrup
  • 1 ounces allspice liqueur

Preparation

  1. Shake all ingredients, without ice, in a large container. Pour into a punch bowl with ice. For large batches, use the same ratios.
