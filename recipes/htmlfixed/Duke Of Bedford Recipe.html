Orange Soda, Sherry

Jonathan Miles 1 serving

Evan Sung for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 3 oz. Dry Sack sherry
  • 6 mint leaves, crushed
  • 1 oz. San Pellegrino Aranciata or orange soda
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • Dash of Angostura bitters
  • Mint sprig and cucumber slice, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a rocks glass with crushed ice. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the sherry and mint, then add the Aranciata, sugar and bitters and shake well. Strain over the ice and garnish with the mint and cucumber.
