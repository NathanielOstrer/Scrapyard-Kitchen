Preparation

  1. Trim and quarter 2 fennel bulbs; cut 2 unripe pears in half. Use a mandoline to thinly slice everything; put in a large bowl. Toss with dressing and add shaved Parmesan. Dressing variation: Skip the vinegar and mustard. Reduce oil to ½ cup; add 3 tablespoons lemon juice and 2 tablespoons chopped parsley.
