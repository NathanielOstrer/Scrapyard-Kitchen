Nut Free, Heavy Cream, Maple Syrup, Pie Shell, American

Florence Fabricant 4 to 6 servings Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups pure maple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons melted sweet butter
  • A baked eight-inch pie shell
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Beat eggs until well-blended. Beat in maple syrup and butter. Pour the mixture into the baked pie shell, place in the oven and bake 25 minutes, just until the filling is set.
  3. Serve while still warm with whipped cream.
