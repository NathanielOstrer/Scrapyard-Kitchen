Plain Low Fat Yogurt, Smoked Salmon

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 thin slices smoked salmon
  • 1 recipe scallion-yogurt-cheese stuffing

Preparation

  1. Cut the salmon slices in half lengthwise and cut across into thin slices. Fill the potatoes with the scallion-yogurt-cheese stuffing and pile the salmon on top. Serve.
