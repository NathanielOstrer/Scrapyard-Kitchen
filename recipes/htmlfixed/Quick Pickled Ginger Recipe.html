Ginger, Radish, Rice Vinegar, Japanese

David Tanis About 2 ounces Time1 hour

Evan Sung for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 three-inch piece ginger, peeled and sliced very thin
  • 1 red radish, sliced or 1 slice red beet, optional, for color
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar

Preparation

  1. Put all the ingredients in a small jar and stir. Make sure the ginger is submerged. Leave at room temperature for 1 hour. Discard the radish or beet. The ginger may be refrigerated for up to 2 weeks.
