Cassis Syrup, Cranberry

Barbara Kafka One 4 3/4-cup mold

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups cassis syrup (not liquor or juice)
  • ½ cup water
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ pounds fresh cranberries (6 cups)

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a 2 1/2-quart souffle dish with a tight-fitting lid. Cook, covered, at 100 percent power in a high-power oven for 10 minutes.
  2. Uncover and stir well. Cook, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from oven. Rinse a metal bowl or mold with ice water. Pour in cranberry mixture. Allow to cool. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Just before serving, dip mold into a bowl of hot water for a few seconds and jiggle slightly to loosen. Invert onto a serving platter or into the well of a dinner plate.
