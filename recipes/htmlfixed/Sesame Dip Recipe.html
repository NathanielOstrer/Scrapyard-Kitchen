Dark Soy Sauce, Sesame Paste, Asian

Moira Hodgson 1 1/2 cups Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sesame paste
  • ½ cup sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hot chili oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice or white wine vinegar

Preparation

  1. Place the sesame paste in a mixing bowl. Beat in the sesame oil, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, chili oil and vinegar. Correct seasoning and serve.
