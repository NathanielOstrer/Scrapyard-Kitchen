Crown Roast Of Lamb

Molly O'Neill 6 to 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 crown roast of lamb, prepared by butcher
  • 6 to 8 cutlet frills (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Place the roast crown-side down in a roasting pan. Bake for an hour. Turn the roast over and place on a serving platter. Decorate each rib bone with a paper cutlet frill, if desired (they are available at Zabar's, 2245 Broadway, at 80th Street, Manhattan). Serve immediately.
