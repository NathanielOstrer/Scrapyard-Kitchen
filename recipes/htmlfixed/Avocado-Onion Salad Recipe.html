Vegetarian, Vegan, Avocado, Onion, Roasted Canned Pepper

Marian Burros 3 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe avocado, cut in small cubes
  • 1 small onion, chopped medium fine
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped freshly parsley
  • ½ teaspoon dried marjoram leaves
  • 1 roasted canned red pepper, sliced or 2 tablespoons sliced pimientos

Preparation

  1. Mix avocado and onion with vinegar. Add parsley and marjoram to avocado.
  2. Add pepper. Mix well.
