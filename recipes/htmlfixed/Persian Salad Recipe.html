Vegetarian, Vegan, Cucumber, Tomato, Middle Eastern

Mark Bittman 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 Persian cucumbers, chopped
  • ½ small white onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped purple or green basil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • ½ teaspoon dried mint
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Preparation

  1. Combine all of the ingredients in a bowl; toss, and serve.
