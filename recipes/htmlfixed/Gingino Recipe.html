Campari, Ginger Beer

Peter Meehan 6 servings

Gordon M. Grant for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces Campari or other amaro
  • 2 12-ounce bottles ginger beer
  • 12 ounces sparkling water
  • 1 to 4 tablespoons simple syrup, to taste, optional
  • 6 lime wedges, for garnish
  • 6 slices crystallized ginger, for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a pitcher, combine amaro, ginger beer and sparkling water. Stir. Sweeten if desired.
  2. Fill 6 tall glasses with ice. Pour drink mixture into each and garnish with wedge of lime and slice of ginger. Serve.
