Gluten Free, Kosher Salt, Mascarpone, Yellow Cornmeal, Italian

Molly O'Neill Eight servings Time1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 4 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 cups yellow cornmeal
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a large saucepan. Add the salt. Whisking constantly, add the cornmeal in a very thin, steady stream. Turn the heat as low as possible. Cook for 1 hour, stirring vigorously every 10 minutes. Add the mascarpone and pepper and beat until smooth and creamy. Serve hot.
