Raw, Ginger, Sake, Soy Sauce, Japanese

Shizuo Tsuji Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 knob fresh ginger
  • 6 tablespoons sake
  • 3 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons very finely chopped green onions

Preparation

  1. Make ginger juice by grating the ginger and squeezing the gratings in a cloth to extract the juice. There should be about one tablespoon.
  2. Mix ginger juice and all other ingredients in a bowl.
