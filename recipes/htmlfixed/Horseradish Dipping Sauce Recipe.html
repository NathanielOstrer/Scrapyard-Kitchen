Raw, Horseradish, Soy Sauce, White Vinegar

Florence Fabricant 1/2 cup Time30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons shredded fresh horseradish
  • 3 tablespoons imported light soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons white or rice vinegar

Preparation

  1. Combine above ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Allow to stand 30 minutes, then serve as a dipping sauce with seafood cocktails, grilled or batter-fried shrimp, fish or vegetables
