Orange Bitters, White Rum

Robert Simonson One drink

Theo Morrison for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce white rum
  • ¾ ounce Velvet Falernum
  • ¾ ounce fresh lime juice
  • ¼ ounce simple syrup
  • 4 dashes orange bitters
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • Mint sprig for garnish

Preparation

  1. Pour all ingredients except for mint into a tall glass. Fill glass with crushed ice. Blend ingredients with a genuine swizzle stick or a bar spoon for about 20 seconds. Add more crushed ice to form a mound on top, and top with mint.
