Fruit, Sugar, Turkish

Mark Bittman Time2 hours 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • chopped fruit
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground cloves

Preparation

  1. Combine 3/4 cups sugar, 1 cup water and chopped fruit in a saucepan; bring to a boil.
  2. When fruit is tender, cool slightly and purée.
  3. Cook off liquid, stirring, up to 2 hours.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon honey, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg and a pinch of ground cloves. When thick, cool and serve.
