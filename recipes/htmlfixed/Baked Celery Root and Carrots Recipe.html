Carrot, Celery Root

Moria Hodgson Time1 hour 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 6 medium-sized carrots, scraped
  • 6 small celery root, peeled

Preparation

  1. Put the oil in the Romertopf and add the vegetables. Cover and place in a cold oven.
  2. Turn heat up to 425 degrees and bake for 1 hour 15 minutes. The carrots should caramelize.
