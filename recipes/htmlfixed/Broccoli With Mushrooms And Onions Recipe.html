Dairy Free, Broccoli, Mushroom, American

Marian Burros 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound broccoli florets or 2 pounds whole broccoli
  • ½ to ⅔ pound mushrooms, washed, trimmed and sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup dry white wine

Preparation

  1. If broccoli is whole, cut into florets. Steam until tender but still firm.
  2. Meanwhile, saute mushrooms and onion in hot oil until onion begins to turn golden.
  3. Stir in wine and cook quickly to evaporate most of it.
  4. Spoon mushroom-onion mixture over broccoli.
