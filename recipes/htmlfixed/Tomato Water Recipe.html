Tomato, Vodka

Amanda Hesser Makes about 5 cups

Ingredients

  • 20 plum tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, peeled and chopped
  • ½ cup vodka

Preparation

  1. Combine all the ingredients and let marinate overnight. Purée in a blender, then let it strain through a jelly bag over a bowl. (This will take several hours.) Chill.
