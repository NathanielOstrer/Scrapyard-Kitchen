Baska Snap, Orgeat Syrup, Pineapple Juice, White Rum, American

Robert Simonson 1 drink Time5 minutes

Cheryl Gerber for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Baska Snaps
  • ½ ounce orgeat, preferably Orgeat Works
  • ½ ounce white rum, preferably Banks 7
  • 1 ½ ounces fresh pineapple juice
  • ½ ounce fresh lime juice
  • 21 drops Peychaud’s bitters
  • Lime wheel

Preparation

  1. Fill a pilsner glass with crushed or pebble ice. In a cocktail shaker half-filled with ice, combine all ingredients except the bitters and the lime wheel, and shake heartily. Strain, finely, into the pilsner glass. Top with the bitters and lime wheel. Serve with a straw.
