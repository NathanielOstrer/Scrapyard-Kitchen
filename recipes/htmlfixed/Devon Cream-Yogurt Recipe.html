Devon Cream, Yogurt, British

Jean F. Tibbetts 1 cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 6-ounce jar English Devon cream, available in many specialty food shops and some supermarkets
  • ¼ cup plain, unflavored yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and stir to mix. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
