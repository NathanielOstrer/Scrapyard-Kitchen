Ground Beef, Tomato

Marian Burros Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 fresh ripe medium tomatoes, coarsely chopped
  • 1 12-ounce can tomato paste

Preparation

  1. Brown beef in its own fat. Pour off most of drippings. Push meat aside; add onion and garlic and cook until onion is translucent. Add tomatoes and tomato paste. Divide mixture into two equal portions.
