Ingredients

  • 4 slices of good bacon
  • 4 big scallops
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • salt and pepper (a pepper mix is nice).

Preparation

  1. Cook 4 slices of good bacon until fully cooked, but not completely crisp; drain them on paper towels and cut each slice crosswise into 4 pieces.
  2. Quarter 4 big scallops and toss them with 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice, salt and pepper (a pepper mix is nice).
  3. Top each piece of bacon with a piece of scallop and serve with napkins.
