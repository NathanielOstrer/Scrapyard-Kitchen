Horseradish, Sour Cream, Tarragon, French

Moira Hodgson 10 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ to ¾ cup freshly grated horseradish
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • ½ teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh tarragon leaves, chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • Tarragon vinegar to taste
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine the horseradish, sour cream, mustard and tarragon leaves in a bowl. Mix thoroughly and add vinegar to taste. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Put the sauce in two small bowls.
