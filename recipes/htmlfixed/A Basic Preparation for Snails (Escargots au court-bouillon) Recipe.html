Dry White Wine, Snail, Vegetable, French

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey 18 - 60 snails TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 18 to 60 drained canned snails
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped carrot
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped onion
  • ⅓ cup coarsely chopped celery
  • 4 sprigs parsley, tied into a bundle
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup water
  • 5 peppercorns
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled

Preparation

  1. This is a basic preparation for snails. The snails are cooked and drained before being used in other recipes.
  2. Combine all of the ingredients in a saucepan or skillet. Bring to the boil and let simmer for 10 minutes. Let cool and drain.
