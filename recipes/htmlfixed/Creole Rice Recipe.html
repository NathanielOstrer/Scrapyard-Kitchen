Vegetarian, Butter, Converted Rice, Lemon Juice, Parsley, Tabasco Sauce, Creole, Cajun

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 cup converted or parboiled rice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon Tabasco to taste
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped flat parsley

Preparation

  1. Bring the water, with salt, to a boil in a saucepan. Add the rice and stir well. Bring back to a boil and simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Drain the rice well. Add the butter, lemon juice, Tabasco, ground pepper and parsley. Blend well and serve.
