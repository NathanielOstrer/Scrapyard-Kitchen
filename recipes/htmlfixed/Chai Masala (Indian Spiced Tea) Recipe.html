Cardamom Pod, Tea Leaves

Julia Moskin 6 cups

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons tea leaves
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely chopped ginger (no need to peel)
  • 2 green cardamom pods, cracked
  • ½ cup milk
  • Sugar, to taste

Preparation

  1. Pour six cups water, tea, ginger, and cardamom into a saucepan, bring to a boil and cook 3 minutes. Add milk, and sugar to taste, and boil 2 minutes more. Strain and serve.
