Beef, Boneless Chuck Steak, Carrot, Celery, Leek, Onion, Peppercorn

Craig Claiborne 1 pound of beef Time1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean chuck steak in one piece
  • 1 small leek, trimmed and rinsed well
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped onion
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped celery
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped carrot
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • 12 peppercorns

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients in small kettle or large saucepan. Add water to cover and bring to simmer. Simmer 1 1/2 hours until fork tender. Drain.
