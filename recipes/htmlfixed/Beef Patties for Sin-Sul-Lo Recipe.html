Ground Beef, Sesame Oil, Korean

Craig Claiborne 16 to 20 patties Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅛ pound ground lean beef, about 1/3 cup
  • ¼ teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt to taste if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon sesame seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon finely minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced green onion
  • 1 tablespoon Seasoning Sauce (see recipe)

Preparation

  1. Put the meat on a flat surface and press down into a circle about 5 inches in diameter.
  2. Sprinkle the meat with the remaining ingredients. Fold the meat over itself several times to blend. Chop the mixture with a knife for about 1 minute.
  3. Shape the meat into 16 to 20 equal portions. Shape the portions into small flat patties or rectangles, each of approximately the same dimensions.
