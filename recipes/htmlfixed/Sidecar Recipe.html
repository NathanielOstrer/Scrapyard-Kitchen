Cognac, Triple Sec

William L. Hamilton 1 drink Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Courvoisier
  • ¾ ounce Cointreau
  • ½ ounce of fresh-squeezed lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Shake ingredients with ice. Strain into a glass. For a sugar rim, swipe the rim with a lemon wedge; dab with sugar.
