Gluten Free, Almond Extract, Almond, Heavy Cream, Lemon Juice, Asian

Julia Reed About 1 quart, 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 ⅔ cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped toasted almonds
  • 2 ½ teaspoons grated lemon rind
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon almond extract

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour into an ice-cream machine and process according to manufacturer's directions.
