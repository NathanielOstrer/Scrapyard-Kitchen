Raw, Vegetarian, Vegan, Garbanzo Bean, Garlic, Middle Eastern

Marian Burros

Ingredients

  • 1 16-ounce can garbanzo beans, drained, with 1/2 cup of the juice reserved
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped coarse
  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon hot sauce
  • ⅔ cup coarsely chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. Place beans with 1/3 cup of the juice, garlic, lemon juice, cumin, hot sauce and parsley in a food processor, and chop. Add more juice if hummus is too thick. Do not overprocess, as mixture should have some texture.
