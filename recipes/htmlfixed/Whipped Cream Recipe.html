Heavy Cream, Sugar, Vanilla Bean

Jonathan Reynolds 4 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • Vanilla bean (or extract) to taste
  • 3 tablespoons sugar

Preparation

  1. Infuse the cream with vanilla bean in the refrigerator for 24 hours. Discard the bean. Whip the cream, sugar and vanilla extract (if not using bean) to soft peaks.
