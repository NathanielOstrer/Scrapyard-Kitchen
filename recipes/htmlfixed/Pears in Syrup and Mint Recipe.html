Raw, Corn Syrup, Mint, Pear

Jacques Pepin 6 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup corn syrup
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ⅓ cup shredded mint
  • 3 ripe pears (1 1/2 pounds), Bartlett, Comice or Bosc, whichever is least expensive

Preparation

  1. Mix the corn syrup, lemon juice, and mint together in a bowl.
  2. Peel the pears and cut the flesh into 1-inch pieces. Add to the mixture, and refrigerate until cold.
  3. Serve the pears in wine glasses with some of the syrup and mint.
