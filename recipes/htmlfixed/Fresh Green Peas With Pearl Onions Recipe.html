Pierre Franey 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pearl onions
  • 2 cups shelled fresh peas
  • 1 red sweet pepper, cored and seeded and cut into 1/4-inch cubes
  • 1 cup finely shredded lettuce
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Place unpeeled onions in a small saucepan, cover with water and bring to a boil. Drain. Let cool, then peel.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a small heavy saucepan with a tight-fitting cover. Add 3 tablespoons of water, bring to a boil and simmer over low heat for 10 minutes. Do not overcook.
