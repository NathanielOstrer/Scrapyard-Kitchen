Dry Sherry, Lemon, Mott's Clamato Juice

William L. Hamilton Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces Mott's Clamato juice
  • Dash each of celery salt,black pepper,Tabasco and freshly grated horseradish
  • Dash of black pepper
  • Dash of Tabasco
  • Dash of freshly grated horseradish
  • 2 ounces Tio Pepe dry sherry
  • Lemon wedge

Preparation

  1. Mix Clamato juice with spices and seasonings. Pour the sherry into an 8-ounce glass filled with ice. Top off with spiced Clamato juice, and garnish with the lemon wedge.
