Pork Tenderloin, Rosemary, Whole Grain Mustard

Marian Burros 4 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds pork tenderloins
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons grainy mustard
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh or dried rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry

Preparation

  1. Wash and dry the tenderloins.
  2. Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl and stir. Add pork tenderloins and turn to coat well with marinade. Marinade can be left on for 15 minutes or as long as overnight.
  3. Broil or grill tenderloins, cooking for about 10 minutes on one side, and turning and cooking about 5 minutes on other side. The meat should be only slightly pink.
  4. Slice in inch-thick slices and serve.
