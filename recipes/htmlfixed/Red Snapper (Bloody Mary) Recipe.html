Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Tomato Juice, Vodka

William L. Hamilton 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce of vodka
  • 2 ounces of tomato juice (The St. Regis uses Sacramento tomato juice.)
  • 1 dash of lemon juice
  • 2 dashes of salt
  • 2 dashes of black pepper
  • 2 dashes of cayenne pepper
  • 3 dashes Worcestershire sauce

Preparation

  1. Add the salt, peppers, Worcestershire sauce and lemon juice to a shaker glass.
  2. Add ice, vodka and tomato juice. Shake, pour into a highball glass, garnish if you wish, and serve.
