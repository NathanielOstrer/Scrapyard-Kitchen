Fish, Smoked Salmon, Jewish

Jason Epstein About 24 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds smoked salmon(preferably a mild variety like Gaspe Atlantic salmon or Western Nova),cut into small pieces
  • 1 medium red onion, peeled and very finely chopped
  • ½ cup finely chopped scallions
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons olive oil

Preparation

  1. Combine salmon, red onion, scallions, parsley, vinegar and oil in a large bowl and mix gently.

Tip

  • Tartare is nice served on cocktail-size sliced pumpernickel bread spread with unsalted butter.
