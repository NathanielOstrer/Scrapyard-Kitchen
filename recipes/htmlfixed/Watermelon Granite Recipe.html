Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Low Fat, Mint Leaves, Watermelon

Molly O'Neill Four to six servings Time2 hours

Ingredients

  • 3 cups watermelon puree
  • ⅓ cup superfine sugar
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 4 sprigs fresh mint

Preparation

  1. Combine the watermelon puree, sugar and lemon juice in a large shallow pan. Put it in the freezer and stir every 20 minutes until no liquid remains, about 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Serve with sprigs of fresh mint.
