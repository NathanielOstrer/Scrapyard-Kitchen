Cucumber, Red Bell Pepper

Marian Burros 5 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 2 ½ large red bell peppers, cut in julienne strips
  • 5 Kirby cucumbers, scrubbed or 2 large regular peeled cucum bers, cut into batons
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 ½ tablespoons chopped fresh chives

Preparation

  1. Whisk vinegar with oil in salad bowl and stir in scallions. Add red peppers and cucumbers; toss with pepper. Sprinkle with chives and serve.
