Vegetarian, Vegan, Carrot

Marian Burros 3 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound tiny peeled carrots
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
  • 1 heaping teaspoon Dijon mustard

Preparation

  1. Grate carrots in food processor.
  2. Whisk oil, vinegar and mustard in serving bowl; stir carrots in thoroughly to coat with dressing.
