Low Fat, Watermelon

Mark Bittman Time15 minutes, plus freezing

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Randi Brookman Harris

Ingredients

  • 1 pound watermelon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice

Preparation

  1. Cut the watermelon into small chunks (discarding the rind), and put them in a blender with the sugar and lime juice. Process until smooth, adding enough water (or fruit juice) so that the mixture liquefies. Pour into popsicle molds, and freeze.
