Lemon Juice, Orange Bitters, Tequila

Rosie Schaap 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces reposado tequila
  • 1 ounce fresh-squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 scant teaspoon simple syrup
  • Dash of Angostura orange bitters
  • 1 teaspoon egg white (optional)
  • Half-wheel of orange, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a shaker with ice, and add all ingredients except half-wheel of orange. Shake vigorously. Strain into an old-fashioned glass over ice. Garnish with the orange.
