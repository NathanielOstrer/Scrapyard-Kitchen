Corn, Crab Meat, Mayonnaise, American

Mark Bittman Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups corn
  • ½ pound crab meat
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • Flour

Garnish

  • Lemon wedges

Preparation

  1. Heat one tablespoon of olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Beat 1 egg, and add corn, crab meat, mayonnaise and Dijon mustard.
  3. Stir in enough bread crumbs to bind.
  4. Shape into cakes, dredge in flour, and brown in pan on both sides, about 8 minutes total.
  5. Garnish with lemon wedges.
