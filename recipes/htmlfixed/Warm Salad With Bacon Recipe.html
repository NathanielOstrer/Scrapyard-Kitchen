Bacon, Tomato, American

Mark Bittman Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Chopped slab of bacon
  • Olive Oil
  • Corn
  • Chopped scallions
  • Chopped parsley
  • Chopped tomatoes (optional)

Preparation

  1. Render chopped slab bacon in olive oil until nearly crisp
  2. Remove with a slotted spoon and pour off excess fat. (You define ‘‘excess.’’)
  3. Add corn (skip the saffron and onion) and brown.
  4. Return the bacon to the pan along with chopped scallions and parsley.
  5. Forget the cheese; chopped tomatoes optional.
