Buckwheat Flour, Russian

Fred Ferretti 18 blini Time2 hours 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buckwheat or rye flour
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 4 teaspoons granular yeast
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • Salt
  • 4 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons chopped dill

Preparation

  1. Combine the flours in a mixing bowl, and add the egg yolks. Dissolve the yeast in the milk and add it, stirring with a wire whisk. Add salt to taste. Set in a warm place, and let stand for about 2 hours, covered with a towel.
  2. When ready to cook, add the cream and chopped dill. Beat the egg whites and fold in. Cook in a crepe pan as with ordinary crepes.
