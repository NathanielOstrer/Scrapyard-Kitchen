Green Bean, Red Bell Pepper, White Mushroom

Marian Burros 2 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces green beans
  • 8 ounces white mushrooms
  • 2 whole roasted red peppers
  • 2 green onions
  • 5 Greek, Italian or French black olives
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 slices country bread

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim the green beans, and steam over hot water for about 7 minutes, until tender but still firm.
  2. Wash, trim and quarter the mushrooms. After the green beans have been cooking for about 2 minutes, add the mushrooms. When the green beans are ready, the mushrooms will be, too.
  3. Rinse peppers if they come from a can or jar, and cut into thin strips.
  4. Wash, trim and thinly slice the green onions. Pit the olives.
  5. Whisk the oil, vinegar and mustard in a bowl large enough to hold all the ingredients.
  6. When the green beans and mushrooms are cooked, cut the beans in half, and add to the dressing along with the mushrooms, peppers, olives and green onions. Mix well. Serve with one slice of bread.
