Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, Low Calorie, Olives, Spinach

Marian Burros 2 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh loose spinach or 10 ounces fresh packaged spinach
  • 1 large clove garlic
  • 8 medium French, Italian or Greek black olives
  • Pan spray
  • ⅛ to ¼ teaspoon hot pepper flakes
  • Few shakes salt

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim tough stems from spinach.
  2. Mince garlic; pit olives.
  3. Heat small nonstick pot and spray lightly with pan spray; saute garlic for 30 seconds.
  4. Add spinach, olives and pepper flakes; cover and steam spinach for a few minutes, until wilted.
  5. Season with salt, and serve.
