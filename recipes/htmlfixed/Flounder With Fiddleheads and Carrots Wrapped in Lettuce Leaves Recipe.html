Carrot, Fiddlehead Fern, Flounder Fillet

Molly O'Neill Four servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 flounder fillets, cut in half lengthwise
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into fine julienne and steamed
  • 1 cup trimmed and cleaned fiddleheads, steamed
  • 4 large lettuce leaves, blanched
  • 4 scallion stems, blanched

Preparation

  1. Season the flounder fillets with salt and pepper. Make a layer of carrots and then a layer of fiddleheads down the length of each strip of flounder. Tightly roll up each strip. Wrap each flounder roll in a lettuce leaf to make a neat package and tie with a scallion stem. Steam for 10 minutes. Slice the packages in half and divide among 4 plates. Serve immediately.
