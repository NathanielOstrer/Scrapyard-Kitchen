Nut Free, Grand Marnier, Mascarpone, Strawberry, Italian

Elaine Louie 8 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs, separated
  • 1 pound, 1 ounce mascarpone cheese (one containerful)
  • ½ cup Grand Marnier
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 6 dozen strawberries or enough blueberries, raspberries or peeled sliced kiwis for 8 servings, or 8 slices genoise cake

Preparation

  1. In blender, blend egg yolks for 1 minute. Add mascarpone and blend for 30 seconds. Push mixture down with rubber spatula, and blend 30 seconds more.
  2. Add Grand Marnier and sugar. Blend for another 30 seconds.
  3. Beat egg whites until stiff and glossy. Fold into sauce.
  4. Put sauce into refrigerator until serving time.
  5. Pour sauce into serving dish, and serve with fresh fruit or genoise cake.
