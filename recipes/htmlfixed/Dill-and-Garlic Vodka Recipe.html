Dill, Garlic, Vodka

Rosie Schaap

Victor Prado for The New York Times; Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Randi Brookman Harris.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup loosely packed dill, washed, patted dry, tough stems removed
  • 1 clove peeled garlic
  • 750 milliliters vodka

Preparation

  1. Put the dill, garlic and vodka in an airtight glass container (like a Mason jar).
  2. Seal tightly and refrigerate for 24 to 48 hours.
  3. Strain into a glass pitcher; add fresh sprigs of dill and a few cucumber slices. Serve very cold.
