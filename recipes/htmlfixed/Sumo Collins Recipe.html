Robert Willey 1 drink

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces gin
  • 2 ¼ ounces fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ½ ounces simple syrup
  • Chilled seltzer
  • 1 orange wheel

Preparation

  1. Pour the gin, lemon juice and simple syrup into a cocktail shaker, fill with ice, and shake vigorously. Strain into a 24-ounce glass filled with fresh ice, top with seltzer. Garnish with the orange wheel.
