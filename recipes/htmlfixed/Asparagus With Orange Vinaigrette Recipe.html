Asparagus, Mustard, Orange

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound asparagus
  • 1 teaspoon grated orange rind
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sweet hot mustard
  • 1 tablespoon orange juice

Preparation

  1. Wash asparagus, then trim by breaking at the point where the tough woody part of the stem meets the tender part. Steam over hot water for about 7 minutes, depending on thickness of spears; then, drain.
  2. Whisk the orange rind with oil, mustard and orange juice, and spoon over the asparagus, turning them to coat.
