Vermouth

Rosie Schaap 1 cocktail Time2 minutes

Davide Luciano for The New York Times. Food stylist: Claudia Ficca. Prop stylist: Gozde Eker.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz. Perucchi white vermouth, chilled
  • Lemon or orange slice or twist
  • Green manzanilla olive

Preparation

  1. Fill an old-fashioned glass with ice. Add vermouth. Give it a little stir. Garnish with citrus and olive.
