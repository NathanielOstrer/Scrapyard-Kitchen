Mark Bittman Time1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • 2 cups basil leaves
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • 2-4 tablespoons oil

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 375.
  2. Slice potatoes up to 1/4-inch thick. Skip the butter.
  3. In a food processor, combine 2 cups basil leaves, 1 clove garlic, 2 tablespoons pine nuts, 4 anchovy fillets, 1/4 cup olive oil and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan; pulse until blended, adding another 2-4 tablespoons oil until smooth.
  4. Toss with potatoes; layer in an 8-by-10 baking dish and bake until top is browned and potatoes are done, about 55 minutes.
