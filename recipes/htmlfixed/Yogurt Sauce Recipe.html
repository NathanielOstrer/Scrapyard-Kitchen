Raw, Vegetarian, Drained Yogurt, Garlic, Salt

Julia Reed 2 cups Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain whole milk yogurt
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed or pushed through a garlic press
  • Pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. Place yogurt in cheesecloth or a muslin-lined strainer and drain over a bowl for 1 hour. Mix with garlic and salt to taste.
