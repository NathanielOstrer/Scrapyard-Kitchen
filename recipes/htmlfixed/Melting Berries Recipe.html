Gluten Free, Berry

Barbara Kafka 6 servings Time1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 cups berries as below or in any combination:
  • 2 cups (12 ounces) blackberries
  • 2 cups (10 ounces) raspberries
  • 2 cups (12 ounces) strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 ½ cups sugar

Preparation

  1. Mix berries well with sugar. Leave at room temperature, stirring from time to time, until the sugar dissolves and you are left with a sauce.
