Garlic, Jalapeno, Mint

Melissa Clark 1 3/4 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons mint jelly
  • 1 cup tightly packed fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
  • 5 shallots, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. In a saucepan, combine vinegar and mint jelly. Bring to a boil, and simmer until reduced by half, about 10 minutes. Cool.
  2. Add mint, shallots, garlic, jalapeno and vegetable oil. Transfer to a blender, and puree until smooth. Season with salt and pepper. Serve as a condiment for grilled or roasted meats, vegetables or poultry.
