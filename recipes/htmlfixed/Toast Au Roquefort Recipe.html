Raw, Blue Cheese, Creme Fraiche, Whole Wheat Bread, French

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey Eight sandwiches Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces Roquefort or blue cheese
  • ⅓ cup creme fraiche or sour cream
  • ¼ cup coarsely chopped walnuts
  • 8 slices whole-wheat bread, sliced as thinly as possible
  • Chervil sprigs for garnish, optional

Preparation

  1. Put the cheese and creme fraiche into the container of a food processor or electric blender. Blend thoroughly. Scrape the mixture into a small bowl and add the walnuts. Blend well.
  2. Toast the bread on both sides.
  3. Spread one slice of toast with one-sixth of the cheese mixture. Top this with a second slice of toast and spread this with another sixth of the cheese mixture. Repeat with a third slice of toast and top with a final slice of toast. Press gently to make a sandwich. Prepare a second sandwich in the same manner as the first.
  4. Using a sharp knife with a serrated blade, carefully trim off and discard the crusts of each sandwich. Cut each of the trimmed sandwiches into four rectangles of equal size. Arrange on a platter and garnish with sprigs of chervil.
