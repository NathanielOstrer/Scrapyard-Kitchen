Cream Of Coconut, Pineapple Juice, Rum

Rosie Schaap 2 to 3 drinks

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces golden rum
  • 4 ounces pineapple juice
  • 4 ounces Coco Lopez cream of coconut
  • 3 cups ice
  • Pineapple slices, for garnish
  • Maraschino cherries, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Add rum, pineapple juice, cream of coconut and ice to blender. Blend until smooth and frosty; add more ice as necessary. Pour into tall glasses and garnish with pineapple slices and maraschino cherries (and paper umbrellas, if you have them).
