Vegetarian, Vegan, Tarragon, Tomato Juice

Molly O'Neill One cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tomato juice (see recipe)
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped shallots
  • ½ teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. In a medium-size bowl, combine the tomato juice, tarragon and shallots. Whisk in the olive oil, salt and pepper.
