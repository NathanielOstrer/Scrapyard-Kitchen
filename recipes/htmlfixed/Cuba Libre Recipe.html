Coca Cola, Rum

Jesse Mckinley 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces Coca-Cola
  • 1 ounce rum
  • ½ ounce gin
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • ¼ lime
  • Dash of club soda

Preparation

  1. Mix the Coca-Cola, rum and gin in a highball glass. Add the bitters, and squeeze in the juice from the lime. Top off with the club soda to add bubbles.
