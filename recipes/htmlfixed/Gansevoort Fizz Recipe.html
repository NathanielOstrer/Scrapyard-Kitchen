Rum, Seltzer, Whiskey

William L. Hamilton

Phil Mansfield for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Appleton V/X rum
  • 1 ounce Drambuie
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 ashes Peychaud's bitters
  • 2 to 3 ounces chilled seltzer

Preparation

  1. Shake first four ingredients with cracked ice; strain into a nine-ounce glass; top with chilled seltzer
