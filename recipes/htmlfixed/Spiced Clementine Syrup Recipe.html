Clementine

Rosie Schaap Time35 minutes

Ingredients

  • Per 3 cups fresh clementine juice
  • 1 cup water
  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 teaspoon whole allspice
  • 1 3-inch chunk fresh ginger, peeled.

Preparation

  1. In a large pan with a heavy bottom, combine clementine juice with other ingredients by stirring gently. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes. Remove from heat, strain the liquid (discard the solids) and allow to cool before making the punch.
