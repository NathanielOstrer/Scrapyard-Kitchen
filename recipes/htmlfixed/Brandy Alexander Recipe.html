Brandy, Creme De Cacao, Heavy Cream

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. brandy
  • 1 oz. crème de cacao
  • 1 oz. heavy cream
  • Nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Shake brandy, crème de cacao and cream with ice.
  2. Strain into a coupe.
  3. Grate a little nutmeg on top.
