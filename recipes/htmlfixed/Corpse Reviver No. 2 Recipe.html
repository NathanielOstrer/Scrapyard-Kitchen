Cointreau, Gin

Rosie Schaap

Photograph by Grant Cornett. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • ¾ oz. dry gin
  • ¾ oz. Cointreau
  • ¾ oz. Cocchi Americano
  • ¾ oz. fresh lemon juice
  • Scant teaspoon absinthe

Preparation

  1. Chill a coupe glass, and evenly coat the inside with absinthe. Shake remaining ingredients with ice. Strain into coupe.
