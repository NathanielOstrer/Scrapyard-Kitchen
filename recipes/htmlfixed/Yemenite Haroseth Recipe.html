Raw, Date, Fig, Sesame Seed

Marian Burros 2 1/2 cups

Ingredients

  • 24 dried figs
  • 24 pitted dates
  • ½ cup sesame seeds
  • 4 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon ground coriander
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more to taste

Preparation

  1. In food processor, grind the figs and dates to a sticky paste.
  2. Place in bowl and mix in the rest of the ingredients. Cover and store refrigerated for up to 2 weeks. Serve at room temperature.
