Dairy Free, Cherry, Ginger, Tarragon

Molly O'Neill 1 quart Time15 minutes, plus freeze time

Ingredients

  • ½ cup sugar
  • 4 cups pitted fresh or frozen cherries
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped tarragon leaves
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Preparation

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the sugar and 13/4 cups water. Bring to a boil and cook until the sugar is dissolved, about 2 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  2. Pass the cherries through a food mill or puree in a food processor and then pass them through a fine-mesh sieve. Discard the solids and place the juice in a large bowl. (You should have about 2 cups.) Whisk in the ginger, tarragon, lemon juice and sugar-water mixture. Freeze in an ice-cream maker according to the manufacturer's directions.
