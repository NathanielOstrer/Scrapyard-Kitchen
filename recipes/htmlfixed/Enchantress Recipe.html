Cognac, Port

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces port
  • 1 ounce cognac
  • ½ ounce orange Curaçao
  • ½ ounce lemon juice
  • Orange twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all liquid ingredients with ice. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. Garnish with an orange twist
