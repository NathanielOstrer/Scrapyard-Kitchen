Chorizo, Mussel, Onion, Spanish

Mark Bittman 4 servings Time15 minutes

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times; Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • ¼ pound chorizo, or linguiça, chopped
  • 4 pounds mussels, rinsed and debearded
  • ¼ cup white wine, optional
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Preparation

  1. Put the olive oil in a large pot that can later be covered, and turn the heat to medium. Cook the onion and chorizo, stirring occasionally, until the onion softens, about 5 minutes.
  2. Put the mussels in the pot; cover and cook, shaking the pot occasionally, until mussels open, about 10 minutes. If they have not generated enough broth for you, add the wine and cook, uncovered, another 5 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley, and serve.
