Vegetarian, Honey, Poppy Seed, Walnut

Craig Claiborne An 8-inch-long ''salami.'' Time10 minute, plus chill time

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups honey
  • ¾ cup ground poppy seeds
  • 1 ½ cups ground walnuts
  • ¼ teaspoon ground clove
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Bring honey to a boil, preferably in top of double boiler.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and blend well. Let cool.
  3. Put mixture on cold marble slab and roll into salami-shaped cylinder. Roll cylinder in wax paper and chill thoroughly. Serve sliced.
