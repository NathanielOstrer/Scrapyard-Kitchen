Baguette, Cheddar, Dijon Mustard, Italian

Moira Hodgson 24 crostini

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette cut into 1/2-inch diagonal slices (or 8 slices country-style bread cut into 3-by-3-inch slices 1/2-inch thick)
  • Dijon mustard
  • ¼ pound good-quality cheddar cheese
  • Cayenne pepper

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Bake the slices of bread until lightly browned (about 10 minutes). The slices may be toasted under a broiler or over a grill instead.
  2. Preheat broiler. Spread the slices with mustard, topped with slices of cheddar cheese and sprinkle with cayenne. Brown under the broiler and arrange on a serving platter.
