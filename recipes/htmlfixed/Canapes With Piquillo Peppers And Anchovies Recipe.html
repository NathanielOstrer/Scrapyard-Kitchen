Anchovy Fillet, French Bread, Piquillo Pepper

Mark Bittman 20 canapes

Ingredients

  • 10 3/4-inch-thick (roughly) slices French or Italian bread, cut in half
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 10 piquillo peppers, cut in half (or 4 or 5 roasted peppers)
  • 20 anchovies
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Lightly toast bread. Top each piece with a tiny bit of garlic, then layer with a piece of piquillo and an anchovy. Drizzle with a little olive oil. Serve within one hour.
