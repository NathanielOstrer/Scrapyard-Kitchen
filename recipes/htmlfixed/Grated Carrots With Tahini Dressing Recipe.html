Mark Bittman Time5 minutes, plus refrigeration

Sam Kaplan for The New York Times. Food stylist: Suzanne Lenzer. Prop stylist. Maeve Sheridan.

Ingredients

  • carrots
  • tahini
  • olive oil
  • lemon juice
  • cumin
  • harissa or other hot sauce
  • parsley.

Preparation

  1. Toss raw grated carrots with a dressing made with tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, harissa or other hot sauce, chopped fresh parsley and a little water to thin it out.
  2. Refrigerate until serving.
