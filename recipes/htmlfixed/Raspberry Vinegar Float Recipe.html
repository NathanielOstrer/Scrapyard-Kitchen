Raspberry Vinegar

Amanda Hesser 1 serving Time5 minutes

Tom Schierlitz for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce raspberry vinegar (see recipe)
  • 2 ounces pisco Italia
  • ¼ ounce fresh-squeezed lemon juice
  • ¼ ounce maraschino liqueur
  • Club soda
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • 1 sprig mint

Preparation

  1. Place the first four ingredients in a shaker and stir well to mix. Add plenty of ice and shake well. Strain into a tall float glass. Top the drink with club soda to taste (¼ to ½ cup). Carefully place a scoop of ice cream into the glass. Garnish with a mint sprig and serve with a tall bar spoon and a straw.
