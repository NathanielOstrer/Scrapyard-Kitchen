Vegetarian, Milk, Yam

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 yams or sweet potatoes, about 2 pounds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup warm milk
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ⅛ teaspoon nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Place the yams in a saucepan and add cold water to cover. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes or until done. Drain.
  2. Peel yams when cool enough to handle. Cut into 1-inch cubes and put them through a food mill or ricer. Put the yams in a saucepan and add remaining ingredients. Blend thoroughly, stirring until blended and hot.
