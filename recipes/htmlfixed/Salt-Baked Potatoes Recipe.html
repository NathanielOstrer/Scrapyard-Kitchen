Kosher, Kosher Salt, Potato, Rosemary

Jonathan Reynolds 6 servings Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 13-pound box kosher salt
  • 2 pounds fingerling potatoes
  • 6 sprigs rosemary
  • Good-quality fruity olive oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. In a 13-by-9-inch baking pan, add salt to a depth of 1/3 inch. Place the potatoes in a single layer on top. Scatter the rosemary, cover with the remaining salt and bake until tender, 35 to 45 minutes.
  2. Leave the potatoes in the salt until ready to serve. Brush off as much salt as you like. Drizzle with olive oil and serve immediately.
