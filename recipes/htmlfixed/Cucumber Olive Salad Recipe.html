Low Calorie, Black Olives, Cucumber

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 small Kirby cucumbers
  • 6 Kalamata or other large Italian, French or Greek black olives
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar

Preparation

  1. Wash, dry, trim and slice the cucumbers thin; place in serving bowl.
  2. Pit and chop olives and add to bowl.
  3. Add oil and vinegar to salad, and stir to mix well.
