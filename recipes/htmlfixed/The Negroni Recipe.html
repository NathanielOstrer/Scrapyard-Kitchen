Campari, Gin, Sweet Vermouth, Italian

Toby Cecchini 1 serving Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Campari
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth (Carpano Antica Formula or Martini & Rossi)
  • 3 ounces gin
  • Orange slice or lemon twist for garnish

Preparation

  1. In a shaker filled with ice, combine liquid ingredients. Stir until exterior beads with frost. Strain into chilled cocktail glass; garnish with orange slice, lemon twist or both.
