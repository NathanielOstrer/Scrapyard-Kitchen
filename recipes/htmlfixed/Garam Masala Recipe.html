Black Cumin Seed, Cardamom Seed, Cinnamon, Clove, Coriander Seed, Mace, Indian

Moira Hodgson Time35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces peeled cardamom seeds
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 ounces ground cloves
  • 4 tablespoons ground mace
  • 4 tablespoons coriander seed
  • 2 ounces whole peppercorns
  • 3 tablespoons black cumin seed

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Roast the cardamom seeds for half an hour without browning.
  2. Remove the pods and combine the seeds in a blender or spice grinder with the remaining ingredients. Grind all the spices very fine.
