Bourbon, Sugar

Rosie Schaap

Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Dash of water
  • 2 ounces bourbon (I like Elijah Craig 12-year-old, a great value).

Preparation

  1. Lightly muddle sugar cube with bitters and water in an old-fashioned glass. Add whiskey and ice.
