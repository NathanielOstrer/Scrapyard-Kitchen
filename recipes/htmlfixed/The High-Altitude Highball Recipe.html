Christina Holmes for The New York Times. Food stylist: Maggie Ruggiero. Prop stylist: Rebecca Conroy.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces Hakushu single-malt whiskey
  • ½ ounce Dolin Génépy des Alpes
  • 3 ounces club soda
  • Lemon slice.

Preparation

  1. Pour all ingredients into tall glass.
  2. Add ice, and stir gently.
  3. Garnish with a lemon twist.
