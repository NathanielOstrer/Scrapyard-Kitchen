Low Calorie, Low Fat, Low Sodium, Cauliflower, Ginger, Yogurt

Marian Burros 3 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds whole cauliflower
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • ½ cup nonfat plain yogurt
  • ½ teaspoon low-salt soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Trim cauliflower and break into florets. Steam for 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Coarsely grate ginger and stir into yogurt with soy sauce. Use as dipping sauce for cauliflower.
