Superfine Sugar

Melissa Clark 4 cocktails Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup superfine sugar
  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 12 ounces ice cubes (about 2 to 3 cups)
  • 4 ounces cherry juice
  • 4 ounces pear nectar

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, combine sugar with 1/4 cup warm water. Stir until sugar is completely dissolved. Stir in lemon and lime juice.
  2. In a blender, combine ice, cherry juice, pear nectar and 4 ounces lemon-lime mix. Blend until smooth. Pour into cocktail glasses and serve.
