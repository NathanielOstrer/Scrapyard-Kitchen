Lime Juice, Orange Curacao, Rum

Jim Meehan 1 drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rum
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • ½ ounce orange Curaçao
  • ½ ounce orgeat
  • Mint sprig, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake all liquid ingredients with ice. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice. Garnish with a mint sprig.
