Dijon Mustard, Olive Oil, Red Wine Vinegar, Shallot, French

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About one cup Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ tablespoons imported mustard, preferably Dijon
  • 1 tablespoon red-wine or malt vinegar
  • ¼ cup dry white vermouth
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped shallots
  • Salt, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper

Preparation

  1. Put the mustard and vinegar in a mixing bowl and add the vermouth while beating with a wire whisk.
  2. Add the oil gradually, beating vigorously with the whisk. Add the shallots, salt and pepper to taste.
