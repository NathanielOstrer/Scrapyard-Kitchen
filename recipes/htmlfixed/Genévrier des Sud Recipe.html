Cardamom, Gin, Tonic Water

Jeff Gordinier 1 drink

Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • Pinch of ground cardamom
  • 2 ounces gin, preferably Hendrick’s
  • Tonic water
  • 1 slice grapefruit
  • 1 basil leaf

Preparation

  1. Fill a cocktail glass with ice. Add cardamom and gin, and top with tonic water. Garnish with grapefruit slice and a basil leaf that has been clapped between your hands to release the flavor.
