Bread, Capers In Salt, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Red Wine Vinegar, Italian

Molly O'Neill 4 servings Time1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup capers preserved in salt
  • ⅓ cup fine red-wine vinegar
  • ⅓ cup best-quality extra-virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Rinse the capers and drain well. Pour the vinegar over the capers, tossing them gently, to cover. Soak for 1 hour and drain well. Pour the olive oil over the capers, toss to cover well and serve, with bread, before dinner.
