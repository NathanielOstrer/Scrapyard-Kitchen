High Fiber, Blackberry, Strawberry, Yogurt

Marian Burros 4 servings Time5 minutes, plus refrigeration

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ⅛ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ⅛ teaspoon mace
  • 1 tablespoon bitter orange marmalade
  • 1 pint strawberries, cut in half or quarters
  • 1 pint blackberries

Preparation

  1. Mix yogurt with nutmeg, mace and marmalade. Chill until serving time or serve over mixed berries.
