Bryan Miller about 1 1/2 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 7 scallions, white parts only, minced
  • ¼ cup minced fresh coriander
  • 1 tomato, peeled, seeded and minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced hot red or green chilies (or to taste)
  • 2 ounces white wine vinegar
  • 2 ounces water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl, and stir well. Prepare at least a half hour before serving. This sauce will keep for more than a week tightly covered in the refrigerator.
