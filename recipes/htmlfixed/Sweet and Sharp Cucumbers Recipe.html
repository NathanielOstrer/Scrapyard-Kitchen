Vegetarian, Cucumber

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ pound Kirby cucumbers

Preparation

  1. Mix vinegar and sugar in serving bowl large enough to hold cucumbers.
  2. Scrub cucumbers and slice in food processor on fine slicer. Stir into vinegar-sugar mixture.
