Coconut Water, Lime Juice, Simple Syrup, White Rum

The New York Times 1 drink Time2 minutes

Tony Cenicola/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce white rum
  • 1 ounce coconut water
  • 1 ounce coconut water simple syrup
  • ½ ounce lime juice
  • Pinch of mint

Preparation

  1. Take an ounce each of white rum, coconut water and coconut water simple syrup, a half-ounce of lime juice and a pinch of mint. Combine, shake with ice and strain into two shot glasses.
