Fluke, Lime Juice, Onion, South American

Florence Fabricant 6 servings Time10 minutes

Hiroko Masuike/The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless fluke fillets
  • 1 ⅓ cups red onion sliced paper-thin
  • ⅓ cup fresh lime juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 habanero chile, seeded and finely minced

Preparation

  1. Cut fish in 1-inch chunks. Place in a bowl. Toss with onions, then lime juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Place on a rimmed platter and serve at once, with a small dish of minced chile on the side for guests to add.
