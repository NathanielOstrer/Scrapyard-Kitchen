Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Green Bean, Savory

Marian Burros 2 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ pound fresh green beans
  • 2 teaspoons fresh savory (optional)

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim green beans.
  2. Cook in boiling water, 5 to 7 minutes, until beans are tender but crisp. Meanwhile, wash, dry and mince savory. Sprinkle over drained beans, and serve.
