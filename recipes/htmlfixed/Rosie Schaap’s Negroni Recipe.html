Campari, Gin, Sweet Vermouth

Rosie Schaap 1 drink Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce London Dry-style gin
  • 1 ounce Campari
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • Orange twist, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a mixing glass with ice and add gin, Campari and sweet vermouth. Stir well. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with an orange twist.
