Sticky Rice, Asian

Molly O'Neill About 4 cups Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups medium-grain sticky rice
  • 2 ½ cups water

Preparation

  1. Rinse and drain the rice. In a medium saucepan, combine the rice and water and bring to a boil. Lower the heat, cover the pot and simmer for 12 minutes. Turn off the heat and allow to stand, covered, for 10 minutes before serving.
