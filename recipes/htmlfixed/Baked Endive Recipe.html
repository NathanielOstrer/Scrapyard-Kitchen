Endive

Moira Hodgson 2 - 3 servings Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 heads endive
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup chicken stock
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Place the endive in a buttered baking dish and add the butter, chicken stock, salt and pepper. Cover with waxed paper or foil.
  2. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes, or until endive is tender.
