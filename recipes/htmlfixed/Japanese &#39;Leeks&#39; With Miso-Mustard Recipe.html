Leek, Japanese

David Tanis 4 to 6 servings TimeAbout 20 minutes

Evan Sung for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 6 negi (Japanese long onion), about ½-inch diameter, or substitute spring onions or small leeks
  • 1 tablespoon hot Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons brown rice miso
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim negi. Cut them to divide the white bottoms from the more tender green tops.
  2. In a covered bamboo steamer set over rapidly boiling water, steam white bottoms for about 4 minutes. Add green tops and continue steaming for another 4 minutes, until negi are tender. Set aside uncovered to cool.
  3. Stir together mustard, miso and vinegar. Transfer negi to a serving bowl and gently fold in dressing.
