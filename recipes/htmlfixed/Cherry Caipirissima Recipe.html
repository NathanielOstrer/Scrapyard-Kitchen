Cherry, Rum

Robert Willey 1 drink

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • ¾ ounce simple syrup
  • 3 cherries, pitted and halved
  • ½ a lime, cut into quarters
  • 2 ounces white rum

Preparation

  1. In a cocktail shaker, press and mix the simple syrup, cherries and lime quarters with a muddler or wooden spoon. Add the rum and 6 ice cubes. Shake hard and pour everything into a rocks glass.
