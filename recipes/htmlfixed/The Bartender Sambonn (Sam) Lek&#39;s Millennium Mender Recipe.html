Egg Yolk, Ginger Ale, Orange Juice

Molly O'Neill 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces orange juice
  • ¼ cup ginger ale
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • ¾ cup crushed ice

Preparation

  1. In a bar shaker, combine all the ingredients and shake vigorously. Strain into a highball glass and serve.
