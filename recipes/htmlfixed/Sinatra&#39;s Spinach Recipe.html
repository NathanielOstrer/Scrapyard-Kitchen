Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free, Spinach

Alex Witchel 4 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 (1-pound) can whole-leaf spinach
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup finely chopped red onion or other mild onion

Preparation

  1. Drain the spinach and place in a serving dish. Drizzle with the oil and toss to coat. Sprinkle with the onion.
