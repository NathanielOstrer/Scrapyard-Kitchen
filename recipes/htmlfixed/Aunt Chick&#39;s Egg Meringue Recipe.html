Egg White, Sugar, Vanilla

Molly O'Neill About 3/4 cup Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg white
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, sifted
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. In a clean bowl, beat the egg white until very frothy. Gradually beat in the sugar. Beat in the salt and vanilla. Continue beating until the mixture forms stiff peaks.
