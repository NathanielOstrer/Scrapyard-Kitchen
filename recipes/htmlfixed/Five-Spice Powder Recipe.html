Clove, Peppercorn, Star Anise, Chinese

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey About three tablespoons TimeAbout 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 60 peppercorns
  • 4 whole star anise
  • 2 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 12 whole cloves

Preparation

  1. Blend all the ingredients in a spice mill to a fine powder.
