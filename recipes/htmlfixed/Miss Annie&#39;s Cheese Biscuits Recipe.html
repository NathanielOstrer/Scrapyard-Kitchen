All Purpose Flour, Pecan, Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Southern

Molly O'Neill 18 - 20 biscuits TimeAbout 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
  • ½ pound margarine, cut into slices
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Up to 4 tablespoons water, as needed
  • 20 pecan halves

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Place the Cheddar cheese, margarine, salt, paprika, cayenne pepper and flour in a large bowl and, using your fingertips, begin to work them together. Add the water a tablespoon at a time until the dough comes together.
  2. Roll the dough into cylinders about 3 inches in diameter, then slice it into rounds about 1 1/2 inches thick. Place the rounds on a baking sheet, press a pecan half into the center of each and bake until the biscuits are golden, about 12 to 15 minutes.
