Raw, Egg, Garlic, Lemon Juice

Susan Herrmann Loomis 1 1/3 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon, about 2 tablespoons
  • 10 cloves garlic, peeled
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard
  • 1 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients except olive oil in work bowl of a food processor fitted with a steel blade; puree them. With food processor on, gradually add oil in a thin stream. Aioli will thicken, but it will be less thick than regular mayonnaise.
