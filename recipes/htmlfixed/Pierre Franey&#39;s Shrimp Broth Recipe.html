Shrimp

Pierre Franey About 3 cups Time25 minutes

Ingredients

  • The peeled shells from 1 pound of raw shrimp
  • 3 cups water
  • 6 peppercorns
  • ½ cup coarsely chopped onion
  • 1 rib celery, coarsely chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste if desired

Preparation

  1. Put all the ingredients into a saucepan and bring to a boil. Let simmer about 20 minutes.
  2. Strain the broth, discarding the solids. There should be about three cups.
