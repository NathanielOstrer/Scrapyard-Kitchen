Dijon Mustard, Tarragon Vinegar

Bryan Miller Three-quarters of a cup vinaigrette Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • ¼ cup tarragon vinegar
  • ⅔ cup olive oil

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, combine pepper, salt, mustard and vinegar. Mix well. Slowly add the oil while stirring.
