Sage

Jonathan Reynolds Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Sage leaves, rinsed and patted dry
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. In a medium skillet, heat 1/4 inch oil over medium-high heat until hot enough to toast a bread crumb in 30 seconds. Toss sage in flour, shake vigorously to remove as much flour as possible and fry, 30 seconds per side. Drain on paper towels, sprinkle with salt and pepper and serve with cocktails.
