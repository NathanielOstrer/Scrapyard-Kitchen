Apple Cider, Bourbon, Ginger

Jonathan Miles 1 serving Time45 minutes

David Corio for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces ginger-infused apple cider
  • ¼ inch piece of ginger
  • 2 ounces Jim Beam bourbon

Preparation

  1. To infuse the cider, peel and chop the ginger. Bring the cider to a boil, add the ginger, and turn off the heat. Steep about 30 minutes, then purée the mixture in a blender and strain through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth. (Refrigerate until ready to use.) To serve hot, heat the cider almost to a boil, add the bourbon, and serve in a mug. To serve cold, mix the cider with the bourbon and pour into a glass over ice.
