Wine

Robert Simonson 1 drink TimeAbout 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce red wine (a cabernet, zinfandel or similarly bold red)
  • 1 ounce Drambuie
  • 1 ounce rye whiskey
  • 2 dashes Fee Brothers Old Fashioned Aromatic Bitters (or 3 dashes Angostura bitters)

Preparation

  1. Chill a cocktail coupe by filling with ice or placing in freezer for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large glass, combine all ingredients and half-fill with ice. Stir until chilled, about 30 seconds, and strain into the coupe.
