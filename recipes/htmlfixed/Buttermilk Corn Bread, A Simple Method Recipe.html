Vegetarian, Buttermilk, Flour, Yellow Cornmeal, New England

Florence Fabricant 6 to 8 servings Time40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups yellow cornmeal
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Butter a nine-inch to 10-inch round baking pan or a cast-iron skillet.
  2. Mix cornmeal, flour, baking soda and salt together. Stir in the buttermilk and two tablespoons of the butter. Spread in prepared pan and bake 30 minutes.
  3. Brush with remaining melted butter. Serve warm or at room temperature.
