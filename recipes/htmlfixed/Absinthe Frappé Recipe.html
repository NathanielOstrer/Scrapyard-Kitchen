Absinthe, Heavy Cream

Robert Willey 1 drink

Andrew Scrivani for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ ounces absinthe
  • 1 ounce heavy cream
  • ¾ ounce lime juice
  • ¾ ounce simple syrup

Preparation

  1. Pour the ingredients into a cocktail shaker, add cracked ice and shake hard for 20 seconds. Pour it all into a highball glass and serve with a straw.
