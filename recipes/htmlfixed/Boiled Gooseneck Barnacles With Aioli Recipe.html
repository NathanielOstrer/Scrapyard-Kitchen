Gooseneck Barnacle, Lettuce

Susan Herrmann Loomis 4 appetizer servings Time7 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound gooseneck barnacles, rinsed
  • 12 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • ½ medium-size onion
  • ½ lemon
  • 6 curly green lettuce leaves, rinsed and dried
  • 1 lemon cut in four wedges for garnish
  • Aioli (see recipe)

Preparation

  1. Rinse barnacles; pat dry.
  2. Bring water to a boil with bay leaf, salt, onion and half lemon in a large saucepan over high heat. Add barnacles and stir. Reduce heat to medium high and cook until base of the barnacles turns a deep pink (no longer than 4 minutes).
  3. Drain barnacles and cover with ice until they cool.
  4. To serve barnacles, arrange on a platter lined with curly green lettuce leaves. Garnish platter with lemon wedges in a bowl of aioli. Serve immediately.
