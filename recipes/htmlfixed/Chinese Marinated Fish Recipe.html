Dry Sherry, Scrod, Sesame Oil, Chinese

Marian Burros 2 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons dry sherry
  • 1 teaspoon toasted or Asian sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon coarsely grated fresh or frozen ginger
  • 10 ounces fillet of scrod or other white fish fillet

Preparation

  1. Turn on broiler in oven or toaster oven.
  2. Combine the sherry and sesame oil. Grate the ginger, and add.
  3. Wash and dry fish. Coat it with marinade, until it's time to cook it -- just a few minutes.
  4. Remove the fish from the marinade, and broil it according to the Canadian rule: measure the fish at its thickest part, and cook it 8 to 10 minutes to the inch. Scrod will take about 5 minutes. Brush once with marinade.
