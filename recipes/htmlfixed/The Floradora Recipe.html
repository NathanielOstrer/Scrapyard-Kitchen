Vegetarian, Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Nut Free, Raspberry Syrup, Raspberry Vodka

William L. Hamilton

David Corio for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ Absolut Raspberri
  • ½ ounce fresh lime juice
  • 1 ounce Monin raspberry syrup
  • Ginger ale (about 6 ounces)
  • Lime slice
  • Edible flower optional

Preparation

  1. Put all ingredients, except ginger ale and lime slice, into a 12-ounce highball glass filled with ice. Top with ginger ale and garnish with lime slice and flower.
