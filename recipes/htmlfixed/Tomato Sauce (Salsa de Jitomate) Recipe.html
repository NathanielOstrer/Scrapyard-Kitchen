Garlic, Onion, Tomato Sauce, Mexican

Marian Burros 2 1/2 cups Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • ¼ cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Saute onion and garlic in oil until onion is soft.
  2. Add tomato sauce, chicken broth, salt and pepper and cook for 15 minutes at a simmer. Serve over chiles rellenos.
