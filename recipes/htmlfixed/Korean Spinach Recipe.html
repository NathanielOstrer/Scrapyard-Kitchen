Sesame Oil, Soy Sauce, Spinach, Korean

Molly O'Neill About 2 cups Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds (about 3 bunches) spinach, stems removed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced scallion
  • ¾ teaspoon toasted sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Bring a large kettle of salted water to a boil and have ready a large bowl of very cold water. Add the spinach to the boiling water and let cook for one minute.
  2. Immediately drain the spinach and immerse it in the cold water. When cool, drain again and gently dry it between several layers of paper towel.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the remaining ingredients. Add the spinach and toss. Serve at room temperature along with barbecued meat.
