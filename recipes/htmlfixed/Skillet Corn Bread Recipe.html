Vegetarian, Buttermilk, Cornmeal, Egg

Moira Hodgson 6 servings Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup yellow corn meal
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Preaheat oven to 425 degrees or prepare a fire so you will have hot coals in hearth.
  2. Put dry ingredients unsifted in mixing bowl. Mix in buttermilk and egg. Add 2 tablespoons vegetable oil and mix thoroughly.
  3. Put remaining oil in 9-inch cast- iron skillet. (If baking in fireplace use cast-iron skillet with legs, called spider pan.) Heat skillet in oven or over coals for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Pour batter into skillet and bake 25 to 30 minutes or until brown. Cut into 6 wedges and butter at table.
