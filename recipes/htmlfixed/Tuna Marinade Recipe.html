Lemon Juice, Mirin, Soy Sauce, Chinese

Robert Hughes 1/2 cup Time2 minutes

Peter Lippmann for The New York Times. Food Stylist: Juan Sanchez.

Ingredients

  • 1 scallion, trimmed, minced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons mirin (sweet rice wine)
  • 1 teaspoon light soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Whisk ingredients together in a stainless-steel bowl. Serve on the side.
