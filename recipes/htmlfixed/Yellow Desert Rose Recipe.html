Southern Comfort, Tequila, Southern

William L. Hamilton 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Southern Comfort
  • 1 ounce tequila
  • Orange juice
  • Grenadine
  • Slices of pineapple, orange and lime, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Shake liquors with ice; strain and pour into a 5-ounce Collins glass over fresh ice. Fill glass with orange juice. Top off with a drop of grenadine, and garnish with pineapple, orange and lime slices.
