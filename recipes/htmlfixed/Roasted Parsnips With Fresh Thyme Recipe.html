Vegetarian, Parsnip, Thyme

Florence Fabricant 10 servings Time1 hour

Ingredients

  • 15 parsnips, peeled and cut on a slant in slices 1 inch thick
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh thyme leaves

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Put parsnips in a bowl and toss with olive oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Spread parsnips on a large, heavy baking sheet or in a shallow baking pan. Put in oven and roast about 40 minutes, until tender and lightly browned.
  4. Put parsnips in a warm serving dish. Toss with thyme, adjust seasonings and serve.
