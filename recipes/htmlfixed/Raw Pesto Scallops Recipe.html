Ingredients

  • 6 large scallops
  • 1 cup basil
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ½ small garlic clove
  • ¼ cup Parmesan
  • salt and pepper

garnish

  • Toasted pine nuts (optional)

Preparation

  1. Slice 6 large scallops into thin rounds and put them on a plate.
  2. Put 1 cup basil in a food processor with 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1/2 small garlic clove, 1/4 cup Parmesan, salt and pepper.
  3. Process, stopping to scrape the sides of the bowl, until smooth.
  4. Spread the pesto over the scallops. Garnish: Toasted pine nuts (optional).
