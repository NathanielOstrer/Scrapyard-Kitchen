Lime, Red Wine

Jesse Mckinley Time2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • ½ lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 3 ounces red wine, preferably Rioja

Preparation

  1. In a highball glass, muddle the sugar and the lime wedges. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour the wine into the glass. Stir well, and serve.
