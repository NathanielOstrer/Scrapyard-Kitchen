Vegetarian, Dairy Free, Gluten Free, Egg White, Shredded Sweetened Coconut, Jewish

Molly O'Neill About 40 cookies Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 5 ⅓ cups sweetened flake coconut
  • 3 egg whites
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees. With your hands, mix all the ingredients together. Make clumps about inch in diameter and about 1 inch high. Place the clumps about 1 inch apart on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes.
