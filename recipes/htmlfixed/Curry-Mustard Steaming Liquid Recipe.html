Vegetarian, Vegan, Curry, Dijon Mustard

Molly O'Neill About 3 1/2 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 3 ½ cups water

Preparation

  1. Whisk together the mustard, curry powder and water. Place in the desired steaming equipment. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 minute. Begin steaming. Add boiling water if too much of the liquid evaporates before cooking is complete.
