Beer, Bourbon, Orange Bitters

Rosie Schaap Two cocktails

Tom Schierlitz for The New York Times. Prop stylist: Theo Vamvounakis.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. Bulleit Bourbon
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • Krusovice Cerne beer to top
  • Grated nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Shake bourbon and bitters over ice.
  2. Strain into a highball over fresh ice.
  3. Top up with beer.
  4. Grate a little nutmeg on top.
