Carrot, Heavy Cream, Mushroom, French

Pierre Franey 4 servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ pound mushrooms
  • 4 or 5 small carrots, about 3/4 pound, trimmed and scraped
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • ¼ cup water
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. If the mushrooms are small, leave them whole. If they are large, cut them lengthwise in half. There should be about two and one-half cups.
  2. Cut the carrots into thin, quarter-inch rounds. There should be about one and one-quarter cups.
  3. Heat the butter in a saucepan and add the mushrooms, shaking the skillet and tossing them so that they cook evenly, about two minutes. Add the carrots, celery, water, salt and pepper and cover closely. Cook eight to 10 minutes.
  4. Add the cream and cook down over high heat about two minutes. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve hot.
