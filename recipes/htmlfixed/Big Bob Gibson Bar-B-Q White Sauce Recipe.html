Barbecue

Annemarie Conte 4 cups Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 1 cup distilled white vinegar
  • ½ cup apple juice
  • 2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and blend well. Store refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks. Serve with grilled chicken.
