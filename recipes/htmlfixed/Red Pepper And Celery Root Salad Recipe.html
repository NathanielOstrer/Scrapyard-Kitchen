Vegetarian, Vegan, Celery Root, Red Bell Pepper

Marian Burros 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 large red bell peppers
  • 2 large celery roots
  • 4 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons dried chervil
  • 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash, core and seed peppers and cut into very fine julienne strips. Julienne celery root by hand or with extra-fine julienne blade of a food processor.
  2. Whisk together vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, chervil, tarragon and 2 teaspoons parsley. Whisk in olive oil. Season with pepper.
  3. Combine peppers and celery root. Pour the dressing over vegetables and mix well. Refrigerate at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  4. To serve, drain off excess dressing, spoon salad into a bowl and sprinkle with remaining parsley.
