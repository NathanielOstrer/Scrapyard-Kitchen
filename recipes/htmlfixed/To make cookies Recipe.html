Egg, Milk, Oatmeal Cookie Mix

Marian Burros 2 to 2 1/2 dozen Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups oatmeal cookie mix
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Preparation

  1. Place mix in bowl; add egg, milk and vanilla: mix well.
  2. Drop from teaspoon onto greased baking sheets.
  3. Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes, until golden. Remove from sheet and store. Keeps eight months.

Tip

  • Variations for two cups mix -one-half cup chopped nuts, raisins or dates.
