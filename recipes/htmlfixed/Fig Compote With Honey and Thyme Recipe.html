Dried Fig, Honey, Thyme

Molly O'Neill 4 servings TimeAbout 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • ¾ cup honey
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 pound dried figs, stemmed

Preparation

  1. Place the water, honey, sugar and thyme in a large saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer and add the figs. Cook until figs are softened, about 10 minutes. Remove the figs to a bowl with a slotted spoon. Increase the heat and reduce the liquid to 1 cup. Let cool slightly and pour the liquid over the figs. Serve at room temperature.
