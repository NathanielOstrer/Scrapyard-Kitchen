Raw, Chutney, Curry Powder, Egg

About two cups of mayonnaise Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup curry powder
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chutney
  • 1 ½ tablespoons tarragon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 ¾ cup soy or light vegetable oil, approximately

Preparation

  1. Put all the ingredients except the oil in a food processor or mixing bowl. If mixing by hand, it is advisable to chop the chutney into small pieces.
  2. Puree or whisk together, slowly adding the oil to reach desired mayonnaise consistency. Taste and adjust salt and pepper seasoning.
