Pomegranate Juice, Sake, Vermouth

Keith Dixon 1 cocktail (about 0.74 ounces alcohol, depending on sake) Time5 minutes

Evan Sung for The New York Times

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces dry, filtered sake
  • 1 ounce pomegranate juice
  • ½ ounce orange liqueur
  • ½ ounce vermouth bianco (or substitute sweet vermouth)
  • 4 pomegranate seeds
  • 1 mint leaf

Preparation

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add all ingredients except pomegranate seeds and mint leaf. Stir rapidly until outside of shaker is frosted. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with pomegranate seeds and mint.
