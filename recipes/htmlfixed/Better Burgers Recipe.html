Low Fat, Dijon Mustard, Ground Turkey Breast, Ketchup, Lean Ground Beef, Worcestershire Sauce

Marian Burros 4 servings Time15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound ground turkey breast
  • ¾ pound very lean ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon oil

Preparation

  1. Put all the ingredients except the oil in a food processor and grind completely, but not too finely.
  2. Shape into 4 patties.
  3. Heat a heavy skillet, preferably one with a non-stick lining, until it is very hot. Grease lightly with oil, and saute burgers until they are crusty on both sides and medium done inside.
