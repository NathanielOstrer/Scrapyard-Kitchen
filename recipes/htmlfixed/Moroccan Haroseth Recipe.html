Kosher, Date, Passover Red Wine, Spice, Walnut, Jewish, Moroccan

Molly O'Neill Six to eight servings Time45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pitted dates, chopped
  • 1 ½ cups sweet red Passover wine
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 cup walnuts, coarsely chopped

Preparation

  1. Put the dates in a pan with the wine and seasonings and simmer until the mixture resembles a soft paste, about 30 to 40 minutes, stirring occasionally. Cool. Add the walnuts and serve.
