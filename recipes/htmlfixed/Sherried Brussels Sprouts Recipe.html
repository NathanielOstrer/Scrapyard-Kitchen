Brussels Sprout

Marian Burros 3 servings Time10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 15 ounces brussels sprouts
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons sherry vinegar

Preparation

  1. Wash and trim brussels sprouts.
  2. Place in steamer basket, and place basket in pot in which bow ties are cooking. Cook brussels sprouts about 7 minutes, until they are tender but firm. Remove and drain.
  3. Combine oil and vinegar, and stir sprouts in.
