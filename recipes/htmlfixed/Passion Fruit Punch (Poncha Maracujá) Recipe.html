Cachaca, Honey, Passion Fruit, Brazilian

Florence Fabricant 4 drinks

Robert Presutti for The New YorkTimes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups passion fruit juice
  • 4 teaspoons honey
  • 1 cup cachaça

Preparation

  1. Place 1/2 cup juice and the honey in a pitcher. Stir to dissolve honey. Stir in remaining juice.
  2. Add cachaa and stir. Place ice in 4 highball glasses; pour juice mixture over it. Serve with straws.
