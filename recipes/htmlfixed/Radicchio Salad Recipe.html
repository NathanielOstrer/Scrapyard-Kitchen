Raw, Vegetarian, Vegan, Balsamic Vinegar, Olive Oil, Radicchio, Italian

Marian Burros 2 servings Time5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 small head radicchio (2 ounces)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon Dijon mustard

Preparation

  1. Trim, wash and separate leaves of radicchio.
  2. Whisk together oil, vinegar and mustard in serving bowl; add radicchio, and stir to coat well.
