Egg White, Sugar

Craig Claiborne About 30 meringues Time2 hours 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 egg whites
  • ¾ cup sugar

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
  2. Put whites into bowl of electric mixer and start beating on high speed while gradually adding sugar. Continue beating until stiff.
  3. Outfit pastry bag with No. 9 round pastry tube. Fill bag with mixture.
  4. Line baking sheet with parchment paper (available in specialty kitchen-equipment shops). If parchment paper is not used, grease baking sheet lightly with butter. Squeeze out meringue into small mounds, slightly pointed on top (''kisses''). There will be about 30.
  5. Place in oven and bake for 2 hours. When done, meringues should be dried throughout and should not brown. Leftover meringues will keep for weeks if stored in airtight container.
