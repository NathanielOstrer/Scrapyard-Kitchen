Vegetarian, Dried Fruit, Dry Red Wine, Honey

Florence Fabricant 6 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup slivered orange peel
  • ½ cup honey
  • 2 pounds mixed dried fruit
  • 1 bottle dry red wine
  • ½ cup (approximately) orange juice
  • 2 cinnamon sticks

Preparation

  1. Place the orange peel and honey in a heavy sauce pan, simmer slowly about 10 minutes, until the peel begins to look translucent.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and simmer about 40 minutes, until the fruit is tender. If too much of the liquid evaporates during cooking, add a little additional orange juice or some water.
  3. Cool to room temperature, remove cinnamon sticks, then chill before serving. Spoon into individual goblets or bowls.
