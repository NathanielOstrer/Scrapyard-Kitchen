Nut Free, Blue Cheese, Shrimp, Spinach

Craig Claiborne And Pierre Franey Eight or more servings Time20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 6 tablespoons flour
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups milk
  • ¼ pound blue cheese, crumbled
  • ½ pound fresh spinach
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste, if desired
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • ½ pound fresh shrimp, shelled and deveined

Preparation

  1. Heat the butter in a kettle or saucepan and add the onions. Cook, stirring, about three minutes.
  2. Sprinkle the mixture with the flour and stir. Add the chicken broth and bring to the boil. Add the milk and cook about five minutes.
  3. Add the cheese and cook, stirring, until melted.
  4. Meanwhile, pick over the spinach and remove and discard any tough stems and blemished leaves. Rinse and drain the spinach well. Coarsely chop the spinach and add it to the soup. Cook, stirring, about five minutes. Add the cream, salt and pepper and bring to the boil.
  5. Meanwhile, cut the shrimp into small pieces. There should be about one cup. Add the shrimp to the soup and bring to the boil again. Serve.
